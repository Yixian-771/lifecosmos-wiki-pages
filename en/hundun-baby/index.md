# Hundun Baby

Hundun Baby is the dharma name Xuefeng uses within Lifechanyuan, marking the level of wisdom from which he speaks on the way of the cosmos and life — embodiment of gods, Buddhas, celestials, and saints; messenger of the Greatest Creator; the Dharmakāya of Xuefeng's three-body structure; and the only bridge by which humanity may reach the Thousandyear World, the Tenthousandyear World, and the Elysium Islands of the Celestial Islands Continent.
