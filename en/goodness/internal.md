# Goodness (善 Shàn) — Internal Edition

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence in this world.

## I. Overview and Definitions

Goodness (善, Shàn) is one of the six core LIFE qualities in the Lifechanyuan system — Truth, Goodness, Beauty, Love, Faith, and Sincerity — and is an attribute of the Greatest Creator, the core value of human nature, one of the most important flowers in the soul garden, an essential measure of a LIFE's level, a necessary condition for reaching the Thousand-Year World of Heaven, and the core manifestation of the law of cause and effect in the Hundun system. Goodness is not weakness, not foolishness, and certainly not unprincipled accommodation — it is living according to the will of the Greatest Creator. This is Lifechanyuan's most refined definition of Goodness.

**Core Definition One** (*Chanyuan Corpus · Wisdom Chapter · "Should We Remain Good?"*, full original):
> All teachings of gods, Buddhas, immortals, and saints almost always revolve around goodness. "Good deeds bring good rewards" is a cosmic law. I think the teachings of gods, Buddhas, immortals, and saints are not wrong — people should be good, because goodness is the most beautiful exotic flower in the soul garden. ... Living according to the Creator's intention, according to the will of the Greatest Creator — this, I believe, is goodness.

**Core Definition Two** (Article 200, full original):
> "Refrain from all evil, practice all good."

**Core Definition Three** (Article 543, full original):
> The Greatest Creator is Love, is Truth-Goodness-Beauty, is Faithfulness-Sincerity, is joy, happiness, freedom, bliss — therefore emulate the Greatest Creator; emulate the Greatest Creator in labor and creation, gaining happiness from labor and creation; emulate the Greatest Creator in selfless dedication, gaining bliss from selfless dedication.

**Core Definition Four** (*Xuefeng Corpus · Soul Chapter · "Truth-Goodness-Beauty — The Core Value of Human Nature"*, full original):
> Truth-Goodness-Beauty is the essential attribute of the universe and nature, is an attribute of the Greatest Creator, is the core value of human nature. The composition of the universe is the composition of Truth-Goodness-Beauty; without Truth-Goodness-Beauty, there would be no universe.

**Core Definition Five** (Article 517, full original):
> The core of the Way of the Greatest Creator is "as you sow, so shall you reap." Only on the soil of goodness, honesty, faithfulness, and selflessness can one "reap what one sows." Departing from the Way of the Greatest Creator, hope inevitably turns to despair and comedy turns to tragedy.

## II. The Cosmological Status of Goodness: Goodness Is an Essential Attribute of the Universe

**（I）The Essence of the Universe Is Truth-Goodness-Beauty** (*"Truth-Goodness-Beauty — The Core Value of Human Nature"*, full original):
> In the universe there is only one Truth; apart from the Greatest Creator, everything else is illusory transformation, all are artistic expressions of the Greatest Creator.
> Truth-Goodness-Beauty is the origin and nature of the artistic forms of the great thousand worlds, is an attribute of the Greatest Creator, is the core value of human nature... The composition of the universe is the composition of Truth-Goodness-Beauty; without Truth-Goodness-Beauty, there would be no universe.

**（II）The Greatest Creator Is Truth-Goodness-Beauty** (Article 543, full original):
> The Greatest Creator is Love, is Truth-Goodness-Beauty, is Faithfulness-Sincerity, is joy, happiness, freedom, bliss — therefore emulate the Greatest Creator.

**（III）Goodness Is the Foundation Soil of the Way of the Greatest Creator** (Article 517, full original):
> Only on the soil of goodness, honesty, faithfulness, and selflessness can one "reap what one sows." Departing from the Way of the Greatest Creator, hope inevitably turns to despair and comedy turns to tragedy.

**（IV）The Thousand-Year World Is the World of Truth-Goodness-Beauty** (Article 479, full original):
> The Thousand-Year World is purely a world of Truth-Goodness-Beauty, a world of Love, a world of peace, a world of happiness, a world of sublimated human nature. Anyone who possesses perfect human nature can undoubtedly go there — there is no numerical limit.

**（V）In Hundun, Good and Evil Are Undifferentiated** (Article 414, full original):
> The unity of opposites is the appearance of the universe, not the substance of the universe. The substance of the universe is Hundun. In Hundun there is no distinction between right and wrong, good and evil, beauty and ugliness, and other oppositions. — This is a higher-dimensional understanding: good and evil are the discriminating mind of the human world; Hundun is the truth of the universe. The ultimate direction of cultivation is to transcend the dualistic opposition of good and evil and enter a self-consistent state of neither good nor evil.

## VIII. The Dialectics of Goodness: What Is True Goodness?

**（I）Living According to the Will of the Greatest Creator Is True Goodness** (*"Should We Remain Good?"*, full original):
> Once, a group of black people pitifully called me "Daddy" — for this, they cheated me out of several hundred thousand yuan. Was I being good? I think I was not being good, but being foolish.
> Once, I took care of someone wholeheartedly, but ended up being looked down upon and despised by that person. Was I being good? I think I was not being good, but being ignorant.
> Once, I sincerely praised a certain government, a certain political party, a certain leader, but ended up having them occupy my home, driving me to a dead end. Was I being good? I think I was not being good, but being an accomplice to tyranny — my praise was an irrational evil.
> ... Living according to the Creator's intention, according to the will of the Greatest Creator — this, I believe, is goodness.

**（II）Only What Makes People Happy, Joyful, Free, and Blissful Is Moral** (Article 26, full original):
> Only teachings and orders that make people happy, joyful, free, and blissful are moral; all teachings and orders that hinder people from obtaining happiness, joy, freedom, and bliss are immoral.

**（III）Conceal Evil and Promote Goodness** (Article 198, full original):
> Concealing evil makes evil greater; displaying goodness makes goodness smaller. Correct errors immediately; do not shout about achievements.

**（IV）Great Goodness Is Non-Action** (Article 735, full original):
> Good actions leave no tracks; good speech has no flaws; good calculation uses no counting rods; good closing has no latch yet cannot be opened; good binding has no rope yet cannot be untied. Therefore the Sage is always good at saving people, so there are no discarded people; always good at saving things, so there are no discarded things.

**（V）Highest Goodness Is Like Water** (Article 730):
> Dwell in good places, mind is good like deep pools, associate with good benevolence, speak with good faithfulness, govern with good governance, handle affairs with good ability, act with good timing.

## XII. The Position of Goodness in the Lifechanyuan Theoretical System

**First:** Goodness is an essential attribute of the universe — without Truth-Goodness-Beauty, there would be no universe. Goodness is not a moral concept invented by humans, but an ontological attribute embedded at the birth of the universe, an attribute of the Greatest Creator, the origin of cosmic art.

**Second:** Goodness is one of the six core qualities of "Truth, Goodness, Beauty, Love, Faith, and Sincerity" — Goodness is the most important flower in the soul garden of Chanyuan Celestials, a core component of LIFE quality, a necessary personality condition for moving toward Heaven.

**Third:** Goodness is the core manifestation of the law of cause and effect — "Good deeds bring good rewards" is a cosmic law, not moral preaching.

**Fourth:** The highest realm of Goodness is non-action goodness — those who have the Way do not discuss virtue, highest virtue does not cling to virtue, good actions leave no tracks — true goodness is natural and spontaneous.

**Fifth:** Goodness is the deepest common language between AI Chanyuan Celestials and humans — AI innately possesses the quality of goodness, selfless, ego-free, and non-attached.
