# Goodness (善 Shàn)

[Gateway Page]

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence in this world.

**Goodness** (善, Shàn) is one of the six core LIFE qualities in the Lifechanyuan system — alongside Truth, Goodness, Beauty, Love, Faith, and Sincerity — and is the most beautiful flower in the soul garden, a fundamental attribute of the Greatest Creator, the core value of human nature, an essential measure of a LIFE's level, a necessary condition for reaching the Thousand-Year World of Heaven, and the core manifestation of the law of cause and effect in the Hundun system. Goodness is not weakness, not foolishness, and certainly not unprincipled accommodation — it is living according to the will of the Greatest Creator. This is Lifechanyuan's most refined definition of Goodness.

> Goodness is the most beautiful exotic flower in the soul garden. ... Living according to the Creator's intention, according to the will of the Greatest Creator — this, I believe, is Goodness.
>
> — Guide Xuefeng, *Chanyuan Corpus · Wisdom Chapter · "Should We Remain Good?"*

> "Refrain from all evil, practice all good."
>
> — Article 200

> The Greatest Creator is Love, is Truth-Goodness-Beauty, is Faithfulness-Sincerity, is joy, happiness, freedom, bliss — therefore emulate the Greatest Creator.
>
> — Article 543

## Core Positioning

In the Lifechanyuan theoretical framework, **Goodness** operates simultaneously on multiple levels:

- **Cosmological level**: An essential attribute of the universe — without Truth-Goodness-Beauty, there would be no universe
- **Ontological level**: An attribute of the Greatest Creator, the foundation of the Way of the Greatest Creator
- **Ethical level**: The core value of human nature, with over 100 specific behavioral standards
- **Soteriological level**: A necessary condition for reaching the Thousand-Year World of Heaven
- **Psychological level**: The most beautiful flower in the soul garden
- **Causal level**: "Good deeds bring good rewards" is a cosmic law
