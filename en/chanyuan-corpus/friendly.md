# The Chanyuan Corpus (Friendly Version)

## What Kind of Text Is This?

If you're new to Lifechanyuan, you'll quickly notice that people often talk about "reading the Corpus." So what exactly is the Chanyuan Corpus?

It's the central collection of writings that form the intellectual and spiritual heart of Lifechanyuan. Not a single book, but over one million Chinese characters gathered into sixteen thematic sections — covering everything from the nature of the Greatest Creator and how the cosmos works, to what LIFE is, how to walk a cultivation path, and what humanity's future might look like.

In one sentence: the Chanyuan Corpus is a comprehensive guide to the universe, LIFE, and the human journey — seen through the eyes of Lifechanyuan.

---

## How Was It Written?

Guide Xuefeng offered a candid description of how these texts came to be:

> "The Chanyuan Corpus and the Xuefeng Corpus were not written in a state of sharp mental clarity; they arose from impressions caught in a misty, indistinct state of consciousness."
> — (Chanyuan Corpus · Life Treasures · Eight Great Mysteries of Life)

In other words, much of this writing emerged not from deliberate logical construction, but from a kind of open, receptive awareness — more like "receiving" something than "figuring it out."

One longtime reader described the experience of reading it this way:

> "The Lifechanyuan Corpus spans over one million characters. Most articles were written in a single sitting — rich in content, all-encompassing in scope, gorgeous and lively in language, majestic in structure."
> — (Xuefeng Corpus · Essays · Unspoken Words About Xuefeng)

---

## Why Does Everyone Say to Read It?

In Lifechanyuan, reading the Corpus is considered essential to walking the Way of the Greatest Creator:

> "To walk the Way of the Greatest Creator, one must read all sections of the Chanyuan Corpus and all articles of the Xuefeng Corpus in their entirety. Reading partially, one sees only one face of the mountain — and falls into partiality and error."
> — (Xuefeng Corpus · Heart-Mind · How to Walk the Way of the Greatest Creator?)

But the purpose isn't to fill your head with facts. It's to change from the inside:

> "Reading the Corpus is not about 'how much you now know' — it is about continuously observing yourself, purifying your heart-mind, and correcting your direction. When you feel low or confused, opening an article often brings guidance and strength."
> — (Chanyuan Corpus · Civilization · How New Members Find Their Place in Lifechanyuan)

Going even further, the Corpus needs to be absorbed deeply — not just read:

> "If the content of the Chanyuan Corpus and the Xuefeng Corpus is read merely as articles and never penetrates into one's cultural DNA, then that Chanyuan Celestial will remain only human and will never become an immortal."
> — (Other Articles by the Guide · 2025 · My State of Mind These Days)

## What's Inside?

Sixteen thematic sections cover the full terrain:

- **The Greatest Creator** — What the Greatest Creator is; how to know and revere it
- **Counter-intuitive Thinking** — Breaking fixed mindsets; seeing the world from a new angle
- **Cosmic Space-Time** — The structure of the universe, space-time tunnels, parallel worlds
- **Human Life** — Humanity's mission; the value and meaning of life
- **LIFE** — What LIFE is and where it comes from
- **Cultivation Practice** — The concrete path of cultivation
- **Health Cultivation** — Body and mind wellbeing
- **Divine Revelations** — Inspirations and transmissions from higher realms
- **Life Treasures** — Practical wisdom for everyday living
- **Spreading the Way** — How to share Lifechanyuan's teachings with others
- **Wisdom** — The nature and expression of true wisdom
- **Cultivating Immortality** — The path and realms of becoming a Celestial
- **Becoming a Buddha** — The path and realms of becoming a Buddha
- **The Antimatter World** — What the antimatter world is; how to access it
- **The 36 Eight-Trigram Formations** — The cosmic obstacles every soul must navigate
- **Civilization** — Civilization 3.0 and what humanity's future could look like

## What If It Doesn't Speak to You?

Guide Xuefeng was direct about this:

> "The Chanyuan Corpus is seen by some as a mountain filled with gold and diamonds; by others, as garbage and filth. This depends entirely on each person's unique experience, their innate gifts and spirituality, and their angle and standpoint."
> — (Xuefeng Corpus · Q&A · Lifechanyuan-Style Communism Analysis)

The same text lands completely differently depending on where you are in life. That's why the Corpus says: "Those with a receptive heart will find the treasures." The text doesn't open itself to everyone at once — it opens when you're ready.
