# The Chanyuan Corpus

The **Chanyuan Corpus** (禅院文集, *Chànyuàn Wénjí*) is the central body of teachings of Lifechanyuan. Spanning over one million Chinese characters across sixteen thematic sections, it covers the nature of the Greatest Creator, cosmic space-time, the mystery of LIFE, cultivation practice, the antimatter world, and the path toward civilization 3.0. Together with the *Xuefeng Corpus*, the Chanyuan Corpus forms the backbone of Lifechanyuan culture and serves as the essential guide for Chanyuan Celestials walking the Way of the Greatest Creator.

> "The Lifechanyuan Corpus and the Xuefeng Corpus tell us how to walk the Way of the Greatest Creator."
>
> — Xuefeng, *Eight Pieces of Advice to Atheists (VIII)*
