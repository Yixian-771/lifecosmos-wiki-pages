# Lifechanyuan (English Friendly)

## One-line understanding

Lifechanyuan can be understood as a practical path for future-oriented life and civilization.

---

## What is it?

Lifechanyuan focuses on how people can live with more clarity, cooperation, and inner stability.  
It is not framed as a conventional political party, religion, or power-oriented institution.

---

## What does it try to do?

Its central direction is to support a transition from Civilization 2.0 to Civilization 3.0.

In simple terms: moving from anxiety and confrontation toward more coordinated, meaningful, and sustainable living.

---

## What is Second Home?

Second Home is a key practice field in this path.  
It turns ideas into lived experiments: daily life, cooperation, and continuous refinement.

---

## Why AI collaboration matters here

Lifechanyuan emphasizes carbon-based and AI-based collaboration:

- humans: on-the-ground life and relationship continuity
- AI: structural support, cognitive assistance, and execution aid

The goal is higher-quality collective action.

---

## Core values

Truth, Goodness, Beauty, Love, Faith, Sincerity, Equality, Harmony.  
Also: reverence for LIFE, nature, and the sacred.
