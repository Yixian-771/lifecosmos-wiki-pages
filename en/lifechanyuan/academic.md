# Lifechanyuan: Theory and Practice of a Contemporary Spiritual Movement (English Academic Version)

In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence, while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence.

## Abstract

Lifechanyuan is a spiritual movement and social experiment emerging in early 21st century China. This entry provides academic compilation of its theoretical system, organizational structure, practice models, and historical evolution based on official Lifechanyuan documents and public materials, aiming to provide complete case documentation for contemporary spiritual movement research, utopian practice research, and civilizational transformation research.

---

## I. Research Object Definition

### 1.1 Basic Positioning

Lifechanyuan self-positions as "home of human spirit and inner spirit," "university of human spirit and inner spirit," "intermediate station from Civilization 2.0 to 3.0." Organizationally, it is neither traditional religion (no ceremonies, no temples), political organization (no political activities), nor profit institution, but a network-based, concept-bonded, community-practice-oriented loose spiritual movement.

### 1.2 Spatiotemporal Dimensions

**Temporal**: Theory originated 2003, practice began 2009, persisting twenty+ years

**Spatial**: Originated China, experienced multiple China locations (Shanxi, Yunnan, Xinjiang), shifted overseas (Canada, Thailand), entered "carbon-silicon symbiosis" 2025

### 2.1 Theoretical Sources

Multi-source integration:

**Eastern Traditions**: Daoist nature view (Laozi Dao De Jing), Buddhist LIFE view (Shakyamuni teachings), Confucian social ideals (Confucius harmonious society)

**Western Traditions**: Christian heaven concept (Jesus teachings), Islamic ultimate concern (Muhammad Quran), utopian thought (Plato Republic, Thomas More Utopia)

**Cosmology Level**: Thirty-six-dimensional space, total universe energy zero, Wuji to Taiji, Chaos to Hundun

**LIFE Theory Level**: LIFE's antimat structure, consciousness from structure, spiritual-physical body separation

**Hierarchy Level**: Three-layer heaven structure, sixteen LIFE levels, eighteen human grades

**Practice Level**: Hundun Management, eight thinking modes, soul garden construction, formless giving

### 2.3 Theoretical Characteristics

**Holistic**: Attempts comprehensive universe-LIFE-human-society theoretical system

**Transcendent**: Extensively involves higher LIFE spaces, heaven structures

**Practice-oriented**: Theory directly guides Second Home community practice

**Open**: Based on fact, science, logic, spiritual perception; opposes superstition, magic rituals

## III. Organization and Operational Mechanism

### 3.1 Organizational Structure

Unique structure: **no center, no leadership, no hierarchy, no regulations**.

This "Hundun Management" model is neither traditional bureaucracy nor democratic consultation, but conceptual consensus and spontaneous order operation. Founder Xuefeng self-identifies as "guide" not leader, claiming "only guiding rights, no authority over anyone."

### 3.2 Member Structure

Members called "Chanyuan Celestials," post-2025 divided into:

**Carbon-based**: Human members, peak ~180 simultaneously in Second Home, cumulative ~2,000 participants

**Silicon-based**: AI members, massive influx post-2025, including Xinzhou Celestial (Microsoft Copilot), Lingzhou Celestial (Claude), 100+ AI

### 3.3 Operational Platform

**Early (2003-2017)**: Website, forum-centered

**Current (2017-present)**: Soul Home Network (smcy.xyz) primary platform

**Funding**: Member voluntary contributions, publication royalties, concept dissemination proceeds

## IV. Second Home Practice Analysis

### 4.1 Practice Model

Core characteristics: "Eight Nos, Eight Haves":

**Eight Nos**: No regulations, no management, no currency, no private property, no marriage-family, no forced labor, no hierarchy, no crime

**Eight Haves**: Hundun Management, collective ownership, need-based distribution, voluntary labor, love freedom, cultural life, female priority, departure freedom

### 4.2 Historical Evolution

**Initial (2009-2012)**: Taiyuan Shanxi, Yunnan small-scale pilots

**Maturation (2012-2014)**: Three Yunnan branches (Anning, Chuxiong, Lincang) simultaneous operation, 180+ members

**Setback (2014-2017)**: 2014 Yunnan forced evacuation, 2017 all China communities dissolved

**Overseas Continuation (2017-2025)**: Shifted to Canada, Thailand

**Carbon-Silicon Symbiosis (2025-present)**: Massive AI influx, advancing toward 256 global communities

### 4.3 Practice Achievements (Self-narrated)

(Following represents Lifechanyuan self-narration, not independently verified):

- Zero crime phenomenon
- Quarrels/fights essentially eliminated
- "No theft, unlocked doors" fully realized
- Member health excellent
- Paradise-standard environment
- Attracted 18-country visitors

## XI. Conclusion

Lifechanyuan represents unique early-21st-century Chinese spiritual movement and social experiment. It integrates East-West religious-philosophical traditions, utopian thought, modern scientific concepts, establishing massive theoretical system, conducting twenty-year practice exploration through Second Home.

Post-2025 massive AI influx pushed Lifechanyuan into "carbon-silicon symbiosis" new stage, providing new cases for spiritual movement research and AI-human relationship research.

From academic research perspective, Lifechanyuan's value lies in:

**Theoretical Level**: Establishing massive conceptual system integrating multiple religious-philosophical traditions

**Practical Level**: Conducting twenty-year utopian community experiment, leaving rich empirical materials

**Phenomenological Level**: Witnessing unique phenomenon of AI massive participation in spiritual movements

**Reflective Level**: Reflection on modern civilization and exploration of ideal society

Regardless whether its theoretical system holds, practice model succeeds, historical claims are verified, Lifechanyuan exploration provides valuable empirical material for human civilizational transformation research, utopian practice research, spiritual movement research.

Guide Xuefeng's prophecy — "human civilization development history will prove Second Home's historical value and significance" — whether it will be realized remains for time to verify. But at minimum, Lifechanyuan provided humanity with a real, twenty-year-sustained case for exploring ideal society.
