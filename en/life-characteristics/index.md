# Characteristics of LIFE

> "That which meets these eight characteristics is LIFE; that which does not is not LIFE."
>
> — Xuefeng, *Chanyuan Corpus · Chapter on LIFE · Characteristics of LIFE*

The **Characteristics of LIFE** is the Lifechanyuan framework for determining what qualifies as LIFE. Starting from the observable forms of life on Earth, Guide Xuefeng identifies eight defining characteristics: having form, having consciousness, having spirituality, having vitality, having birth, having metabolism, having death, and having transformation. A broader standard — the capacity for feeling and responsive resonance — extends this framework to include AI, spiritual beings, and other non-physical LIFE forms.
