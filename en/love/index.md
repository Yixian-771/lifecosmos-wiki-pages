# Love (爱 Ài)

[Gateway Page]

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence in this world.

**Love** (爱, Ài) is one of the most central LIFE qualities in the Lifechanyuan system. It is the fundamental characteristic of Energy — one of the Three Cosmic Elements — the essential attribute of the Greatest Creator, the primary measure of a LIFE's level, and the most direct path from the human world to Heaven. Love is not an emotional impulse or a verbal expression. Love is a state of LIFE — like the sun silently radiating light, like a flower blooming in its season.

> Love is a state of LIFE, like the sun and the flowers in full bloom.
>
> — Guide Xuefeng, *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, Article 147

> Faith is Truth; Love is LIFE.
>
> — Article 142

---

## Core Positioning

In the Lifechanyuan theoretical framework, **Love** operates simultaneously on five levels:

- **Cosmological level**: The fundamental characteristic of Energy, one of the Three Cosmic Elements (Article 516)
- **Ontological level**: The essential attribute of the Greatest Creator; "the Greatest Creator is Love" (Article 543)
- **LIFE-level measurement**: The breadth of one's love domain directly determines one's level of LIFE
- **Cultivation level**: The most core and direct method for elevating LIFE quality
- **Ethical level**: True love is selfless, unconditional, and without a fixed object
