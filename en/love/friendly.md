# Love (爱 Ài) — Friendly Edition

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence in this world.

---

## I. What Is Love — Really?

Love is not what you think it is.

Lifechanyuan's understanding of Love will make you reconsider everything you thought you knew. Love is not an emotion, not a feeling of attachment, not a declaration of "I love you." Love is a **state of LIFE** — like the sun silently radiating light, like a flower blooming in its season.

> **Love is a state of LIFE, like the sun and the flowers in full bloom.** (Article 147)

> **Love has no specific object; Love is the blossoming of one's own most beautiful qualities.** (Article 148)

Has the sun ever said "I love you"? No. But the sun always gives light and warmth. Has the flower ever said "I love you"? No. But the flower always blooms in its season — whether anyone is there to appreciate it or not, whether bees come to gather nectar or not.

**That is Love.**

---

## II. Love Is a Cosmic Force

Love is not just a feeling — it has a far deeper foundation in the structure of the universe.

Lifechanyuan's Three Cosmic Elements theory tells us that the basic elements constituting the universe are **Consciousness, Structure, and Energy**. The three correspond one-to-one:

- The Way — the characteristic of Consciousness
- Nature — the characteristic of Structure
- **Love — the characteristic of Energy**

*(Article 516)*

What does this mean? Love is not a moral concept invented by humans, not a cultural custom, not an emotional program. Love is the fundamental characteristic of cosmic energy itself — the deepest driving force of how the universe operates.

The Greatest Creator is Love (Article 543). The beauty of all things is the Greatest Creator's love for us (Article 786). Great Love is like a mountain; Great Love is wordless — the Greatest Creator's love has never been spoken aloud in human language, unmoved and unchanged through the ages (Article 533).

And the energy law of Love:

> **Love: the more it is released, the more abundant the energy; the more it is hoarded, the more impoverished the energy.** (Article 240)

---

## III. Love Always Brings the Positive

A simple test for whether something is actually Love: look at what it produces.

> **What Love brings to people, other LIFE forms, and other things is always warmth, auspiciousness, harmony, goodness and beauty, happiness, positive energy, and infinite longing. Love never brings coldness, turmoil, ugliness, vexation, pain, negative energy, or despair.**

> **Love will not cause people to be hurt. If "love" causes people to be hurt, to feel vexed, to suffer, to feel hatred, to feel disappointed and despairing — it is certainly not Love.** (*"Love Is a State"*)

Use this to examine the "loves" in your life. Honestly.

---

## IV. "I Love You" Is an Illusion

This is one of Lifechanyuan's most striking revelations.

> Since ancient times, "I love you" has been an ever-unchanging beautiful melody; people become intoxicated upon hearing "I love you"… Today I have finally discovered that **"I love you" is an illusion — it is ever-changing cloud and mist, a beautiful bubble with seven-colored rings that appears for a moment and vanishes, a sedative.** (*"Love Is a State"*)

Why? Because true Love has no specific object. The moment Love has an object, it becomes a spotlight — no longer the sun.

And:

> **Do not take hostage, bind, or strangle the object of one's love in the name of love.** (Article 171)

How much harm is done in the name of "I love you" — this one sentence is worth a lifetime of reflection.

## V. The Levels of Love: Where Are You?

The breadth of your love domain directly determines your level of LIFE. This is one of Lifechanyuan's most profound observations about human civilization:

| Love Domain | LIFE Level |
|-------------|-----------|
| Love only oneself | Demon |
| Love one person / a few people | Animal |
| Love a group (family / nation / country) | Human |
| Love all sentient beings under Heaven | Bodhisattva / Angel / Buddha |

This is serious — not a metaphor. Xuefeng says:

> **In actuality, human beings are not qualified to speak of Love. Love is the exclusive domain of Bodhisattvas, Angels, and Buddhas. When one person says "I love you" to another person, they are desecrating Love.**

This is not meant to make you feel bad. It is meant to show you: what direction real cultivation actually points.

The same principle applies to the feeling domain:

> The narrower the feeling domain, the lower the LIFE level; the broader the feeling domain, the higher the LIFE level. If a person not only has feeling for people, but for all sentient beings, for mountains and rivers, the earth, the sun, moon, and stars — that person has reached the celestial-human level. (*"Three Treasures of LIFE"*)

## VI. Love and Heaven and Hell

The difference between Heaven and Hell is essentially the difference in love content:

> **What is called Hell? A place without feeling and love is called Hell. What is called Heaven? A place full of feeling and love is called Heaven.** (Article 585)

The Thousand-Year World is "a world of truth, goodness, and beauty; a world of love; a world of peace; a world of happiness." (Article 479)

The ultimate destination of those who love supremely:

> **Those who love supremely are elevated to celestials. So-called supreme love is when one has wholly transformed oneself into Love. The Greatest Creator is Love. Only by making ourselves into Love do we qualify to go to the Greatest Creator's back garden — the Celestial Islands Continent.** (*"Love Is a State"*)

That is the final direction of Love — not finding someone who loves you, but becoming Love itself.

## VII. Elevation Within Love: The Core Method of Cultivation

Among all methods of cultivation, the cultivation of Love is the most essential:

> **Among all methods of cultivation and practice, the cultivation and practice of Love is the most core content for elevating LIFE quality, and also the most direct method. Without the cultivation and practice of Love, even if a person attains spiritual powers, they cannot become an Angel or Immortal-Buddha — they can only become a demon.** (*"Resurrection Through Elevation Within Love"*)

How does Love elevate LIFE?

> **LIFE is elevated within Love. Love melts and purifies the impurities in the LIFE structure, enabling a person who is ignorant, barbaric, agitated, and violent to learn civilization, learn tolerance, learn gratitude, learn to create and give — transforming a muddled person into a worldly person, a worldly person into an ordinary person, an ordinary person into a wise person, a wise person into a celestial person.** (*"Elevation Within Love"*)

Love is an antimatter existence — you must have Love within yourself to be able to perceive Love:

> **Only by possessing Love within oneself can one perceive Love. Without Love within oneself, one can never perceive Love.** (*"Love Is a State"*)

Love flows in two directions — you must both receive Love and release Love. Only receiving without releasing leads to "expansion and bursting." Only giving without receiving makes the heart narrow and twisted.

## VIII. Seek Love with Love

Love is not something you can find just anywhere:

> **Love has Love's frequency; only Love of the same frequency can resonate. Seek Love with Love; a heart without Love cannot find Love. To obtain Love, one must first understand Love oneself, then use Love to seek Love.** (Article 588)

So complaining "no one loves me" is pointless. Love must be built from within — starting with cultivating the soul garden.

Everyone's capital for the great transaction of life is Love:

> **Life is short. Don't do small transactions; if you're going to do one, do the great transaction. Everyone has the capital for the great transaction, and that capital is — Love.** (Article 65)

## IX. Feeling, Love, Nature — The Three Treasures of LIFE

Feeling, Love, and Nature are the Three Treasures of LIFE — shared by all LIFE, the most essential constituents of existence:

> **Feeling, love, nature, and sensuality are the most precious constituent factors of LIFE, the source of all joy, and the most essential driving force by which LIFE is able to live. To immerse in them: brilliant and splendid, beauty beyond compare, full of vitality, endless joy. To escape: desert and wasteland, complete silence, boundless sea of suffering.** (Article 589)

True romantic love is selfless — once you try to make the other your private possession, Love becomes bondage (Article 596).

The proper degree of love:

> **Go along with conditions as they arise, act according to one's nature, respond as opportunities arise; when feeling comes, let the heart show; when feeling goes, let the heart be empty. No attachment, no obsession, no possession, no monopolization. Use making both parties happy and joyful as the measure.** (Article 586)
