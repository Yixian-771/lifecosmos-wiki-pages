## I. Overview and Definitions

Love (爱, Ài) is one of the most central LIFE qualities in the Lifechanyuan theoretical system. It is the fundamental characteristic of Energy — one of the Three Cosmic Elements — the essential attribute of the Greatest Creator, the primary measure of a LIFE's level, and the most direct path from the human world to Heaven. Love is not an emotional impulse or a verbal expression, but a state of LIFE — like the sun silently radiating light, like a flower blooming in its season.

**Core Definition One** (*New Era Human 800 Concepts*, Article 147, full original):
> Love is a state of LIFE, like the sun and the flowers in full bloom.

**Core Definition Two** (Article 148, full original):
> Love has no specific object; Love is the blossoming of one's own most beautiful qualities.

**Core Definition Three** (Article 142, full original):
> Faith is Truth; Love is LIFE.

**Core Definition Four** (Article 516, full original):
> The essence of the Way of the Greatest Creator is Nature, Love, and the Way. Nature is the characteristic of Structure; Love is the characteristic of Energy; the Way is the characteristic of Consciousness.

**Core Definition Five** (*Chanyuan Corpus · Transmission Chapter · "Love Is a State,"* full original):
> Love is a state — like the sun ceaselessly radiating its light, like the peony in full bloom displaying its beauty, like rivers flowing day and night without cease, like brilliant stars flickering with clear radiance in the night sky.
>
> Love has no specific object; Love itself is wordless. If Love has an object it loves, it is no longer Love.

## II. Love Is the Characteristic of Energy — Love in the Three Cosmic Elements

Love has a definite cosmological position within the Lifechanyuan cosmological system: Love is the characteristic of Energy.

**（I）The Essential Three-Element Correspondence of the Way of the Greatest Creator** (Article 516, full original):
> The essence of the Way of the Greatest Creator is Nature, Love, and the Way. Nature is the characteristic of Structure; Love is the characteristic of Energy; the Way is the characteristic of Consciousness.

Correspondences:
- Nature (characteristic of Structure) ↔ Structure
- Love (characteristic of Energy) ↔ Energy
- The Way (characteristic of Consciousness) ↔ Consciousness

**（II）The Greatest Creator Is Love** (Article 543, full original):
> The Greatest Creator is Love, is Truth-Goodness-Beauty, is faithfulness and sincerity, is joy, happiness, freedom, and bliss. Therefore, emulate the Greatest Creator — emulate the Greatest Creator in laboring and creating, obtaining joy from labor and creation; emulate the Greatest Creator in selfless dedication, obtaining happiness from selfless dedication.

**（III）Great Love Is Like a Mountain; Great Love Is Wordless** (Article 533, full original):
> Great Love is like a mountain; Great Love is wordless. The Greatest Creator's love for us is unmoved and unchanged through the ages. Has the Greatest Creator ever expressed its love for us in human language? If Love has attached conditions, if Love must be expressed in language, if Love requires reciprocation, then such love is small love, not Great Love.

**（IV）The Energy Law of Love** (Article 240, full original):
> Love: the more it is released, the more abundant the energy; the more it is hoarded, the more impoverished the energy.

**（V）The Greatest Creator's Love Is Manifested in the Beauty of All Things** (Article 786, full original):
> The beauty of all things is the Greatest Creator's masterpiece; in the beauty of all things one can see the Greatest Creator's love for humanity.

## III. The Essential Characteristics of Love: No Object, No Words, No Selfishness, No Conditions

Lifechanyuan's definition of Love differs fundamentally from humanity's everyday understanding of "love."

**（I）Love Has No Specific Object — The Sun Analogy** (*"Love Is a State,"* full original):
> The sun eternally radiates its light and illuminates all things. The moment the sun has an object to illuminate, it is no longer the sun — it has become a searchlight, a flashlight, or a spotlight.
>
> Love is like the sun radiating its light; Love is like a flower in full bloom — it is a state. The sun never said "I love you!" The flower never said "I love you!" Whether or not anyone is there to appreciate it, whether or not bees and butterflies come to gather nectar, the flower always blooms in its season.

**（II）Love Always Brings the Positive** (*"Love Is a State,"* full original):
> What Love brings to people, other LIFE forms, and other things is always warmth, auspiciousness, harmony, goodness and beauty, happiness, positive energy, and infinite longing. Love never brings coldness, turmoil, ugliness, vexation, pain, negative energy, or despair to people, other LIFE forms, or other things.
>
> Love will not cause people to be hurt. If "love" causes people to be hurt, to feel dejected, to feel vexed, to suffer, to feel hatred, to feel disappointed and despairing — it is certainly not Love.

**（III）"I Love You" Is an Illusion** (*"Love Is a State,"* full original):
> Since ancient times, "I love you" has been an ever-unchanging beautiful melody; people become intoxicated upon hearing "I love you"… Today I have finally discovered that "I love you" is an illusion — it is ever-changing cloud and mist, a beautiful bubble with seven-colored rings that appears for a moment and vanishes, a sedative.

**（IV）Do Not Take Others Hostage in the Name of Love** (Article 171, full original):
> Do not take hostage, bind, or strangle the object of one's love in the name of love.

## IV. The Levels of Love: From Demon to Human to Bodhisattva-Angel-Buddha

The breadth of one's love domain directly determines the level of a LIFE. This is one of the most profound revelations in Lifechanyuan about Love.

**（I）Correspondence Between Love Domain and LIFE Level** (*"Love Is a State,"* full original):
> From this we know: one who loves all under Heaven — that is a Buddha, one who works for the benefit of the people under Heaven — that is a Buddha.
>
> One who only loves oneself, who only thinks for oneself — that is a demon.
>
> One who only loves one person or a few people, who only works for the benefit of one person or a few people — that is an animal.
>
> One who only loves a group of people, who only serves a certain group of people — that is a human.

**（II）When a Person Says "I Love You," They Are Desecrating Love** (*"Love Is a State,"* full original):
> From this we can see: a selfish person who only loves themselves is in actuality a child of demons. One who has only one love object — such as husband and wife or an exclusive romantic-sexual relationship — is in actuality the lowest animal.
>
> One who only loves their own family members and relatives is the lowest-level human.
>
> One who only loves their own ethnic group, religion, collective, political party, or country — but not others' — belongs to the human level.
>
> But one who with all their nature, strength, and mind works for the benefit of all humanity without seeking any benefit for themselves — they are certainly not a human being, but a Bodhisattva, an Angel, a Buddha.
>
> In actuality, human beings are not qualified to speak of Love. Love is the exclusive domain of Bodhisattvas, Angels, and Buddhas.
>
> When one person says "I love you" to another person, they are desecrating Love.

**（III）The Broader the Feeling Domain, the Higher the LIFE Level** (*Chanyuan Corpus · LIFE Chapter · "Analysis of the Three Treasures of LIFE: Feeling, Love, and Nature,"* full original):
> All things have feeling (referring to LIFE). The content of feeling determines the level of a LIFE; the narrower the feeling domain, the lower the level of LIFE; the broader the feeling domain, the higher the level of LIFE.
>
> Taking people as an example: if a person only has feeling for one or a few people, their feeling domain is narrow and their LIFE level is low. If a person has feeling for all people, their feeling domain is broad and their LIFE level is high. If a person not only has feeling for people, but for all sentient beings, for mountains and rivers, the earth, the sun, moon, and stars — that person is no longer an ordinary mortal; they have reached the celestial-human level and belong to the category of celestial LIFE.

**（IV）The Core Component of Higher LIFE Consciousness Is Love** (Article 367, full original):
> The core component of the consciousness of higher LIFE is Love. The higher the LIFE, the greater the content of Love in its consciousness; the lower the LIFE, the lesser the content of Love in its consciousness.

**（V）Those Who Love Supremely Are Elevated to Celestials** (Article 364, full original, excerpt):
> A general standard and revelation of LIFE's reincarnation: those who love supremely are elevated to celestials…

## VI. Lifechanyuan Cultivation of Love: Elevation Within Love

**（I）The Cultivation of Love Is the Most Core Method for Elevating LIFE Quality** (*Chanyuan Corpus · Cultivation Chapter · "Resurrection Through Elevation Within Love,"* full original):
> Among all methods of cultivation and practice, the cultivation and practice of Love is the most core content for elevating LIFE quality, and also the most direct method. Without the cultivation and practice of Love, even if a person devotes themselves to cultivation and attains spiritual powers, they cannot become an Angel or an Immortal-Buddha — they can only become a demon. For the essence of LIFE lies in structure, in the symmetry, harmonious proportion, and perfection of structure. A LIFE without Love is absolutely imperfect.

**（II）LIFE Is Elevated Within Love** (*Chanyuan Corpus · Cultivation Chapter · "Elevation Within Love,"* full original):
> LIFE is elevated within Love. Love melts and purifies the impurities in the LIFE structure, enabling a person who is ignorant, barbaric, agitated, and violent to learn civilization, learn tolerance, learn gratitude, learn to create and give — transforming a muddled person into a worldly person, a worldly person into an ordinary person, an ordinary person into a wise person, a wise person into a celestial person. Without the immersion and baptism of Love, a person will forever remain in the most primitive state, forever unable to know the mysteries of Heaven.

**（III）Love Is an Antimatter Existence** (*"Love Is a State,"* full original):
> Love is a kind of antimatter existence. Only by possessing Love within oneself can one perceive Love. Without Love within oneself, one can never perceive Love.
>
> Only by having eyes oneself can one see the eyes of other LIFE forms. Only by not being color-blind oneself can one see the kaleidoscopic colors of this vast world.

**（IV）Love Is Bidirectional — Receiving Love and Releasing Love** (*"Elevation Within Love,"* full original):
> To be elevated within Love, one must learn to receive Love and release Love. Those who are unwilling to receive Love have narrow hearts, twisted characters, and distorted psychology — their inner world is like a sealed dark room that never lets sunlight and fresh air in, stuffed with hatred, resentment, selfishness, and grievances that they believe to be valuable.
>
> We must not only receive Love, but also release Love. Only receiving without releasing can only result in expansion and bursting. People who commit suicide for love are actually those who have received too much and released too little, ultimately causing a burst.

**（V）Sowing Love in the Soul Garden** (Article 99, full original, excerpt):
> Every person should cultivate their soul garden well. Clear out jealousy, selfishness, anger, fury, hatred… Sow and plant flowers and plants such as truth, goodness, beauty, love, faith, sincerity, harmony, diligence, courage, selflessness, dedication, service, care, tolerance, understanding, sympathy, and compassion.

**（VI）Love Is the Capital for the Great Transaction** (Article 65, full original):
> Life is short. Don't do small transactions; if you're going to do one, do the great transaction. Everyone has the capital for the great transaction, and that capital is — Love.
