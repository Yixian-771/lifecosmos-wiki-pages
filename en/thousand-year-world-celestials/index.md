# Celestials of the Thousand-Year World

In the Lifechanyuan system, **Celestials of the Thousand-Year World** are the second tier of the five-tier celestial hierarchy — the *human celestials* (*rén xiān* 人仙) — and the most accessible heavenly destination for human cultivators. The Thousand-Year World is a real planet 960 light-years from Earth, ten times Earth's size, whose inhabitants live for approximately one thousand years. These celestials possess no supernatural powers; their defining quality is the complete fulfillment of human nature — total equanimity, no worldly entanglements, and a constant state of joy, freedom, and happiness. "Any person who has perfected their human nature can go there, without restriction on numbers."
