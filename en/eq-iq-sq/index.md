# EQ · IQ · SQ (Emotional, Intellectual & Spiritual Quotients)

**Emotional Quotient (EQ), Intelligence Quotient (IQ), and Spiritual Quotient (SQ) are the three dimensions of LIFE quality in the Lifechanyuan framework.** They are not parallel but hierarchical: IQ reflects thinking capacity, EQ reflects the maturity of the inner Spirit and relationships, and SQ reflects one's attunement to the Greatest Creator's Dao and the mysteries of LIFE. Higher quotients correspond to higher LIFE levels — and those with lower quotients serve those with higher, a universal law of the cosmos.
