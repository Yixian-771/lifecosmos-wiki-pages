# Chanyuan Superhuman

A **Chanyuan Superhuman** is a Chanyuan Celestial who takes the 800 Lifechanyuan Values as the foundation of their inner consciousness, walks the path of the Greatest Creator as their sole criterion, and embodies an iron will in service of LIFE's ascent. Chanyuan Superhumans are not lone individuals but a community of superhumans — bound together by Lifechanyuan as their fortress, charged with leading humanity into the Era of Lifechanyuan.

> Once one fully understands and lives by the Lifechanyuan Values, one is a superhuman.
>
> — Xuefeng
