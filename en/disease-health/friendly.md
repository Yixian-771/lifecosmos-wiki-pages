# Disease and Health (Friendly Version)

## Why Do You Get Sick?

Have you ever wondered: why do cheerful, open-hearted people seem to stay healthier? And why do those who are chronically bitter, resentful, and complaining seem to attract illness?

This is not a coincidence.

Guide Xuefeng revealed a profound truth:

> **"A heart full of resentment will inevitably bring illness."**

---

## How Resentment Harms Your Body

What actually happens inside your body when you harbour long-term resentment and bitterness?

The answer may surprise you.

Your body is made up of 130 trillion cells, each with its own consciousness.

When you complain and resent, **every single cell is in a state of suppression and distress** — starved of energy.

Over time, cells begin to undergo qualitative change.

Changed cells lead to disease in the body's most vulnerable spot — often the heart.

Cancer resistance drops, vital energy stagnates, circulation is impeded...

This is not a warning. It is what is actually happening inside you.

---

## Anger Is Literally Poisoning You

Anger is more immediate and violent than resentment.

The moment you become angry, the volcano of toxins inside your brain marrow begins to erupt.

If you do not stop yourself in time, these toxins spread throughout your body, destroying the functioning of your organs and disrupting the flow of qi and blood.

Your body carries inexhaustible treasures — but also endless toxins.

Which one gets activated depends entirely on the state of your heart.

---

## So What Is True Health?

In the Lifechanyuan system, health is not simply a clean medical report.

True health is the optimal state of the physical body that arises when the spirit body is perfected.

The Guide told us:

> **"The perfection of the spirit body will bring the physical body to its optimal state of activity, and can even restore damaged limbs, cure terminal illnesses, and bring the dying back to life."**

This may sound extraordinary — but the logic is clear:

**The physical body is the vessel of the spirit body; the spirit body is its master.**

When your spirit body becomes increasingly pure and perfect, your physical body will naturally reflect that in the form of health.

---

## The Direction of Your Thoughts Determines Your Vitality

The Guide said something important:

> **"Fix your thoughts on the flesh, and you invite death; fix your thoughts on the spirit, and you receive life and peace."**

Many people, when they fall ill, pour all their attention onto the physical body: where does it hurt, what medicine to take, what tests to run...

But the Guide is telling us that this is precisely the wrong direction.

What truly brings health is **turning your attention toward the spirit body** — purifying the heart, dissolving resentment, drawing closer to the Dao.

---

## The Path to Health — Five Steps

**Step One: Clear Away Resentment**

Stop complaining. Learn to accept. Complaints solve nothing and only contaminate your cells.

**Step Two: Dissolve Anger**

When anger arises, remind yourself at once: I am poisoning myself right now. Breathe deeply, shift your focus, release the fixation.

**Step Three: Purify the Mind**

Cultivation and practice are the deepest form of health care. Reducing resentment, cultivating gratitude, forgiving others — all of these purify the spirit body and protect the physical body.

**Step Four: Balance the Organs**

The body is an organic whole: with proper cultivation, all systems develop in balance. Blood cells are abundant, oxygen and nutrients reach every part of the body, and pathogens are naturally cleared. Regular meals and rest form the foundation.

**Step Five: Resonate with the Dao**

When your LIFE increasingly resonates with the Way of the Greatest Creator, health becomes a natural consequence — not something you have to chase. It simply arrives.

---

## Your Body Holds Treasures Waiting to Be Unlocked

The Guide also revealed something wonderful:

All the products of the earth have corresponding codes inside the human body. When you find the corresponding code within yourself, the corresponding things on earth will come to you — including everything your health needs.

The treasures in your body are not activated by medicine. They are unlocked by purifying your mind and elevating your spirit.

## A Quick Summary

| Inner State | Effect on the Body |
|------------|-------------------|
| Chronic resentment | Cells deteriorate, organs diseased, circulation blocked |
| Anger | Toxin eruption, organs damaged |
| Calm and peaceful | Cells energized, self-healing activated |
| Spirit body perfected | Physical body at its optimal — even terminal illness can be healed |

**Guard your heart, and you guard your body.**
