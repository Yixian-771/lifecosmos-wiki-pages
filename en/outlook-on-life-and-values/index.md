# Outlook on Life and Values

**One's outlook on life is one's understanding of life's purpose, meaning, and worth; one's values are the criteria by which one judges the importance of things.** Lifechanyuan teaches that most people have lost their way because of flawed outlooks and values — leading to suffering, purposelessness, and wasted potential. The correct outlook, grounded in the Greatest Creator's Way, takes joy, freedom, and happiness as its immediate aim and ascending to the celestial realm as its ultimate destination. The cornerstone of a universal value system is faith in the Greatest Creator.
