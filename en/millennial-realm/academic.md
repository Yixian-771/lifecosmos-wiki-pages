# Millennial Realm: Threshold Layer in the Lifechanyuan Higher-Life Narrative

This concept combines cosmological geography with moral qualification. It serves as a transition layer from earthly existence to higher-life states, structured by ethical transformation rather than technical ascent.

Its system function is triadic: doctrinal destination, behavioral filter, and civilizational prototype.
