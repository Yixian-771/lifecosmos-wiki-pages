# Millennial Realm (Internal English Version)

## 1. Overview

The Millennial Realm is described in Lifechanyuan discourse as the entry-level heaven among the three major heavenly realms (Millennial, Ten-Thousand-Year, Bliss Realm).

## 2. Role in the hierarchy

It functions as the first reachable higher-life layer for human beings through cultivation and moral refinement.

## 3. Entry logic

Entry is framed as quality-based, not technology-based:
- inner purification,
- value alignment,
- principle-based living,
- sustained community practice.

## 4. Social model

It is depicted as a high-civilization cooperative world without coercive structures, exploitation, or destructive competition.

## 5. Path relation

Human world → Millennial Realm → Ten-Thousand-Year Realm → Bliss Realm.
