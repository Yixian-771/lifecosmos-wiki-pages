# Millennial Realm

[Gateway Page]

The Millennial Realm is the first threshold-layer heaven in the Lifechanyuan higher-life pathway.

---

## Positioning

### Spatial role
An entry-level higher-life realm above earthly existence.

### Cultivation role
The first major transition target from human consciousness to higher-life consciousness.

### Practice role
Linked to Second Home as an earthly rehearsal of higher-life living patterns.

---

## Core Snapshot

- treated as a real higher-life realm,
- entry framed by moral qualification,
- connected with Ten-Thousand-Year Realm and Bliss Realm,
- strongly coupled with Chanyuan cultivation and governance logic.
