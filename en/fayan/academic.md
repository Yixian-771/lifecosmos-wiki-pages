# Dharma Eye (Academic Version)

## The Five Eyes: Structural Analysis

The Buddhist tradition's "Five Eyes" (*wǔyǎn* 五眼) originates in the *Diamond Sutra* and related Prajñāpāramitā texts. Lifechanyuan integrates these into a complete **wisdom-level hierarchy** (*Eight Hundred Concepts* #88):

> **Wisdom has five great levels, ascending: Material Eye wisdom, Celestial Eye wisdom, Wisdom Eye wisdom, Dharma Eye wisdom, Buddha Eye wisdom.**

Functional analysis of each level:

| Level | Corresponding Capacity | Domain of Perception |
|-------|----------------------|---------------------|
| Material Eye | Physical, biological perception | Surface phenomena of the material world |
| Celestial Eye | Trans-spaciotemporal perception | Beyond ordinary vision — past, future, distant spaces |
| Wisdom Eye | Discriminating wisdom | Truth vs. falsehood; good vs. evil |
| Dharma Eye | Principled insight | Operating principles behind all phenomena; LIFE mysteries; Heaven's reality |
| Buddha Eye | Perfect, complete wisdom | All things — the Tathagata's boundless, unobstructed knowing |

The Dharma Eye stands at the fourth level. Its distinctive feature: it doesn't merely discern results (the Wisdom Eye) — it perceives **why** — penetrating to the principles themselves that govern all phenomena.

## The Three Elements of the Universe: Epistemological Key

Xuefeng specifies in "I Open the Dharma Eye for Everyone" that the method for opening the Dharma Eye is:

> **Firmly grasp the Three Elements of the Universe — Consciousness, Structure, and Energy. Once their interrelationship is clarified, the Dharma Eye opens, and one will no longer be confused by any profound doctrine or theory.**

This proposition carries deep epistemological significance:

**Consciousness (*意识*, Yìshí)**
- The fundamental attribute and driving force of all existence
- "Consciousness determines being" — a LIFE's consciousness state determines its antimatter structure, thereby determining its ultimate destination
- Consciousness does not depend on the physical body; it is an antimatter existence independent of matter

**Structure (*结构*, Jiégòu)**
- The organizational form of LIFE in the antimatter world
- Whatever consciousness one has, one forms a corresponding antimatter structure
- Antimatter structure determines which level of LIFE space a being can enter (Thousand-Year World / Ten-Thousand-Year World / Elysium World)

**Energy (*能量*, Néngliàng)**
- The force sustaining LIFE's structure and operation
- Cultivation is in part about accumulating and elevating energy vibration frequency
- Energy interacts dynamically with consciousness and structure

The relationship: **consciousness shapes structure; structure carries energy; energy expresses consciousness** — a self-consistent cosmic operating mechanism. Grasping this mechanism allows evaluation of virtually any deep proposition in religion, philosophy, or science — the Dharma Eye opens.

## The Dharma Eye and the Antimatter World

Xuefeng further illuminates the connection between the Dharma Eye and the antimatter world in "The Key to the Kingdom of Heaven — Life Mysteries II" (2007):

> "LIFE is a spiritually animated antimatter structure. We must set aside the physical body entirely, stop clinging to the material world, then envision the state of LIFE and the space in which it exists — and the Dharma Eye can open, allowing us to see the Tathagata of LIFE and the true face of the antimatter world."

This describes an alternative path to opening the Dharma Eye: **release attachment to the body; enter the antimatter dimension of LIFE in one's thinking**. This is consistent with the Three Elements method — both liberate consciousness from the grip of material appearances, redirecting it toward apprehension of LIFE's essence (antimatter structure).

## Dharma Eye, Wisdom Eye, and Spiritual Eye: The Hierarchy

In Lifechanyuan usage, these terms frequently appear together ("Wisdom Eye and Dharma Eye," "Spiritual Eye") but carry distinct levels:

| Concept | Level | Core Function |
|---------|-------|--------------|
| Spiritual Eye (*灵眼*) | Foundational spiritual sensing | Perceiving the existence of the antimatter world (visual channel of Spiritual Sensing) |
| Wisdom Eye (*慧眼*) | Mid-level discriminating wisdom | Discerning true from false, good from evil |
| Dharma Eye (*法眼*) | High-level principled insight | Seeing the operating principles of all phenomena; discerning right paths; comprehending heavenly reality |
| Buddha Eye (*佛眼*) | Perfect and complete | Boundless, unobstructed — the Tathagata's realm |

In practice: the Spiritual Eye opens through purifying the mind (removing the six obstructing poisons); the Wisdom Eye opens through sustained cultivation; **the Dharma Eye opens through grasping the Three Elements framework** — a kind of "framework awakening" in which possessing the correct cosmological model enables one to read all phenomena through it.

## Correspondence with Cultivation Levels

The opening of the Dharma Eye corresponds to a significant threshold in the cultivation journey:

- **Elementary cultivation stage**: Purifying the mind is primary; the Spiritual Eye begins to loosen
- **Intermediate / Advanced cultivation stage**: The Wisdom Eye opens; true and false, good and evil become discernible
- **Advanced cultivation / refinement stage**: The Dharma Eye opens; cosmic principles are perceived; one stands in the ranks of humanity's highest wisdom
- **Becoming a celestial being or Buddha**: The Buddha Eye opens; union with the Tathagata

Xuefeng's closing statement merits deep reflection:

> "With the Dharma Eye open, plus reverence for the Greatest Creator, concern for all under heaven, and working for the happiness of the suffering — you draw close to the divine."

This means the Dharma Eye alone is insufficient — it must be combined with **reverence** (faith), **aspiration** (care for all), and **action** (service to the suffering) to complete the leap from "wisdom" to "divinity."
