# Cosmic Unified Field | Friendly Version

Here's a puzzle that stumped Einstein for thirty years: is there one single law — one ultimate formula — that governs *everything* in the universe?

Scientists call this the **Cosmic Unified Field**. Einstein never found it. Neither has anyone else.

Lifechanyuan has a very specific answer for why — and for what the real unified field actually is.

---

## Why Scientists Can't Find It

Scientists have identified four fundamental forces in the universe:
- Gravity
- Electromagnetism (magnetic force)
- Strong nuclear force
- Weak nuclear force

Their approach: find one formula that unifies all four.

The problem, according to Xuefeng: **they've only found half the forces.**

The universe actually has **eight forces**. The four scientists know are all in the material world. The other four operate in the antimatter realm and the domain of consciousness:

| Force | What it does |
|-------|-------------|
| **Constructive force** | The structural organization force — every structure absorbs energy in its own unique way |
| **Repulsive force** | Like repels like |
| **Intentional force** | The force of intention on matter — when your brain decides to move, your body responds |
| **Spiritual force** | The force that lets things sense each other across distance |

Science can't measure these four. And if you can't include them in your formula, you can never build a complete unified field theory.

That's why Einstein failed. That's why modern physics still hasn't cracked it.

## What the Real Unified Field Is

Here's the surprising part: the Cosmic Unified Field **already exists**.

Xuefeng writes:

> *The Cosmic Unified Field was established long ago. It is the Tao of the Greatest Creator — the Hundun system of Consciousness, Structure, and Energy.*

In other words, the "terminal formula" scientists are searching for isn't a mathematical equation at all. It's **the Tao** — the law that pervades everything, material and antimatter alike, in all times and all spaces.

The universe is holographic. The Tao contains all eight forces and coordinates them seamlessly. Scientists just couldn't see it because they were only looking at half the picture.
