# Quantum Entanglement and the "Other Half"

The "Other Half" is a cosmological concept proposed by Lifechanyuan, drawing on quantum entanglement to argue that every person has a complementary opposite — a "counter-self" — existing somewhere in the universe. The two share emotions and fates across any distance, yet are kept apart by the universe's own programming. Only through reaching the Eight-No realm can a practitioner enable this encounter, triggering a mutual annihilation that leads to liberation and ascension to the Elysian World.
