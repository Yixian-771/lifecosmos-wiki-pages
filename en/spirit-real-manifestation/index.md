## Overview

"The Spirit Manifests Reality" is one of the central propositions of the Lifechanyuan cultivation system. It holds that a purified spirit can transform inner mind-images — vivid mental pictures of a future reality — into actual existence in the material world. The underlying principle is that all phenomena arise from the interaction of three elements: structure, consciousness, and energy. The spirit is the primary component of consciousness, and the purer the spirit, the greater its capacity to focus energy toward a single intention, enabling mind-images to become real.

This is illustrated by the analogy of focusing lenses: a single small lens reflects little energy, but many lenses focused on one point can ignite wood or boil water — this is the principle of the solar cooker. A purified spirit operates exactly this way.

The proposition applies at two levels: the everyday level (circumstances are created by the mind; consciousness shapes existence) and the ultimate level of spiritual cultivation — as consciousness rises, vibration frequency increases, and the spirit becomes increasingly pure, becoming a celestial being shifts from the unimaginable to the inevitable.
