## Two Stories to Begin

**Story One: The Sculptor Whose Statue Came to Life**

Long ago there was a sculptor who carved a life-sized statue of a supremely beautiful woman — not from a model, but from the image already alive in his heart. Throughout the entire carving, he poured everything into his work: his breath, his spirit, his heart, his devotion. He spoke with the celestial woman of his imagination, loved her completely. When the final chisel blow fell, something extraordinary happened: the woman's nonmaterial form stepped out of the stone and entered a bond with the sculptor that would last for all eternity — ultimately, they both became celestial.

**Story Two: The Divine Painter Whose Cranes Flew**

There was a divine painter famous for painting celestial cranes. One day, before a crowd of astonished onlookers, he began painting cranes on a wall. Each crane he painted promptly lifted off the wall and soared into the sky. No matter how many he painted, every single one took flight.

Both stories illustrate the same truth: a focused, pure spirit has the power to make what lives in the heart become real in the world.

## The Focusing Lens: Understanding the Power of the Spirit

Imagine a small hand mirror held up to the sun. It reflects a little light, but not enough to do much. Now imagine hundreds of such mirrors, all angled to focus the sunlight onto a single point. That point can ignite a piece of wood. It can boil a pot of water. This is exactly how a solar cooker works.

A pure spirit works the same way. The purer your spirit — the more you let go of greed, anxiety, resentment, and self-absorption — the more precisely your consciousness focuses its energy on one intention. The universe picks up this clear signal and cooperates with you.

---

## What Is a Mind-Image?

A mind-image is not something you deliberately conjure up out of wanting. It is more like a clear, vivid picture that *arrives* when you are still and peaceful — a scene of a future life that feels completely real, as though you could step right into it.

It tends to appear when you are not busy, not anxious, not full of craving. It arises in a state of inner quiet, and when it comes, it feels unmistakably different from ordinary daydreaming.

---

## How Does a Mind-Image Become Reality?

The universe is remarkably cooperative. It does not judge you — it simply responds to the clarity and sincerity of your intention:

> The universe will do everything in its power, following the path of least resistance, to help you realize your wish as quickly as possible.
>
> — New Era Human 800 Concepts, 4th Edition

The practice is straightforward:

1. **Quiet your mind** — set aside busyness and worry, let yourself settle
2. **Form the image** — in that stillness, clearly picture the beautiful future you hope for
3. **Hold it steady** — anchor this image in your consciousness; do not doubt it or keep changing it
4. **Be patient** — leave the rest to the universe; realization typically takes years, but it is inevitable

The purer and more wholesome your spirit, the faster the image will manifest.

---

## One Important Warning: Do Not Picture Bad Things

The universe's energy is neutral — it does not pick sides. It cooperates with whatever is clearly held in your consciousness, whether good or bad. If you worry obsessively about a plane crash, a failed meeting, or harm coming to a loved one, that energy cooperates too.

Whenever a fearful or negative image arises in your mind, deny it immediately — say "No!" within yourself and return to a positive, peaceful vision.

## What Does This Have to Do with Becoming Celestial?

At its deepest level, "the spirit manifests reality" is the secret of celestial transformation.

As a person's spirit becomes purer over years and years of cultivation — as their thinking frequency rises and their understanding of the nonmaterial world deepens — they naturally come to resonate with the higher life spaces. At that point, the passage from human life to celestial life happens as naturally as a ripe fruit falling from a tree.

It is not magic. It is how the universe works.
