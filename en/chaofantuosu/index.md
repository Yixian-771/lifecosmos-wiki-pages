# Transcending the Ordinary (Chaofan Tuosu / 超凡脱俗)

**Transcending the Ordinary** (*chaofan tuosu* 超凡脱俗) is both a necessary state for becoming a celestial being and the defining mark of the highest grade of human life. In the Lifechanyuan system, "the vulgar" (*sú* 俗) — money, power, fame, cunning, flattery, and social posturing — is captured in one word: **agitation** (*zào* 躁). "The ordinary" (*fán* 凡) — human sentiments, social ties, worldly affairs — is captured in one word: **noise** (*nào* 闹). The celestial realm, by contrast, is captured in two words: **stillness and clarity** (*qīng jìng* 清静). Transcending the ordinary is simply the process of moving away from agitation and noise toward stillness and clarity. Its counterpart concept is **Inverted Advance** (*fǎncháng tuìjìn* 反常退进): the value-system of celestial beings is the value-system of ordinary people turned upside-down.
