# Huaxiang, Butterflies Come Naturally

[Entry Gateway]

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological
> essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across
> incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage
> of human existence in this world.

**"When flowers emit fragrance, butterflies come naturally"** (花香蝶自来, *Huā xiāng dié zì lái*) is a core cultivation principle in Lifechanyuan — and the full saying continues: *when flowers emit fragrance, butterflies come; when the mind is still, meaning flows; when the body is clean, illness departs; when the heart is pure, immortals arrive.* It expresses a fundamental law of the universe: goodness attracts goodness, purity attracts the divine — not through striving, but through being.

> Tend your inner garden. The butterflies will come on their own.
>
> — Guide Xuefeng

---

## Core Positioning

In the Lifechanyuan system, this principle encapsulates the cultivation logic of "consciousness determines existence" — the outer world responds to the inner state. It is not a passive waiting, but the confident understanding that an authentic inner life creates its own corresponding outer reality. Seeking outward is the Civilization 2.0 approach; cultivating inward is the Civilization 3.0 approach.
