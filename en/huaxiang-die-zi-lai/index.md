# When Flowers Emit Fragrance, Butterflies Come; When the Mind Is Still, Meaning Flows

---

## Entry Overview

"When flowers emit fragrance, butterflies come; when the mind is still, meaning flows; when the body is clean, illness departs; when the heart is pure, immortals arrive."

This teaching, proposed by Guide Xuefeng of Lifechanyuan, explains the correspondence between inner state and life environment.
