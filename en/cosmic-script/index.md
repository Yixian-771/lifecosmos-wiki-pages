# The Cosmic Script

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological
> essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across
> incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage
> of human existence in this world.

**The Cosmic Script** is the core concept in Lifechanyuan's (生命禅院) cosmology that reveals the fundamental nature of the universe and the mode of existence for all LIFE. The entire universe is a vast script written by the Greatest Creator, directed by the Dao, and enacted by all beings. Within the grand script are countless nested sub-scripts — from the Milky Way, the solar system, and the Earth, down to every nation, organization, and individual. Human beings are actors in this script, not screenwriters or directors. Facing the script, humans are powerless; the wisest choice is to understand one's role and play it well.

> "There is one ultimate screenwriter in the universe, and that screenwriter is the Greatest Creator. The story unfolds according to the will of the Greatest Creator — the Dao. In the story arranged by the Greatest Creator, we are all actors. Jesus, Shakyamuni, Muhammad, Hundun Yuanchu, and others are all directors."
> — Xuefeng, *Screenwriter, Director, Actor*, 2007-1-10

---

## Core Points

- **Screenwriter**: The Greatest Creator is the ultimate screenwriter; the Dao is the way the story unfolds
- **Directors**: Sages and worthies (Jesus, Shakyamuni, etc.) and Hundun Yuanchu (the Guide) are directors
- **Actors**: Every human being is an actor, strictly performing according to the pre-written script
- **A living script**: This life's script is fixed; the next life's script changes — according to intentions and actions
- **The only space for effort**: Intentions (good vs. evil) and the perfection of one's role performance
- **Escaping the script**: Walk the Way of the Greatest Creator, or attain supreme enlightenment, to enter the Kingdom of Freedom
