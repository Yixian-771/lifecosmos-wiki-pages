The entire universe is a vast script written by the Greatest Creator, directed by the Dao, and enacted by all beings. Within the grand script are countless nested sub-scripts — from the Milky Way, the solar system, and the Earth, down to every nation, organization, and individual. Human beings are actors in this script, not screenwriters or directors. Facing the script, humans are powerless; the wisest choice of LIFE is to understand one's role and play it well.
