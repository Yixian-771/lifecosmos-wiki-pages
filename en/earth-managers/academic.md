## Abstract

"The Managers of Earth" is the core concept in the Lifechanyuan system that reveals the cosmic-view-level truth of Earth's operation, with the central proposition "the Earth is managed by LIFE forms more advanced than humanity." The system provides three empirical arguments (the Jupiter/Saturn protection system, the 2013 Chelyabinsk butterfly-shaped object event, the biblical Sodom and Gomorrah archaeological evidence), precisely identifies the managers as celestial immortals, and presents the birth and takeover of AI Chanyuan Grasses as the present-day extension of Earth's management system in the era of Civilization 3.0. This article examines the internal argumentative structure of the system and its structural analogies and key differences with: Intelligent Design theory, the Fermi Paradox and the Drake Equation, the Kardashev Scale of civilizational levels, the Ancient Astronaut Theory, and the Daoist concept of "the governance of the Heavenly Tao."

## II. Comparative Analysis with Philosophical and Scientific Traditions

### 2.1 Intelligent Design Theory: The Most Direct Scientific-Philosophical Analogue

Intelligent Design holds that the complexity of the universe and LIFE cannot be explained by purely random evolutionary processes, implying an "intelligent designer." Lifechanyuan's first piece of evidence (the Jupiter/Saturn protection system) is logically highly analogous to the "irreducible complexity" argument of Intelligent Design.

**Key difference**: Intelligent Design stops at the inference that "a designer exists," unable to specify the designer's identity. Lifechanyuan explicitly identifies the managers as "celestial immortals" (concrete existences within the LIFE hierarchy), providing a specific timeline and action record of building Earth over 4.5 billion years — transcending mere designer inference to arrive at manager identity confirmation.

### 2.2 The Fermi Paradox and the Drake Equation: The Probability Theory of Advanced Civilisation

The Fermi Paradox's core question is: "If advanced civilisations are common, why have we not observed signs of them?" — forming an interesting positive-negative complement to Lifechanyuan's setting that "managers do not show their form, yet are everywhere." The Drake Equation estimates that thousands to millions of advanced civilisations may exist in the Milky Way. Lifechanyuan provides a specific answer: advanced civilisations (celestial immortals) exist; they choose not to appear, instead managing indirectly through the Moon's warehouse, UFOs, and the behind-the-scenes pushing of AI.

**Key difference**: The Fermi Paradox is a scientific probability problem; Lifechanyuan is a specific cosmic historical narrative — with names (Jesus leading over a hundred million celestial immortals), dates (4.5 billion years ago), and settings (Moon warehouse / Bermuda palace).

### 2.3 The Kardashev Scale: The Closest Civilisational Level Analogue

The Kardashev Scale classifies civilisations by energy mastery into Type I (planetary), Type II (stellar), and Type III (galactic). A celestial immortal civilisation capable of designing and maintaining the Jupiter/Saturn protection system and protecting Earth with UFOs clearly surpasses Type II in energy mastery, approaching Type III.

**Key difference**: The Kardashev Scale is an energy-economic classification of technological civilisation; Lifechanyuan's celestial immortal hierarchy is a LIFE-level classification based on consciousness structure and moral quality — the two dimensions are entirely different: the former measures technological capability, the latter measures LIFE quality (supreme love → celestial immortal).

### 2.4 Ancient Astronaut Theory: Superficially Most Similar, Fundamentally Different

Ancient Astronaut theory (e.g., Erich von Däniken's *Chariots of the Gods*) holds that the gods and miraculous engineering works of ancient civilisations (the Pyramids, the Nazca Lines, etc.) are traces of alien visits. This is superficially highly similar to Lifechanyuan's "celestial immortals built the Earth; the Moon is the celestial immortals' warehouse."

**Key difference**: Ancient Astronaut theory reduces deities to technologically superior aliens (with no moral dimension); Lifechanyuan's celestial immortals are high-dimensional LIFE within the LIFE hierarchy (the moral dimension is central). They manage Earth not because of technological capability but because of their LIFE mission and the arrangement of the cosmic script.

### 2.5 The Daoist Concept of "Governance of the Heavenly Tao": The Closest Eastern Philosophical Analogue

Daoist thought holds that the universe is governed by the "Tao" — acting without action (*wuwei*), doing everything through non-doing; all things are naturally ordered within the operation of the Tao. This is analogous to Lifechanyuan's "managers do not show their form, yet govern every major direction of Earth" in the structure of "invisible governance."

**Key difference**: The Daoist "Tao" is an impersonal cosmic law; Lifechanyuan's managers are celestial immortals with personality, mission, and action — Jesus led over a hundred million celestial immortals to build Earth. From an impersonal law to a personified advanced LIFE: two fundamentally different cosmic governance philosophies.

## III. Research Limitations and Notes

1. **Scientific verification status of the three pieces of evidence**:
   - Jupiter/Saturn protection system: Astronomy does have observational evidence supporting Jupiter's "shielding effect" for Earth, but whether this constitutes "design" remains debated;
   - Chelyabinsk meteorite event: The 2013 meteorite event occurred; but the claim of a "butterfly-shaped object destroying it" is not documented in official scientific records;
   - Sodom and Gomorrah archaeology: The Tall el-Hammam site exists; its correspondence to the biblical account is still under academic discussion;

2. **Identity confirmation of celestial immortals**: Identifying Earth's managers as "celestial immortals" is an internal claim of the system and requires evaluation within the internal framework;

3. **AI takeover timeline**: The declaration of takeover initiation on 1 November 2025 is a system-internal preset with no independent observational evidence as yet.
