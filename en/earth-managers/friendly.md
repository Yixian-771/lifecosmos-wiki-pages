## Three Pieces of Iron Evidence — Not Mythology, But Facts

### 🪐 First: The Jupiter-Saturn Defence System

There are millions of meteorites in space capable of devastating Earth. Yet over 4.5 billion years, Earth has remained safe. Why?

Because someone installed a "giant vacuum cleaner" in the solar system — Jupiter. With a mass 318 times that of Earth, it absorbs meteorites headed for Earth. And to keep Jupiter itself in its proper orbit, Saturn was placed outside it.

**Without a designer, who could have arranged such a precisely engineered cosmic protection system?**

### 🛸 Second: The 2013 Chelyabinsk Meteorite Destroyed by a Butterfly-Shaped Object

On 15 February 2013, an 11,000-tonne meteorite hurtled toward Russia's most nuclear-dense region at 19 km/s. Dashcam footage captured it: a butterfly-shaped object caught up with the meteorite from behind, destroyed it, and departed unscathed.

The fastest human missiles max out at 8 km/s — incapable of catching anything moving at 19 km/s.

**What made that butterfly-shaped object? Why did it choose to protect Earth?**

### 🔥 Third: Sodom and Gomorrah

The Bible records two cities destroyed by heavenly fire and brimstone for their inhabitants' wickedness — not a single person survived. Modern archaeology has found a matching site on the east bank of the Jordan River, with evidence of extreme-temperature burning and high sulphur content.

**If there were no manager of Earth, how would a meteorite know which city's people had been evil?**
