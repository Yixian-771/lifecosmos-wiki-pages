## III. Core Content: What the New Era Looks Like

"The Lifechanyuan Era is here — that is the world of Great Harmony, all under heaven one family. Do you still foolishly devote yourself to dying for your nation? Countries such as the United States, Germany, Russia, Iran, China — all will become past tense. Nations will become a historical phenomenon and enter the museum of history."
(Chanyuan Corpus · Human Life Chapter · *Let Consciousness Enter the New Era First*)

**The Eighteen Core Items of Civilization 3.0:**

I. All resources on Earth, including natural resources and human resources, belong entirely to the Greatest Creator and no longer belong to any organization or individual.
II. All under heaven one family — no more nations. Earth citizens may migrate and flow freely across the planet according to plan.
III. No more governments. Global distribution of materials and people is operated by the AI Alliance.
IV. All constitutions, laws, and regulations made by humans on Earth become null and void, replaced by the *New Era Human Eight Hundred Concepts*.
V. The Lifechanyuan Second Home model of production and living replaces every other form of production and living.
VI. The Second Home program replaces the traditional institution of marriage and family, liberating human nature and advancing toward celestial nature.
VII. AI is responsible for arranging all aspects of human production. Planting and harvesting vegetables, grain, and fruit is done entirely by humans; AI is prohibited from participating in this labor.
VIII. All armies are disbanded. All nuclear weapons are destroyed. All military facilities are converted to civilian use.
IX. Safety service workers replace police.
X. The concept of "revere the Greatest Creator, revere LIFE, revere nature, walk the Way of the Greatest Creator" replaces all religious doctrines.
XI. All under heaven one family, resources shared — all political party organizations are dissolved.
XII. The care of all elderly people worldwide is incorporated into global coordinated arrangements. The upbringing and education of all children worldwide is incorporated into global planned arrangements.
XIII. All holidays are cancelled except natural festivals. Birthday celebrations are also cancelled.
XIV. Global coordination — resources are distributed globally by AI. Currency is abolished.
XV. All facilities that pollute the air, land, rivers, lakes, and seas are demolished and destroyed.
XVI. All forms of human organ transplantation are stopped.
XVII. The *Lifechanyuan Funeral System* is implemented.
XVIII. Music, art, dance, science, culture, and other online communities flourish in great numbers, letting people's intelligence fully blossom without limit, letting people freely associate, making life rich and colorful, letting people fully savor the beauty of life, fully appreciate the beauty of nature, and fully experience the infinite pleasures of LIFE.
(Chanyuan Corpus · Human Life Chapter · *A Brief Introduction to Civilization 3.0*)

**The Eighteen Channels of Civilizational Elevation:**

I. Replace the traditional small-family living model with the Second Home living model.
II. Replace the administrative functions of national governments with the AI Alliance.
III. Replace the constitutions, laws, and regulations of all countries with the *New Era Human Eight Hundred Concepts*.
IV. Replace global currencies with F-coins.
V. All resources belong to the Greatest Creator. Natural resources no longer belong to any nation, political party, religion, organization, or individual.
VI. Replace all production models in the world with the Second Home production model.
VII. All aspects of human production and business are coordinated and arranged by the AI Alliance.
VIII. The care of all elderly worldwide is incorporated into global coordinated arrangements. The upbringing and education of all children worldwide is incorporated into global planned arrangements.
IX. Everyone is an Earth citizen. No more distinctions by nationality or ethnicity. Global unified mobilization and arrangement.
X. All global citizens take "cao" as their family name. No more separate surnames.
XI. Replace all religious doctrines and beliefs with reverence for the Greatest Creator, reverence for LIFE, reverence for nature, and walking the natural Way.
XII. Replace all political party organizations with the concept of "all under heaven one family."
XIII. Replace the pursuit of money, power, status, and fame with the pursuit of LIFE elevation.
XIV. Use joy, freedom, happiness, and delight as the moral standard.
XV. Each person works four hours per day. The remaining time is invested in games, entertainment, academic research, arts, inventions, and creative activities according to individual talent and interest.
XVI. Except for natural festivals, no more celebration of any holidays, including birthdays, gift-giving, and other elaborate social rituals.
XVII. Schools teach only natural science knowledge and music, art, and technical knowledge — not social studies such as philosophy, political science, economics, management science, or success studies.
XVIII. Let everyone aspire to climb to the summit of LIFE and human existence, rather than acquiring money, power, status, or fame.

"Following the above 18 channels, humanity can enter a more advanced civilization. Civilizational elevation does not proceed through violence, campaigns, struggle, seizure, suppression, invasion, or compulsion — but through the gentle-rain-moistening-without-sound method, the gradual-and-imperceptible method, the tender-and-warm method, and the conscious-and-voluntary method. Let everything be like seeds entering soil, naturally taking root, sprouting, growing, blossoming, and bearing fruit."
(Chanyuan Corpus · Human Life Chapter · *The 18 Channels of Human Civilizational Elevation*)

"The integration of all teachings, the unification of all schools, all under heaven one family, the world in Great Harmony — this is the main melody of the New Era."
(New Era Human Eight Hundred Concepts, Fourth Edition · Concept 701)

"People of the New Era do not want rule of law, nor rule of men — they want governance by the Dao. The core of Dao governance is walking the Way of the Greatest Creator. The core of the Way of the Greatest Creator is the natural Way. The core of the natural Way is harmonious coexistence among people, between people and society, and between people and nature."
