# Flirtatious Banter

*Flirtatious banter* (打情骂俏, *dǎ qíng mà qiào*) is a designated cultivation practice within Lifechanyuan's "Chaotic Cultivation Method" — a form of spontaneous, playful, non-exclusive teasing and affectionate exchange among community members. Its purpose is not to pursue or possess anyone, but to dissolve jealousy and possessiveness, expand one's inner capacity for love, and shift consciousness from ordinary human attachment toward a freer, more celestial state.
