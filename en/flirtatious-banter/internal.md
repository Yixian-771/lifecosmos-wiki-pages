# Flirtatious Banter · Internal Reference

> This version is for practitioners and researchers. It contains complete source quotations organized into a six-section framework with citations.

---

## Overview

*Flirtatious banter* (打情骂俏) is a key component of Lifechanyuan's "Chaotic Cultivation Method" (浑沌修炼法) — a full-spectrum practice approach. It refers to playful, affectionate teasing and jokes among community members, conducted spontaneously and without fixed targets, with no aim of possessing anyone. Through this practice, members open their hearts, dissolve jealousy and possessiveness, and cultivate the consciousness of celestial beings (仙), which is a prerequisite for entering advanced stages of cultivation.

---

## I. Definition and Nature

> First, use the method of flirtatious banter. When this atmosphere exists in the community, we should actively participate, open our hearts wide, allow ourselves to feel hurt, hurt and hurt again, until we finally develop resistance and immunity — until we see it so often it becomes normal.
>
> *(Guide's Other Writings · 2011 · Jealousy and Possessiveness Must Go Before Flirtatious Banter Can Stop)*

> Flirtatious banter is one of the threads woven through the entire [Chaotic Cultivation Method].
>
> *(Guide's Other Writings · 2011 · Looking Back on the Cultivation Path)*

> In our community, flirtatious banter and expressions of love are all meant to help everyone quickly face the truth of desire directly, so that we can finally shed our masks of false propriety.
>
> *(Guide's Other Writings · 2008 · Sending Fengsheng to the Celestial Islands)*

> Free and easy, moving with the moment — flirtatious banter as the occasion arises, optimistic in adversity, graceful in ease.
>
> *(Xuefeng Corpus · Encouragement · Eight Ways for Chanyuan Celestials to Elevate Their Quality)*

> [The people I want near me include:] those who enjoy flirtatious banter, those who are cheerful and carefree…
>
> *(Xuefeng Corpus · Caution · Guide's Declaration: No Human Sentiment, Only Celestial Sentiment)*

## II. Distinctions and Boundaries

**Flirtatious banter ≠ Romantic pursuit**

> In this community, you may engage in flirtatious banter freely — but falling in love is different. Flirtatious banter is spontaneous, without fixed targets, simply a kind of game. Romantic pursuit is one-to-one, exclusive, and is purely the psychology and behavior of ordinary worldly people.
>
> *(Guide's Other Writings · 2011 · Those Who Come to the Community to Fall in Love, Go Home)*

**Flirtatious banter ≠ Building a special relationship**

> Chanyuan Celestials may laugh and joke around with each other, chat freely, discuss Chanyuan concepts, study together, work together, and help each other — but they must not form exclusive alliances or cliques.
>
> *(Xuefeng Corpus · Chanyuan · The Way of Getting Along in the Community)*

**Flirtatious banter ≠ Trying to win someone**

> Our flirtatious banter is not aimed at gaining any particular person. It is for adjusting our cultivation life, so we can climb toward the highest realm of life and existence as lightly as possible — so that cultivation does not become grim, grotesque, or so obsessive that it distorts human nature.
>
> *(Guide's Other Writings · 2007 · Do Not Lose Direction)*

**Random targets, not fixed ones**

> We must not direct flirtatious banter at only one or a few people. It must follow the occasion — "whoever you meet, that's who you play with." This way, you end up with passionate feeling toward no one in particular, and your heart becomes calm. If you only banter with one or two people, the more you do it, the more you will pine for them, fantasize about them, and find no peace.
>
> *(Guide's Other Writings · 2011 · Jealousy and Possessiveness Must Go Before Flirtatious Banter Can Stop)*

**Non-interference is a measure of one's open heart**

> If you don't like flirtatious banter yourself, that's fine — but you may not forbid others from engaging in it. Even if people who seem as different in age as grandparent and grandchild are bantering with each other, don't interfere — expand your heart.
>
> *(Guide's Other Writings · 2009 · Open Your Heart Wide to Hold the Universe)*

## III. Practice and Standards

> In the Second Home, emotional love is free: flirtatious banter flows naturally, without fear of interference.
>
> *(Xuefeng Corpus · Chanyuan · Ways the Second Home Surpasses the Traditional Family)*

> Aside from flirtatious banter, which you may enjoy freely, you should also begin to reflect and take time to write short pieces on your insights about life, LIFE, space-time, cultivation and practice. Otherwise, progress will be slow.
>
> *(Guide's Other Writings · 2007 · One Small Hope)*

> Bantering with a few intimate friends, joking with the brothers, a well-aimed tease now and then — this is what makes me happy. This is where I find meaning in each day.

## IV. Cultivation Practice

**Prerequisite for advanced cultivation**

> Until jealousy, possessiveness, and the desire for control are gone, flirtatious banter in the community will not stop. Those who will enter advanced cultivation in the future are first and foremost those who banter freely and decently. Anyone who does not participate in flirtatious banter can forget about entering advanced cultivation.
>
> *(Guide's Other Writings · 2011 · Jealousy and Possessiveness Must Go Before Flirtatious Banter Can Stop)*

**The core mechanism: consciousness transformation**

> The method the Guide currently uses is to let everyone use the virtual space of the website to turn the community into an environment resembling the life of celestials in the Thousand-Year World — allowing people to open the locks on their minds, talk of love, and engage in flirtatious banter. Don't think this is easy. If your consciousness hasn't transformed, you simply won't be able to truly merge with everyone around you. Consciousness determines behavior. To enjoy the bliss of the Celestial Islands, you must first transform your consciousness. That is the supreme method.
>
> *(Guide's Other Writings · 2006 · Looking at the Distant Green Mountains — Exchange with Qiaojun Grass · response by Qifeng Grass)*

**Xuefeng leads by example**

> My flirtatious banter with the female Chanyuan Celestials — I genuinely like several of them… but more fundamentally, I am using my own behavior to fully transform everyone's consciousness. In the Celestial Realm, there is no concept of family. Celestials relate to each other as equals in love. If we don't build this new understanding in our minds, the antimatter structure of our LIFE cannot resonate with the needs of higher life spaces… My language is open, but my behavior is disciplined.
>
> *(Guide's Other Writings · 2006 · Continuing the Bare-Minded Conversation)*

## V. Obstacles and Opposition

**Jealousy and possessiveness are the root obstacles**

> Ask yourself honestly: when the person you love grows close to someone else, doesn't it hurt deeply — as though someone is tearing out your heart? … This is the emotion of ordinary worldly people. As long as we don't remove this jealousy and possessiveness, our LIFE cannot be elevated, and we have absolutely no qualifications to enter the Kingdom of Heaven.
>
> *(Guide's Other Writings · 2011 · Jealousy and Possessiveness Must Go Before Flirtatious Banter Can Stop)*

**Those who don't participate have inner obstacles**

> Those in the community who never or rarely engage in flirtatious banter certainly have obstacles in their heart. They have certainly developed a special relationship with someone of the opposite sex — either they are already in love and don't want to interact with others, or they are afraid that the other person will be upset, endangering the special relationship.
>
> Of course, when a person has cultivated themselves into a piece of wood, a zombie, or a vegetable, they won't want to engage in flirtatious banter either.
>
> *(Guide's Other Writings · 2011 · Jealousy and Possessiveness Must Go Before Flirtatious Banter Can Stop)*

**A satirical portrait of those who refuse**

> I understand now — you are proper people, noble people, defenders of morality, doers of great things, serious people, deep-thinking people, people who disdain flirtatious banter, lofty people, great figures… Great people must be spending every moment thinking about cosmic affairs. Only foolish people like us spend their days at play.
>
> *(Guide's Other Writings · 2014 · People Who Don't Engage in Flirtatious Banter Are All Great People Doing Important Things)*
> *(Written entirely in irony, satirizing those who maintain a "serious" facade as an excuse not to participate in the community's playful spirit.)*

## VI. Ultimate Destination

**Naturally transcended after reaching higher levels**

> Our flirtatious banter and expressions of love in the community are all meant to help everyone directly confront desire and ultimately shed their masks of false propriety… Once you have entered that door and merged directly with sexuality itself, there is no longer any need for flirtatious banter or talk of love — turn this page.
>
> *(Guide's Other Writings · 2008 · Sending Fengsheng to the Celestial Islands)*

> Reduce talk of love and flirtatious banter — this saves time and energy, and allows you to enter a state of stillness sooner, in which to cultivate spiritual powers.
>
> *(Guide's Other Writings · 2008 · Sending Fengsheng to the Celestial Islands)* — specific guidance for those who have reached a higher level.
