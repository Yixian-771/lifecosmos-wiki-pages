# Celestials of the Ten-Thousand-Year World (Internal Edition)

**Overview**

In the Lifechanyuan system, "Celestials of the Ten-Thousand-Year World" refers to the third tier of the five-tier celestial hierarchy — the *terrestrial celestials* (*lù dì xiān*) — along with their corresponding abode and mode of being. This is a heavenly realm one tier above the Thousand-Year World. The Ten-Thousand-Year World is a real planet 3,480 light-years from Earth, sixteen times Earth's size, with sixteen equidistant suns. The celestials who inhabit it — approximately one hundred million in total — have lifespans of roughly 35,000 years by Earth reckoning, which is why it is called the "Ten-Thousand-Year World." Its celestials share essential characteristics with the terrestrial immortals described in Daoist tradition: they possess extraordinary abilities, are adept at longevity cultivation, require no food or water, are unaffected by heat or cold, live without aging, and determine the moment of their own passing. Within the Ten-Thousand-Year World, a celestial's abilities and way of life advance in distinct stages as cultivation deepens — from walking the ground to hovering three feet above it, from flying over mountain peaks to ascending eighty thousand meters high; diet progresses from fruit to spring water to directly drawing on the luminous essence of sun and moon. All celestials in the Ten-Thousand-Year World are unclothed, and the two sexes can enter a state of profound bliss without physical contact.

## I. Position in the Celestial Hierarchy

### (i) The Correspondence Between Terrestrial Celestials and the Ten-Thousand-Year World

> Among celestials there are five tiers: heavenly celestials, divine celestials, terrestrial celestials, human celestials, and ghost celestials. Heavenly celestials dwell on the Celestial Islands Continent; divine celestials have no fixed abode; terrestrial celestials are in the Ten-Thousand-Year World; human celestials are in the Thousand-Year World; ghost celestials dwell between the yin and yang realms.

(Source: Chanyuan Corpus · On Cultivation · The Difference Between Heavenly Celestials and Buddhas)

### (ii) The Essential Nature of Terrestrial Celestials

> Terrestrial celestials possess extraordinary abilities or outstanding arts; they can benefit both humanity and themselves, can speak the language of humans to humans and the language of ghosts to ghosts, move about without constraint, coming and going freely… Terrestrial celestials are adept at the way of life cultivation… they have cultivated to the point of needing no food or water, unaffected by heat or cold, roaming islands at will, not aging, and determining their own death.

(Source: Chanyuan Corpus · On Cultivation · Classifications and Realms of Celestial Beings)

### (iii) Corresponding to the Second Step of the Three-Step Path to Becoming a Celestial

> The second step: the mind dwells nowhere; the mind has no obstruction; moving freely along with conditions; wandering at ease in accordance with nature; dwelling continually in delight — this is already being a celestial.

(Source: Chanyuan Corpus · On Cultivation · Three Steps to Becoming a Celestial)

> The three realms of celestial being… Second: no worldly dust, no attachments, no debts, no self, no selfishness, no clinging; unified with heaven and earth, unified with the Tao; having already reached having-nothing yet possessing-everything, having no home yet having home everywhere, having no kin yet having warmth everywhere.

(Source: Chanyuan Corpus · On Cultivation · The Three Realms of Celestial Being and the Three Steps to Becoming One)

## II. Physical Form of the Ten-Thousand-Year World

### (i) Location and Scale

> The heavenly Ten-Thousand-Year World. 3,480 light-years from Earth in distant space, there is a beautiful planet, sixteen times larger than Earth, with sixteen equidistant suns. Approximately one hundred million celestials live there. Their essential characteristics are similar to those of the terrestrial immortals described in Daoism. Their lifespans, relative to the human world, are approximately 35,000 years — which is why it is called the Ten-Thousand-Year World.

(Source: New Era Humanity Eight Hundred Concepts, 4th ed., Article 480)

### (ii) The Ten-Thousand-Year World Within the Three-Tier Heavenly Realm

> In the human world, the length of a person's life is just over eighty years; in the Thousand-Year World, the length of life is one thousand years; in the Ten-Thousand-Year World, the length of life is more than thirty-five thousand years; in the Elysium World's Celestial Islands Continent, there is no limit to the length of life.

(Source: Chanyuan Corpus · On Cultivation · Climbing Toward the Celestial Realm of Eternal Youth)

### (iii) Cosmic Coordinates

> The universe contains twenty parallel worlds… The Thousand-Year World is in the xy-z world; the Ten-Thousand-Year World is in the x-y-z world; the Celestial Islands Continent is in the xyz world.

(Source: New Era Humanity Eight Hundred Concepts, 4th ed., Article 404)

## III. The Staged Progression of Abilities

### (i) The Phased Development of Flight

> Celestials who first arrive in the Ten-Thousand-Year World have no ability to fly at all; they must walk on the ground. Only after approximately ten thousand years of cultivation can they hover three feet above the ground; after twenty thousand years of cultivation they can fly over high mountain ranges; and to fly at eighty thousand meters, three full decades of cultivation — thirty thousand years — are required.

(Source: Guide's Other Articles · 2008 · A Foretaste of the Atmosphere of "A Glimpse of the Ten-Thousand-Year World")

### (ii) The Phased Transformation of Diet

> The food of Ten-Thousand-Year World celestials varies according to the depth of their cultivation. Those newly arrived in the Ten-Thousand-Year World need to eat hard fruits such as walnuts and dates; celestials who have been there for ten thousand years eat softer fruits like peaches and grapes; those who have been there for twenty thousand years no longer eat fruit but drink only spring water rich in minerals; while those who have been there for more than thirty thousand years no longer consume any tangible substance — they draw directly on the luminous essence of sun and moon, the spiritual energy of heaven and earth.

(Source: Guide's Other Articles · 2008 · Please Enjoy Another Third of the Atmosphere of "A Glimpse of the Ten-Thousand-Year World")

### (iii) The Realm of the Highest Cultivation

> Wangtian Cliff is the most famous scenic spot in the Ten-Thousand-Year World. Those among the celestials whose cultivation runs deepest often fly there to absorb the essence of heaven and earth, refine their character, gather to discuss teachings and the Tao, and learn from one another. Of the one hundred million celestials in the Ten-Thousand-Year World, only a little over eight thousand can reach this place — the rest cannot. Why? Because reaching this place requires flying; climbing is absolutely impossible, as there are no branches to grip, no paths to walk.

(Source: Guide's Other Articles · 2008 · A Foretaste of the Atmosphere of "A Glimpse of the Ten-Thousand-Year World")

## IV. Life in the Ten-Thousand-Year World

### (i) All Celestials Are Unclothed

> In the Ten-Thousand-Year World, the celestials are all unclothed; nothing is hidden.

(Source: Guide's Other Articles · 2006 · What Does It Mean to Have Crossed the River? (Continued))

> Because Ten-Thousand-Year World celestials are all unclothed, their dance movements are especially captivating and endlessly memorable — every movement transmits energy, every form expresses subtle wonder…

(Source: Guide's Other Articles · 2008 · A Foretaste of the Atmosphere of "A Glimpse of the Ten-Thousand-Year World")

### (ii) The Sixteen Suns and the Eternal-Night Gemstone Zone

> The Ten-Thousand-Year World is sixteen times larger than Earth, with sixteen stars of varying density arranged equidistant in the sky, dividing it into sixteen zones of different luminosity. One of these zones is the night zone — an eternal darkness. And it is precisely this night zone that contains countless gems of every conceivable variety, glittering in the dark. As a result, all Ten-Thousand-Year World celestials go to this zone to gather gems to adorn their dwelling caves — the gems can illuminate their cave palaces, absorb the essential energy within the gems, and make the cave dwelling radiant and dreamlike.

(Source: Guide's Other Articles · 2008 · Please Enjoy Another Third of the Atmosphere of "A Glimpse of the Ten-Thousand-Year World")

### (iii) The Degree of Freedom

> Either break out of prison, or endure the torment of waiting out your sentence… So long as one is a Ten-Thousand-Year World celestial, one must still follow one's teacher and continue cultivating the Tao. Only upon reaching the Celestial Islands Continent of the Elysium World can one be truly free, able to follow one's every wish, transcending the constraints of necessity.

(Source: Guide's Other Articles · 2006 · Reply to Santiancao's Questions)

## V. The Core of Cultivation: Sublimation of Sexuality

### (i) The Core Sexual Cultivation of the Ten-Thousand-Year World

> In the Thousand-Year World, sexual cultivation is accomplished primarily through physical contact between the two sexes, merging into one… In the Ten-Thousand-Year World, physical contact between the sexes has ended; instead it is accomplished through the art and imagination of sexuality — it is an ascent from the bodily pleasure of the senses to the pleasure of spirit and mind. That is to say, the vibrational frequency of LIFE has risen: merely thinking of or seeing the object of one's love immediately brings stillness to the heart-mind, a continuous gentle bliss, a doubling of energy, and a radiant, luminous brilliance of love.

(Source: Guide's Other Articles · 2019 · Opening the Journey to the Ten-Thousand-Year World Today)

### (ii) The Graduated Rise in Freedom

> From human being to Thousand-Year World celestial to Ten-Thousand-Year World celestial, the degree of freedom rises by degrees. Of course, only by becoming a heavenly celestial and going to the Celestial Islands can one enter the completely free world — where nothing depends on external environment or factors, and everything can be created by oneself, achieving self-coherence.

(Source: Guide's Other Articles · 2019 · Opening the Journey to the Ten-Thousand-Year World Today)

### (iii) Dream Signs of the Ten-Thousand-Year World

> If you often dream of unusual and beautiful scenery, frequently fly in your dreams, frequently experience wondrous merging of heart, spirit, and body with someone of the other sex — with not the slightest trace of worry or fear — that is a sign that you are able to go to the Ten-Thousand-Year World.

(Source: New Era Humanity Eight Hundred Concepts, 4th ed., Article 488)

### (iv) The Orientation of This Life's Consciousness Determines the Next

> If you frequently fly freely in your dreams, and remain continually in a state of bliss without physical contact with the other sex, your next life will go to the heavenly Ten-Thousand-Year World.

(Source: New Era Humanity Eight Hundred Concepts, 4th ed., Article 496)
