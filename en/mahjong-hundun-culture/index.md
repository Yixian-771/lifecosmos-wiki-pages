# Mahjong and Hundun Culture

**Mahjong and Hundun Culture** is Lifechanyuan's central thesis for understanding **Hundun culture** — the true root of Chinese civilization. Xuefeng argues that the primary bloodline of Chinese culture is not Confucianism, Buddhism, or Taoism, but Hundun (primordial chaos). Mahjong embodies the Hundun principle: the astronomically vast combinations of 136 tiles appear random and disordered, yet they follow strict hidden laws — and "luck" at the table is the cosmic Hundun mechanism at work. To understand Mahjong is to understand Hundun; to understand Hundun is to understand the Tao of the Greatest Creator.
