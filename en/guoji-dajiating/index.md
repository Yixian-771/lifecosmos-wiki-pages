# International Family

The **International Family** is the central civilizational vision of the Lifechanyuan system. It transcends every boundary of nation, ethnicity, religion, and political party, grounded in the principle that "people of all ethnicities, religions, political parties, and groups across the world are indispensable members of the International Family." Its practical vehicle is the Second Home (Life Oasis); its mode of life is sharing fortune and hardship, pooling resources; its essential path is the purification of the heart-mind. In the New Era Human 800 Concepts, "All laws returning to their source, all teachings becoming one, all under heaven as one family, global unity" is declared the keynote of the new era. The ultimate vision is to establish 256 homes across the Earth — bringing humanity to the end of war, famine, and conflict — and ultimately to transform Earth into a paradise, with members welcomed into the Kingdom of Heaven after departing human life.
