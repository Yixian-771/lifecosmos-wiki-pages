# Truth (真 Zhēn)

[Gateway Page]

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence in this world.

**Truth** (真, Zhēn) is the foremost of the six core LIFE qualities in the Lifechanyuan system — Truth, Goodness, Beauty, Love, Faith, and Sincerity — and is the ontological foundation of the universe, the very attribute of the Greatest Creator (God), and the ultimate direction of all cultivation practice. It is the most essential seed in the soul garden of every Chanyuan Celestial.

In the Lifechanyuan framework, Truth is not merely a moral concept but a cosmological proposition: **there is only one Truth in the universe, and that is the Greatest Creator (God) himself**. All things are manifestations and artistic expressions of the Greatest Creator. Everything else is illusion.

At the level of conduct, Truth means "say yes when it is yes, say no when it is no" — faithfully reflecting the reality of things, being an honest person, speaking honest words. Lies and falsehood are tickets to hell; those who are honest will ultimately reach a beautiful destination.
