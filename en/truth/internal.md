## I. Overview and Definition

Truth (真, Zhēn) is the foremost of the six core LIFE qualities in the Lifechanyuan theoretical system — Truth, Goodness, Beauty, Love, Faith, and Sincerity. It is the ontological foundation of the universe, the attribute of the Greatest Creator (God), the fundamental direction of cultivation practice, the most essential seed in the soul garden, and the most indispensable quality for Chanyuan Celestials in all their actions. Truth is not merely a moral concept; it is a cosmological proposition — there is only one Truth in the universe, and that is the Greatest Creator himself.

**Core Definition I** (Essays of Xuefeng · Heart Chapter · Truth, Goodness and Beauty — The Core Values of Human Nature, complete original text):

> There is only one Truth in the universe. Apart from the Greatest Creator, everything else is an illusion — an artistic expression of the Greatest Creator.

**Core Definition II** (Article 118, complete original text):

> One who can rectify body and mind will have True Essence and True Spirit dwelling within, and great talent and great virtue emerging from within.

**Core Definition III** (Article 203, complete original text):

> Think something, and it comes; as long as the heart is sincere and the mind is true. Fear something, and it comes; as long as the mind is restless and unsettled.

**Core Definition IV** (Article 99, complete original text excerpt):

> Sow and cultivate Truth, Goodness, Beauty, Love, Faith, Sincerity, Harmony, diligence, courage, selflessness, dedication, service, care, tolerance, understanding, compassion, benevolence — these flowers and plants.

**Core Definition V** (Article 474 · Awakening to the Primordial Truth, complete original text):

> Eight: Awaken to the Primordial Truth. All things have a source; the source is the Primordial Truth. Consciousness, structure, and energy are the three elements constituting the universe; all else is "a dream, a phantom, a bubble, a shadow — like dew and like lightning, thus shall you regard them." The Buddha-God is the source of all, the Primordial Truth. Know the Greatest Creator, walk the path of the Greatest Creator — all 84,000 paths lead through. Without knowing the Greatest Creator, without walking the path of the Greatest Creator — all 84,000 paths end at sheer cliffs.
