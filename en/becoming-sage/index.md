# Becoming a Sage

**Becoming a Sage** (*chéng shèng* 成圣) is the highest human attainment in Lifechanyuan's cultivation system — a state in which one has fully dissolved the small self and acts solely for the benefit of all living beings. It is defined by eight qualities: no self, no selfishness, no personal agenda, no attachment, no desire, no resentment, no debt, and no hatred. Becoming a sage is both the culmination of human-level cultivation and the gateway to the Elysium Islands Continent in the highest life spaces.
