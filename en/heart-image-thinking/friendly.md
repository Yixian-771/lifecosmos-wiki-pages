# Heart-Image Thinking: Build It in Your Mind, and the Universe Will Build It in Reality

> "Heart-Image Thinking is using consciousness to create reality."
>
> — Guide Xuefeng, *Chanyuan Corpus · Revelation Chapter · Heart-Image Thinking*

---

## First, a Question

You want to live in a magnificent villa.

Conventional wisdom says: work hard, earn enough money, then build it. That might take ten or twenty years — and still might not happen.

Guide Xuefeng says: that's the foolish method.

The skillful method is this: **first calm your mind, then build the villa clearly inside your heart.** What style is it? How many floors? What flowers grow in the courtyard? What does life there feel like? … Fix that image in your consciousness.

And then?

> The Negative Universe will mobilize everything — including mobilizing yourself — to help you realize this wish. What remains is only a matter of time. Some may take a few years, some longer, but realization is inevitable.

That is **Heart-Image Thinking**.

---

## What Is Heart-Image Thinking?

Heart-Image Thinking is the fifth rung of the Eight Thinking Ladders.

The first four rungs (Material, Imagery, Associative, Illusory Thinking) are all addition — accumulating more knowledge, more perceptions, more deductions, more beliefs.

Heart-Image Thinking is the beginning of subtraction — no more accumulation; instead, clarity. **Use the image in your mind to directly drive cosmic energy, and let reality manifest according to that image.**

The core is one sentence: **use consciousness to create reality.**

---

## This Is Not Mysticism — It Is How the Universe Works

Negative Universe energy is neutral. It doesn't distinguish good from evil, rich from poor. It is impartial to all.

Whoever turns on the tap, water flows out for them.

Your heart-image is that tap.

- Heart-image a beautiful future → the universe cooperates to realize it.
- Spend your days worrying about terrible things → the universe cooperates to bring those terrible things about.

This is why Guide Xuefeng emphasizes with such urgency: **always think of good things; never think of bad things.** Before boarding a plane, don't imagine the plane crashing. Going to handle affairs, don't preset that things won't work out. The moment a negative thought arises, immediately say "No!" in your heart.

This is not psychological suggestion. It is the objective program by which the universe operates.

## Xuefeng's Personal Proof

This is not idle talk. Guide Xuefeng wrote down his own lived experience:

While studying in Zhangjiakou, he sat alone by a raised section of railway track, and a clear image appeared in his mind. Several years later, he found himself in the very reality that image had depicted.

In Zimbabwe, a friend told him: scenes he had imagined many years earlier had appeared in reality exactly as imagined.

Xuefeng then reflected on his own life: his coming to Zimbabwe, the shop he opened in Harare — all of it had been things he had previously imagined.

> Reality is unfolding strictly according to the images in my mind. Everyone take note: this is epoch-making evidence and thinking.

This is the cosmic law recorded in a real life — not coincidence.

## Heart-Image Thinking Is the Key to Becoming an Immortal

Heart-Image Thinking is not only for attracting villas and good fortune — it is the **core secret of becoming an immortal**.

Guide Xuefeng says:

> True images emerge from your mind. Becoming an immortal requires the purity of the mind. Heart-Image Thinking is the activity of the mind; the result of this activity is the secret of becoming an immortal.

What is the method of becoming an immortal?

> Within our consciousness we must create the future, create the immortal realm. If we spend our days imagining life in the Thousand-Year World, gradually our LIFE structure will evolve toward the Thousand-Year World. When the fruit is ripe and the water flows to its channel, one can go to the Thousand-Year World.

Every day you heart-image the Thousand-Year World, your LIFE structure evolves toward the Thousand-Year World. This is the genuine engineering of cultivation — not reciting sutras, not sitting in meditation, but building every day in your mind the world you want to reach.

## How to Do It: Enter Stillness Daily and Create Heart-Images

The practice is simple:

1. **Set aside a little time each day to become still.** It doesn't need to be long — even a few minutes.
2. **In that stillness, create heart-images.** Begin by imagining the Elysian sanctuary — the joyful, free life of Chanyuan Celestials together. Then imagine the Thousand-Year World. Then the Celestial Islands Continent of the Elysium World…
3. **The clearer the image the better.** Not vague — specific. Feel yourself there.
4. **Only imagine good things; never imagine bad things.** The Negative Universe energy is neutral and impartial — it cooperates with whatever you imagine.

That's it.

---

## The Watershed Between Addition and Subtraction

The first four rungs are addition: keep piling things into the brain — knowledge, perceptions, patterns, beliefs.

Heart-Image Thinking is where subtraction begins: stop piling in; instead, empty out. Clearer. Purer. More direct.

Further along — Taiji Thinking, Non-Form Thinking, Hundun Thinking — "thinking" itself grows less and less. Eventually nothing needs to be thought at all; one simply is the universe itself.

Guide Xuefeng's requirement for Chanyuan Celestials: abandon the first four types of thinking. **Enter Heart-Image Thinking directly.**
