# New Era Consciousness

**New Era Consciousness** is a vast, integrative system of awareness that draws from the full spectrum of human wisdom traditions — from Seth and Orin to *The Law of One*, *Conversations with God*, and *A Course in Miracles* — spanning science, philosophy, psychology, cosmology, and spirituality. In the Life Chanyuan framework, **Life Chanyuan consciousness is New Era Consciousness**: it synthesizes all these "pearls" on a single thread — the path of the Greatest Creator.

> New Era Consciousness asks us to awaken from the slumber of our souls, enter a more genuinely real reality, and enjoy a life that is freer, more comfortable, and free from fear.
>
> — Xuefeng, *Fully Embrace New Era Consciousness*
