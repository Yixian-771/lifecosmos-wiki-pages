# Yangsheng — Life Cultivation (Elementary · Intermediate · Advanced)

**Yangsheng** (养生, life cultivation or nourishing life) in the Lifechanyuan system is a three-tier cultivation framework: the elementary level focuses on protecting the physical body; the intermediate level addresses both the physical body and the nonmaterial LIFE body simultaneously; the advanced level works primarily through the nonmaterial LIFE body via conscious visualization. Its overarching purpose is to "preserve health, attain longevity, shed mortal bones, and achieve the Celestial Way" — ultimately serving the perfection of the nonmaterial structure of LIFE and the elevation toward becoming a Celestial or Buddha.

> Everyone in the New Era needs yangsheng, minimizing illness to the greatest extent possible. Yangsheng primarily cultivates essence, qi, spirit, and form. Cultivating yangsheng begins with cultivating the heart-mind; cultivating the heart-mind begins with cultivating virtue; cultivating virtue begins with entering the Dao.
>
> — 800 Values for New Era Humanity (4th ed.), Value 743
