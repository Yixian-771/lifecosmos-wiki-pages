# Retained Information Space

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological
> essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across
> incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage
> of human existence in this world.

**The Retained Information Space** is one of the 36-dimensional spaces in Lifechanyuan's cosmological system — a universal recording medium in which every word, action, and inner thought of every living being is faithfully documented. These records persist as long as the universe exists; they never vanish.

> "After any matter moves, it leaves information in the places where it has been active — information that does not disappear in the short term. All matter is a kind of 'photographic film' that accurately records everything that has taken place around it. The Retained Information Space is the space that can display scenes that have already occurred."
> — *Chanyuan Corpus · Cosmic Spacetime · 36-Dimensional Spaces*

---

## Core Overview

- **Cosmic dimension**: The Fifth Dimension (beyond the Fourth Dimension of time; preceding the Sixth Dimension of spatial movement)
- **Function**: Holographic, perpetual recording of all speech, action, and thought in the cosmos — in documentary rather than photographic form
- **Relationship to karma**: A person's future is determined by the rewards and penalties applied on the basis of their record in the Retained Information Space
- **Relationship to memory**: Memory is the brain retrieving data from the Retained Information Space — the brain does not store the data itself
