None of this is mysticism. The Retained Information Space is the dimension of the universe dedicated to "displaying" these already-occurred scenes.

## The Universe Is a Camera That Never Turns Off

In our modern world, cameras follow us everywhere — banks, airports, street corners, hallways.

But that is only the human-scale version of surveillance.

**The universe's surveillance is far more complete.**

It is not that cameras are installed in certain places. Rather: **everything in the universe is itself a camera.**

- Every word you speak is entered into the Retained Information Space the moment it leaves your lips.
- Every word you write is entered the moment you put it down.
- Every thought that arises in your mind is converted into a frequency wave, transmitted into the universe, and recorded.

These records are **not like photographs — they are like documentary films**: complete, continuous, with full context and cause-and-effect. No detail is left out.

And most importantly: **as long as the universe exists, these records exist.** Words spoken years ago, centuries ago, millennia ago, hundreds of millions of years ago — all of it is completely archived, permanent and indelible.

## Your Memory Is Not Stored in Your Brain

This may reshape the way you think about your own mind.

We usually believe memory is stored in the brain's neural connections. When brain cells die, memories are lost.

Lifechanyuan reveals a different reality:

**"Memory" is the brain retrieving data from the Retained Information Space. The brain does not store the data itself.**

The brain is not a warehouse. The brain is an interface.

The real database is in the universe.

This is why hypnosis can allow a person to "watch" their own past like a television series — hypnosis opens that interface, and consciousness retrieves directly from the Retained Information Space.

This is also why, in dreams, you sometimes meet loved ones who have passed away — so real, so clear. **You did not enter the underworld. In the dream state, your consciousness entered the Retained Information Space, where the information left by that person still exists in complete form.** Those records are there, waiting to be encountered.
