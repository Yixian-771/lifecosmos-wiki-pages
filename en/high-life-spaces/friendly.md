# Where Do We Go After Death? Lifechanyuan's Most Specific Cosmic Map

> "Heaven is the collective name for the Thousand-Year World, the Ten-Thousand-Year World, the Elysium World, and its Celestial Islands Continent."
>
> — *Eight Hundred Concepts*, Concept 477

> "The most urgent matter in life is how to develop and evolve the antimatter structure of LIFE with spirituality toward the higher LIFE spaces."
>
> — *Eight Hundred Concepts*, Concept 60

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence in this world.

## Not a Vague "Heaven" — But Real Cosmic Space With Coordinates

Religion says "go to heaven after death" — but where is heaven? What kind of person can go? How do you get there?

Lifechanyuan gives the most specific answer ever offered: **Heaven is not a vague concept — it is six real higher LIFE spaces that genuinely exist in the cosmos.**

## Which Space You Go To Depends on Your LIFE Structure

> "Whatever kind of LIFE structure one has, that is the kind of space one lives in." (Concept 468)

Not faith. Not prayer. It depends on the **degree of perfection of your antimatter structure**:

- Develop perfect humanity (kindness, honesty, no selfishness…) → **Thousand-Year World**
- Develop terrestrial-immortal qualities (no illness, no worries, long lifespan…) → **Ten-Thousand-Year World**
- Develop the consciousness of a Buddha → **Elysium World**
- Develop celestial-immortal qualities (self-coherent, completely free) → **Celestial Islands Continent**

**Dreams are omens**: frequent dreams of green mountains and clear water → Thousand-Year World; frequent dreams of flying freely → Ten-Thousand-Year World; dreaming of transforming at will → Elysium World.

## The Route Map to Heaven: Three Steps

1. **Enter the Peach-Blossom Land** (Second Home life) → path to the Thousand-Year World
2. **Understand the eight modes of thinking** → path to the Ten-Thousand-Year World
3. **Understand the eight mysteries of LIFE** → path to the Celestial Islands Continent of the Elysium World

The Second Home is a copy of the Thousand-Year World in the human world — living there is practising the way of life of the immortals.

## Two Mountains to Cross

On the road to heaven, two blocking mountains stand in the way:

**Mountain One**: Programmes created by humanity — family, ethnicity, nation, religion, political party… You cannot reach the Heavenly Kingdom without walking out of these.

**Mountain Two**: The Egypt of the heart — jealousy, comparison, blame, arrogance, anger, greed… You cannot reach the Heavenly Kingdom without clearing out this mental rubbish.

> "Walk out of the programmes created by humanity; escape 'the Egypt of the heart'; break free from the chains; head toward freedom."
