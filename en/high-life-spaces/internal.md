## I. Overview and Definition

Higher LIFE Spaces is one of the three major LIFE-space systems in the Lifechanyuan cosmological view — alongside lower LIFE spaces and the neutral world. The universe has 36-dimensional space, and these 36 dimensions are not equal and homogeneous; rather, according to the spiritual level of LIFE and the degree of perfection of the antimatter structure, they form three clearly distinct tiers of LIFE existence: higher, neutral, and lower. The higher LIFE spaces are the world of truth, goodness, and beauty; the world of greatest freedom; the ultimate destination of LIFE's cultivation and elevation.

**Core Definition One** (*Chanyuan Corpus · Antimatter World · The Elysium World*, complete original excerpt):

> The six realms of the Serene Realm, the Celestial Realm, the Elysium World, the Yin-Pole Black Hole, the Ten-Thousand-Year World, and the Thousand-Year World are the higher LIFE spaces.
>
> The six realms of the Domestic Animal Realm, the Animal Realm, the Plant Realm, the Netherworld, the Frozen Layer, and the Fire-Tempering Layer are the lower LIFE spaces.
>
> The six realms of the Hundun Realm, the Yang-Pole Black Hole, the Dream Realm, the Insect Realm, the Bacterial Realm, and the Mountains-Rivers-Climate Realm are the neutral world.

The human world happens to sit precisely at the boundary surface between these two symmetrical tiers — thus forming the symmetry of LIFE in the universe as a whole, which is the simplified form of the overall LIFE structure of "heaven, the human world, and hell" in the ordinary sense.
