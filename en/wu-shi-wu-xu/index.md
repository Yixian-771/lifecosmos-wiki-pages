## Overview

In the Lifechanyuan system, the **practical** (еӢҷеҜҰ, *wГ№ shГӯ*) refers to pursuing material survival needs вҖ” food, clothing, shelter, safety; the **ideal** (еӢҷиҷӣ, *wГ№ xЕ«*) refers to pursuing faith and ideals, cultivating consciousness and character quality. The two are dialectically unified: the lower a being's level, the more it focuses on the practical; the higher, the more on the ideal. Walking the path of the Greatest Creator requires balancing both вҖ” the practical secures the foundation, the ideal elevates LIFE, with the ultimate aim of attaining the qualities of a celestial being and entering higher life spaces.
