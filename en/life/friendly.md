# What Is LIFE? You May Have It Completely Backwards

> "LIFE is a spiritual antimatter structure."
>
> — *Eight Hundred Concepts for the New Era of Humanity*, Concept 357

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence in this world.

---

## The Common Assumption Is Wrong

Most people believe: LIFE = the physical body. You have a heartbeat, you're breathing — you're alive. Your heart stops — you're dead.

Lifechanyuan says: This is completely backwards.

> "All matter is not LIFE — it is the vehicle of LIFE. Just as a person's watch, car, house, and clothing are not the person himself, but rather appendages and decorations of his LIFE. To know him, one cannot know him through his appendages and decorations."

The physical body is only **LIFE's vehicle**. The real LIFE is that invisible, intangible, spiritual **antimatter structure** — in other words, the soul.

---

## What Is an Antimatter Structure?

Time, space, thought, consciousness, mental intentions, spirit, soul — these are all antimatter. They are invisible and intangible, yet they truly exist and act upon the material world.

The dreams you dream are manifestations of the antimatter world. Your thoughts are antimatter. Your soul is a spiritual antimatter structure — and that is LIFE.

---

## LIFE = Spiritual Body + Physical Body (1+1=1)

LIFE is composed of two parts:

- **Spiritual body** (formless): consciousness, thought, spirit, soul
- **Physical body** (formed): heart, brain, organs

But this is 1+1=1, not 1+1=2. Only their union is called LIFE — just as hydrogen and oxygen united become water.

When the soul departs the body, the physical body immediately dies. As long as the soul remains, no matter how damaged the body, LIFE itself continues to exist.

---

## LIFE Does Not Die

One of Lifechanyuan's most astounding truths: **death is not the terminus of LIFE.**

LIFE's indestructibility has five grounds:
1. The universe does not perish, therefore LIFE is indestructible
2. The morality of the universe is eternal, therefore LIFE is indestructible
3. LIFE bodies undergo reincarnation and transformation, therefore LIFE is indestructible
4. The spirit of God — the Tao — endures forever, therefore LIFE is indestructible
5. The antimatter structure cannot disappear, therefore LIFE is indestructible

What we call "death" is simply the soul leaving this particular physical vehicle and going to the next LIFE space. Like sending a fax: the original (the physical body) can be discarded, but the information (the soul) has already been transmitted to the other end.

---

## LIFE Has Levels — Where Are You Now?

LIFE has sixteen levels. Whatever level you are at, that is the space in which you live:

> "Animals toil on the ground; birds soar through the air; bees weave through the flowers; flies go back and forth in garbage heaps; ordinary worldly people bustle about in a society of familial private ownership; wise persons swim freely in a state of public ownership; immortals are at ease in an environment of non-ownership; celestial immortals do as they please in their own world."

The higher the level of LIFE, the fewer the contradictions, the greater the freedom, and the higher the content of love in consciousness.

What determines the level? **LIFE's antimatter structure.** Change your consciousness, and you change your structure. Change your structure, and LIFE elevates to a higher level.

---

## What Determines Where LIFE Goes After Death?

The reincarnation standard is precise:

- Those of **supreme love** → elevated to celestial immortals
- Those of **supreme goodness** → elevated to Buddhas
- Those of **cruelty** → descend to the fire-refining layer
- Those of **indifference** → pass on to the plant realm

Reincarnation is not determined by religious rituals, not by praying, not by money — it is determined entirely by the antimatter structure of LIFE itself. What structure you have cultivated, that is where you go.

---

## The Most Wonderful Experience of LIFE

> "The most wonderful experience of LIFE is the experience of consciousness in a 'zero state' — for it can feel everything."

Zero state — complete release, complete openness — this is the highest freedom state of consciousness. It is not disappearance; it is merging with everything.

---

## The Most Urgent Thing in Life

Not earning money. Not finding a partner. Not getting promoted.

> "The most urgent thing in human life is how to cause the antimatter structure of LIFE — which is spiritual — to develop and evolve toward high-level LIFE spaces, and how to elevate one's character and perfect one's spirituality."

We have whatever LIFE future corresponds to whatever LIFE structure we have. **Cultivation is not of behavior — it is of structure.**
