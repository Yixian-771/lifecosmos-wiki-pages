# Advanced Cultivation

**Advanced Cultivation** is the third and highest tier of the Lifechanyuan cultivation system. Beginning from the state of "forgetting both self and world," it centers on breaking through all prescribed forms and regulations, awakening to Buddha-nature, creating the LIFE structure of a Celestial Being, and ultimately entering the higher LIFE spaces of the negative universe — becoming a Celestial Being. As the Guide Xuefeng teaches: "Elementary Cultivation is spinning the cocoon; Intermediate Cultivation is nurturing new LIFE within the cocoon; Advanced Cultivation is breaking free from the cocoon and becoming a Celestial Being."
