# the Ten-thousand-year World

[Gateway Page]

The Ten-thousand-year World is the middle-layer higher-life realm in the Lifechanyuan Heaven hierarchy:
it is above the Thousand-year World and below the Elysium World, forming a key link in the progressive structure of the Kingdom of Heaven.

This page is a multi-version gateway, providing positioning, reading guidance, and version navigation (not full body content).

---

## Positioning

### Spatial role
A material world about 3,480 light-years from Earth (within the positive universe frame).

### Hierarchical role
The middle layer in the three-level Heaven sequence:
Thousand-year World → Ten-thousand-year World → Elysium World.

### Cultivation role
A target realm reached through further refinement of LIFE's nonmaterial structure.

---

## Key Points

1. The Ten-thousand-year World is presented as an objective realm, not symbolic metaphor.
2. In the twenty parallel-world coordinate narrative, it corresponds to the x-y-z world.
3. It differs from earthly life in lifespan scale, life mode, and cultivation capability.
4. It functions both as an ascent target and as a post-sitting transition node.
5. Its entry and destination logic are linked to dream signs, consciousness state, and structure refinement.
