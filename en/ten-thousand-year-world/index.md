# The Ten-Thousand-Year World

[Entry Gateway]

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological
> essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across
> incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage
> of human existence in this world.

**The Ten-Thousand-Year World** (万年界) is the middle layer of the three heavenly tiers in the Lifechanyuan framework — above the Thousand-Year World and below the Elysium World. LIFE here exists for ten thousand years in the subjective experience of the heavenly realm, in a state of beauty, harmony, and freedom that far surpasses the Thousand-Year World.

> The Ten-Thousand-Year World is for those whose antimatter structure has been refined beyond the Thousand-Year level — who have cultivated toward the qualities of the divine.
>
> — Guide Xuefeng

---

## Core Positioning

In the Lifechanyuan system, the Ten-Thousand-Year World is the middle link in the progressive structure of the Kingdom of Heaven. It is the destination for LIFE that has cultivated beyond the level corresponding to the Thousand-Year World but has not yet reached the celestial level of the Elysium World.
