## II. What Is Vibrational Frequency? Three Ways to Understand

**Analogy One: The Dragonfly's Wings**

> When a dragonfly or bee is still on a branch or petal, its wings vibrate at a very low frequency and we can clearly see them. When it takes off, we can only vaguely see its wings. Once it flies at high speed, we can barely see them at all. The slower the wings beat, the more visible; the faster, the less visible — even invisible. (Raise Vibrational Frequency)

This answers one of humanity's oldest questions: why can't we see God? Not because God doesn't exist — but because God's vibrational frequency is so high that it exceeds the range our eyes can detect. Invisibility is not absence. It is proof of energy vastly beyond human scale.

**Analogy Two: The High-Performance Car**

> The more advanced the car, the more responsive the steering, the sharper the brakes, the easier the acceleration — the better the overall performance — because the more advanced something is, the higher its sensitivity, the faster its response, the higher its vibrational frequency. (Baobei Cao Asks the Guide about Frequency)

A person with high vibrational frequency is like a high-performance vehicle: fast response, sharp sensing, strong energy. A person with low frequency is like an old, failing car: sluggish, dull, draining.

**Analogy Three: The Radio Tuner**

The universe broadcasts on countless channels — the channel of Heaven, the channel of the immortal realm, the channel of the human realm, the channel of the lower realms. Which channel you receive depends entirely on what frequency your LIFE is tuned to. When your frequency matches, resonance happens naturally. When it doesn't, all you get is noise — no matter how hard you try.
