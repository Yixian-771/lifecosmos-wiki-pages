# Xuefeng Communism (Internal Edition)

## Overview

Xuefeng Communism is the new model of human production and living proposed and personally practiced by Lifechanyuan's Guide Xuefeng. Built on the core principle of "each contributing according to ability, each taking according to need," it is characterized by the dissolution of nations, religions, political parties, money, power, and the institution of marriage and family. Using the Second Home (Life Oasis) as its living carrier, it has been in practice since 2009 — over fifteen years. It is regarded as the highest form of human civilization, the only true path to happiness for the laboring masses, and the practical arena for practitioners on the path to immortality and Buddhahood.

Marx's communist theory was incomplete, and the methods used by Lenin, Mao, and others — seizing the property of capitalists and landlords — violated the principles of natural law, making resistance and eventual failure inevitable. Was the ideal of communism wrong? No. To embody the true meaning of communism, I have named my theory and practice "Xuefeng Communism," to distinguish it from the traditional communism that has been demonized.

*(Source: Xuefeng Anthology · Friendship Section · An Introduction to Xuefeng Communism)*

---

"Xuefeng Communism" operates according to natural principles. It absolutely does not seize or plunder the wealth of capitalists and landlords. It is a brand new model of production and living, unlike anything in human history, entered into freely and willingly. One core factor is that in Xuefeng Communism communities there can be no marriage or family, no private property — all wealth is shared: "each according to ability, each according to need." Every member must achieve owning nothing personally while possessing everything. Xuefeng Communism implements Hundun Management: no leaders, no management hierarchy, no managers — only servers. No one can become powerful or elevated above others — it is impossible and absolutely not permitted. Everyone is a worker and a server, differing only in division of labor, with absolutely no distinctions of high or low, noble or base, rich or poor.

*(Source: Xuefeng Anthology · Friendship Section · An Introduction to Xuefeng Communism)*

## III. The Eight Core Features (Eight Defining Characteristics of the Second Home)

Summarizing thought and experience, the communist community — the Second Home — has the following eight defining characteristics.

**I. The Way of the Greatest Creator · Hundun Management**

The Way of the Greatest Creator is the natural way. We always operate according to natural principles — the way of the scientific development concept. Hundun Management means non-management, because the highest level of management is non-management: let everything proceed according to natural principles, fully humanistic management. You will find no leaders, no offices, no managers in the community — because everyone is a manager, everyone is the master of the community. No hierarchy, no ranks, everyone equal.

**II. Owning Nothing · Possessing Everything**

Members of the Second Home own nothing personally. Individuals no longer hold any private belongings or money. Everything in the community belongs to the community, not to any individual. Each person may freely use everything in the community as needed, without extravagance or waste.

**III. Each According to Ability · Each According to Need**

Ability and physical strength differ among individuals. In the community, each member need only wholeheartedly do what they are capable of each day. There is no forced uniformity, no compulsion to do what one cannot do. Any personal need is fully met — there is no distribution problem.

**IV. No Marriage · No Family**

Marriage and family are the breeding ground of human selfishness, the source of troubles and suffering, and the root of endless plundering and waste of natural resources. Therefore, in the Second Home there is no marriage and no family.

**V. The Old Are Cared For · The Young Are Raised**

All members' needs for food, clothing, shelter, transportation, and care from birth to death are fully borne by the community. The elderly are cared for by the community; children are raised and educated by the community. Children no longer worry about their parents; parents no longer worry about their children.

**VI. Away from Politics · Away from Religion**

No political activities of any kind. No religious ceremonies of any kind. All activities follow natural principles — no idol worship, no political or religious holidays or rituals. Love mountains, rivers, and grass; protect insects, birds, and wildlife.

**VII. Regardless of Background · Everyone Works**

Once you enter the Second Home, everyone is equal — no more distinctions of high or low. Whether a PhD or illiterate; whether a millionaire or pauper; whether a scholar or shepherd; whether an official or commoner; whether man or woman; whether white or black — everyone becomes a laborer. We care nothing about a person's background; we care only about the present.

**VIII. Fluid and Adaptive · Infinitely Changing**

Everything in the community follows the principle of least resistance. The motto: change within constancy, constancy within change, no fixed dharma, infinite transformation. To enrich cultural life, residences and assignments change frequently, people are constantly relocated, entertainment activities constantly vary — keeping the community in constant motion. Two people cannot stay together indefinitely; everyone is always in a fresh environment. This prevents entropy from reaching maximum and the community becoming stagnant; it stimulates interest and thinking; it prevents factions from forming and ensures the ultimate formation of a globally unified, harmonious, peaceful family.

*(Source: Chanyuan Anthology · Human Life Section · The Eight Defining Characteristics of the Second Home Communist Community)*
