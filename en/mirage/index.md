# Mirage (海市蜃楼)

In the Lifechanyuan cosmological framework, a mirage is not an atmospheric optical illusion but a genuine glimpse of the Elysium World — a temporary opening in which the higher-dimensional reality of the thirty-six-dimensional universe becomes briefly visible to those who are spiritually attuned. Rather than reflected light from distant earthly objects, a mirage represents a deliberate act of communication from the Creator: a signal that another, more beautiful realm exists beyond the material world we perceive.

> "A mirage is a revelation from celestials and Buddhas — a fragmentary display of the Elysium World within the thirty-six-dimensional space made visible in the human realm. Put simply, a mirage is the true landscape of the Elysium World."
>
> *(Chanyuan Corpus · The Creator Chapter · Mirage — A Manifestation of the Elysium World)*
