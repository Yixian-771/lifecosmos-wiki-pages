# Spacetime Tunnel (Friendly Version)

## What if the Universe Has More Than One Room?

Imagine the universe isn't just one place — it's a building with 20 rooms, all existing at the same time.

In Lifechanyuan's cosmology, the world we live in — the human world — is just one of those rooms, labeled "xy." Other rooms include the world of gods and celestial beings (xyz, also called the Celestial Island Continent), the realm of lingering spirits (-x-y), the Millennium Realm, the Ten-Thousand-Year Realm, and more.

These worlds all coexist, right now. We simply can't perceive most of them.

The spacetime tunnel is what connects these rooms — the universe's hidden highway system.

---

## Two Types of Tunnel

**The Time Tunnel** — travels up and down through time  
Think of it as a dense web of "time threads" woven from both positive and negative time, spread throughout the entire universe. If you want to see your past or your future, this is the path.

**The Space Tunnel** — travels sideways between worlds  
This is the channel linking the 36 dimensions — the express route from one parallel world to another. Want to reach the Millennium Realm? The Celestial Island Continent? Take the space tunnel.

One is vertical. One is horizontal. Together, they form the universe's three-dimensional transit network.

---

## The Greatest Creator: The Center of Everything

Here's the remarkable part: if you map the entire universe as a coordinate system, all 20 worlds meet at a single point — the origin point O. That origin is the dwelling of the Greatest Creator, called Qinglianjie (the Pure-Cool Realm).

> All 20 parallel worlds are connected to the O point, so the Greatest Creator exists simultaneously in all 20 worlds. Without the Greatest Creator, no world could exist.

This means: to travel from one world to another, you have to pass through that origin. Walk the path of the Greatest Creator — otherwise, you're stuck circling your own room forever.

---

## Why Can't We See Other Worlds?

Our five senses — sight, hearing, smell, taste, touch — are only calibrated for the material world, the xy plane. It's like being stuck reading a 2D map when the territory is actually 3D.

When your *spirit eye* opens — when your awareness expands — other worlds become visible:

> If our spirit eye opens and our thinking reaches that level, we can "see" the other 19 worlds and know that what seems illusory is not — "emptiness is form."

---

## How Do You Enter?

**Method 1: Deep Stillness**

> Close your eyes. Become completely still. Clear your mind of all material-world consciousness. Enter a nirvana-like state. You will immediately enter the space tunnel — and based on the state of your spiritual body, you can reach another world in an instant.

Silence and stillness are the doorway. This is why meditation traditions across cultures describe journeys to "other realms."

**Method 2: You May Already Be on the Way**

> The moment you became a Chanyuan practitioner, you already entered the space tunnel leading to the Celestial Island Continent. Though your physical body remains in the human world, your spiritual body has been roaming the space tunnel in the company of the guide. We set out long ago.

If you're already a Chanyuan grass practitioner, there's no need to wonder if the journey has started. It has.

## What Awaits at the Other End?

> Crossing the spacetime tunnel of ten thousand years, breaking free from shackles of a thousand years, we enter a realm of exquisite perfection — mountains verdant, streams clear, birdsong everywhere, fragrance in the air, immersed in the sacred realm of heaven, overflowing with joy.

That destination is the Celestial Island Continent — the place Lifechanyuan points to as the ultimate home for those who complete the journey.
