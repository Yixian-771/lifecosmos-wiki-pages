# Spacetime Tunnel (Internal Reference)

## I. Definition and Nature

**Time Tunnel**

The dense network of time formed by positive and negative time permeating all of space is called the time tunnel.  
*(New Era Humanity's 800 Concepts, 4th Ed., Entry 429)*

Time has eight characteristics: (1) Time is antimatter; (2) Time has positive and negative polarity; (3) Time has both particularity and universality; (4) Time is a variable; (5) Time permeates all material space; (6) Time has both vertical and horizontal dimensions; (7) Time has different meanings in different spaces; (8) Time can be compressed, expanded, or made to disappear.  
*(New Era Humanity's 800 Concepts, 4th Ed., Entry 428)*

**Space Tunnel**

The channels connecting the 36-dimensional spaces are called space tunnels.  
*(New Era Humanity's 800 Concepts, 4th Ed., Entry 425)*

**Comprehensive Definition**

The dense network of time formed by positive and negative time permeating all of cosmic space is called the time tunnel. The channels through which the 36 dimensions are interconnected are called space tunnels. The time tunnel is always vertical; the space tunnel is always horizontal. To travel to the past or the future, one uses the time tunnel; to travel among the 36 dimensions, one uses the space tunnel.  
*(Chanyuan Corpus, Antimatter World Chapter, "The Spacetime Tunnel and 20 Parallel Worlds")*

---

## II. Distinguishing the Two Tunnels

The antimatter world appears in two forms: what is seen through the time tunnel, and what is seen through the space tunnel. What is seen through the time tunnel is vertical — to see your past or future state, you must enter the time tunnel. What is seen through the space tunnel is horizontal — to know the other antimatter worlds existing at this very moment, you must enter the space tunnel.  
*(New Era Humanity's 800 Concepts, 4th Ed., Entry 436)*

The universe contains horizontal spacetime, within which time does not exist. When in horizontal spacetime, one feels no passage of time. Yet measured in Earth-time, a few seconds in horizontal spacetime may equal decades, or even thousands of years.  
*(New Era Humanity's 800 Concepts, 4th Ed., Entry 431)*

## III. The 20 Parallel Worlds — The Structural Context

**The Mathematical Model of 20 Quadrants**

Time and space intersect to form 20 quadrants. Time has positive and negative polarity; space has positive and negative polarity. Taking only positive time and positive space gives one quadrant — the first quadrant of a Cartesian plane. Adding negative time and negative space yields four quadrants: xy, x-y, -xy, -x-y. When entering the antimatter world, an imaginary axis *i* is discovered; substituting z for i creates a three-dimensional coordinate system with 20 quadrants: xyz, xy-z, x-y-z, x-yz, -x-y-z, -xyz, -x-yz, -xy-z, xz, x-z, -xz, -x-z, yz, y-z, -yz, -y-z, xy, x-y, -xy, -x-y. This means the entire universe contains 20 worlds, which may be called 20 parallel worlds because they coexist simultaneously. Among these, humanity's world is xy; what is called the ghost world is -x-y; the world of gods and buddhas is xyz; the world of demons is -x-y-z; the Millennium Realm is xy-z; the Ten-Thousand-Year Realm is x-y-z; and the Celestial Island Continent is xyz.  
*(Chanyuan Corpus, Antimatter World Chapter, "The Spacetime Tunnel and 20 Parallel Worlds")*

**The 20 Parallel Worlds**

The universe contains 20 parallel worlds: humanity's world is xy; the ghost world is -x-y; the world of gods and buddhas is xyz; the world of demons is -x-y-z; the Millennium Realm is xy-z; the Ten-Thousand-Year Realm is x-y-z; and the Celestial Island Continent is xyz.  
*(New Era Humanity's 800 Concepts, 4th Ed., Entry 404)*

**The Greatest Creator as Point O — The Nexus of All Worlds**

Where is the Greatest Creator? The Greatest Creator is in Qinglianjie (the Pure-Cool Realm). Qinglianjie is the origin point O of the coordinate system — every axis, x, y, and z, passes through this O point. All 20 parallel worlds are connected to the O point; thus the Greatest Creator exists simultaneously in all 20 worlds. Without the Greatest Creator, no world could exist.

Why should we walk the path of the Greatest Creator? Because only through the Greatest Creator can we reach any other space. Without going through the Greatest Creator, one can only circle within one's own world, never reaching another.  
*(Chanyuan Corpus, Antimatter World Chapter, "The Spacetime Tunnel and 20 Parallel Worlds")*

The Greatest Creator's essence resides in Qinglianjie; the Greatest Creator's spirit pervades all things in the universe. Qinglianjie is the 0 point of the coordinate system. Every axis passes through this 0 point; all 20 parallel worlds are connected to it. Thus the Greatest Creator exists simultaneously in all 20 parallel worlds. Without the Greatest Creator, no world could exist.  
*(New Era Humanity's 800 Concepts, 4th Ed., Entry 511)*

**Why Humans See Only One World**

When our thinking and spirituality are confined to the material world, we cannot see the other 19 worlds. When we only recognize the vertical dimension of time and are unaware that time also has a horizontal dimension, we are limited to the flat coordinate plane with only 4 quadrants. When we look at the world through the Buddha's eye, we can see the imaginary axis — the axis passing through point O. 

We are now in the xy world — the human world. To us, the other 19 worlds appear illusory. But they are not truly illusory. The reason we perceive them as illusory is that our five senses cannot detect them, our spirit eye is not open, and our thinking is limited. If our spirit eye opens and our thinking reaches that level, we can "see" the other 19 worlds and know that what seems illusory is not — "emptiness is form."  
*(Chanyuan Corpus, Antimatter World Chapter, "The Spacetime Tunnel and 20 Parallel Worlds")*

## IV. Entering the Spacetime Tunnel

**Stillness as Entry**

Close your eyes. Become still. Completely clear your consciousness of the material world and enter a state of nirvana. You will immediately enter the space tunnel — not the time tunnel. Based on the state of your life's antimatter structure, you can reach another world in an instant. What you see is a scene from the antimatter world — a world of vastness and complexity trillions of times greater than Earth.  
*(Chanyuan Corpus, Antimatter World Chapter, Preface)*

**Meditation as Entry**

Since ancient times, meditation has been a way to enter the antimatter world. In the deepest stillness, by letting your thinking traverse the spacetime tunnel, your consciousness can roam freely — beholding the beauty of the heavenly realm and envisioning an unlimited future. This experience is not available to those whose minds dash outward in endless pursuit.  
*(Chanyuan Corpus, Wisdom Chapter, "The Scholar Need Not Leave Home")*

**Chanyuan Practitioners Are Already on the Path**

To travel from xy to xyz, one must make use of both the time tunnel and the space tunnel — primarily the space tunnel, because we are not returning to the past but moving into the future. From xy to xyz is a lateral movement, but the process takes a brief moment of time, so the time tunnel is also briefly involved. Primarily, though, one enters the space tunnel and travels through it to the Celestial Island Continent.

Perhaps you have not yet realized that the moment you became a Chanyuan practitioner, you already entered the space tunnel leading to the Celestial Island Continent. Though your physical body remains in the xy world, your spiritual body has been roaming the space tunnel in the company of the guide. We set out long ago.  
*(Chanyuan Corpus, Antimatter World Chapter, "The Spacetime Tunnel and 20 Parallel Worlds")*

**Prerequisites**

What is required? First, one must thoroughly understand the Chanyuan Corpus and the Xuefeng Corpus. One must actively participate in building Lifechanyuan. The core requirement is to fully comprehend the Eight Ways of Thinking and the Eight Mysteries of Life — both are indispensable.  
*(Chanyuan Corpus, Antimatter World Chapter, "The Spacetime Tunnel and 20 Parallel Worlds")*

**Breaking Through Spacetime Constraints**

To break through the constraints of spacetime, one must think beyond convention, think in reverse, and act contrary to expectation.  
*(New Era Humanity's 800 Concepts, 4th Ed., Entry 433)*

Antimatter is the opposite of matter — it does not share matter's properties. All material things undergo creation, growth, decay, and extinction; yet everything in the antimatter world can transform instantaneously. From the perspective of cosmic time, all material things are fleeting, insubstantial, without lasting value. To attain eternal existence, one must escape the control of time; to break free from time's shackles, one must cultivate the spiritual body. When the moment of ripeness arrives — like a melon falling from the vine or water finding its way — one can enter horizontal spacetime at any time, with a feeling of immeasurable joy and beauty.  
*(New Era Humanity's 800 Concepts, 4th Ed., Entry 435)*
