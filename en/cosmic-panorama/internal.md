# Cosmic Panorama (Internal Edition)

## I. The Nature and Origin of the Universe

**On the origin of the universe** (Concepts 402 / 416):

> The origin of the universe is not the Big Bang, but **No-Polarity (Wuji) giving birth to the Supreme Polarity (Taiji), and Chaos (Hundun) emerging from disorder (Huncun).**
> Before the universe was born, there was No-Polarity — no inside or outside, no large or small, no boundary, no distinction between the void and the real, no time, no space, no matter, no spirit; a field of pure clarity that was also a sea of undifferentiated chaos; the sum of all having, and the sum of all not-having.

**On the essence and constitution of the universe** (Concepts 98 / 402 / 403 / 412 / 418):

- The universe is **the product of the Greatest Creator's consciousness** (Concept 402)
- The **three elements** constituting the universe: **Consciousness · Structure · Energy** (Concepts 98 / 403)
- The universe contains **eight forces**: magnetic force, gravitational force, strong force, weak force, constructive force, repulsive force, intentional force, spiritual force (Concept 403)
- The total energy of the universe is zero; the sum of positive and negative energy is permanently zero — from which arises the **law of cause and effect** (Concepts 412 / 418)
- **The greater the energy, the more formless; the smaller the energy, the more form-bound**; the higher the vibrational frequency, the less visible (Concepts 97 / 370)
- Universal holography: **no exterior beyond the largest, no interior within the smallest**; the universe does not judge good from evil — it only reflects accurately (Concept 413)

**On the macrocosm and the Earth-cosmos** (Concept 415):

- The universe is divided into the macrocosm and the microcosm; the one humanity inhabits is called the **Earth-cosmos** (the microcosm) (Concept 415)
- The Earth-cosmos has a Taiji elliptical structure; it is called the **Faxuan System**
- Using space tunnels to traverse the entire Earth-cosmos would require approximately **1,860 years** (roughly 586.57 billion light-years)

**Structural hierarchy of the Earth-cosmos**:

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Faxuan System (Earth-cosmos)** | Taiji elliptical body, rotating around the Celestial Realm as its core |
