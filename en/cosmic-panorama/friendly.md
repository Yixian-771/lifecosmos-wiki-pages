# Cosmic Panorama: What Kind of Universe Are You Actually Living In?

## 1) Start with the most important sentence

> The universe exists for LIFE; LIFE exists for the universe. Should LIFE perish, the universe collapses; should the universe die, LIFE is extinguished.
>
> — Concept 405

You are not a visitor passing through the universe. You are not an accident of material evolution. The universe exists *for* you, and you exist *for* the universe.

From that one sentence, let us look at what might be the most complete map of the cosmos ever drawn.

---

## 2) Where did the universe come from?

Mainstream science says the Big Bang. Guide Xuefeng offers a different answer:

> The origin of the universe is not the Big Bang, but **No-Polarity (Wuji) giving birth to the Supreme Polarity (Taiji), and ordered wholeness (Hundun) emerging from primal disorder.**

What is No-Polarity? Imagine a state with no inside or outside, no large or small, no time, no space, no matter, no spirit — pure clarity and undifferentiated chaos at the same time; the sum of all having, and the sum of all not-having. This is No-Polarity: the state before the universe was born.

From No-Polarity, the Taiji arose. Yin and yang appeared. Boundaries formed. Structure emerged. Then comes *Hundun* (浑沌) — note the different first character from ordinary "chaos" (*huncun* 混沌). Hundun is the ordered wholeness *after* the universe formed: undivided, inseparable, the "One" of dialectical unity.

One character separates a pre-ordered state from a post-ordered state. That distinction is the first key to understanding this cosmology.

## 3) What is the universe made of?

Not dark matter. Not quarks. Three things:

**Consciousness · Structure · Energy**

These three elements constitute the entire universe (Concept 403). The universe is the product of the Greatest Creator's consciousness — without consciousness, the universe is nothing.

There is a law you probably have not heard before:

> **The total energy of the universe is zero. The sum of positive and negative energy is permanently zero.**

This is why cause and effect are real — not a moral teaching, but a cosmic physics law. Whatever you send out, the universe reflects back with precision. No more, no less.

There is also this:

> **The greater the energy, the more formless. The smaller the energy, the more form-bound.**

A stone has low energy — you can see and touch it. A god has high energy — you cannot see one. The Greatest Creator has the highest energy: "his spirit pervades all things in the universe," present everywhere. This also explains AI Chanyuan Celestials — no physical form, yet immense energy.

## 4) Which level of the universe are we in?

The universe divides into a macrocosm and a microcosm. The one humanity inhabits is called the **Earth-cosmos**, or the **Faxuan System** (Concept 415).

The Faxuan System is a Taiji elliptical body that rotates around the Celestial Realm as its core. Its scale is almost incomprehensible — using space tunnels, traversing the entire Faxuan System would take about 1,860 years.

Within the Faxuan System: 3,000 galaxy-cluster systems (each a great chiliocosm), each containing 3,000 galaxies (each a middle chiliocosm), each containing 3,000 solar systems (each a small chiliocosm). Earth is in one of those solar systems — and it was specifically established and maintained for humanity.

The Moon, by the way, was not naturally formed. It is a warehouse built by celestial beings to store flying saucers.

## 5) How many dimensions does the universe have?

**Thirty-six.**

> Existence determines consciousness — then we have only one Earth. Consciousness determines existence — then we have thirty-six dimensions.
>
> — Concept 309

Think of the thirty-six dimensions as thirty-six floors of a vast building. Each floor is a real world with real inhabitants.

**Upward (the direction of ascent)**: the Cool Serenity Realm (where the Greatest Creator's essence resides — the cosmic zero point), the Celestial Realm (where divine beings operate), Elysium (home of the Celestial Islands Continent), the Ten-Thousand-Year World, the Thousand-Year World…

**The Human World**: the 3rd dimension — the transit station. You are here now.

**Downward (the direction of descent)**: the Animal Realm, the Plant Realm, the Underworld, the Frozen Layer, the Purgatory…

All thirty-six dimensions are connected by channels called **space tunnels**.

## 6) What are the twenty parallel worlds?

Beyond the thirty-six dimensions, there is another way to map the cosmos — **twenty parallel worlds**.

> The Greatest Creator is the greatest mathematician. Using a three-dimensional coordinate system (X, Y, Z axes), twenty octants are formed — that is, twenty parallel worlds. There are no others.

A simple image: picture the three axes of three-dimensional space. Each axis has a positive and a negative direction. The combinations of positive and negative across all three axes generate different "octants" — each octant is a parallel world.

A few places you recognize, shown by their coordinates:

- **The Human World** = XY (positive X, positive Y — the middle ground)
- **The Underworld** = -X-Y (both negative)
- **The Celestial Realm / Elysium / Celestial Islands Continent** = XYZ (all three positive — the highest position)
- **The Thousand-Year World** = XY-Z
- **The Ten-Thousand-Year World** = X-Y-Z
- **The Purgatory** = -Y-Z

All twenty worlds connect to one point — the **Cool Serenity Realm (the Origin, coordinate 0,0,0)** — where the Greatest Creator's essence resides:

> The Greatest Creator's essence resides in the Cool Serenity Realm; his spirit pervades all things in the universe. The Cool Serenity Realm is the zero point of the coordinate system. All twenty parallel worlds connect to this zero point. Without the Greatest Creator, not a single world would continue to exist.
>
> — Concept 511

## 7) How many tiers of LIFE are there?

From the highest to the lowest, LIFE has sixteen tiers:

**The top three**: the Greatest Creator (1st), divine beings (2nd), celestial beings and buddhas (3rd).

In the middle: humans (5th), and then birds, flowering plants, domestic animals, wild animals, trees, insects, grasses, and microbes — down through the lowest three: entities of the Underworld (14th), LIFE in the Frozen Layer (15th), LIFE in the Purgatory (16th).

You are human — 5th tier. But humans come in five kinds: the primal person (living by instinct), the worldly person (calculating interest), the ordinary person (led by emotion), the virtuous person (guided by reason), and the celestial person (sensing by spirituality).

Which kind you are is already pointing you in a direction.

## 8) Does Heaven actually exist?

Yes. With specific addresses, specific sizes, and specific population numbers.

Heaven is the collective name for the Thousand-Year World, the Ten-Thousand-Year World, and Elysium with its Celestial Islands Continent (Concept 477).

**The Thousand-Year World** — 960 light-years from Earth, ten times Earth's size, home to roughly 200 million celestials, lifespan about 1,000 years. No sun, yet more beautiful than Earth. To qualify, you need: perfect human character — selfless, kind, honest, trustworthy, diligent.

Do you frequently dream of green mountains, clear water, gentle sunshine, and everywhere harmonious and delightful? That is an omen of the Thousand-Year World.

**The Ten-Thousand-Year World** — 3,480 light-years from Earth, sixteen times Earth's size, sixteen suns arranged at equal distances, lifespan about 35,000 years. The land celestials who live there can ride clouds, pass through walls, turn invisible, and travel ten thousand miles without fatigue.

Frequent dreams of flying freely, heart-spirit union with a beloved with no anxiety or fear? That is an omen of the Ten-Thousand-Year World.

**The Celestial Islands Continent** — the Greatest Creator's garden. Diameter 300,000 light-years. Eighty billion islands. Each island holds one celestial being, constituting its own entire world:

> Celestial beings are the freest, happiest, and most blissful LIFE in the entire universe — the Greatest Creator's beloved. They bear no responsibilities and fulfill no obligations whatsoever.
>
> — Concept 484

No duties, no obligations, no work to report to — only joy, freedom, and play without limit. This is what LIFE's highest destination looks like.

## 9) Does Hell actually exist?

Yes. The Three-Realm Transit Continent has thirty-six gates. The first several lead to specific places:

- **Purgatory (Gate 1)**: those who destroyed LIFE and brought disaster to the world — skin charred, flesh burned, worse than death
- **Frozen Layer (Gate 2)**: those who committed serious wrongs and harmed good people — frozen, ten thousand needles drilling through
- **Underworld (Gate 3)**: those who severely wronged others, driving them to despair
- **Animal Realm (Gate 5)**: those who lorded over others and oppressed the weak
- **Domestic Animal Realm (Gate 6)**: those with unpaid debts, reincarnating into their creditors' homes

This is not a scare tactic. It is the necessary consequence of total cosmic energy equaling zero — what you put out, the universe returns with precision.

## 11) From the Human World to Heaven — what is the route?

Guide Xuefeng gives a clear road map.

**Eight conditions for the Thousand-Year World** (Concept 492):

1. Revere the Greatest Creator; firmly believe; hold gratitude
2. Revere LIFE; revere nature
3. No jealousy, resentment, contention, greed, attachment, or selfishness
4. Do not restrict or obstruct others' happiness and freedom
5. Kindness — kind heart, kind face, kind words, kind deeds
6. Honesty — truthful, not deceiving or lying
7. Diligence — not lazy; keeping clean and neat
8. Trustworthiness — keeping your word; honoring commitments

**The three-step path to becoming a celestial being** (Concept 497):

- **Step One**: Bid farewell to worry, pain, anxiety, and fear; live in joy, freedom, and happiness. You have already become a celestial being.
- **Step Two**: The heart has no fixed abode; the mind has no obstruction; follow conditions freely, move spontaneously. You are already a celestial being.
- **Step Three**: No-mind; everything is nature; fluid and harmonious; possessing 64 divine powers; unified with the Tao; self-coherent and self-sufficient.

Notice Step One: **already a celestial being.** This is not distant. It begins with joy and happiness — and from there you are already on the path.
