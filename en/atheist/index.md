# Atheist

In the Lifechanyuan system, an "atheist" is not simply someone without religion — it is someone who holds one or more of eight specific beliefs that deny the existence of the Greatest Creator, the indestructibility of LIFE, and the law of cause and effect. Guide Xuefeng addressed this in his landmark series *Eight Counsels to Atheists*, systematically dismantling atheism's three foundational pillars and showing why atheism leaves one without moral roots, long-term vision, or a path to the Kingdom of Heaven.
