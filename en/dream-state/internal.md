## I. Overview and Definition

Dreams are one of the core concepts in the Lifechanyuan system for revealing the antimatter world, the destination of life, and the actual effects of cultivation and refinement. Dreams themselves are the antimatter world. They are the negative space entered by the LIFE's spirit body in the subconscious. They are an infinitely vast, timeless space of existence. Dreams reflect the dreamer's history, present condition, and mainly the future. Dreams verify the actual effects of cultivation and refinement: the more perfect the nonmaterial structure of life is, the more beautiful the dreams become. Places that cannot be reached in dreams cannot be reached after death. This is Lifechanyuan's most striking revelation concerning dreams.

That is to say, only experiences personally undergone can remain in the subconscious. If one has never experienced them, then there is no corresponding information in the subconscious.

**Core Definition Four** (*Chanyuan Corpus, Antimatter World Section, Entering the Antimatter World Through Dreams*, excerpt from the complete original text):

**Core Definition Five** (*Chanyuan Corpus, Cultivation and Refinement Section 06, “Cultivating Immortality” Part Ten: Entering Virtual Space*, excerpt from the complete original text):
