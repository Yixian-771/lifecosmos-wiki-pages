# Qing (Affection) · Internal Edition

---

## I. What Qing Is: The Core Definition

*Qing* is a form of antimatter energy embedded in LIFE's antimatter structure — energy that LIFE must pursue and satisfy in order to maintain the bonds of heaven, earth, immortals, and humanity. LIFE must have *qing*; without *qing*, LIFE perishes. The divine and the Buddha have *qing*; Immortals have *qing*; birds and beasts have *qing*; plants have *qing*; demons and ghosts have *qing* as well.

*(Chanyuan Corpus · The Thirty-Six Trigram Formations · Qing Formation: The First)*

*Qing* is the seasoning of LIFE, the harmony and expression of nature (*xìng*). All things contain *qing*; all living beings are beings with *qing*. *Qing* is the total expression of the Tao of nature.

*(Chanyuan Corpus · Heavenly Revelations · Qing, Love, Nature, and Color and the Tao of Nature)*

---

## II. The Structure of Qing: Consciousness, Structure, and Energy — Essentially Energy

Consciousness, structure, and energy are the three elements constituting the universe. Nature (*xìng*), love, and Tao are LIFE's three great treasures: nature is the characteristic of structure, love is the characteristic of energy, Tao is the characteristic of consciousness. So what is *qing*?

*Qing* encompasses all three characteristics — consciousness, structure, and energy. It has elements of consciousness, elements of structure, and above all elements of energy. That is, *qing* is a need of consciousness and a need of structure, but its essential nature is energy — an antimatter energy that consciousness and structure require in order to function.

Therefore *qing* is inseparably linked to nature, love, and Tao: wherever there is nature, there is *qing*; wherever there is love, there is *qing*; wherever there is Tao, there is *qing*. Conversely, without nature there is no *qing*; without love there is no *qing*; without Tao there is no *qing*.

*(Chanyuan Corpus · The Thirty-Six Trigram Formations · Qing Formation: The First)*

---

## III. Why Qing Exists: The Cosmic Web

*Qing* is the bond and bridge connecting living beings across all of nature. Wherever there is life, there is *qing*.

*(Xuefeng Corpus · Miscellaneous Essays · Ghost Qing, Human Qing, Immortal Qing)*

*Qing* forms layer upon layer of trigram formations: the divine have divine *qing*, demons have demonic *qing*, Immortals have immortal *qing*, humans have human *qing*, ghosts have ghost *qing*, beasts have beast *qing*, birds have bird *qing*, trees have tree *qing*, grass has grass *qing*, flowers even more so. This forms the levels and rungs of LIFE, constituting the cosmic web, binding each level of life to its own space so that all levels flourish and maintain order across countless ages.

*(Chanyuan Corpus · The Thirty-Six Trigram Formations · Qing Formation: The First)*

---

## IV. The Level of Qing: Qing Domain Determines LIFE's Level

Where *qing* is fullest and richest, there is greatest closeness to heaven; where *qing* is most sparse and impoverished, there is greatest closeness to the lower realms. The broader the *qing*, the closer to heaven; the narrower the *qing*, the closer to the lower realms.

All living beings have *qing*, and the content of one's *qing* determines one's level of LIFE. The narrower the *qing* domain, the lower the level; the broader the *qing* domain, the higher the level. If a person has *qing* for only one or a few people, their domain is narrow and their LIFE level is low. If a person has *qing* for all people, their domain is broad and their level is high. If a person has *qing* not only for people but for all living beings, for rivers and mountains, sun and moon and stars — that person is no longer an ordinary mortal; they have reached the level of a celestial being.

As a seasoning for life: too little *qing* and life has no flavor; too much *qing* and life becomes bitter.

*(Chanyuan Corpus · The Thirty-Six Trigram Formations · Qing Formation; Chanyuan Corpus · LIFE · Analysis of LIFE's Three Treasures: Qing, Love, and Nature)*

---

## V. Ghost Qing, Human Qing, Immortal Qing

**Ghost qing** is heart-rending affection — loyal unto death, absolutely one-to-one, tragic in outcome.

**Human qing** is lingering affection — filled with joy, anger, sorrow, fear, love, hatred, and desire; shifting as conditions shift; impossible to block and impossible to hold; debt-like in its entanglement; destined for reincarnation, endless encounter, endless repayment without end.

**Immortal qing** is affection that holds only joy, freedom, and happiness — free of debt, eternally flowing without break, fragrant and graceful across time and space, bringing beautiful longing and ease of body and mind without trouble or pain.

The hallmark of immortal *qing* is that it is neither one-to-one nor wandering and fickle, but at ease in all conditions, following affinity, following nature, following the moment: "When the wind comes through sparse bamboo, the wind passes and the bamboo holds no sound; when the wild goose crosses the cold pond, the goose passes and the pond holds no reflection."

To have ghost *qing* is to be denied human *qing*. To have human *qing* is to be denied immortal *qing*. Without immortal *qing*, LIFE cannot rise to the immortal realm.

*(Xuefeng Corpus · Miscellaneous Essays · Ghost Qing, Human Qing, Immortal Qing)*

---

## VI. Qing and Love: The Distinction

*Qing* is an expression of a person's psychological state. Humans have eight forms of *qing*: joy, anger, sorrow, pleasure, grief, worry, fear, and shock. What we commonly call *qing* refers mainly to familial affection, friendship, romantic love, and gratitude.

Love (*ài*) is an expression of a person's consciousness state. Love has only one form of expression: joy.

Psychological state and consciousness state are entirely different. A psychological state shifts with external conditions — you do me a favor and I feel *qing* toward you; you irritate me and I feel resentment toward you. A consciousness state is a mode of being that expresses LIFE's essential nature and does not change with external conditions — as with a parent's love for a child: no matter how the child changes, that innate love is difficult to alter.

*(Chanyuan Corpus · LIFE · The Difference Between the Qing Domain and the Love Domain)*

---

## VII. The Qing Formation: The Hardest Trigram Maze to Escape

Of the thirty-six trigram formations, the hardest to escape is the *qing* formation.

As long as there is someone we are attached to, someone we think about, someone whose relationship with us we wish to protect — we are inside the *qing* formation.

When the day comes that we carry no attachments in our hearts — that our hearts are without hindrance, without fixed abode — that *qing* fills the mountains and rivers, that *qing* is set toward the Celestial Islands, that we are at ease wherever we are, transformed by affinity, moved by nature, accomplished by timing — that is the sign that we have escaped the formation.

To rise to the immortal realm, one must escape human *qing*. Without escaping human *qing*, immortal *qing* cannot be entered. Without immortal *qing*, LIFE cannot ascend.

In a word: release human *qing*, take up immortal *qing*. Specifically: soften the bonds of parents and children, deepen the bond with the Greatest Creator and the Buddha; soften the bonds of siblings and friends, deepen the bond with all things in the cosmos; soften the one-to-one bond of romantic love, deepen the qing of the immortal realm that follows affinity freely; soften the bonds of teacher and student, deepen the bond with Tao and truth.

*(Chanyuan Corpus · The Thirty-Six Trigram Formations · Qing Formation: The First; Chanyuan Corpus · The Thirty-Six Trigram Formations · Escaping the Qing Formation)*

---

## VIII. The Supreme Tao Cannot Be Sought Through Qing

*Qing* is necessary — like blood in the body, one cannot live without it for a day. Where there is *qing* there is heaven; where there is none there is the lower realm. But to reach heaven, one must resonate at the same frequency as heaven and its beings — *qing* alone cannot get you there.

The Supreme Tao cannot be sought through *qing*. True affection is beautiful, but it cannot reach the Tao. No matter how sincere the *qing* — if someone drinks arsenic, they die; no matter how sincere the *qing* — marriages still end; no matter how sincere the *qing* — if LIFE's structure does not change, a person remains a person and can never become a Buddha or an Immortal.

The Supreme Tao is reached by perfecting LIFE's antimatter structure and resonating at the same frequency as one's destination — not by the sincerity of *qing* alone.

*(Chanyuan Corpus · Spreading the Way · The Supreme Tao Cannot Be Sought Through Qing)*

---

## IX. Qing Debt

*Qing* debt: receiving another's help, or physical, psychological, or spiritual solace, without returning equivalent compensation — this creates *qing* debt.

*Qing* debt divides into interpersonal debt and romantic debt. Interpersonal *qing* debt arises when someone has helped you and you have not returned the help in kind. Romantic *qing* debt arises when someone has given you physical, spiritual, or emotional solace that you have not returned.

*Qing* debt also includes the *qing* of unresolved affinity (*qíng yuán*): when one feels indebted or cannot let go because of deep mutual feeling — that too is *qing* debt.

As long as such debts remain unpaid, reincarnation is inevitable; there is no possibility of reaching the heavens.

*(Chanyuan Corpus · Spreading the Way · Qing Debt)*

## X. Emotional Dependence Obstructs Entry into Advanced Civilization

The primary factor obstructing humanity's entry into advanced civilization is emotional dependence between people. Within the family structure, every person is in a relationship of mutual emotional hostage-holding: parents hold children hostage, children hold parents hostage. The result: no autonomy, no independent consciousness, no personal freedom.

When every person is independent — when there is no emotional dependence between people, no marriage and family structure — each person is born free and independent, not relying on any other, and the result is that everyone lives with joy, freedom, and happiness.

The Thousand-Year World is exactly this state, and this is why all living beings there have become Immortals, and it has become heaven.

*(Chanyuan Corpus · Inverted Thinking · Emotional Dependence Obstructs Human Entry into Advanced Civilization)*
