# Roadmap of Human Life and LIFE (Friendly Version)

---

## Your Human Life Is a Journey — But Where Are You Going?

Have you ever stopped to wonder: where did you come from, and where are you headed?

Lifechanyuan offers a clear answer: **your human life is just a brief journey that your LIFE is taking through the human world.** Like a traveler passing through a city — the city matters, and you have real things to do there — but it is not your final destination.

Beyond this life, there is a far larger "LIFE" — one that flows from the cosmic source, passes through countless spaces and eons, and never truly ends. Your current lifetime is one chapter in that vast story.

---

## Two Purposes, Not One

The roadmap begins with a simple insight: your human life has **two purposes**, and both matter.

**Purpose 1: Live joyfully, happily, freely, and blissfully right now.**

This is not a bonus — it is your birthright. The Greatest Creator made you to be happy. Anything that blocks your joy deserves to be questioned.

**Purpose 2: Elevate your LIFE and eventually ascend to the Celestial Kingdoms.**

The human world is not the end of the road. The most beautiful future for LIFE lies in the Thousand-Year World, the Ten-Thousand-Year World, or Elysium.

Both purposes point in the same direction. Happiness now and growth toward Heaven are not in conflict — they reinforce each other.

---

## The Five Stations

### Station 1: Get clear on what morality actually means

Not the guilt-and-duty version. The Lifechanyuan version is refreshingly direct:

> Teachings, behaviors, and ways of living that allow people to be joyful, happy, free, and blissful are moral. Those that obstruct joy, happiness, freedom, and bliss are immoral.

Apply this standard honestly to your current life. You may find that some "obviously right" things are actually holding you back.

### Station 2: Leave the old structures behind — enter the Second Home

Marriage, family, and national identity create invisible cages. This sounds radical, but the roadmap offers practical guidance: for those already married, the path is to fulfill your duties lovingly — care for your parents, raise your children well, repay your debts — then enter the Second Home together with your partner.

The Second Home is a real community: a way of living where there are no national borders, no religious gatekeepers, no coercive marriage contracts — just a large family of people helping each other live well.

### Station 3: Repay your debts and dissolve your karmic bonds

You came to the human world for a reason. For most of us, that reason is **debt** — obligations from past lives that pull us here like gravity.

Until those debts are cleared, we cannot truly move on. Christ told us: even a single unpaid debt keeps us trapped.

The best method? **Formless giving** — giving without expecting anything back. Participating in the Lifechanyuan project, helping humanity step into a new era, is the most powerful way to dissolve old debts quickly.

### Station 4: Put treasure in Heaven

Where your treasure is, there your heart will be — and where your heart is, there your future will be.

The merit you accumulate through your practice and service is your treasure in Heaven. Building the Lifechanyuan Era for all of humanity? That is the greatest merit of all.

### Station 5: Transform your consciousness

This is the culmination of the journey:

> Reality is the projection of consciousness. When we possess the consciousness of a celestial in the Thousand-Year World, we can go there. When we possess the consciousness of a Buddha in Elysium, we can enter Elysium.

You do not have to wait until death. When your consciousness genuinely shifts — when your LIFE structure has truly been elevated — you naturally arrive where you belong.

---

## The Destination Is Real

The Thousand-Year World, the Ten-Thousand-Year World, Elysium — these are not invented comforts:

> They are necessities arising from the law that the sum of positive and negative energies equals zero — just as the solar system necessarily contains Mercury, Saturn, and Mars. They are inevitable.

---

## The Roadmap in One Line

**Know your purpose → realign your life morally → enter the Second Home → clear debts and dissolve bonds → accumulate merit → transform consciousness → ascend to your Celestial Kingdom.**
