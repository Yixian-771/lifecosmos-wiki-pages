# Roadmap of Human Life and LIFE

**The Roadmap of Human Life and LIFE is Lifechanyuan's complete guide for practitioners — from their current existence on Earth all the way to the Celestial Kingdoms.** It distinguishes two related but fundamentally different concepts: "human life" (the brief journey a LIFE takes through the human realm) and "LIFE" (the continuous thread stretching from the cosmic source through infinite time and space). The roadmap identifies two purposes — finding joy, happiness, freedom, and bliss in the present; and elevating one's LIFE to ascend to a higher realm (the Thousand-Year World, the Ten-Thousand-Year World, or Elysium). The core path: clarify morality → leave marriage, family, and nation → enter the Second Home → repay debts and dissolve worldly bonds → accumulate merit → transform consciousness → arrive in the corresponding Celestial Kingdom.
