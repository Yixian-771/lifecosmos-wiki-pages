# Good, Evil, and the 108 Evils & 108 Virtues

**Good and evil**, in the Lifechanyuan system, operate on two distinct levels: at the cosmic level of Hundun and the Tao, there is no good or evil — only symmetric balance; but for every living being, the distinction between good and evil absolutely exists and is the fundamental basis for whether LIFE can elevate and reach Heaven. Concept 200 of the *New Era Human 800 Concepts* (4th edition) presents the most complete and specific inventory of good and evil conduct in the Lifechanyuan system: **108 Evils** and **108 Virtues** — the practical reference standard for daily cultivation.

> Heaven belongs to the good; hell belongs to the evil; the human world belongs to those who are a mixture of both.
