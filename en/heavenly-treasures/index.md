# Heavenly Treasures

**Heavenly Treasures** in the Lifechanyuan system refers specifically to **soul energy** — one's faith in the Greatest Creator, divine beings, and the Kingdom of Heaven; one's conviction and longing for a beautiful future; and qualities such as love, goodness, truth-goodness-beauty-love, and artistic beauty. Heavenly Treasures is one of the four core tasks for Chanyuan Celestials and one of three indispensable conditions for entering the Kingdom of Heaven (the Thousand-year World, Ten-thousand-year World, and Elysium with its Celestial Islands Continent). Jesus Christ's teaching — *"Store up for yourselves treasures in heaven"* — is the foundational scriptural source of this concept.

> "Heavenly Treasures refer mainly to soul energy. Material energy and mental energy do not count as Heavenly Treasures."
> — Xuefeng, *Heavenly Treasures and How to Calculate Them*, 2009
