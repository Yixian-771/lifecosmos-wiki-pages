## I. Definition: Heavenly Treasures Are Soul Energy

Jesus Christ taught us that to enter the Kingdom of God, we must store up treasures in heaven.

So what treasures belong to heaven?

I have given everyone a master key that opens all mysteries: consciousness, structure, and energy.

Whether tangible or intangible, all treasures fall within the category of energy.

What is energy? Answer: The capacity that enables the formation of antimatter structures and sustains the operation of living and non-living matter is called energy.

Energy is divided into three types: material energy, mental energy, and soul energy.

Material energy includes light energy, electrical energy, wind energy, heat energy, proteins, food nutritional reactions, chemical reactions, and other kinetic energies, and so on.

Mental energy includes friendship, affection, kinship, knowledge, experience, skills, property, fame, status, power, physical appeal, and other potential energies, and so on.

Soul energy includes faith in the Greatest Creator, divine beings, Buddhas, immortals, and the Kingdom of Heaven; belief in and longing for a beautiful future; friendship, romantic love, sexual love, spiritual wisdom, truth-goodness-beauty-love, music, dance, poetry, and other forms of artistic beauty, and so on.

**Heavenly Treasures refer mainly to soul energy. Material energy and mental energy do not count as Heavenly Treasures.**

*(Xuefeng's Collection · Heart-Mind Chapter · Heavenly Treasures and How to Calculate Them, 2009/11/29)*

## II. Eight Ways to Accumulate Heavenly Treasures

How do we accumulate Heavenly Treasures?

1. Revering the Greatest Creator, revering LIFE, revering nature, and walking the Way of the Greatest Creator is storing up treasures in heaven.

2. Selflessly giving love is storing up treasures in heaven.

3. Dissolving grievances and enmity, bringing peace, serenity, and warmth to others and society, is storing up treasures in heaven.

4. Generating good thoughts and performing good deeds is storing up treasures in heaven.

5. Praising the Greatest Creator, praising Jesus Christ, praising the Buddha Shakyamuni, praising the immortal Laozi, praising the prophet Muhammad, praising the sages and wise men throughout human history, cherishing LIFE, cherishing living, cherishing others, and cherishing and protecting nature, is storing up treasures in heaven.

6. Giving people confidence in life, inspiring them to look forward to a beautiful future, and dispelling anxiety, worry, fear, and apprehension, is storing up treasures in heaven.

7. The beautiful expression of romantic and sexual love that brings not one iota of harm to others — spiritually, emotionally, or physically — is storing up treasures in heaven.

8. Giving full expression to one's artistic beauty is storing up treasures in heaven.

*(Xuefeng's Collection · Heart-Mind Chapter · Heavenly Treasures and How to Calculate Them, 2009/11/29)*

Paying and giving are the best ways to deposit treasures in the Kingdom of Heaven.

*(New Era Human 800 Concepts, 4th Edition · Concept 111)*

## III. Eight Behaviors That Deplete Heavenly Treasures

The following eight things deplete your treasures in heaven:

1. Desecrating the Greatest Creator or insulting divine beings, Buddhas, immortals, or sages.

2. Harming and abusing LIFE.

3. Damaging nature.

4. Harboring hatred, jealousy, complaint, or blame.

5. Engaging in conflict, quarreling, debating, fighting, competing, seizing, or plundering the energy of others.

6. Creating fear, panic, intimidation, or deception; causing others pain, anxiety, worry, trouble, or misfortune.

7. Threatening or controlling others physically, mentally, or spiritually.

8. Suppressing human nature.

*(Xuefeng's Collection · Heart-Mind Chapter · Heavenly Treasures and How to Calculate Them, 2009/11/29)*

## IV. How to Calculate Your Heavenly Treasures

Strictly speaking, Heavenly Treasures are difficult to quantify. We can calculate how much money we have in a bank, but it is hard to calculate exactly how much treasure we have stored in heaven. If a rough calculation is needed, one can use the above eight ways of accumulating Heavenly Treasures and subtract the eight ways of depleting them to get a sense of how much one has.

Another practical method: when visiting a friend or relative, we can sense from the depth of our relationship and what we have contributed to them how long they would welcome us to stay and how they would treat us. That duration and quality of treatment reflects the treasure stored in that relationship.

For Chanyuan Celestials, a further reference: how much treasure you have stored in the Lifechanyuan homeland gives you a sense of whether you qualify to enter the Second Home and the paradise — if enough, you may enter at any time; if not, continue accumulating. And if after entering you stop contributing and only draw down your reserves, the day those reserves run out is the day you should leave.

The accumulation and calculation of Heavenly Treasures, the qualifications to enter heaven, and how long one may remain there — all can be thought through by analogy with the above examples.

*(Xuefeng's Collection · Heart-Mind Chapter · Heavenly Treasures and How to Calculate Them, 2009/11/29)*

## V. Position in the Cultivation Path

**One of Three Necessary Conditions for Ascending to Higher Life Spaces**

For a soul to ascend to higher life spaces, three conditions must be met:
- First: having repaid all karmic debts accumulated over past lives
- Second: having accumulated Heavenly Treasures (love, goodness, service, virtue — how many beings have benefited from you)
- Third: having achieved the consciousness and thinking of an immortal

These three conditions cannot be met through reading, earning money, or worship — only through real-life practice and training.

*(Chanyuan Collection · Civilization Chapter · What I Learned from Guide Xuefeng)*

**One of the Four Core Tasks for Chanyuan Celestials in the Life Oasis**

Chanyuan Celestials residing in the Life Oasis have four major tasks: 1. Repay karmic debts; 2. Accumulate Heavenly Treasures; 3. Perfect the soul garden; 4. Replace human consciousness with the consciousness of an immortal.

*(Xuefeng's Collection · Heart-Mind Chapter · Persisting in the Ordinary to Become a True Person)*

**The Third Gate to Reaching Peak Energy**

Merit is the third gate to reaching an energy level of 1000. Without merit, one can never reach the Eight-No Realm or the summit of life. Therefore, give selflessly, always contribute more than you take, practice formless giving generously, and store up treasures in heaven — in time, water flows and the melon ripens of its own accord.

*(Chanyuan Collection · Propagation Chapter · How to Climb the Summit of Life and Living, 2016-3-1)*

## VI. The Second Home: The Best Place to Accumulate Heavenly Treasures

Jesus Christ taught us, "Store up for yourselves treasures in heaven" — where your treasure is, there your heart will be also; where your heart is, there your future will be.

Our future as Chanyuan Celestials lies in the Lifechanyuan era; our LIFE's future lies in the Thousand-year World, Ten-thousand-year World, and the Elysium Celestial Islands Continent. The Second Home is the transit station from humanity to immortality, from the human world to the Kingdom of Heaven. To accumulate merit in the Second Home is one effective path to storing up treasures in heaven.

*(Xuefeng's Other Writings · 2009 · Accumulating Merit in the Homeland)*

The Lifechanyuan homeland is not only the place where we enjoy freedom, joy, happiness, and contentment, but also the base and transit station for perfecting our soul garden and achieving the qualities of immortals and Buddhas — and the LIFE stage where we repay karmic debts and accumulate Heavenly Treasures.

*(Chanyuan Collection · Civilization Chapter · How New Members Can Find Their Place in Lifechanyuan)*

Whatever any Chanyuan Celestial contributes to the Second Home, no matter how small, will not be wasted. Whatever we give, we shall receive in return — in riches not only material but spiritual and of the soul. In ordinary society, our efforts may go unrecognized. But in Lifechanyuan, as long as we are devoted and do not give up, everything we contribute is like storing treasures in heaven: no thief can steal it, no bird can carry it away, no pest can destroy it.

## VII. Qualification Standards for Entering Different Levels of Heaven

Having repaid all debts, accumulated sufficient merit, and achieved excellent life quality, one can proceed to paradise in the next life. If you have removed from your consciousness the concepts of family, ethnicity, nation, party, and religion, and love freedom and happiness, you will go to the Thousand-year World. If in your dreams you often fly freely, and you are in a state of continuous joy without physical contact with the opposite sex, you will go to the Ten-thousand-year World. If you have attained the consciousness of a Buddha, you will enter the corresponding Elysian realm. If you revere the Greatest Creator and LIFE, walk the Way of the Greatest Creator, and possess divine abilities — the ability to conjure a world of your own imagining — you will undoubtedly spend your next life in the Celestial Islands Continent of the Elysium World.

*(New Era Human 800 Concepts, 4th Edition · Concept 496)*
