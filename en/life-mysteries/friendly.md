# The Mysteries of LIFE Are Right Here — Have You Discovered Them?

> "As long as you sense quietly, you will surely discover the mysteries of LIFE within Lifechanyuan. Once discovered, you will step onto the great road toward higher LIFE spaces."
>
> — Xuefeng, *Grass and Milk*

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence in this world.

---

## What Are the Mysteries of LIFE?

The "Mysteries of LIFE" are the deep truths about LIFE that Xuefeng repeatedly reveals throughout the Lifechanyuan Corpus — not religious doctrine, not philosophical inference, but Xuefeng's direct vision of cosmic reality: where LIFE comes from, what it is, how it works, and where it goes.

> "LIFE's mysteries, for 9,999 out of every 10,000 people, are mysterious, unthinkable, and impossible to decipher — but for me, they are as clear as looking at fish in an aquarium. Simple. Very simple."

---

## The Main Mysteries of LIFE Xuefeng Has Revealed

### 🔑 The Nature of LIFE: Antimatter Structure, Not the Physical Body

> "LIFE is a spiritual antimatter structure." (Concept 357)

Your physical body is not you — it is your vehicle. The real you is that invisible, intangible soul structure — it never disappears.

### 🔑 Consciousness Is LIFE

> "Consciousness determines the nature and level of LIFE." (*Brain, Consciousness, and the Great Thousand World*)

Your state of consciousness is your state of LIFE. As consciousness elevates, LIFE elevates.

### 🔑 The Future Is Created by Consciousness

> "One's future living environment is composed of the elements in one's own consciousness. Imagination creates the future."

Whatever you imagine, that is what you move toward. What is not in consciousness does not exist in LIFE.

### 🔑 LIFE Does Not Die

> "Death is not the terminus of LIFE." (Concept 308)

The physical body is only the vehicle; the soul is indestructible. What we call "death" is simply LIFE continuing its existence in a different space.

### 🔑 Reincarnation Is Instantaneous — No Judgment, Direct to Destination

> "Reincarnation and transformation happen instantaneously, with no process in between, no judgment by the King of Hell — it goes directly to the destination." (*80 New Concepts*, No. 75)

Not the slow, lengthy reincarnation of Buddhism — it is immediate and direct. Whatever quality of LIFE you are, that is the space you go to.

### 🔑 LIFE Has Universal Gravitation

> "In the universe, all LIFE beings with karmic connection have mutual attraction; the magnitude of this attraction is directly proportional to the amount of mutual debt, and inversely proportional to the amount of mutual benefit." (Concept 362)

Who you meet is never coincidence. Debt and kindness are the gravity of the universe.

---

## How Do You Discover the Mysteries of LIFE?

No merit exchange is needed (that's the Eight Mysteries of LIFE series) — the broad-sense Mysteries of LIFE are scattered throughout the entire Corpus. Sense quietly, and you will find them.

> "'Whatever way of thinking one has, one has that corresponding LIFE form' — this mystery of LIFE is not abstruse. Once its meaning is understood, a person's LIFE structure will undergo a qualitative leap."
