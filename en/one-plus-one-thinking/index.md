# 1+1 Thinking

> 1+1=2 is an increase in quantity; 1+1=1 is an increase in energy. The sage embraces the One and becomes the model for all under heaven.
>
> — *Eight Hundred Concepts for the New Era of Humanity*, Concept 311

> 1+(−1)=1 is a key to knowing the mysteries of the universe.
>
> — *Eight Hundred Concepts*, Concept 312

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence in this world.

**1+1 Thinking** is the most precise and operational tool in Lifechanyuan's *Chapter on Anti-Conventional Thinking*. Through a comprehensive deconstruction of the most fundamental mathematical equation "1+1," it reveals the deep laws governing the universe: the result of any calculation is not fixed — it is determined by the **nature (attribute)** of what is being added. The universe is not the linear 1+1=2 world; it is a Hundun (primordial chaos) world, holographic, governed by the unified polarity of yin and yang.

From 1+1=2 (material thinking) → 1+1=1 (energy thinking / union with the Tao) → 1+1=0 (returning to zero / Non-Form realm) → 1+(−1)=Universe (unity of opposites / holographic cognition) → 10−9="Tao" (the path of subtraction) → 1+0=infinity (merging with the anti-universe): 1+1 Thinking uses the simplest mathematical symbols to map a complete route of consciousness elevation.
