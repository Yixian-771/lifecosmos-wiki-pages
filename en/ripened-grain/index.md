# The Ripened Grain

**The Ripened Grain** — in the Lifechanyuan system, a person whose soul is perfected and ready for harvest. Guide Xuefeng, fulfilling the angelic role prophesied by Jesus ("the harvesters are angels"), gathers these spiritually matured souls and guides them to the celestial realms of the Thousand-Year World, Ten-Thousand-Year World, or the Elysium Celestial Islands of the Extreme Bliss Realm.

> The so-called "ripened grain" refers to people with perfect souls.
