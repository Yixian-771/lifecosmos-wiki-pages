## [II] The Eight Hallmarks

**First version (2010)**

"The ripened grain I am to harvest has the following hallmarks:

1. Revering the Greatest Creator, revering LIFE, revering nature, and walking the Way of the Greatest Creator.

2. Not being in a marriage, family, nation, political party, or religion.

3. Having no status, no property, no reputation, no power.

4. Having no envy, complaint, or comparing mind; no selfish, greedy, or fighting mind; no lazy, arrogant, or resentful mind; no anxious, worried, or fearful mind.

5. Not clinging to personal views; being flexible and fluid; moving in harmony with one's nature; following only the Way.

6. Being humble, kind, diligent, honest, trustworthy, simple, healthy, and civilized.

7. Knowing how to play, how to have fun; enjoying collective activities and communal life; being cheerful and lively; usually in a state of joy and happiness.

8. Pursuing the life of the celestial rather than the human; embracing celestial ethics rather than human ethics; walking the celestial path rather than the human path; longing for the celestial realm rather than the human world.

Those who do not possess all eight hallmarks are not 'ripened grain.' Without being ripened, one simply cannot be gathered into the Greatest Creator's granary (Kingdom) and cannot enter the Celestial Kingdom."

(Source: Xuefeng Corpus · Words of Warning · The Hallmarks of the 'Ripened Grain', 2010/9/1)

---

**Refined version (2011)**

"1. The ripened grain unquestionably believe in the Greatest Creator with their whole heart and soul. If anyone has doubts about the Greatest Creator, you are not the person I am looking for.

2. The ripened grain long for the heavenly life without marriage and family. If you love marriage and family, you are not the person I am looking for.

3. The ripened grain love the state of owning nothing yet possessing everything. If you like owning things, you are not the person I am looking for.

4. The ripened grain have no 'heart' — only nature. They must move in harmony with their nature, not guided by the thoughts and desires of the heart. If you have a heart — especially one full of jealousy, comparison, resentment, selfishness, greed, complaint, competitiveness, or fear — you are not the person I am looking for.

5. The ripened grain follow only the Way, have no personal views, do not rely on their own wisdom, are one with the Way, and are flexible and fluid. If you insist you are right, you are not the grain I am looking for.

6. The ripened grain are humble, kind, diligent, honest, trustworthy, simple, healthy, and civilized. If you lack even one of these qualities, you are not the person I am looking for.

7. The ripened grain are positive, optimistic, cheerful, lively — they love playing, laughing, joking, and teasing. If you are pessimistic, or deeply serious, or too dignified to laugh and play, you are not the person I am looking for.

8. The ripened grain have only celestial ethics, not human ethics. If your mind is full of human consciousness, you are not the person I am looking for."

(Source: Xuefeng Corpus · Words of Warning · Once More: I Am Here to Harvest the Ripened Grain, 2011/9/1)

Any Chanyuan Celestial, upon reading the Chanyuan Corpus and Xuefeng Corpus, should immediately resonate and align — upon learning of the Second Home scenario, they should instantly feel 'this is the home I have always been searching for,' and need not ask or worry about anything else. As far as the Guide is concerned, whether there are more or fewer Chanyuan Celestials is not my concern — I only care about how many ripened grain can be harvested."
