# Miracles

In the Lifechanyuan system, a miracle is "something that was thought impossible actually happening, or the most beautiful wish coming true." Miracles are not mysterious, random gifts of fate — they are the automatic operation of cosmic programs when the right conditions are met. Through focus, persistence, and heart purification, anyone can activate these programs and allow miracles to arrive naturally and silently.

> "Those who pursue miracles will never obtain them; those who hold fast to ordinariness will ultimately have miracles descend."
>
> *(Chanyuan Corpus · The Way of Transmission · Do Not Pray for Miracles)*
