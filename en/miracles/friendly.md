# Miracles [Friendly Version]

> This version is for first-time readers, explaining what "miracles" mean in the Lifechanyuan system and how to cultivate them.

---

## What Is a Miracle?

Have you ever wished for something that felt completely impossible — and then, somehow, it happened? Or perhaps your deepest hope was suddenly, unexpectedly fulfilled?

Xuefeng offers a clear and simple definition:

> "Something thought impossible actually happening — that is a miracle. Examples include all kinds of divine abilities, recovery from terminal illness, gender transformation, rejuvenation, turning stone into gold, traversing wormholes… The realization of one's most beautiful wish is also a miracle."
>
> *(Chanyuan Corpus · The Way of Transmission · Focus and Persistence Yield Miracles)*

But Lifechanyuan tells us: miracles are not random gifts from heaven, not luck. They are the automatic operation of cosmic programs when the right conditions are in place — like a chick hatching from an egg. Twenty-one days of warmth and patience, and the chick breaks free. That is a miracle. That is the program completing itself.

---

## Why Do We Rarely Receive Miracles?

It is not that miracles don't exist. It is that our "effort" hasn't reached far enough yet.

> "We long for miracles to occur, yet in reality they appear rarely… not because there are no miracles, but because our effort is insufficient."
>
> *(Chanyuan Corpus · The Way of Transmission · Focus and Persistence Yield Miracles)*

The two most common obstacles are:

- **Scattered goals**: Wanting one thing today, another tomorrow — energy disperses and concentrates nowhere.
- **Lack of persistence**: Tasting the surface, never going deep enough to break through.

The solution is four words: **focus and persistence**.

---

## How to Obtain a Miracle

**Step One: Fix a single goal**

Don't chase multiple things at once. Choose the one that matters most, then give it everything.

**Step Two: Repeat every day, without stopping**

Same time, same place, same posture, same focus — every single day, until the miracle arrives. Water wears through stone not by force but by constancy.

**Step Three: Purify your heart**

The calmer, more spacious, and more clear your inner world, the more easily miracles come. Reaching the "Eight Non-Being States" — no clinging, no ego, no fear, no attachment — makes miracles vastly more accessible.

---

## The Most Important Reminder: Don't Pray for Miracles

This is one of the most distinctive teachings in the Lifechanyuan system:

> "Those who pursue miracles will never obtain them; those who hold fast to ordinariness will ultimately have miracles descend."
>
> *(Chanyuan Corpus · The Way of Transmission · Do Not Pray for Miracles)*

Why not pray for miracles?

- **If you pray and a miracle comes**: You will forever dismiss process and program, becoming reliant on chance — and slide toward going off the rails spiritually.
- **If you pray and no miracle comes**: You will lose faith, blame heaven, and your inner foundation will crumble.

The right attitude: do each step well, and let miracles arrive in their own silent, natural time.

> **One exception**: In a moment of extreme, immediate danger — when your consciousness is still clear — simply say inwardly: *"Greatest Creator, save me!"* In most cases, a miracle will occur. This is "training troops for a thousand days; deploying them for one moment."

## Your Body Already Contains the Seeds of Miracles

Lifechanyuan reveals that the human body itself has eight built-in "miracle programs" — like switches waiting to be activated by the right conditions:

- **Spontaneous Combustion** — When cultivation reaches its peak and consciousness leaves the body, the physical structure dissolves in a luminous dispersion. Ancient traditions called this "rainbow dissolution."
- **Rescue** — In mortal danger, calling on divine help brings instant safety.
- **Toxin Release** — When rage reaches its limit, the body releases toxins. A warning: unchecked anger poisons the self.
- **Elixir Dispensing** — When the heart is empty, selfless, joyful, unimpeded — the body releases a life-giving substance that can heal terminal illness.
- **Divine Abilities** — Sustained focus on a single thing, without a single disturbance, opens the door to acting freely in the world.
- **Madness** — Emotional tangles with no outlet lead to the loss of sanity. A warning.
- **Possession** — Unchecked ego-desires lead to being controlled by outside forces. A warning.
- **Supreme Bliss** — When the inner world is flawless and one becomes pure love radiating outward — the state of Supreme Bliss unfolds naturally.

The first five are cultivation goals. The last three are cautions: imbalance in the heart triggers programs too, just in the opposite direction.

## Start Small

Don't begin by aiming for the greatest miracle. Xuefeng suggests starting with things within reach:

- Letting a minor physical ailment disappear
- Noticing your complexion gradually improve
- Having someone help you in an unexpected way
- Turning grey hair back to black
- Accurately sensing tomorrow's weather

Each small miracle builds confidence. One key leads to the next.

> "Wherever consciousness reaches, power reaches. When consciousness creates a structure from nothing, corresponding energy gathers — and where energy gathers, miracles are born."
>
> *(Guide's Other Articles · 2015 · Please Focus on Small Miracles)*

---

## The Miracle Already Waiting in Ordinary Life

Here is perhaps the most grounding teaching of all:

> "Planting trees is an utterly ordinary task. But if a person does it season after season, year after year? 380,000 acres of desert became forest. That is the extraordinary miracle Zhang Yinglong created in the Mu Us Desert."
>
> *(Guide's Other Articles · 2023 · The Extraordinary Comes from the Ordinary)*

Miracles don't live in the spectacular. They live in every ordinary day of repetition, patience, and commitment.
