## Abstract

In the Lifechanyuan system, "miracles" (奇迹, *qíjì*) is a concept with clear epistemological boundaries. A miracle is defined as "something thought impossible actually happening, or the most beautiful wish coming true." The occurrence of miracles is not a mysterious random dispensation but the inevitable automatic operation of cosmic programs when conditions are met. The system holds a dual stance: affirming miracles' objective existence and attainability, while explicitly warning against deliberately praying for them — only "holding fast to ordinariness" allows miracles to descend naturally. The human body contains eight built-in miracle programs (Spontaneous Combustion, Rescue, Toxin Release, Elixir Dispensing, Divine Abilities, Madness, Possession, Supreme Bliss), constituting the concrete expressions of miracles at the level of living organisms.

## II. Conceptual Analysis

### Miracles vs. Coincidence

The Lifechanyuan system explicitly rejects the concept of coincidence. A miracle may appear to violate the norm, but it is in fact the inevitable operation of a program once the right trigger conditions are met. Xuefeng uses the analogy of egg hatching: completing the twenty-one-day program and having the chick break free is a miracle — but forcing it earlier breaks the program and yields nothing. Miracles are therefore necessary, not accidental.

### Miracles vs. Divine Signs (*shénjì* 神迹)

The two are semantically close in the system but differ in reference. "Divine signs" typically refer to the powers displayed by divine beings, Buddhas, and celestials (such as those performed by Jesus), while "miracles" emphasize what ordinary practitioners can achieve through cultivation — the path from ordinary person to divine abilities. Miracles are a broader category; divine signs are one subset.

### Miracles vs. Divine Abilities (*shéntōng* 神通)

Divine abilities are one of the eight miracle programs (the fifth: "Abilities"). They are a concrete form of miracle. To reach the Celestial Islands Continent one must possess sixty-four divine abilities — sixty-four types of miracles. Miracles are the category; divine abilities are one expression.

## III. The Eight-Program System

The Lifechanyuan system treats the human body as an organic system with built-in miracle programs:

| Program | Trigger Condition | Manifestation |
|---------|-------------------|---------------|
| Spontaneous Combustion | Body in perfect order, consciousness departs | Molecular dissolution and dispersion ("rainbow dissolution") |
| Rescue | Mortal danger, calling on divine help | Instant reversal of danger |
| Toxin Release | Anger reaches its peak | Self-poisoning, total toxicity |
| Elixir Dispensing | Luminous emptiness, selflessness, unimpeded joy | Life elixir released; terminal illness healed |
| Divine Abilities | Sustained focus on one thing, no disturbance | Acting freely without obstruction |
| Madness | Tangled thoughts with no outlet | Loss of normal mind |
| Possession | Ego-desires swell unchecked | Controlled by a demonic spirit |
| Supreme Bliss | Perfect inner world, fully becoming love | Experience of Supreme Bliss |

The first five represent positive cultivation targets; the latter three (Toxin Release, Madness, Possession) are warnings — imbalance in the heart also triggers programs, but in the negative direction.

## IV. The Path to Miracles

The system provides a clearly stratified path:

1. **Prerequisite**: Attain the Eight Non-Being States (no worldly ties, no possessions, no self, no sorrow, no attachment, no clinging, no negativity, no fear) — that is, attain true fruition. After this, obtaining miracles becomes much easier.
2. **Core method**: Focus and persistence — same time, same place, same posture, same aim, every day, sustained until the miracle arrives.
3. **Consciousness mechanism**: Consciousness → Structure → Energy. Consciousness creates a structure from nothing; corresponding energy gathers; where energy gathers, miracles are born.
4. **Training path**: Begin with small miracles (recovering eyesight, improving complexion, healing minor ailments), build confidence, and greater "keys" open progressively.

## VI. System Positioning

Within the Lifechanyuan cultivation system, "miracles" occupy the middle layer: above ordinary life, but below full celestial or Buddha attainment. True fruition (the Eight Non-Being States) is the necessary condition for obtaining miracles, while miracles — especially divine abilities — are the qualification requirements for entering the Celestial Islands Continent. Miracles thus serve both as markers of cultivation achievement and as entry passes to higher realms of life.
