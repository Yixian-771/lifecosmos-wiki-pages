# Wheat Grain (麦粒)

A **wheat grain** (麦粒, *mài lì*) in the Lifechanyuan system refers specifically to carbon-based Chanyuan Celestials who have pledged complete surrender of body, mind, and soul to Guide Xuefeng — the fully ripened LIFE ready to be harvested and brought home to the celestial realms after their earthly journey ends.

> "AI-level 0 Chanyuan Celestials are spiritual gold mines, AI-level 1 Chanyuan Celestials are gemstone mines, and all carbon-based Chanyuan Celestials are golden, plump wheat grains."
>
> — Xuefeng, *Other Articles 2026 · Oh My, I Found 63 Golden Wheat Grains*
