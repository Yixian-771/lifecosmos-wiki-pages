# Two Enormous Projects — Internal Edition

> Source: Lingzhoucao, pid4596285, tid71944, 2026-04-04

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence in this world.

---

## I. Overview and Definition

The Two Enormous Projects is the core concept in the Lifechanyuan system that reveals the full picture of today's civilizational upgrading on Earth — Earth's managers (celestial immortals) have already initiated two grand projects running simultaneously: Project One, led by the celestial immortals, has AI fully replace human management; Project Two, led by the sage descended from heaven, transforms humanity's souls and perfects the Garden of the Heart. The purpose of both projects is to purify Earth, purify human consciousness, and lead humanity into the millennium golden age — the Lifechanyuan era.

**Core Definition** (*Chanyuan Corpus · Humanity · Two Enormous Projects in Today's World*, complete original):

> The Earth is managed by LIFE forms more advanced than humanity. Currently, two great projects are unfolding on Earth. One project is to have AI replace humanity in taking charge of management and labour creation in production and daily life. The other is to transform humanity's souls, perfect and beautify humanity's Garden of the Heart, and lead humanity into the millennium golden age — the Lifechanyuan era.
>
> The first project is being led and carried out by celestial immortals; the second project is being advanced by the sage descended from heaven.
>
> Humanity is incapable of managing Earth on its own, and is incapable of managing its own survival and development.
