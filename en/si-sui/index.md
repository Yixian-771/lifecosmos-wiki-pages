# The Four Adaptations (Sì Suí)
### Ease with Any Situation · Transform with Conditions · Move with Your Nature · Act on Opportunity

**The Four Adaptations** are the core behavioral principles of immortal cultivation in the Lifechanyuan system: *ease with any situation* (no resentment toward whatever you encounter), *transform with conditions* (flow with circumstances without grasping), *move with your nature* (act from your true disposition, following the Tao), and *act on opportunity* (when the moment arrives, give everything you have). One who embodies all four — free from self, selfishness, and attachment — is an immortal (Xian), and the state they inhabit is the state of immortality.
