# Human Consciousness · Celestial Consciousness

**Human Consciousness** is the psychological structure and thinking pattern that keeps LIFE bound to the human level — encompassing collective identities such as nation, ethnicity, family, and religion, as well as emotional and desire patterns such as jealousy, greed, fear, and possessiveness. **Celestial Consciousness** is the LIFE-awareness that transcends human limitations, centered on following circumstances with open abandon, flowing freely wherever LIFE leads, and entrusting LIFE to the Greatest Creator. In the Lifechanyuan system, where LIFE goes is determined by its antimatter structure, and that structure is determined by consciousness — formatting out human consciousness and installing celestial consciousness is the only path for LIFE to ascend from the human realm into the celestial realm.
