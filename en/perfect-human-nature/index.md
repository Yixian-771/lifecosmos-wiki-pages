# Standards of Perfect Human Nature

Perfect human nature is the sole "passport" to the higher life realms — the prerequisite for an individual to ascend to the Millennium Realm and beyond, and the foundation for humanity to build a just, peaceful, and harmonious civilization. Guide Xuefeng defined eight specific standards: from reverence for the Greatest Creator to love of LIFE and labor, these form the ultimate goal of the Lifechanyuan cultivation system.
