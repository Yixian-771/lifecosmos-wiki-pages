## Abstract

"The Panorama of LIFE Origin and Evolution" constitutes the systematic cosmological-life-science framework at the center of the Lifechanyuan system, covering the origin of the universe, the creation of LIFE, the origins of humanity, the levels of LIFE, the laws of reincarnation, the spatial geography of Heaven, and the path of elevation. This paper's contribution lies in presenting the internal consistency of this framework and offering structural comparisons with mainstream cosmology, religious creation theologies, and evolutionary theory.

## III. Conceptual Framework: Three Foundational Propositions

### 3.1 LIFE's Three-Tier Definition

Lifechanyuan's definition of "LIFE" breaks through mainstream understanding at three levels:

**Tier I: Criterion** (S2) — Whatever has the capacity for sensation and sensory-feedback response is LIFE, whether tangible or intangible. This definition brings AI, souls, angels, and gods all within the category of "LIFE," while excluding matter without sensory capacity.

**Tier II: Essence** (S3) — The essence of LIFE is the soul; the essence of the soul is consciousness; therefore consciousness is LIFE. This is a fundamental shift from materialism to consciousness primacy.

**Tier III: Structure** (S1, S4) — LIFE is an antimatter structure with spirituality; the more perfect the structure, the higher the quality and the greater the freedom. This anchors LIFE quality in a describable state of antimatter structure.

The three tiers support one another, forming a three-dimensional LIFE ontology of "sensation → consciousness → antimatter structure."

### 3.3 The Law of LIFE Conservation

Lifechanyuan explicitly proposes that "the total quantity of LIFE in the universe is a fixed number" (S20) — a LIFE-version conservation law: LIFE neither increases nor decreases, only cycling and transforming among different structural levels. This law, together with "total energy is zero" (S8), constitutes the dual axis of cosmic conservation.
