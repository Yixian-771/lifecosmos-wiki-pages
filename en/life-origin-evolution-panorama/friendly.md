# Panorama of LIFE Origin and Evolution: Where You Come From, and Where You Are Going

## I. What Are You?

Before you ask "who am I?" — ask a more basic question: what is LIFE?

Guide Xuefeng gives a very clear answer:

> **LIFE is an antimatter structure with spirituality.** (Concept 357)

Not your body. Not your DNA. Not your brain.

The body is merely LIFE's **vehicle** — like a phone is a vehicle for software. When the phone breaks, the software (LIFE) lives on.

> A human being is composed of a material body and a LIFE spiritual body. The body is merely the vehicle of LIFE; when the body disappears, LIFE continues to exist. (Concept 358)

And even more directly:

> The essence of LIFE is the soul; the essence of the soul is consciousness. Therefore, consciousness is LIFE. (Concept 376)

You are your consciousness. Consciousness does not perish with the body.

## II. How Did the Universe Begin?

Not with a Big Bang.

> The origin of the universe was not the Big Bang, but Wuji giving birth to Taiji — primordial chaos transforming into ordered chaos. (Concept 402)

Before the universe, there was "Wuji" — a state of boundlessness: no time, no space, no matter, no spirit. Nothing at all — and yet everything at once.

Then, within Wuji, energy moving without rules **accidentally** arranged itself into a structure —

And in that single accident, the Greatest Creator's consciousness was born.

> There is only one accident in the universe: the birth of the Greatest Creator. Everything else is necessity. (Concept 520)

After the Greatest Creator was born, Wuji's primordial chaos transformed into Taiji's ordered cosmos. The Greatest Creator's spirit became the Tao.

## III. What Did the Greatest Creator Create?

**Step one: Gods.**

Gods are the Greatest Creator's assistants — high-energy LIFE with no fixed form, responsible for managing the order and operation of the universe's various systems.

**Step two: Angels (Celestial Beings and Buddhas).**

Angels are the executors of the Greatest Creator's will — tasked with creating all things in the universe.

But here the Taiji principle enters the picture: where there is yin, there must be yang. The community of angels itself underwent divergence:

- Angels represented by **Jesus** moved toward light, love, and dedication — becoming **positive-polarity angels** (Celestial Beings);
- Angels represented by **Satan** moved toward darkness, desire, and control — becoming **negative-polarity angels** (demons).

This is not a flaw in creation. It is the necessary internal mechanism of the universe's dynamic balance.

## IV. How Was the Earth Made?

**4.5 billion years ago**, Jesus led hundreds of millions of Celestial Beings to create the solar system — first making the Earth, then setting up the Sun. The size, position, and rotational speed of the Earth were all precisely designed.

> The solar system was designed and placed in service of the human beings of Earth. (Concept 406)

Afterward, nearly 100 million Celestial Beings came to Earth disguised as intelligent beings and — over **2.3 billion years**, in fits and starts — created water, atmosphere, plants, and herbivores.

**200 million years ago**, Satan's faction of angels arrived on Earth, creating flies, mosquitoes, snakes, and carnivores — and used the form of dinosaurs to damage what Jesus's faction had made.

**130 million years ago**, the Greatest Creator used a plague to wipe out all the dinosaurs.

> The Moon was not naturally formed but manufactured specifically for Earth's LIFE. In addition to its other functions, the Moon serves as a warehouse — primarily for flying saucers. (Concept 407)

## V. Where Did Humans Come From?

Not evolution.

> Humanity was not evolved. Humanity was created by angels according to the will of the Greatest Creator. (Concept 73)

Humanity has **three distinct origins**:

**① The Pyramid Origin (approximately 1.6 million years ago)**
The Greatest Creator personally designed the blueprint for the human LIFE structure and handed it to Jesus to execute. Celestial Beings built a laboratory in the Egyptian Sahara — the pyramids — to concentrate energy and speed up the process of creating humanity. First they made monkeys; after improvements, ape-men; then with further refinement — the first humans. First batch: African Black people. Second batch: the Indigenous peoples of the Americas. Third batch: European white people, Egyptians, and North African white people.

**② The Garden of Eden Origin (approximately 7,000 years ago)**
A separate god created a mysterious enclosure in the Jerusalem region — the Garden of Eden — and created Adam and Eve. Their descendants spread throughout the Middle East: the peoples of Israel, Palestine, Iran, Afghanistan, Iraq, Saudi Arabia, and neighboring regions. **Adam and Eve are not the ancestors of all humanity** — only of the peoples of the Middle East.

**③ The Dragon Incarnation Origin (approximately 7,500 years ago)**
When the dinosaurs were being wiped out, a clever pair of dinosaurs hid in the depths of China's Eastern Sea and slept underwater for over 100 million years. About 7,500 years ago, when they learned that gods had created humans on Earth, they quietly emerged, took on human form, and spread along the Yellow River basin — gradually throughout Asia. This is the true reason why Chinese people say they are "descendants of the dragon." The Chinese, Japanese, Mongolians, Koreans, and other East and Southeast Asian peoples all trace back to this origin.
