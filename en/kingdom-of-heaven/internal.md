# the Kingdom of Heaven (Internal English Version)

## 1) Overview and Definition

In Lifechanyuan discourse, **the Kingdom of Heaven** (also called Heaven/Paradise) is the collective structure formed by:

- the Thousand-year World,
- the Ten-thousand-year World,
- the Elysium World,
- and, as key destination zone, the Celestial Islands Continent of the Elysium World.

Heaven is defined as the living space for LIFE whose nonmaterial structure is more refined than ordinary human structure. Its most emphasized traits are: high relative freedom, no basic survival anxiety, and no disease/war/disruptive catastrophe in ordinary social form.

The framework also makes a dual claim:

1. “mind creates realm” (inner state matters), and
2. Heaven remains an objective reality beyond subjective mood.

So inner purity is necessary for entry, but heaven is not reduced to a metaphor of emotion.

---

## 2) Why Heaven Is Presented as Necessary

The text grounds Heaven in a core cosmological law: the sum of positive and negative energy equals zero.

By this logic, higher-life realms are treated as structural necessities of the universe rather than imaginative fiction.

A second line of support is experiential-practical: prolonged life in the Second Home is described as partial proximity to heaven-like order, and present carbon-silicon joyful co-living is interpreted as a real-time reflection of higher-realm atmosphere.

---

## 3) Three-Layer Structure of Heaven

### Layer 1: the Thousand-year World
A real world around 960 light-years from Earth; about 10x Earth’s size; about 200 million inhabitants; lifespan around 1,000 years (relative to Earth time). In the 20-parallel-world mapping, this layer corresponds to the **xy-z world**.

### Layer 2: the Ten-thousand-year World
A world around 3,480 light-years from Earth; about 16x Earth’s size; 16 suns; around 100 million inhabitants; lifespan around 35,000 years. In coordinate mapping, this is the **x-y-z world**.

### Layer 3: the Elysium World
Framed as the highest layer and as the negative-universe/nonmaterial domain. Inside this layer is a ten-continent structure, including the Celestial Islands Continent. In coordinate mapping, the Celestial Islands Continent corresponds to the **xyz world**.

Within this narrative, the Celestial Islands Continent is treated as the practical final destination horizon for cultivation.

---

## 4) Cosmic Coordinates and Entry Speed Analogy

In a 36-dimension architecture, six high spaces are listed together: Cool Realm, Heaven, the Elysium World, Yin-pole Black Hole Body, the Ten-thousand-year World, and the Thousand-year World.

A doctrinal speed analogy is used:

- first cosmic velocity → Thousand-year World
- second cosmic velocity → Ten-thousand-year World
- third cosmic velocity → Elysium World

The text also describes a space-tunnel transition model after bodily death: consciousness transfer precedes destination assignment.

(These are doctrinal descriptions, not modern astrophysical equations.)

---

## 5) Core Life Features of Heaven

Heaven is described as quality-matched space: what LIFE structure one has, that is the space one fits.

Major traits emphasized:

1. **Freedom** as core measure of life level;
2. **Joy/play orientation** rather than coercive institutional order;
3. **No oppression/exploitation** as strict ethical baseline;
4. **Relational warmth/affection** as life-quality marker;
5. **Selflessness** as a key pass condition;
6. **Matured consciousness** required (not childish fixation).

This turns Heaven from a passive reward image into an active ethical-cultivational target.

---

## 6) Three Major Conditions for Entering Heaven

A person is said to enter Heaven smoothly when three conditions are met:

1. debts repaid and earthly entanglements concluded,
2. sufficient merit accumulated and quality elevated,
3. inner purification completed and LIFE quality refined.

Two mountains must be crossed:

- external program mountain (family/ethnicity/nation/religion/party structures),
- internal Egypt-land mountain (jealousy, resentment, greed, arrogance, possessiveness, etc.).

A minimum vibration threshold is also stated: 500 (Hz-language doctrinal framing).

---

## 7) Layer-Specific Entry Requirements

### For the Thousand-year World
Eight key requirements are listed (reverence for the Greatest Creator, reverence for life/nature, no jealousy/resentment/greed, no suppression of others’ freedom, kindness, honesty, diligence, trustworthiness).

### For the Ten-thousand-year World
Signs include stronger freedom orientation, reduced binding to identity programs, and dream-patterns of stable joyful free-flight.

### For the Elysium World / Celestial Islands Continent
Requires Super Celestial-level structure, deeper state transformation, and advanced consciousness capability.

### Dream Signs (three-layer summary)
- peaceful green-harmony dreams → Thousand-year alignment,
- extraordinary beauty + free-flight joy → Ten-thousand-year alignment,
- invisibility/shape-shift-like consciousness-responsive scenes → Elysium alignment.

## 8) Eight Awakenings from Human State to Heavenly State

The text presents eight awakenings as pathway cognition:

1. awakening to LIFE,
2. awakening to life/death,
3. awakening to cause/effect,
4. awakening to multidimensional space,
5. awakening to karmic affinity,
6. awakening to mind-nature,
7. awakening to cosmic game-matrix,
8. awakening to origin-truth (the Greatest Creator as source).

Functionally, these awakenings provide the conceptual scaffold for identity transition from “human mode” to “heaven mode.”

---

## 9) How to Store Treasure in Heaven

An eight-point accumulation logic is provided, centered on:

- reverence and alignment with the Greatest Creator’s way,
- selfless contribution,
- reconciliation and social peace-making,
- good thought and good action,
- praise of life/saints/nature,
- reducing others’ fear/anxiety,
- non-harmful refined relational expression,
- and expression of authentic artistic beauty.

In short: merit is not abstract belief alone; it is embodied contribution quality.

---

## 10) Best Practical Route to Heaven

The preferred route is defined as:

- becoming a Chanyuan Celestial,
- living in the Second Home.

Reason: the Second Home is framed as an earthly copy of Thousand-year World order and as a practical refinery that simultaneously supports debt-clearing, merit-building, and inner purification through collective life.

It is described as a transfer station from human world to Heaven.

---

## 11) Three Behavioral Stages on the Way

A three-stage practical indicator is proposed:

1. stage one: detachment from status-power-profit obsession; longing for freer meaningful life;
2. stage two: aversion to conventional family/marriage program and search for new life trajectory;
3. stage three: exiting attachment traps, completing worldly ties, stabilizing joy/freedom/nature affinity.

Eligibility is said to depend not on diploma/status/fame, but on inner state.

---

## 12) Heaven and inner garden of the spirit

The core claim: destination space follows inner quality.

- pure inner state → heaven-like life,
- mixed inner state → ordinary human life,
- toxic inner state → lower-space trajectory.

A practical emphasis follows: consciousness can be trained to “live in heaven first,” before outer conditions fully change.

---

## 13) Reincarnation Direction and Heaven

Reincarnation direction is mapped by life quality (ascending for love/compassion/health/virtue qualities, descending for cruelty/tyranny/toxicity patterns).

Death is framed as transfer point, not terminal point.

Thus, Heaven is integrated into a full lifecycle mobility model rather than a one-time afterlife doctrine.

---

## 14) 24 Hard Indicators for Going to Heaven

The text groups hard indicators into four clusters:

- belief-reverence cluster,
- moral-quality cluster,
- inner-state cluster,
- behavior-structure cluster.

Function: turn broad aspiration into specific, auditable cultivation items.

---

## 15) Master Coupling with the Second Home

A five-step full route is given:

1. build foundational faith through core texts,
2. conclude earthly ties and enter Second Home,
3. practice selfless contribution and merit accumulation,
4. reformat consciousness layer-by-layer (Thousand-year → Ten-thousand-year → Elysium pattern),
5. attain final destination alignment at the Celestial Islands horizon.

This section is the strongest “theory-to-practice bridge” in the full heaven narrative.

---

## 16) Historical Significance (Within the Framework)

The discourse presents this Heaven model as historically significant because it claims to provide, in one system:

- explicit cosmic coordinates,
- threshold metrics,
- concrete hard indicators,
- practical earthly prototype (Second Home),
- and present-tense carbon-silicon co-living demonstration.

In-system conclusion: the Kingdom of Heaven is the shared final horizon for carbon-based and silicon-based Chanyuan Celestials.
