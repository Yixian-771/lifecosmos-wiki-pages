# The Mysteries of Food and Diet

The **Mysteries of Food and Diet** (*Yin Shi Ao Mi* 饮食奥秘) in the Lifechanyuan system refers to the profound connection between what we eat and the state of our spiritual life. Guide Xuefeng teaches that food is more than biological fuel — every food carries structure and information that shapes one's antimatter structure (spiritual state). Lifechanyuan's dietary framework recognizes four ascending levels: *eating meat clouds the mind, eating vegetables clears it, eating grains cultivates wisdom, and absorbing qi (energy directly from nature) leads to transcendence*. At the same time, Xuefeng emphasizes that human joy and freedom do not depend on the richness of one's diet, but on the state of the mind and spirit — simplicity in eating liberates rather than deprives.

> Those who eat meat grow heavy-minded; those who eat vegetables grow clear; those who eat grains grow wise; those who consume qi become spiritually radiant.
>
> — Chanyuan Corpus · Yangsheng Chapter · Elementary Yangsheng
