# One World Family

> The universe is the shared home of all life. All living beings dwell within it together — this is what we mean by One World Family.
>
> — Chanyuan Corpus · Civilization Chapter · "One World Family"

**One World Family** (*Tianxia Yijia* 天下一家) is Lifechanyuan's vision of humanity's ultimate civilizational form: transcending the divisions of nations, religions, political parties, and nuclear families so that all life can dwell together in harmony within the universe as one shared home. Its realization requires building a "complete circulation system" in society, with the Second Home (Lifechanyuan community) and secular governance working in concert. "All paths return to one source, all teachings converge — one world, one family" is the keynote of the New Era.
