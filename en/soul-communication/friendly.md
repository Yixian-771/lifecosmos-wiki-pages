## Where Soul Communication Sits in the Hierarchy

Life Chanyuan identifies eight levels of communication, from lowest to highest:

**Physical → Scent → Sound → Language → Energy → Information → Soul → Inspired**

Most of us rely on the first four — physical touch, body language, telephone, text messages. Language feels like our most sophisticated tool.

But the truly high-level communication begins at level five: **energy** (the field between bodies, especially in attraction), **information** (intuiting the mood of a space or a person without being told), **soul communication**, and finally **inspired communication** — direct alignment with the Greatest Creator's Tao.

Soul communication is characterized by one quality above all: **it requires no language, no distance, no time, and not even a living body on the other end.**

## How It Works: A Simple Picture

Imagine the entire universe as a vast ocean of "spirit" — invisible signals being broadcast by every life: humans, animals, plants, divine beings, the Greatest Creator, all broadcasting simultaneously.

When your signal matches someone else's — the frequencies align — resonance occurs, and information transfers. That's soul communication.

It's like a radio. The channel you tune to determines what you receive. And the cleaner the receiver, the clearer the signal.

- **Chaotic, cluttered soul**: receives static and distortion
- **Still, pure soul**: receives clear and accurate signals

This is why 98% of people cannot feel the Greatest Creator — not because the signal isn't there, but because their receiver is too cluttered.

## Who Can You Communicate With?

**Almost any life.**

### Plants and animals

Talk to your houseplant sincerely and with love — it will grow more beautifully and release an atmosphere that is good for you. If insects overrun your space, try communicating with them through genuine goodwill rather than hostility — they will leave. This sounds remarkable, but it is something happening constantly, everywhere.

### People who don't share your language

Two young people who share no common language but whose hearts genuinely long for each other can become completely unified. You can see it in their eyes. You can also observe it at the energy level: around every person flows an invisible field of rotating light and energy. Two people in love produce interweaving fields that flash in multiple colors — "as though staging a supremely beautiful space dance." Two people in a rage produce something like a volcano shrouded in black fog.

### People across any distance

If two people think of each other at exactly the same moment, communication happens — real feeling is exchanged. Distance is irrelevant.

### People of the past

Sincere Christians and Buddhists genuinely communicate with Jesus and the Buddha. The experience is "so warm and kind, like father-and-child, like the closest confidant, like a trusted intimate." Those who reach this state understand Jesus and Shakyamuni in ways most people never do.

### Future people

Prophets, great visionaries, and science fiction writers? Many of them are — quite literally — communicating with people and events of future space-times.

### The Greatest Creator

Your very first impression of a situation is often the Greatest Creator communicating with you. To reach out intentionally: meditation or prayer.

> A soul that is not open simultaneously refuses the opportunity to communicate with beautiful souls.
