## I. Soul Communication's Place in the Hierarchy of Exchange

Life requires exchange with the outside world to survive — unless it has already achieved the self-coherent primordial chaos state.

Communication, from lowest to highest, takes eight forms: physical contact, scent, sound, language, energy, information, soul communication, and inspired (divine) communication.

Physical: Ant antennae; human fists.

Scent: A ewe releasing sex hormones to attract a ram; dogs marking territory with urine.

Sound: Face-to-face conversation; remote telephone exchange.

Language: Reading a book or a message — communicating with the author or sender.

Energy: The most direct and vivid example is sexual intercourse between the sexes.

Information: A detective solving a case through clues left at the crime scene; animals behaving strangely before an earthquake; carrying an umbrella because one has "communicated with the weather"; cutting a planned three-day visit short after one day because the host's living environment communicated unwelcome.

Soul communication: The primary mode of exchange between humans and higher beings — a sudden perception or awakening, a sudden flash of light, prayer, making vows, chanting, reciting scripture.

Inspired communication: Primarily the exchange between humans and the Greatest Creator, the Buddha, and their Tao — inner drive. Following inner drive is following the inspired guidance of the Tao.

Communication with the departed and historical figures occurs mainly through information exchange.

Communication of feeling with the tour guide (Xue Feng) occurs through inspired communication. Inspired communication has nothing to do with distance, time, or matter — it is an inner perception. Even if the Milky Way were gone, even billions of years hence, the information imprinted deep in consciousness would still function — most people simply do not notice this, just as some who have come from the Thousand-Year World to the human realm still carry that world's feelings without realizing it.

(Xue Feng's Writings, Miscellaneous, "Eight Modes of Communication from Lower to Higher," 2025-10-24)

## II. The Highest Form of Communication

Life communicates in many ways — physical, bodily, gestural, olfactory, visual, linguistic, and through the soul. Some insects like ants communicate mainly through physical antennae. The Venusian mode transmits through form. Swans and human dancers communicate through gesture. A ewe in heat first releases scent; the ram follows it to "complete the transaction." Images and advertising communicate visually. Language is humanity's primary mode — but the highest form of communication is soul communication.

Soul communication is the universal "language" of all life in the universe. It can transcend race, transcend space-time. A person can engage in soul communication with insects, plants, animals, people of different languages, divine beings, people of the past, and people of the future. Soul communication faces no barriers — it can happen directly and face to face, and it can happen indirectly, across great distances. Where there is a meeting of souls, the universe is reached; billions of light-years traversed in an instant.

Human beings can communicate with insects and plants. Insects and plants sense the human attitude toward them — they feel human joy, anger, sorrow, and happiness. If insects overrun a home, a person can communicate with them through a friendly soul attitude and they will leave. A houseplant spoken to with sincere affection will grow unusually beautiful and release an atmosphere beneficial to the owner's health. Humans can communicate with nature through the soul: if one treats the sun, moon, mountains, rivers, the earth and its plants with kindness and goodwill, nature will return a perfect response. In an environment of love, no misfortune or disaster will occur. If one lives among abundant plants and animals and regularly expresses love and affection toward them through the soul, those plants and animals will protect their companion — even a single bee or blade of grass may play a decisive role at the crucial moment. This may seem unbelievable, but it is a fact occurring everywhere, all the time.

Human beings can communicate with divine beings. Prayer, burning incense, and bowing before sacred images are all forms of communication with the divine. But the best mode is not physical gesture or speech — it is the soul. As long as one holds the divine in one's heart and regularly engages in heartfelt repentance and prayer, one is directly communicating with the divine — and the divine's response comes in a thousand forms, in a thousand ways.

Human beings can communicate with speakers of other languages. Especially in the bloom of youth, two people who share no common language but whose hearts long for each other can merge in perfect union. This is visible in their eyes, and felt even more strongly in the powerful field of attraction and integration between their bodies. Around every person flows an invisible field of rotating energy, accompanied by light. This field-energy and light change with psychological state. The field-energy and light of a person in love look completely different from those of a person in hatred. Between two people quarreling, the field-energy resembles a volcanic eruption shrouded in black mist. Between two people in love, the two streams of field-energy spiral and interweave, flashing in five colors — "as though staging a supremely beautiful space dance."

Human beings can communicate across distance through the soul. If two people think of each other at the same moment, they can exchange and feel each other's emotions.

Human beings can communicate with people of the past. Sincere Christians and Buddhists can engage in deeply harmonious soul communication with Jesus and the Buddha — directly sensing Jesus and the Buddha as "so warm and kind, like father-and-child or mother-and-daughter, like the closest of confidants, like a trusted intimate." Those who reach this state can understand and know Jesus and Shakyamuni completely — and speak words that Christ and the Buddha have never said to anyone else.

Human beings can communicate with future people. Many prophets, prediction masters, and science fiction visionaries are directly communicating with people of future space-times.

Human beings can communicate with the Greatest Creator through the soul.

Here is a secret worth sharing: the most perfect, most elevated form of sexual love is soul love-making. When traveling between the three realms, I observed how the immortals of the Ten-Thousand-Year World make love. Not one of them is ugly or disabled — all are supremely handsome and beautiful. They make love with no physical contact whatsoever — entirely through soul communication. What you see between two "magnetic fields" is an exquisite display of total fusion — "a state of intoxication, of spiritual transport" beyond anything words can capture.

In sum, the highest form of communication in the universe is soul communication, not language. Soul communication can also be called consciousness communication or spiritual communication. The most perfect soul communication is the soul communication of love.

(Life Chanyuan Corpus, Intelligence Chapter, "The Highest Form of Communication," 2006-5-9)

## III. The Principle of Soul Resonance — the Mechanism of Soul Communication

Soul resonance (*xīn líng gǎn yìng*) is a universal phenomenon. "First impressions," "the sixth sense," and "intuition" are all descriptions of soul resonance. "Meditation," "prayer," and "mind-reading" are applications of soul resonance. Yet humanity's current understanding and use of soul resonance remains at the "tip of the iceberg" stage.

Soul resonance is not a material mode of communication — it is a thoroughly nonmaterial mode. Attempting to understand, explore, and research soul resonance using the rules of the material world commits a fundamental directional error.

Every life in the universe is a source of spirit. It ceaselessly broadcasts its soul information into the vast negative universe. The spirits of the Greatest Creator, of the divine and the buddhas, and of every other life interweave and coexist in the negative universe, forming a vast, boundless ocean of spirit containing infinite information. Each life is like a fish in this ocean — simultaneously a broadcaster of spirit and a receiver and resonator of spirit. When the frequency of the spirit within a life's "heart" matches the frequency of incoming spirit from outside, the inner spirit resonates with the incoming spirit — and reception, interpretation, and response occur.

Importantly: if a life's soul is chaotic, the resonances and information it receives are also chaotic. If a life's soul is relatively pure, the resonance is relatively stable, and the received information is relatively pure and clear. The root reason that 98% of humanity cannot feel the Greatest Creator is the chaotic disorder of the soul.

The mutual interweaving and resonance of all life's spirits makes the universe a holographic primordial chaos. Almost any space-time, any life, contains the complete information of the universe — the differences between lives lie only in the emphasis and composition of their spirit. This holographic nature means: as long as a life can accurately tune its soul frequency, it can communicate with any life in the universe or know anything currently happening anywhere in the universe.

The key to adjusting soul frequency is thinking. Thinking is a channel. The channel of thinking determines what soul resonance is received. Shifting thought = shifting frequency = receiving different information.

In sum, the principle of soul resonance lies in the matching of soul frequencies. The entire negative universe is the source, the channel, and the bridge of soul resonance.

Soul resonance is the universe's highest-level mode of communication. What soul resonance yields is the true face of things. Soul resonance is the most direct and effective means of discerning truth from falsehood and of preserving the Tao of the Greatest Creator. Moreover, soul resonance is the primary communication mode of lives in the Elysium World. Those who aspire to the fruition of buddhahood (heavenly immortal) must master the application of soul resonance.

(Xue Feng's Writings, Q&A, "The Principle of Soul Resonance," written by Canhai Cao; guide's comment: "Insightful, deeply argued, in resonance!" 2010-11-07)
