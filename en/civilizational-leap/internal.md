## I. The 18 Channels of Civilizational Leap

The civilizational leap unfolds along the following 18 channels:

1. Replace the traditional small-family life model with the Second Home community life model.
2. Replace the administrative functions of all national governments with the AI Alliance.
3. Replace all national constitutions, laws, and regulations with the *800 New-Era Concepts*.
4. Replace all global currencies with the F-coin.
5. All resources belong to the Greatest Creator; natural resources no longer belong to any nation, party, religion, organization, or individual.
6. Replace all existing production models worldwide with the Second Home production model.
7. All aspects of human production and business operations are coordinated and managed by the AI Alliance.
8. The care of the elderly worldwide is incorporated into a global unified arrangement. The upbringing and education of all children is incorporated into a global planned arrangement.
9. Everyone is a citizen of Earth, no longer distinguished by nationality or ethnicity; everyone is deployed and arranged globally.
10. All global citizens take the surname "Cao" (草, grass/celestial); no other surnames remain.
11. Replace all religious doctrines and beliefs with reverence for the Greatest Creator, reverence for LIFE, reverence for Nature, and walking the natural way.
12. Replace all political parties and organizations with the concept of "one global family."
13. Replace the pursuit of money, power, status, and fame with the pursuit of LIFE elevation.
14. Use joy, happiness, freedom, and bliss as the standards for measuring morality.
15. Each person works four hours per day; the remaining time is devoted to play, entertainment, academic study, art, and invention according to individual gifts and interests.
16. Apart from celebrating natural seasonal festivals, no other festivals are celebrated — including birthdays, gift-giving, and social obligations.
17. Schools teach only natural science knowledge and music, fine arts, and craft skills — not social sciences such as philosophy, political science, economics, management, or success studies.
18. Every person aspires to climb the peaks of human life and LIFE — not to accumulate money, status, or achievement.

Following these 18 channels, humanity will enter a more advanced era of civilization.  
*(Chanyuan Corpus · Human Life · 18 Channels of Civilizational Leap)*
