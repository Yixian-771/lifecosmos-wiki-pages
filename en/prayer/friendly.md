# Prayer (Friendly Version)

## Have You Ever Had a Moment Like This?

Things are tough. You feel stuck. And somewhere in you, there's an impulse to look up and say — *"Please. Help me."*

Or maybe in a quiet night, you just want to talk to something larger than yourself. To say thank you. To share your fears. To describe your dreams.

That impulse is the most natural form of prayer.

The Lifechanyuan system tells us: this is not superstition, and not just psychological comfort. This is an authentic, effective mode of communication between the human soul and the Greatest Creator (God).

---

## What Is Prayer?

In the Lifechanyuan system, prayer has a very clear position:

> **Prayer is one of the two primary means of directly connecting with the Greatest Creator — the other being meditation.**

It is not a religious ceremony. It's not the kind where you kneel in a church, face a statue, and recite words aloud. It is a **quiet, genuine conversation between your soul and the Greatest Creator**.

> Prayer is a daily conscious activity; without prayer, one cannot receive divine assistance.

> Prayer has an unimaginable power. When you feel trapped by circumstances and unable to realize your beautiful ideals, pray more!

---

## Why Is Prayer Real and Effective?

This isn't mysticism — it rests on cosmic principles:

**1. The Tao has instantaneous resonance**

Guide Xuefeng tells us that the Tao (the spirit of the Greatest Creator) is the most sensitive "sensor" in the universe. Every inner movement we have, the Tao perceives instantly — including our prayers.

**2. Your thoughts become thought-waves**

> Our mental activity transforms into thought-waves — like an EEG or ECG — broadcasting our heartfelt intentions into the vast universe. The Greatest Creator will surely respond.

**3. The universe is holographic**

The spirit of the Greatest Creator permeates every realm of existence. Every thought you have is intimately connected to the Greatest Creator's spirit — no signal needed, no intermediary, no middleman.

## How Do You Pray?

Lifechanyuan offers a clear structure for prayer:

**Step 1: Gratitude**
Thank the Greatest Creator for today's grace — food eaten, a place to sleep, safety. These are worth being grateful for.

**Step 2: Praise**
Praise and glorify the Greatest Creator's greatness, wisdom, and love.

**Step 3: Submission**
Tell the Greatest Creator: *I am willing to act according to Your will, without concern for my personal success or failure.*

**Step 4: Repentance**
Honestly and sincerely acknowledge to the Greatest Creator any errors or wrongs from recent days.

**Step 5: Petition**
Ask the Greatest Creator to help you walk the right path and, in your next lifetime, enter a higher realm of life.

**Closing**: Conclude the prayer in the name of Jesus ("Amen").

Simple structure. But it requires sincerity.

## Three Important Things to Remember

### Prayer is private — not a performance

> Prayer is a personal act, not a social performance. The kind of shouting aloud, deliberately letting others hear — that is false and useless. We must carry it out quietly, silently, without speaking aloud.

No ceremony required. No sacred space required. No witnesses required. Just you, your heart, and the Greatest Creator.

### Never carry evil intentions into prayer

> When praying, be absolutely certain never to harbor evil intentions. Praying for someone's downfall, praying for promotions and wealth, praying for family glory, complaining about God's injustice — these will be received by demons, and the one who prays will be the ultimate victim. Evil intentions are not on the same frequency as the Greatest Creator — they only resonate with demons.

Prayer's energy matches the frequency of your intention. Good intent → reaches the Greatest Creator. Evil intent → received by demons.

### Don't pray for miracles in everyday life

> Miracles do occur frequently, but we must not pray for their appearance.

The Greatest Creator will not break the universe's program to make you instantly rich or immediately enlightened just because you asked. Prayer is a way of tuning your soul's frequency — not a vending machine.

**The exception:** In a genuine emergency or crisis, praying for a miracle is exactly right — "spend a thousand days training troops for use at the critical moment." In true danger, full-hearted prayer to the Greatest Creator brings intervention at the decisive instant.

## A True Story: The Guide's Prayer

Once, Guide Xuefeng suddenly felt deeply uneasy. His wife was riding a long-distance bus, and his instinct told him something was wrong. He immediately prayed:

> "O Almighty God, my beloved is of utmost importance to me — please do not allow the bus she is riding to have a serious accident. I beg you, God! Amen!"

What followed: his wife, for no clear reason, suddenly felt restless and switched seats. Minutes later, the exact tire beneath her original seat exploded, blowing out the floor of that spot.

A real story. Prayer, at the critical moment, can connect you to a guardian force far greater than yourself.
