# Prayer

**Prayer**, in the Lifechanyuan system, is one of the two fundamental ways human beings can connect directly with the Greatest Creator (God/the Tao) — the other being meditation. Prayer is not a religious ritual but an authentic dialogue between the soul and the Greatest Creator. Its mechanism: human thoughts transform into thought-waves broadcast into the universe; the Tao possesses "instantaneous resonance" — every heartfelt prayer is perceived and responded to. Prayer is an indispensable daily practice for spiritual cultivation; without prayer, one receives no divine assistance.

> Prayer has an unimaginable power. When you feel trapped by circumstances and unable to realize your beautiful ideals, pray more!
