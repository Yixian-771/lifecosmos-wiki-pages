# Spiritual Purification Course

[Gateway Page]

The Spiritual Purification Course is a core required course explicitly proposed by Guide Xuefeng. It centers on systematic Soul Garden construction and is treated as the urgent starting point for entering the new era of LIFE.

---

## Core Definition

**The Spiritual Purification Course is the systematic practice of Soul Garden construction.**

Its central task is to remove the "weeds and poisonous plants" in consciousness and cultivate "flowers and crops"—so that LIFE's antimatter structure evolves toward greater purity, stability, and higher vibration.

---

## Key Points

### Position
- Not an elective, but a foundational required course
- The inner preparation project for moving from civilization 2.0 to civilization 3.0
- The first gate in personal cultivation and collective renewal

### Practical Focus
1. Identify negative patterns in consciousness
2. Remove weeds and poisonous plants
3. Cultivate positive qualities and stable virtue
4. Verify change through daily life, work, and relationships
5. Sustain growth through reading, service, and communal refinement

### Why It Matters
- For individuals: less inner conflict, greater clarity and freedom
- For communities: more trust, less friction, healthier collaboration
- For civilization: inner maturity as the true base layer of social progress
