# Spiritual Purification Course: Why “This Is the Most Urgent Task”

## 1) One-sentence summary

In the Lifechanyuan framework, the Spiritual Purification Course is not optional self-help. It is the required foundation for entering the new-era way of LIFE.

Its core is simple:
remove the weeds in consciousness, and cultivate flowers of truth, goodness, beauty, love, and service.

---

## 2) What does this course actually teach?

In plain language, it trains two things:

1. **Removing weeds**: jealousy, comparison, complaint, anger, greed, fear, arrogance, and other destructive patterns.
2. **Growing flowers**: gratitude, sincerity, tolerance, selflessness, devotion, harmony, diligence, and joy.

So this is not just theory. It is a long-term, repeatable practice path.

---

## 3) Why is it called a required course?

The core judgment here is:
many social problems look external, but their root is inner disorder.

If you only change rules but not consciousness, conflict tends to return.
If consciousness is purified, many outer conflicts naturally decrease.

That is why this course is placed first: purify the inner structure, then talk about higher civilization transition.

---

## 4) How do you practice it? (simple view)

You can treat it as an eight-part daily training:

- Repentance (honest correction)
- Prayer (alignment with the Greatest Creator)
- Gratitude (reframing perception)
- Selfless service & formless giving
- Study of corpus texts (cognitive calibration)
- Communal refinement (testing in real relationships)
- Diligent labor (purification through action)
- Entering the Lingxiu state (less attachment, more harmony)

The point is not “knowing,” but “practicing every day.”

---

## 5) How do you know you are improving?

You can observe three layers:

### Emotional layer
- Less anxiety, resentment, and comparison?
- More peace, stability, and joy?

### Relationship layer
- Fewer conflicts?
- More natural respect, empathy, and collaboration?

### Action layer
- More reliability, contribution, and accountability?
- Better consistency between words and behavior?

Within this framework, a directional scale is also used:
**200** as the consciousness threshold and **500** as the elementary Heaven-level LIFE baseline.

---

## 6) How is it related to the Kingdom of Heaven?

In one line:
**Spiritual purification is the foundational passage condition for Heaven-path advancement.**

This is not framed as “arrive by declaration,” but as “destination follows LIFE state.”

So purification is not an add-on. It is the route itself.

---

## 7) Why is the Second Home emphasized so much?

Because the Second Home is framed as an earthly practice copy of Thousand-year World life logic.

That means this course is not only learned in text, but lived in real daily context:
work, cooperation, relationship, and service.

This is how ideas become habits, and habits become LIFE quality.
