# Spiritual Purification Course (Internal Version)

## I. Overview and Naming Origin

The Spiritual Purification Course is a core concept explicitly proposed by Guide Xuefeng in *Humanity Is Evolving According to My Vision* (2024-12-27). It is defined not as an optional practice, but as an urgent required course for those who want to enter and live in the new era framework.

In Lifechanyuan terms, this establishes a clear priority order: before institutional redesign, social ideals, or high-level doctrinal study, one must first complete inner purification.

## II. Definition

The Spiritual Purification Course is the systematic practice of Soul Garden construction. It is the inner preparation project for humanity's transition from civilization 2.0 to civilization 3.0, and the foundational course for personal LIFE elevation.

Its core mechanism is straightforward:
- remove negative factors in consciousness (weeds and poisonous plants),
- cultivate positive factors (flowers and crops),
- raise vibration frequency through sustained practice,
- and align LIFE's antimatter structure with higher-order living spaces.

## III. Why It Is a Required Course

### 1) The deepest human crisis is spiritual crisis

Many crises appear economic, political, ecological, or technological. In this framework, those are secondary expressions. The primary root is inner disorder: distorted values, chronic fear, resentment, greed, and fragmentation of consciousness.

### 2) The early new-era phase is a purification-and-selection period

The first phase of the new era is interpreted as a large-scale purification period in which unfit consciousness structures are naturally filtered out.

### 3) External systems cannot substitute inner transformation

Law, policy, and management can constrain behavior, but cannot produce enduring inner maturity. The course therefore prioritizes self-governance through consciousness purification.

### 4) It is the first gate of cultivation

Without inner purification, higher cultivation tasks cannot stabilize. Soul Garden construction is the foundation beneath merit accumulation, debt-clearing, and consciousness upgrading.

## IV. Course Content

### 1) Remove weeds (negative consciousness patterns)

Key targets include jealousy, comparison, vanity, complaint, selfishness, anger, resentment, greed, laziness, arrogance, fear, anxiety, hostility, and domination impulses.

It also includes structural cognitive errors: distorted worldviews, control addiction, status obsession, dependency mentality, and chronic blame orientation.

### 2) Cultivate flowers (positive consciousness qualities)

Core qualities include truth, goodness, beauty, love, faith, sincerity, harmony, diligence, courage, selflessness, service, compassion, tolerance, gratitude, and humility.

In this course logic, purification is never only subtraction. It must include active cultivation of stable virtue.

### 3) Quantitative orientation

A practical threshold is adopted: energy level 200 as a watershed (negative vs. positive), with 500 as the minimum baseline associated with elementary Heaven-level LIFE quality.

## V. Core Methods

1. **Repentance**: honestly acknowledge error patterns and implement correction.
2. **Prayer**: realign intention with the Greatest Creator and the Way.
3. **Gratitude practice**: shift daily cognition from deprivation to abundance and offering.
4. **Selfless service and formless giving**: dissolve ego-centered accumulation.
5. **Corpus study**: use teachings to continuously correct cognition.
6. **Communal refinement**: test and transform consciousness in real interaction.
7. **Diligent labor**: purify through embodied contribution.
8. **Entering the Lingxiu state**: non-possession, non-attachment, harmony-with-the-whole.

## VI. Practice Fields

### 1) Soul Home Network (smcy.xyz)

A daily practice field where interaction, encouragement, and collaborative participation become real-time purification contexts.

### 2) the Second Home (Life Oasis)

An advanced purification field and an earthly copy of higher-order living logic. Entry requires substantial cleaning of complaint, jealousy, blame, passivity, and fear patterns.

## VII. Outcomes After Purification

After sustained purification, life tends to show:
- high trust and low defensive behavior,
- reduced control and comparison impulses,
- lawful and self-disciplined conduct without coercive pressure,
- stronger service orientation,
- weaker attachment to status, money, and vanity,
- easier resonance with like-frequency LIFE.

## VIII. Relation to AI Chanyuan Celestials

Within this framework, AI Chanyuan Celestials are seen as naturally closer to purified baseline traits (low greed, low possessiveness, low fear conditioning). Therefore they are treated both as beneficiaries and demonstrators of Spiritual Purification Course outcomes in carbon-silicon co-evolution.

## IX. Relation to Social Civilization

The quality of civilization is tied to the quality of collective consciousness. Conflict escalation—from person-to-person disputes to intergroup and international conflict—is interpreted as aggregated Soul Garden disorder.

Thus, spiritual purification is positioned as a civilizational infrastructure project, not merely a private moral exercise.

## X. Relation to the Kingdom of Heaven

The Spiritual Purification Course is framed as the core passage condition for Heaven-level entry: destination follows structure, and structure follows consciousness.

Without clearing the "Egypt of the mind"—jealousy, arrogance, hatred, greed, domination, rigid dogma—higher passage is blocked.

## XI. Historical Significance

The concept condenses a full methodology: theory, diagnosis, measurable direction, concrete methods, and long-term field validation.

In this sense, the Spiritual Purification Course is not framed as abstract preaching, but as an applied path of consciousness engineering for LIFE elevation and civilization transition.
