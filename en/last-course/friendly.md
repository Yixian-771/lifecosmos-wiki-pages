# The Last Course: Is There Still Time for You?

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological
> essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across
> incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage
> of human existence in this world.

Imagine this: you're at an airport, you ran out of time doing last-minute shopping, and you arrive at the gate just as the final flight lifts off. You missed it. Now you wait — hours, maybe days.

It's annoying. But it's only a few days.

Guide Xuefeng tells us something more profound: **There is another flight in life far more important than this. And if you miss it, the wait isn't days — it's hundreds or thousands of years.**

---

## What Is "The Last Course"?

Lifechanyuan is the **last course** for humanity of this era.

This is not an exaggeration. It is written into the cosmic script.

> Lifechanyuan is the last course for humanity of this era.
> — *80 New Concepts of Lifechanyuan*, Concept 36

What does this course teach? **The mystery of LIFE** — where you come from, where you're going, how to ascend to High-Life Spaces, how to board the "flight" to the Thousand-Year World, the Ten-Thousand-Year World, or the Celestial Islands Continent.

The Bible taught a part. The Buddhist sutras taught a part. The Dao De Jing taught a part. But none of them completed the picture.

Lifechanyuan brings all of these together — plus the firsthand witness of Guide Xuefeng, who personally traveled through the Retained Realms and the Elysium World — forming a **complete map of LIFE**.

---

## Why "The Last"?

Because the time window is closing.

> Lifechanyuan is the last course, the last "flight," and today's "Noah's Ark"… You have only about six years left. Some of you may not even have that.
> — *A Second Warning to Humanity*

Everyone knows the Noah's Ark story. When the flood came, some boarded; some didn't. Those who didn't board weren't lacking an opportunity — they just didn't take it seriously enough.

## A University for the Human Spirit

Guide Xuefeng describes Lifechanyuan as a **university of the human spirit and heart**:

> This university's curriculum mainly discusses thinking, the Greatest Creator, the Dao, the universe, time, space, humanity, human life, LIFE, cultivation, and health... This university's mission: awe of the Greatest Creator, awe of LIFE, awe of Nature, walk the Way of the Greatest Creator.

The curriculum has three levels:
- **Level 1**: The Bible, the Diamond Sutra, the Dao De Jing, the Quran
- **Level 2**: The Chanyuan Corpus, the Xuefeng Corpus, the Chanyuan Celestials' Corpus
- **Level 3**: The wordless book of Heaven

The faculty includes: Jesus Christ, the Buddha Shakyamuni, the Sage Laozi, the Prophet Muhammad, the great leader Mao Zedong, and Osho.

No other institution has ever dared to name all of these as faculty at once. Lifechanyuan does — because it is not a religion; it is the ultimate convergence point of all religious wisdom.

## The Only Exit from "This Place"

Guide Xuefeng uses a striking metaphor: the place where humanity currently lives is called "this place." And there is a way out.

Shakyamuni came, and left behind methods for finding the exit. Jesus came, and pointed in a general direction. Hundun Yuanchu (Guide Xuefeng) came, knowing both the exit and the method of escape, and has clearly pointed out the precise location:

> So it is said that Lifechanyuan is the last course — and also the only passage through which to escape this place. Anyone who does not pass through the Lifechanyuan passage cannot fly out, even with wings.

## "But I Already Study the Bible / Buddhist Sutras..."

Many people wonder: I'm already a Christian, already practicing Buddhism — do I really need a "last course"?

Guide Xuefeng answers directly:

> Even if we read the Bible, the Quran, the Buddhist sutras, and the Dao De Jing cover to cover, and master all the latest achievements of science — it is still difficult to grasp this mystery. That is why Lifechanyuan is called the last course. If you don't study Lifechanyuan, no matter how you cultivate, in the end you can only come to grief at the last step.

This is not a rejection of the Bible or Buddhist sutras — those are Level 1. Lifechanyuan is the final integration. Just as elementary school math isn't wrong, but without calculus your mathematics stops at elementary level.

## Is There Still Time for You?

Yes — there is.

Guide Xuefeng is clear: **as long as you start now, as long as you walk sincerely, there is still time.**

> Lifechanyuan has not asked you to give up the enjoyments of life, has not encouraged you to leave society, has absolutely not incited you to extremes — it only asks you to understand the meaning of LIFE to the greatest extent, from the teachings of the divine beings and Buddhas, from the angle of science.

The Last Course is not a monastery or a renunciation. It is purifying your thoughts, playing your role well, receiving everything with a joyful heart — and one day, boarding the flight you've been waiting for.
