# The Script of Human Life: Your Story Was Written Before You Arrived

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological
> essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across
> incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage
> of human existence in this world.

Life is a performance.

Not in a dismissive sense — this is the actual truth about the universe.

Guide Xuefeng puts it most concisely:

> The process of LIFE traveling through the human world is called life. The meaning of life is to experience, perceive, and ascend — serving the Greatest Creator. The essence of life is to perform according to the requirements of the cosmic script.

You didn't come to this world to "figure it all out." You came to **play your assigned role well.**

---

## Your Script Was Already Written

The entire universe — the entire development of humanity — was written long ago into a script. Your birthplace, family, encounters, personality, name, the illnesses you'll suffer, the people you'll meet — all of it is in the script.

*The Time Array* tells us: even people's names were already scripted.

Hard to accept? Consider this: prophecies written hundreds or even thousands of years ago came true with stunning precision. The Bible, Nostradamus, China's ancient *Golden Mausoleum Tablet Inscription*. If the script weren't written in advance, how could any of this be possible?

**Everything has already been arranged. Time, as we experience it, is an illusion.**

## Greatest Creator Scripts, Dao Directs, You Act

*Screenwriter, Director, Actor* makes this clear:

- **The Greatest Creator** is the ultimate screenwriter. The story unfolds according to the Greatest Creator's will — the Dao.
- **Jesus, Shakyamuni, Muhammad, Hundun Yuanchu**… are all directors.
- **You** are an actor.

Actors must perform according to the director's requirements. This isn't tragedy — it's the rule of the universe.

But there's one thing you are free to do: **choose which director to follow.**

Hundun Yuanchu (Guide Xuefeng) directs according to the script of the Thousand-Year World, the Ten-Thousand-Year World, and the Celestial Islands Continent of the Elysium World. Following him means choosing the script that leads to Heaven.

## This Life's Script Is Fixed; the Next Life's Script Is Alive

This is the most important piece:

- **This life**: The major outlines are set. You can't change them.
- **Your next life**: Changes at any time — based on your **intentions** and **quality of performance** right now.

*Exploration of the Script of Human Life* says:

> The past script is fixed; the future script is alive. The living script changes at any moment based on every person's intentions and the quality of their role performance.

Good intentions in this life → next life's script rises. Evil intentions → falls. This is karma.

## Never Try to Write Your Own Script

*The Sorrow of Self-Scripting, Self-Directing, Self-Acting* warns against the most common human mistake — thinking you are your own screenwriter, designing your own fate on your own terms.

The result? Only sorrow.

Satan was once an archangel. He fell precisely because he wanted to become the Greatest Creator. And he became a devil.

**Harboring the thought of writing your own script is walking in the direction of the devil.**

The wisest way to live: be at ease with whatever comes, go with the flow, act naturally, act in the moment.

---

## How to Play Your Role Well

In the script of human life, the only thing you genuinely can do:
1. **Form good intentions** — your intentional activity is entirely your free will
2. **Play your role well** — no matter what role, be calm and joyful and grateful at all times

The verification standard:

> No matter what role you are playing — whether a role of suffering or enjoyment, spectacular or mundane — if at any moment you are calm and at ease, joyful and happy, without hesitation, worry, pain, anxiety, dread, or fear, but rather grateful, then your role performance is good.

---

## Heaven or Hell Has Nothing to Do With the Role You Play

The most counterintuitive finding:

> Whether you go to Heaven or Hell has nothing to do with the role you play — it has everything to do with whether you play your role well.

If Hitler played his assigned role with genuine excellence — he might still go to Heaven.
A thief who plays their role with true grace — might go to Heaven.
A "good person" who lives in constant resentment and anxiety, playing their role poorly — goes to Hell.

**Inner state, not outer role, determines where LIFE goes next.**
