# The Script of Human Life

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological
> essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across
> incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage
> of human existence in this world.

**The Script of Human Life** is the core concept in Lifechanyuan's cosmology that focuses on the nature of each individual's LIFE trajectory. The entire universe, and the entire development of humanity, was written long ago into a vast script. A person's life is the performance of their assigned role in this cosmic script. The meaning of human life is: **to experience, perceive, and ascend — serving the Greatest Creator. The essence of life is to perform according to the requirements of the cosmic script.**

> "The process of LIFE traveling through the human world is called life. The meaning of life is to experience, perceive, and ascend — serving the Greatest Creator. The essence of life is to perform according to the requirements of the cosmic script."
> — Xuefeng, *Answering Yinyin's Questions on Behalf of the Extinct Celestial*

---

## Core Points

- **The essence of life**: Perform according to the cosmic script — experience, perceive, ascend, serve the Greatest Creator
- **Screenwriter, director, actor**: The Greatest Creator is the ultimate screenwriter; the Dao is the director; each person is an actor
- **This life fixed, next life alive**: This life's script is determined; the next life's script changes — with intentions and actions
- **The prohibition against self-scripting**: Never attempt to write your own script or be your own director — that is the path of Satan
- **Playing your role well**: Intentions and perfection of role performance are the only real space for personal effort
- **Hundun Yuanchu is the earthly director**: Following the Guide means choosing the script that leads to Heaven
