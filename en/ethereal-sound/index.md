# Ethereal Sound · The Authentic Chan Melody

> **This is the ethereal sound of life. … The ethereal sound is the authentic Chan melody — it can only be grasped intuitively, never replicated. It is existence within non-existence, non-existence within existence — the ineffable truth of life itself. To hear the ethereal sound marks that one has stepped onto the celestial path. Though the celestial realm is still far, the dawn of hope has already appeared.**
>
> — Xue Feng, Life Chanyuan Corpus, "Cultivation Chapter VIII: Listening to the Ethereal Sound, Awakening to the Authentic Chan Melody"

---

Ethereal sound (*kōng líng miào yīn* 空灵妙音) is the inner spiritual perception that naturally arises in advanced cultivators who have entered the ling-empty state — a non-material experience that is neither ordinary hearing nor imagination, but rather the reception of celestial resonance combined with the flow of one's own life-consciousness structure. It is one of the eight great mysteries of LIFE.

The authentic Chan melody (*běn zhēn chán qū* 本真禅曲) is another name for the same phenomenon: the original melody closest to life's true nature — beyond any music, any language, irreproducible by any means.
