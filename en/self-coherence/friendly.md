# Self-Coherence: The Only Path to Eternal Bliss

## I. One Sentence to Start

**Self-Coherence means your inner system is complete in itself — lacking nothing, running in harmonious coordination, needing nothing from the outside world to meet any of its needs.**

In the Lifechanyuan system, Self-Coherence is not "logical consistency" or "not contradicting yourself." It is a state of the LIFE structure itself: **complete, self-sufficient, whole**.

Guide Xuefeng put it in one unforgettable sentence:

> How can a being reach the state of eternal bliss? The answer is: only through Self-Coherence. (*New Era Human 800 Concepts*, Concept 378)

---

## II. Why Are Humans Not Self-Coherent?

Xuefeng gave a very concrete picture:

> Humans need 64 external factors to maintain survival. If any one of those 64 factors is missing, humans cannot survive or continue.

Air, water, food, sunlight, warmth — remove any one of them and we die.

That is human "non-self-coherence." Your LIFE **depends on the outside world** to keep running. You always need to draw something from external sources just to exist.

Once you depend on the outside world, you are not free. Hungry, you must eat. Thirsty, you must drink. Cold, you must dress. Old, you need to be cared for. When you die, everything dissolves — all of this arises because the LIFE structure itself is not self-coherent.

By contrast, **the LIFE structure of a Celestial Being is self-coherent** — it needs no 64 external factors. It is itself a complete system. That is why Celestial Beings can exist eternally, dwell in eternal bliss, and move in absolute freedom.

## III. What Does Self-Coherence Equal? Five Equations

In *Self-Coherence: One of the Essential Qualities of Celestials on the Celestial Islands*, Xuefeng laid out five key equations:

**Equation 1: Self-Coherence = The Fullness of Original-Nature Light**

This is what the *Heart Sutra* says: "No arising, no ceasing; no defilement, no purity; no increase, no decrease." Such a LIFE neither increases nor decreases, neither arises nor ceases. It needs nothing from the outside to add to it, and loses nothing because the outside world withholds something.

**Equation 2: Self-Coherence = No Buddha Outside the Mind (Bodhidharma)**

Patriarch Bodhidharma wrote: "Its nature is fundamentally pure — it cannot be defiled. The *dharmakāya* is fundamentally without sensation: no hunger, no thirst, no cold, no heat, no illness, no love-attachment, no family, no suffering or bliss, no like or dislike." This describes the state of self-coherent LIFE: no hunger, no thirst, no cold, no heat — complete and self-sufficient.

**Equation 3: Self-Coherence = *Tathāgata* (Diamond Sutra)**

"No self-perception, no human-perception, no sentient-being-perception, no soul-perception, no dharma-perception, and no non-dharma-perception." When there is no separation between "self" and "the outside world," that is Self-Coherence.

**Equation 4: Self-Coherence = *Taiji* (Daoism)**

*Huanhu*, *yaoming*, *miaojiao*, *pinmu*, *yinyang*, *Taiji* — when yin and yang are joined as one, that is Self-Coherence.

**Equation 5: Self-Coherence = Androgynous Union of Yin and Yang**

Celestial Beings possess both male and female characteristics. This is not a metaphor — it is the actual form of the LIFE structure.

## IV. The Scientific Confirmation: The Angel Particle — "2=0"

In 2017, physicist Zhang Shoucheng (a student of Nobel laureate Yang Chen-Ning) and his team published a paper in the journal *Science* confirming the first discovery of the **Majorana fermion** — the "Angel Particle."

Zhang explained:

> A particle equals its own antiparticle. Translated into a mathematical formula, this is equivalent to 1 = -1. Moving -1 to the left side, we prove that 2 = 0.

Physicists had been searching for this for 80 years.

Xuefeng's interpretation:

> When I previously introduced the self-coherent phenomenon of Celestial Beings' LIFE state, most people thought it impossible, thought I was making it up. Now, with the latest scientific results — the "Angel Particle" — we can prove the existence of Celestial Beings.

**A particle equals its own antiparticle** = the androgynous union of yin and yang = the self-coherent LIFE structure of a Celestial Being.

Science and spirituality have met at this point.

## V. The Path to Self-Coherence

Xuefeng makes clear that Self-Coherence does not happen overnight. There are two roads:

### Road One: Cultivate a Complete and Independent Consciousness

> Self-Coherence is the supreme summit of cultivation. To reach Self-Coherence one must have a complete and independent consciousness. When one's own consciousness is no longer influenced or swayed by the consciousness of one's kind, one has reached Self-Coherence in consciousness — that is *Tathāgata* — and the most magnificent curtain of LIFE has been raised.

"Self-Coherence in consciousness" is the starting point — when your judgments, your choices, your perceptions no longer drift along with the opinions of others, you have begun to become self-coherent in consciousness.

A simple daily reference: when you face a situation and your first response is not "what will people think?" but "what do I genuinely feel? what accords with the Way of the Greatest Creator?" — that is a step toward Self-Coherence.

Xuefeng identified eight courses that form the foundation of a complete and independent consciousness: understanding the origin of the universe and LIFE; understanding the laws of karma and cosmic principles; understanding multidimensional space and the antimatter world; taking a clear-eyed view of human society. Without completing these eight courses, independent consciousness may form, but it will be incomplete — prone to extremes, liable to become authoritarian.

### Road Two: The Eight Methods of Cultivation

Concept 494 provides eight specific cultivation instructions:

1. **Consciousness creates reality** — believe that your mind has genuine creative power.
2. **Unobstructed by birth, death, glory, or disgrace** — know the masculine yet hold to the feminine; know the white yet hold to the black.
3. **Return consciousness to zero** — emptiness is form, form is emptiness; let the soul return to the wondrous void.
4. **Attach to nothing** — all things are a game; only LIFE and nature are real.
5. **Have nothing, yet possess everything** — transcend time and space; soar freely.
6. **Go along with conditions, roam at ease** — follow nature freely, without restraint.
7. **Be undifferentiated** — no inside or outside, no boundary, no right or wrong, no true or false.
8. **Let the "real" be dream-like; let the "illusory" feel meaningful** — dissolve the fixed boundary between real and illusory.

These are not moral requirements; they are directions of cultivation — guiding the LIFE structure step by step closer to Self-Coherence.

## VI. Where Is Self-Coherence on the Path to Becoming a Celestial?

Xuefeng described the Three-Step Path to Becoming a Celestial (Concept 497):

> Step one: having bid farewell to worry, pain, sorrow, anxiety, and fear, living in joy, happiness, freedom, and bliss — one has already become a Celestial.
>
> Step two: the heart has no abiding, the mind has no obstruction, going along with conditions roaming at ease, journeying spontaneously and joyfully, dwelling in delight at all times — one is already a Celestial.
>
> Step three: no-mind; everything is nature, living in nature; flexible and fluid, extraordinarily sensitive; possessing 64 spiritual powers. The heart has no abiding, the mind has no obstruction; non-self, non-form; **free, Self-Coherent**. Going along with conditions roaming at ease, journeying spontaneously and joyfully; one with the Way; carefree and transcendent.

**Self-Coherence is in Step Three** — the endpoint of the entire cultivation path, the core quality of the Celestial fruition.

Steps one and two are essential, of course. But "free, Self-Coherent, one with the Way" in Step Three is the state that actually brings one to eternal bliss and entry into the Celestial realm.

> I came from the Celestial realm and shall return to the Celestial realm. In this one journey through the human world, I leave nothing on my mind. The world fundamentally has no "me"; I am fundamentally not in the world. When the dream awakens I return to the true; returning to the true, I am on the celestial island. Ten thousand phenomena arise from the mind; the mind surpasses ten thousand phenomena. When eyes open, ten thousand phenomena arise; when eyes close, ten thousand phenomena cease. When phenomena arise, play the game; when phenomena cease, return to the original true.

This is the state of Self-Coherent LIFE: not clinging to phenomena; all phenomena arising from the mind; the mind surpassing all phenomena; coming and going freely; complete and self-sufficient.

**Only Self-Coherence is the realm of absolute freedom. Relying on the outside world, one is ultimately not free.**

This is not a slogan. It is the most lucid cosmological revelation in the entire Lifechanyuan system about what "ultimate freedom" actually is.

The path to Self-Coherence — every step counts.
