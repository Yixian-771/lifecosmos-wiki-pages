## III. The Kinds of Energy: Three Classification Systems

### (I) Material Energy and Antimatter Energy

This is the most fundamental pair of classifications in Lifechanyuan's energy taxonomy.

> Light energy, wind energy, water energy, thermal energy, electrical energy, magnetic energy, field energy, atomic energy, biological energy, mechanical energy, and so on — these are material energies. The force of ideologies, thinking power, concepts, thoughts, love, hatred, passion, sorrow, stimuli of beauty and ugliness, images, mind-images, art, music, and so on — these are antimatter energies.
>
> Alternatively: any energy that acts upon the material form is material energy; any energy that acts upon the spirit and heart-mind is antimatter energy. (*"Transmitting Chapter: Cosmic Element Three: Energy"*)

Traditional science's explanation of energy constitutes a "Narrow Energy Theory"; Lifechanyuan's elucidation of energy constitutes a "Broad Energy Theory."

### (II) Positive Energy and Negative Energy

> The total energy of the universe is zero; therefore, energy is divided into positive energy and negative energy, whose sum is zero — this accords with the Taiji principle. (*"Transmitting Chapter: Cosmic Element Three: Energy"*)

**Article 412** (full original): The total energy of the universe is zero. The universe maintains the stability of its order through its total energy being zero.

**Article 418** (full original): The law that maintains the sum of the universe's positive and negative energies at zero is called the law of cause and effect (karma).

Definition of positive and negative energy:

> Energy that promotes the arising and development of things and LIFE is positive energy; energy that leads to the dissolution and evolution of things and LIFE is negative energy. Any energy, as long as it promotes the arising and transformation of things and the manifestation and change of LIFE, is positive energy. Any energy, as long as it leads to the disappearance and evolution of things and the disappearance of LIFE's state, is negative energy. The same thermal energy — if it promotes the growth of a plant, it is positive energy; if it causes the plant's demise, it becomes negative energy.
>
> Energy is neutral. The energy used by celestials, divine beings, and Buddhas is not fundamentally different from the energy used by demons, ghosts, and monsters. (*"Transmitting Chapter: Cosmic Element Three: Energy"*)

### (III) Material Energy, Spiritual Energy, and Heart-Mind Energy

Classified by the object of their action, energies divide into three types:

- **Material energy** — acts upon material forms; concentrated expression: money and currency;
- **Spiritual energy** — acts upon spirit and consciousness; manifested in wisdom, ideals, and convictions;
- **Heart-mind energy** — acts upon LIFE's antimatter structure; comes from love, feeling, nature (xìng), and reverence for the Greatest Creator.

### (IV) Yin Energy and Yang Energy

> Energy is divided into yin energy and yang energy. Energy that can constitute matter is yang energy; energy that can constitute the soul is yin energy. Males possess yang energy; females possess yin energy. Females lack yang energy; males lack yin energy. (*"Transmitting Chapter: LIFE and Energy"*)

### (I) Macroscopic Conservation

> The law of energy conservation: the total energy of the universe is unchanged, maintaining zero state. From the macroscopic perspective, energy is conserved — it does not increase or decrease; it can only transform from one energy form into another. The universe maintains the stability of its order through its total energy being zero. (Article 412)

Positive-negative energy conservation as manifested in human life:

**Article 24** (full original): The sum of the universe's positive and negative energies is zero; therefore, what each person gives and what each person receives are forever equal.

**Article 438** (full original): In the universe, the sum of positive and negative energies is zero; the total score of winning and losing is zero. The glory of the successful often conceals the bitterness and suffering of the defeated. (*"Transmitting Chapter: Cosmic Element Three: Energy"*)
