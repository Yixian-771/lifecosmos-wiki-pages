# Thirty-Six-Dimensional Space: A Layered Map for Universe and LIFE

## 1) One-sentence summary

**Thirty-Six-Dimensional Space is Lifechanyuan’s layered cosmic map of where different LIFE states belong and where they can move.**

It is not a math puzzle for abstract debate. It is used as a navigation framework for understanding LIFE level, direction, and cultivation pathway.

---

## 2) Why introduce a “36-space” model?

Because many questions remain unresolved in flat thinking:

- Why do LIFE states differ so dramatically?
- Why do trajectories diverge even under similar outer conditions?
- Why are destination spaces (like Thousand-year World, Ten-thousand-year World, Elysium World) presented as layered rather than equal?

The framework’s answer is straightforward:
**space is layered, and LIFE condition determines matching layer.**

## 3) How is this model built?

It is said to follow three principles:

1. symmetry,
2. scientific coherence,
3. mathematical-logical consistency.

So the model is presented as rule-based structure, not arbitrary naming.

## 4) What does “36 dimensions” mean here?

In this context, it means:

- higher LIFE spaces,
- lower LIFE spaces,
- middle/transitional spaces,
- and ordered relations among them.

Think of it as a multi-layer ecology of existence: different LIFE structures resonate with different space bands.

## 5) Why does this matter for cultivation?

Because cultivation is not treated as moral decoration. It is treated as structural refinement.

- structure changes,
- matching space changes,
- destination tendency changes.

So the model works as a direction instrument, not only a cosmological claim.
