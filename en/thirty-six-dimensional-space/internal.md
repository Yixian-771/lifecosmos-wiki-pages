## 1) Overview and Definition

Thirty-Six-Dimensional Space is a central concept in the Lifechanyuan cosmological system. It is presented as a complete structural map of cosmic existence revealed through Hundun-level thinking, and as a classification system with strong internal logical support.

Core proposition: according to the quality-energy level of material and antimatter LIFE, existence is distributed across thirty-six dimensional spaces.

The framework states that each space is a large world, and that these spaces include higher, lower, and middle-layer LIFE domains. The model is used to support practical orientation toward higher destination spaces such as the Thousand-year World, Ten-thousand-year World, and Elysium World.

---

## 2) Division Principles of the 36-Space Model

The model is explicitly based on three principles:

1. **Symmetry Principle**: cosmic structures are symmetrical in existence (with special treatment of the cool-realm zero-point);
2. **Scientific-Fact Principle**: statements should not violate core scientific plausibility;
3. **Mathematical-Logical Principle**: classification must maintain internal logical consistency.

These principles are used as legitimacy constraints to avoid arbitrary space construction.

## 3) Full Space List and Layering Orientation

The source framework provides a full thirty-six-space list (ordered sequence), including high-level, low-level, and transitional domains.

The model is not merely nomenclature. It is a pathway map:

- space layers correspond to LIFE quality-energy,
- destination is linked with structure and consciousness condition,
- cultivation is interpreted as trajectory movement within this layered architecture.

## 4) Symmetry Logic and Structural Coherence

A key methodological claim is that universe-space architecture follows symmetric logic rather than random dispersion.

The model uses symmetry not only as geometric intuition, but as a rule for validating whether a proposed space relation is plausible inside the system.

This gives the 36-space framework an internal verification axis beyond narrative description.
