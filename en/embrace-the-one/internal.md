# Embrace the One (Internal Edition)

## I. Overview and Definition

Embrace the One (抱一, *bào yī*) is the foundational cultivation method and life orientation of the Lifechanyuan system. Sourced from Laozi's *Tao Te Ching* — "Therefore the sage embraces the One and becomes the model for all under heaven" — in Lifechanyuan's context, "the One" has a clear and unambiguous specific referent: the Way of the Greatest Creator. To embrace the One is to hold the Way of the Greatest Creator; to guard the One is to sustain that hold; to persist in the One means walking only one path in life, trusting only one Greatest Creator with the whole heart, and serving only one goal with every thought. Lifechanyuan brings "Embrace the One" down from its philosophical heights to become the core daily practice — no matter how winds shift or the world turns upside down, hold fast to the Way of the Greatest Creator, unshaken and unmoved. This is Embrace the One. This is one of Lifechanyuan's highest cultivation secrets.

**Core Definition I** (*Tao Te Ching*, Chapter 39, complete quotation):

> Heaven attained the One and became clear.
> Earth attained the One and became still.
> Spirit attained the One and became efficacious.
> The valley attained the One and became full.
> The ten thousand things attained the One and came to life.
> Therefore the sage embraces the One and becomes the model for all under heaven.

**Core Definition II** (*New Era Human 800 Concepts*, Entry 311, complete text):

> 1 + 1 = 2 is an increase in quantity. 1 + 1 = 1 is an increase in energy. The sage embraces the One and becomes the model for all under heaven.

**Core Definition III** (*New Era Human 800 Concepts*, Entry 706, complete text):

> Heaven attained the One and became clear; earth attained the One and became still; spirit attained the One and became efficacious; the valley attained the One and became full; the ten thousand things attained the One and came to life; the sage embraces the One and becomes the model for all under heaven. All of humanity has only one faith, one command; a person can have only one goal in life, one wish, one path of cultivation, one road to walk. Otherwise: difficulty becoming clear, difficulty becoming still, difficulty becoming efficacious, difficulty becoming full. Multiplicity creates disorder; mixture creates complexity. Only when "all methods return to their source," "all teachings return to One," the world one family, can there be "Embrace the One." Only with "Embrace the One" is there harmony, peace, auspiciousness, timely rains, and a life of joy, happiness, freedom, and bliss for all.

**Core Definition IV** (*New Era Human 800 Concepts*, Entry 475, excerpt):

> How does one awaken? If one can correct body and mind, then true essence and true spirit will dwell within, and great talent and great virtue will emerge from within. Without vowing the Great Vehicle aspiration, it is difficult to understand the mysteries of the universe, difficult to awaken. But if one can "embrace the One" and "guard the One," selfless and ego-free, devoting the whole heart and soul to revering the Greatest Creator, revering LIFE, revering Nature, walking the Way of the Greatest Creator, and devoting everything to opening up for humanity a world of "no talent left unrecognized, all under heaven one family," "things left on the road unclaimed, doors not bolted at night," harmonious ten thousand things, timely rains — making everyone joyful, happy, free, and blissful — then the lamp of the heart grows ever brighter, the consciousness ever clearer, and in time one will awaken.
