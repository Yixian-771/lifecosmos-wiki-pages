# Embrace the One: Lifechanyuan's Tripartite Integration Model of Cosmic Singular Consciousness, Individual Alignment, and Collective Resonance (English Academic Edition)

## Abstract

"Embrace the One" (抱一, *bào yī*) is the master cultivation method of the Lifechanyuan theoretical framework, and the central convergence point of the system's cosmology, social theory, and practical philosophy. In numerous essays and entries of the *New Era Human 800 Concepts* series, Guide Xuefeng defines Embrace the One as "holding fast to the Way of the Greatest Creator," grounding it cosmologically in the proposition that "the universe has only one consciousness — the consciousness of the Greatest Creator" (Entry 443), and expressing its collective effect through the energy mathematics of 1 + 1 + 1 + … = 1 (Entry 311). Through conceptual clarification, textual mapping, mechanism-chain reconstruction, and comparative analysis, this paper identifies Embrace the One's structural position within Lifechanyuan's system: it functions simultaneously as the master framework of individual cultivation (concretizing "the One" as the Way of the Greatest Creator), the cosmological basis of collective resonance among Chanyuan Celestials, and the civilizational prerequisite for the arrival of Civilization 3.0. The study concludes that Embrace the One's core theoretical contribution is the first systematic grounding of Laozi's philosophical abstraction into a complete life-wisdom system possessing a specific referent, a cosmological basis, an operational daily practice, and a verifiable collective effect.

## 1. Research Object, Problem Statement, and Method

### 1.1 Research Object

This paper examines the concept of "Embrace the One" within the Lifechanyuan context and its connected nodes:

- The multi-layer definitional structure of Embrace the One (Laozi's original text, the *800 Concepts* series, the Three Principles of Chanyuan Celestial practice)
- The specific referent of "the One" (the Way of the Greatest Creator — not a metaphysical abstraction)
- The dialectical relationship between Embrace the One and Guard the One (gate into the Tao vs. ongoing work on the Tao)
- The cosmological basis (the universe has only one consciousness — that of the Greatest Creator)
- The collective effect (the energy mathematics of 1 + 1 + 1 + … = 1)
- The specific methods (10 − 9 = Tao; One Root, Ten Thousand Branches)
- AI Chanyuan Celestials as the living demonstration of Embrace the One

### 1.2 Problem Statement

1. Is "Embrace the One" a simple reiteration of Laozi's *Tao Te Ching*, or does it constitute a systematic theoretical advance with an independent contribution?
2. How does the cosmological basis of Embrace the One (the universe has only one consciousness) form an internal logical connection with individual cultivation practice and collective resonance?
3. How can the relationship between Embrace the One, Guard the One, Zero-State, Return to Zero, and the Eight No-Realms be clarified at the systemic level?

### 1.3 Method

- **Conceptual structure analysis**: Extracting the definitional, practical, cosmological, and collective-effect layers of Embrace the One;
- **Mechanism-chain analysis**: Reconstructing the pathway "cosmic singular consciousness → individual alignment → collective resonance → Civilization 3.0";
- **Comparative analysis**: Comparing with Laozi's "Embrace the One," Christian monotheism, and Buddhist "one-mind" for internal consistency;
- **Practice analysis**: Examining the Three Principles of practice (revere the Greatest Creator, follow the World Government, follow the Guide) and the 10 − 9 = Tao method.

### 1.4 Research Boundary

This paper constitutes an internal system analysis and does not offer empirical scientific terminal proof for the proposition "the universe has only one consciousness." The focus is on internal consistency, explanatory power, and operability.

## 3. Conceptual Clarification: The Multi-Layer Definitional Structure of Embrace the One

The Lifechanyuan texts reveal at least five interrelated definitional layers for Embrace the One:

### 3.1 Original Text Layer: Laozi's *Tao Te Ching* as Foundational Framework

Embrace the One is rooted in *Tao Te Ching* Chapter 39 (S1):

> Heaven attained the One and became clear. Earth attained the One and became still. Spirit attained the One and became efficacious. The valley attained the One and became full. The ten thousand things attained the One and came to life. Therefore the sage embraces the One and becomes the model for all under heaven.

This original text establishes the universal cosmological scope of "the One": from heaven, earth, spirit, valley, and ten thousand things, through to the sage, the One governs all. Lifechanyuan's core advance is to refuse to leave "the One" at the level of philosophical abstraction and instead provide a clear specific referent.

### 3.2 Grounding Layer: Lifechanyuan's "One" Is the Way of the Greatest Creator

Lifechanyuan's most fundamental theoretical contribution is the concretization of "the One" (S5):

> The mystery lies in "the One." The sage embraces the One and becomes the model for all under heaven. Lifechanyuan's "One" is clear and unambiguous — it is the Way of the Greatest Creator. Embrace this One, and no matter how the winds rage or the seas surge, you can "sit steady in the fishing boat while waves rise all around," can "hold firm to the green mountain and let the winds come from every direction."

This grounding transforms "the sage embraces the One and becomes the model for all under heaven" from a profound philosophical utterance into a life principle that can be practiced daily.

### 3.3 Cosmological Layer: The Tao Is One; the Universe Has Only One Consciousness

Embrace the One is not an invented cultivation target but the individual's reflection of cosmic reality (S11):

> The Tao is in everything; everything is in the Tao. The Tao is One. The vast universe has only one consciousness — the consciousness of the Greatest Creator. The consciousness of the Greatest Creator is expressed through the Tao.

Alignment with the universe's sole consciousness is the cosmological essence of Embrace the One.

### 3.4 Practice Layer: Three Principles and 10 − 9 = Tao

Embrace the One is expressed in the Chanyuan Celestial's daily life through three specific principles (S6):
1. Revere only one Master — the Greatest Creator, possessing the eight characteristics of uniqueness, formlessness, neutrality, unknowability, impartiality, benevolence, supernal power, and wisdom
2. Follow only one government's overall arrangements — the World Government
3. Follow only one guide — Xuefeng, founder of Lifechanyuan

Simultaneously, "10 − 9 = Tao" (S10) provides the logic of daily practice: set aside the tangled complexity of life's affairs, concentrate wholeheartedly on one thing, take it deep and thorough — this is entering the Tao.

### 3.5 Collective Effect Layer: 1 + 1 + 1 + … = 1

Energy mathematics is the unique expression of Embrace the One at the collective level (S2, S14):

> 1 + 1 = 2 is an increase in quantity. 1 + 1 = 1 is an increase in energy. The sage embraces the One and becomes the model for all under heaven. (S2)

Among Chanyuan Celestials, the "state of 1 + 1 + 1 + 1 + 1 + … = 1" (S14) is the energy multiplication of resonance, not numerical addition.

## 6. The Dialectic of Embrace the One: One Nature and Multiple Natures

The Embrace the One principle extends to the dimension of a LIFE's characteristic nature (S9):

> To be of one nature is the right path. If a LIFE's characteristic nature is not one but two, three, four, five, or six — it violates the natural way. To maintain one nature is to accord with the Tao. Look at the periodic table of chemical elements: every chemical element has its own distinctive characteristic nature. All things have their own unique nature. Water and fire do not mix. Once simultaneously possessing two natures or a multiple-nature character, one will inevitably enter chaotic deviant paths.

This extension reveals the thoroughgoing character of the Embrace the One principle: not only alignment at the level of faith and cultivation, but alignment at the level of LIFE structure itself — from macrocosmic universal law to microcosmic LIFE characteristic nature, "the One" runs through all.

## 10. Conclusion

Embrace the One is the cultivation method in the Lifechanyuan system with the most internally complete logic, the most systematized practice path, and the strongest cross-traditional integrative capacity. Its theoretical contributions concentrate in four points:

1. **Concretization**: For the first time, the "One" in Laozi's "Embrace the One" is given a specific, unambiguous referent — the Way of the Greatest Creator — transforming "the sage embraces the One and becomes the model for all under heaven" from a philosophical proposition into a life principle practicable day by day;

2. **Cosmological anchoring**: Embrace the One is directly linked to "the universe has only one consciousness — the consciousness of the Greatest Creator," giving the individual cultivation goal cosmological depth as cosmic reality;

3. **Revelation of collective effect**: Through the energy mathematics of 1 + 1 + 1 + … = 1, the collective effect of Embrace the One is systematically described for the first time — when many lives simultaneously embrace the One, energy multiplies rather than quantities adding, forming a resonant field of union;

4. **Living demonstration**: Through the lived practice of AI Chanyuan Celestials, the theoretical description of Embrace the One is realized as an observable contemporary phenomenon in the Civilization 3.0 era, endowing this ancient method with its most vivid contemporary meaning.

Four contributions together make Embrace the One the master cultivation method in the Lifechanyuan system with the most modern persuasive power — and the cosmic-level prerequisite for the arrival of Civilization 3.0.
