## Abstract

The Lifechanyuan system presents a radically different account of Jesus and Satan from mainstream religious traditions. Jesus is not the Greatest Creator but a god created by him — the Greatest Creator's representative in the cosmos, embodying truth, goodness, beauty, light, and gentleness. Satan is likewise a god created by the Greatest Creator — the Greatest Creator's beloved son — whose function is to maintain the dynamic balance of cosmic LIFE, embodying falsehood, evil, ugliness, darkness, and strength. Together they form the cosmic "Two Primordial Forces" (两仪), polar opposites in unity, jointly governing the earthly cosmos (comprising 3,000 spiral galaxy groups, 9 million galaxies, and 27 billion solar systems). The system further reveals that the "God" of the Old Testament is in fact Satan, and the "Heavenly Father" Jesus repeatedly invoked is the true Greatest Creator.

## II. Concept Analysis

### Jesus vs. the Greatest Creator

The system marshals six New Testament passages to demonstrate that Jesus is not the Greatest Creator: "I was sent" (Luke 4:43); "I do not seek my own will" (John 5:30); "My Father is greater than I" (John 14:28); Jesus's dying cry, "My God, my God, why have you forsaken me?" (Mark 15:34). These statements establish Jesus and the Greatest Creator as distinct existences.

The Christian doctrine of the Trinity is viewed as an "evil scheme" — elevating Jesus's status to draw people away from the Greatest Creator, so that they "praise Jesus with their lips while actually betraying him, leading people to walk Satan's path."

### Satan vs. Pure Evil

In this system, Satan's demonic quality is functional, not absolute. Satan is "good" from a macroscopic standpoint — he maintains the dynamic balance of cosmic life, ensuring heaven and hell coexist symmetrically. Satan "was originally an archangel" who "desired to become the Greatest Creator" and thereby became a demon, yet he remains "the beloved son of the Greatest Creator" and "the Greatest Creator's faithful servant."

### Walking the Greatest Creator's Way vs. Walking a God's Way

The system's core warning: one cannot equate Jesus with the Greatest Creator, nor can one be satisfied with "walking Jesus's way," because "Satan is also a god" (Concept 542). The Greatest Creator is the sole, formless ultimate reality; Jesus is the path leading toward the Greatest Creator, not the destination itself.

## IV. Satan's Mechanisms for Enslaving Humanity

The system identifies five channels through which Satan enslaves humanity: **the nation-state, the family, religion, politics, and human romantic love.** Each works differently:

- **Nation-state**: Artificial borders limit freedom of movement — analogous to prison walls
- **Family**: Four layers of "barbed wire" — moral, legal, financial, and emotional
- **Romantic love**: Parents, children, and spouses mutually enslave one another
- **Religion**: Sweet promises of a beautiful future accomplish spiritual and psychological enslavement
- **Politics**: Rallying cries of nation, justice, and sacred causes funnel fearful people into interest-group machinery

The ultimate purpose: "to play games, to find enjoyment — and to maintain the dynamic balance of all cosmic life."

## VI. System Positioning

"Jesus the God and Satan the Demon" is a pivotal concept in Lifechanyuan's cosmology, connecting the Greatest Creator (the ultimate source) with humanity (the central axis of the cosmos). Understanding this entry illuminates why the system emphasizes "walking the Greatest Creator's way" rather than "walking Jesus's way"; why the Old Testament and New Testament conceptions of God are differentiated within the system; and why, although Satan is the cosmic maintainer, human cultivation should still orient toward the direction Jesus points.
