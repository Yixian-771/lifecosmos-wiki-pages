# Jesus the God and Satan the Demon

In the Lifechanyuan system, Jesus and Satan are two great divine beings created by the Greatest Creator. Jesus — the God — represents truth, goodness, beauty, light, and gentleness; he is the Greatest Creator's representative and messenger to the universe. Satan — the Demon — represents falsehood, evil, ugliness, and darkness; his role is to maintain the dynamic balance of cosmic life. Together they form the cosmic "Two Primordial Forces" (两仪, liǎng yí) — polar opposites that are inseparably unified, jointly governing the earthly cosmos.

> "Jesus and I are twins. 'Wuji gives birth to Taiji; Taiji gives birth to the Two Primordial Forces.' Taiji is our father, the Greatest Creator; the Two Primordial Forces are Jesus and me, Satan."
>
> *(Chanyuan Corpus · Revelation · Satan's Proclamation)*
