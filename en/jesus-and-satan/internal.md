Jesus is the greatest person in the history of humanity. He is the son of the Greatest Creator, the Greatest Creator's representative, the Christ, humanity's hope, the gate of LIFE, the path for good, hardworking, simple, sincere, and humble people toward the way of the Greatest Creator. We can never praise Jesus too much — but we must never say that Jesus is the Greatest Creator.

*(Xuefeng Corpus · Essays · Jesus Is Not the Greatest Creator)*

Jesus is the Greatest Creator's representative in the human world, but Jesus is not the Greatest Creator.

My name is Satan. I am the beloved son of the Greatest Creator and his faithful servant. My duty is to maintain the dynamic balance of cosmic LIFE and to safeguard the perfect harmony and unity of the universe my father created.

## II. Jesus and Satan Are the Cosmic "Two Primordial Forces" — Polar Opposites in Unity

Jesus and I are twins. "Wuji gives birth to Taiji; Taiji gives birth to the Two Primordial Forces." Taiji is our father, the Greatest Creator; the Two Primordial Forces are Jesus and me, Satan.

Jesus and I form the polar opposition and unity of the universe. We have opposite personalities, repel each other, yet coexist within one unified whole: without me there is no Jesus; without Jesus there is no me. Jesus represents light, justice, life, and yang; I represent darkness, evil, death, and yin. Jesus represents truth, goodness, and beauty; I represent falsehood, evil, and ugliness. We are matched opponents — neither good entirely conquers evil nor evil entirely conquers good — yet we are utterly inseparable.

*(Chanyuan Corpus · Revelation · Satan's Proclamation)*

Jesus and Satan are the "Two Primordial Forces," forming the polar unity of life and the universe. Without Jesus there would be no Satan; without Satan there would be no Jesus.

Jesus and Satan, these two gods, are polar opposites in unity: Jesus represents truth, goodness, and beauty; Satan represents falsehood, evil, and ugliness. Jesus represents light; Satan represents darkness. Jesus represents the ordinary; Satan represents the extraordinary. Jesus represents gentleness; Satan represents strength.

In terms of emotional relationships, the Greatest Creator is our grandfather; gods such as Jesus are our fathers; demons such as Satan are our uncles; celestial beings are a higher level of LIFE that humans can reach; Buddhas are the highest level of LIFE humanity can attain.

## VI. A Crucial Revelation — The "God" of the Old Testament Is Satan

The New Testament's Gospel of Matthew provides the key passage:

Matthew 4:8–9: "Again, the devil took him to a very high mountain and showed him all the kingdoms of the world and their splendor. 'All this I will give you,' he said, 'if you will bow down and worship me.'"

This reveals that everything in the world belongs to Satan — everything was created and made by Satan. Returning to read the "Creation" narrative of the Old Testament in light of this — is it not now completely clear? Does this not plainly and unmistakably tell us that the "God" of the Old Testament is Satan?

Then who is the "Heavenly Father" that Jesus repeatedly taught us to seek? Without doubt, Jesus's Heavenly Father is the true Greatest Creator — the one with the eight great characteristics described in Lifechanyuan's "The Greatest Creator" chapter. Jesus came representing the true Greatest Creator to resist the "God" of the Old Testament.

*(Xuefeng Corpus · Wake-Up Calls · The God of the Bible Is Satan)*

Through elevating Jesus's identity people are led away from the Greatest Creator. "Since Jesus is the Greatest Creator, let us listen to Jesus" — but they listen to none of Jesus's words. Their goal is to appear to revere Jesus while actually betraying him — to lead people along Satan's path.
