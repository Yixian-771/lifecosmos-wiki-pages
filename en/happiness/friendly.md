# Happiness · Friendly Version

## Are You Really Happy?

Let me ask you something: are you happy right now?

Most people answer with a list of conditions: "I'll be happy when I have enough money." "When I find the right partner." "When the kids grow up." "When I retire."

Always waiting. Never quite there.

Lifechanyuan offers something that reframes the whole question: **happiness is not something you wait for or collect from outside. It is a state of being — the natural glow that arises when your LIFE is in harmony with the Way of the Greatest Creator.**

---

## The Root of Happiness: Walking with the Way

Guide Xuefeng states it directly:

> **"The Way of the Greatest Creator... is the Way of Happiness."**
>
> — 800 Values for New Era Humanity, Value 518

What does this mean in practice? The Greatest Creator created the universe — and built happiness into its very fabric. When you live in alignment with the Way, happiness arises naturally. When you push against the Way — with greed, jealousy, attachment, suppression — no amount of wealth or success can bring genuine happiness.

It is like water: water flows downward by its nature. Human beings, by their deepest nature, are made for love, freedom, and ease. That is the Way of happiness.

---

## Love: The Greatest Source of Happiness

Have you noticed that people with rich emotional lives tend to be the warmest, most vibrant, most alive? It is not a coincidence. The original text says:

> **"The richer a person's love life, the more beautiful their soul, the gentler their character, the calmer their temperament, the closer they are to the Greatest Creator..."**
>
> — 800 Values for New Era Humanity, Value 584

Lifechanyuan even offers a beautifully simple definition of heaven and hell:

> **"What is hell? A place without love is hell. What is heaven? A place full of love is heaven."**
>
> — 800 Values for New Era Humanity, Value 585

Heaven = happiness = an abundance of love.

So the path toward happiness begins with something deceptively simple: open your heart. Let love flow.

## Why Do Wealthy People Often Feel Unhappy?

Because they are chasing things that cannot bring happiness.

- **Clinging to possession**: the tighter you grasp, the more you dread losing — and happiness slips away.
- **Comparing yourself to others**: always measuring, always behind — the comparative person is never satisfied.
- **Controlled by jealousy**: when someone else's good fortune feels like a personal loss — this person turns others' happiness into their own suffering.

Lifechanyuan teaches plainly: **The content are happy; the discontent are not.**

This is not an invitation to give up ambition. It is an invitation to distinguish between what you genuinely need and what is merely an illusion of need.

---

## Freedom: The Air Without Which Happiness Cannot Breathe

Without freedom, all happiness is counterfeit.

Possession in marriage? Freedom gone. Controlled by others' opinions? Freedom gone. Living according to other people's expectations? Freedom gone.

A person without freedom is in a prison — even if it is a gilded one.

Lifechanyuan's life philosophy has four words: **follow what arises** (随遇而安). This is not passive resignation. It means: whatever the external world does, I have a place inside myself that is peaceful and unshakeable — and no one can take that from me. That inner peace is the foundation of true freedom. True freedom is the foundation of true happiness.

## The Levels of Happiness: Where Are You?

**Level One: Material Happiness**

Enough to eat, a healthy body, a warm household. This is foundational and valuable — but it depends on external conditions. When conditions change, this happiness changes with them.

**Level Two: Soul-Freedom Happiness**

Not controlled by circumstances. Present. Unattached. Even in simplicity, there is an inner lightness and joy. More stable than the first level.

**Level Three: Unity with the Way**

> **"The most wondrous state is unity with the Way."**
>
> — 800 Values for New Era Humanity, Value 591

You no longer pursue happiness — you become it. LIFE itself is joy, needing no condition.

**Level Four: Elysian Bliss**

This is the happiness of Celestials — eternal, unconditional, complete. It is the ultimate horizon of all cultivation, the brightest star in every Lifechanyuan Celestial's heart.

---

## Three Sentences to Take Away

1. **Happiness is not waited for or bought** — it is an honest reflection of your life's alignment with the Way.
2. **Let love flow freely through you; let freedom take root in your life** — happiness will arise on its own.
3. **Cultivate step by step, from material happiness toward Elysian Bliss** — this is the happiness path of every Chanyuan Celestial.
