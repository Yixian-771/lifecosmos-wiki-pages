## I. Eight Signs of Female Awakening

**Chanyuan Corpus · Life Treasures · Eight Signs of Female Awakening**

1. No longer depending on men for survival, including no longer leaning on power or status.

2. No longer depending on family for survival, but instead entering the Second Home.

3. No longer pursuing or displaying hardness, taking softness and grace as the motto of life.

4. No longer bound to one relationship for life — flowing freely, following circumstances, nature, and opportunity, light and unattached.

5. Neither cutting off emotions nor drowning in them — letting feeling dwell in stars, mountains, rivers, sun, and moon, wherever there is affinity.

6. Not measuring human worth by wealth or status, but defining character by inner quality.

7. Not competing in vanity through clothing and jewelry, but revealing beauty through cleanliness and neatness.

8. Seeking only joy, happiness, freedom, and well-being — casting off every fetter and shackle.

## II. Heaven and Earth Reversed — Celestial Women Are Supreme

**Xuefeng Anthology · Warning to the World · Heaven and Earth Reversed — A New Lifechanyuan Principle**

The Confucian tradition holds "heaven is noble, earth is lowly; men are noble, women are lowly." In Lifechanyuan this is reversed: the male Celestials (xianzi) are the green leaves; the female Celestials (xiannü) are the flower's heart. Above and below, Celestial Women are supreme.

Reasons:

I. The source is supreme. The source of life is the Greatest Creator; the universe reveres the Greatest Creator.

II. Women are supreme. The Greatest Creator created life; thereafter life reproduces of itself — all born from women. Therefore women are supreme.

III. Mothers are supreme. Ten months of pregnancy, one moment of birth — life emerges from mothers. Mothers deserve first respect; therefore women are supreme.

IV. Sisters and daughters are all women. We must protect them; therefore women are supreme.

V. Women are physically less strong than men and more vulnerable to attack. For the sake of balance and equality, we uphold women as supreme.

VI. "The valley spirit never dies; it is called the mysterious female. The gateway of the mysterious female is called the root of heaven and earth." The mysterious female is the mother's generative organ — "the root of heaven" — and must be revered. Therefore women are supreme.

VII. Buddha is nature; nature is Buddha; the gate of nature is the gate of Buddha. Only women have this gate. To revere Buddha is to revere women.

VIII. Women are graceful, still, peace-loving, warm, naturally loving and kind, broad and gentle of heart. For these qualities they deserve reverence.

In Lifechanyuan, in every situation, the Celestial Women are considered first. Celestial Women are the lotus blossoms; male Celestials are the protective green leaves.

Rules:
1. Male Celestials must not compete with Celestial Women.
2. Male Celestials must be willing to sacrifice for Celestial Women.
3. Only Celestial Women may initiate romantic teasing; male Celestials may not.
4. When danger appears, male Celestials step forward; Celestial Women stand behind.
5. When enjoyment is at hand, Celestial Women go first; male Celestials follow.
6. Heavy, dirty, and demanding work — male Celestials claim it first. Light, delicate, and clean work — Celestial Women have priority.
7. Male Celestials must never insult, demean, scold, strike, abuse, coerce, belittle, or mock Celestial Women.
8. Male Celestials must never use the name of love to restrict a Celestial Woman's personal freedom, mental freedom, or spiritual freedom.

## III. Female Awakening and Human Civilization's Leap (Poetry Excerpt)

**Xuefeng Anthology · Poetry · Cao Ni Lianhua Huasheng Da Zizai (excerpt)**

The suffering of millennia continues to this day,

Because women have not yet awakened.

Marriage and family have smothered their celestial nature and Buddha-nature,

Household chores have consumed the lives of countless gifted women.

When women confine their vision to marriage and family,

Men become the appendages of women,

And the bonds and strangulation carried out in the name of love

Keep both women and men cycling through a bottomless pit.

You raise high the banner of liberating women,

And will awaken multitudes of sleeping women.

When women awaken from the sleep of marriage and family,

Human civilization will make a great leap forward.

## IV. New Era Eight Hundred Concepts — Entries on Women

**New Era Eight Hundred Concepts, 4th Edition · Entry 178**

Men find beauty in strength; women find beauty in softness. A man who is overly soft becomes unattractive; a woman who is overly hard becomes unattractive.

**New Era Eight Hundred Concepts, 4th Edition · Entry 599**

Women find beauty in softness. Softness overcomes hardness. Soft beauty is not weakness, not absence of conviction, not willingness to accept anyone, not submission to humiliation — quite the contrary, soft beauty is a form of ultimate strength. It is hardness contained within softness. The tenderness of water can wash away all impurities, pierce through stone, and conquer all things.
