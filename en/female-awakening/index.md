# Female Awakening

**Female Awakening** (*Nüxing Juexing*), in the Lifechanyuan system, refers to women's liberation from the dependent role defined by traditional marriage and family, a return to their innate nature and Buddha-nature, and the pursuit of a free, graceful, and spiritually elevated life. Guide Xuefeng teaches that one root cause of millennia of human suffering is that "women have not yet awakened" — marriage and family have smothered their celestial nature, and domestic labor has consumed the lives of countless talented women. Once awakened, a woman sheds all fetters, no longer depending on men or family, embracing softness as her life's maxim, following her nature freely and joyfully. Lifechanyuan also upholds the principle of *Qiankun Diandao, Xiannü Wei Zun* ("Heaven and Earth reversed — Celestial Women are supreme"), providing institutional respect for women's dignity and freedom.

> Because women have not yet awakened, marriage and family have smothered their celestial nature and Buddha-nature, household chores have consumed countless gifted women… When women awaken from the sleep of marriage and family, human civilization will make a great leap forward.
>
> — *Xuefeng Anthology · Poetry · Cao Ni Lianhua Huasheng Da Zizai* (excerpt)
