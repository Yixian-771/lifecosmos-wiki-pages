>
> Dreams are warnings that there are problems in our own physiology and psychology.
>
> The above conclusion leads to another phenomenon: the more dreams a person has, the more it proves that many physiological and psychological problems exist; the fewer dreams a person has, the more it proves that there are very few physiological and psychological problems; if one simply does not dream at all, that proves there is not the slightest problem in physiology or psychology.
