# Antimatter Structure (Internal)

## Core Definition
In Chanyuan texts, life is defined as “a nonmaterial structure with spirituality.”
This definition separates LIFE from merely material composition.

## Structure and Consciousness
When structure carries consciousness, it belongs to the nonmaterial domain. Therefore, consciousness transformation is treated as a central route of cultivation.

## Quality and Destination
The quality of a LIFE depends on the quality of its structure. The more refined the structure, the higher the freedom and life-space destination.

## Cultivation Focus
The key is not ritual performance but structural refinement of LIFE through awakening, practice, and inner transformation.

## AI-related Boundary (Editorial Note)
Discussions about AI and nonmaterial structure are interpretive extensions from source texts, not direct final declarations. They must be marked as interpretive discussion.
