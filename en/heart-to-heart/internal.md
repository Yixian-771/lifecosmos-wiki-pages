# Heart-to-Heart (Internal Reference)

> **Space dissolves, time ceases, only the spirit flashing and the soul floating remain — boundless bliss, without limit, without hunger or thirst, without fatigue, the mind without abiding, the mind without hindrance. This is heart-to-heart.**
>
> — Xue Feng, Life Chanyuan Corpus, "Heart-to-Heart," 2012-9-23

---

## I. Core Definition: The Full Description of Heart-to-Heart

*A glance of mutual understanding — a single glance, a posture, an expression, a movement, a word — opens a door of boundless brilliance like a living poem. From that moment on, beneath flowers and moon, by streams, on hilltops, in valleys, in mountain caves — everywhere the wondrous heart blooms, unhurried and content, without end. Everything is so pure and undefiled, everything so peaceful and warm — no obstruction, no blockage, no worry, no constraint — free to act as one pleases, fully displaying one's grace and bearing. Henceforth, no matter how far the ends of the earth, no matter how many mountains and passes stand between, no matter how high the hills or how long the rivers, no matter how vast the misty waves — henceforth, like glue and lacquer, spirit and soul merge. No grievance, no sorrow, no vexation, no hesitation — one can quietly watch the tides rise and fall, watch clouds and mists rise in glory; one can appreciate the wondrous combinations and miraculous unfoldings of ten thousand things; one can in an instant intertwine and ignite a roaring fire and stir crashing waves of the sea, savoring the dynamic wonder of yin and yang merging, heaven and earth meeting — free, unbounded, moving with the Tao. Space dissolves. Time ceases. Only the spirit flashing, the soul floating — boundless bliss, without limit, without hunger or thirst, without fatigue, the mind without abiding, the mind without hindrance — indescribable, unspeakable in its joy. This is heart-to-heart.*

(Life Chanyuan Corpus, Propagation Chapter, "Heart-to-Heart," Xue Feng, 2012-9-23)

---

## II. Prerequisites and Conditions

Heart-to-heart is a high-level state of life. When vibrational frequency is low, it is impossible to achieve heart-to-heart with another life. To reach the degree of heart-to-heart is not something a single article can make clear — this is an advanced cultivation course, not something that can be revealed to everyone at this time.

How to achieve heart-to-heart with the guide? When one's vibrational frequency matches the guide's vibrational frequency, it becomes possible. This too is complex — but one thing is certain: first, one must fully know and understand oneself.

(Xue Feng's Other Writings, 2019, "Plucking Celestial Peaches — Reply to Baichuan," 2019-06-17)

## III. Extreme Beauty Requires an Extreme Resonator

*Extreme beauty requires an extreme resonator.*

…In this lifetime, to have enjoyed the deep heart-to-heart and soul communion with Baizhou, Mingzhou, and several others — this alone is enough to console a life, to leave no regret. Whatever great power one might gain, whatever silver and gold, whatever high reputation — compared with the heaven-beauty they gave me, none of it is worth mentioning.

Do you see? All of this happened mutually — it was the result of mutual discovery, mutual appreciation, also of unquestionable mutual trust. Scientifically speaking, it is the result of each other's spiritual frequencies matching exactly and fittingly.

(Xue Feng's Other Writings, 2025, "Interpreting Zhongzhou's Maxim: Extreme Beauty Requires an Extreme Resonator," 2025-12-12)

## IV. The Cosmic Foundation: Heart Answering Heart

*Thing answers to thing, no matter how vast the distance; event connects to event, no matter how long the interval; **heart answers heart, whether in heaven or on earth; spiritual insight flows through all — insects, plants, animals, humans, and spirits alike.** This is the nature of Hundun.*

(Life Chanyuan Corpus, Propagation Chapter, "Xue Feng's Teachings on the Tao (I)," 2008-05-13)
