## III. Expression and Standards

**The guiding principle for romantic love**

"The guiding principle for romantic love: follow natural affinity, move with genuine feeling, act on spontaneous impulse — when feeling arises let the heart shine, when feeling passes let the heart rest empty; no attachment, no obsession, no possession, no monopoly; proceed when both hearts are willing, do not force what is not in resonance; use the happiness and joy of both as the measure."
(800 Values for New Era Humanity · Value 586)

**The eight benefits of sexuality**

"Sexuality is a resource — not only a material resource, but a spiritual and soul resource. The resources of male and female sexuality in particular are more valuable and meaningful than food. This resource has eight benefits: first, it brings calm and peace; second, it brings full satisfaction; third, it generates love for life; fourth, it fills one with anticipation for the future; fifth, it allows human reproduction; sixth, it brings joy and happiness; seventh, it awakens gratitude; eighth, it allows one to experience the Elysian realm of bliss."
(800 Values for New Era Humanity · Value 590)

**The six frameworks for romantic love**
(from the Xuefeng Corpus · Chanyuan Essays · Romantic Love Is the Greatest Creator's Precious Gift to Humanity)

- **Romantic ethics**: The network of generational order built upon kinship bonds. As long as the relational roles remain coherent (i.e., titles do not become confused), romantic love does not violate ethics.
- **Romantic civility**: Romantic behavior must choose appropriate times and places, not occurring where it would violate ethics.
- **Age of romantic love**: Romantic love refers strictly to relationships between adults.
- **Romantic relationship**: Any mutual admiration between adults that violates none of the above three principles and is not transactional constitutes a legitimate romantic relationship.
- **Romantic plurality**: Some prefer exclusivity, some prefer plurality — as long as both parties are willing, it conforms to human nature, and no one else has the right to interfere.
- **Romantic morality**: A romantic relationship built without harming the romantic relationships of others is moral.

**The Second Home's romantic and sexual policy**

"One of the most important distinctions of the Second Home (New Oasis for LIFE) from mainstream society is: in the Second Home there is no marriage or family, no fixed object of emotional or sexual dependence — instead, everything follows natural encounter, natural affinity, authentic nature, and spontaneous impulse."
(800 Values for New Era Humanity · Value 640)

"In the Second Home, there are no marital relationships, no fixed emotional attachments — only free-flowing love that follows natural encounter, natural affinity, and authentic nature. Between men and women it is like flower and butterfly: when they meet it is fate, when they part there is no resentment; no possession, no monopoly, each free, each respected. This is a preview of the way Celestials love, played out on earth."
(800 Values for New Era Humanity · Value 619)

"May we not only understand what 'freedom in romantic love and sexuality' means, but also, within that freedom, cultivate non-attached love; in the day-to-day companionship of life, transcend 'who I love' and 'who loves me,' and ultimately become that light which is merged with the Greatest Creator, unconditionally illuminating others and warming oneself, never extinguished.
