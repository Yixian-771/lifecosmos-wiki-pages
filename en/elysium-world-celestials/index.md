# Super Celestial Beings of the Elysium World

In the Lifechanyuan system, **Super Celestial Beings of the Elysium World** (*tiān xiān* 天仙) represent the pinnacle of the five-tier celestial hierarchy — the highest state any human being can reach through cultivation. Their home is the **Celestial Islands Continent** within the Elysium World: a galaxy of approximately 80 billion islands, each roughly the size of Earth, each home to one Super Celestial Being. They are, in the words of the source texts, "the most free, the most joyful, and the most blessed beings in the entire universe" — bearing no responsibilities, fulfilling no obligations, their sole purpose being self-generated delight. They possess 64 supernatural abilities and were created directly by the Greatest Creator.
