# Feathered Transformation into a Celestial Being (Yuhua Chengxian / 羽化成仙)

**Feathered Transformation** (*yǔhuà chéngxiān* 羽化成仙) is a classical Taoist term for the completion of cultivation: the practitioner, having attained the Tao, becomes light as a feather and ascends into the celestial realm. In the Lifechanyuan system, this ancient concept receives a physics-based reinterpretation: **when the vibrational frequency of a person's body, mind, and spirit reaches a sufficiently high level, the life-structure moves beyond the perceptual range of the human senses, "disappearing" from the three-dimensional material world and entering a higher-density, higher-frequency life space.** This is not death or annihilation — it is a dimensional leap into a domain that was always co-present but invisible. The path runs: **raising the vibrational frequency** → **entering the empty-luminous state** (*kōnglíng zhuàngtài*) → **merging with the universe, entering higher life spaces**.
