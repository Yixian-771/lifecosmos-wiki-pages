# Everything Is Destined: Accepting Your Fate Is the Highest Wisdom

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological
> essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across
> incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage
> of human existence in this world.

Have you ever wondered why some people are born into wealth while others struggle all their lives? Why some things never work out no matter how hard you try? Why two twins raised in completely separate families end up with wives of the same name, dogs of the same name, and cars of the same color?

Lifechanyuan offers an answer that is both stunning and profoundly liberating: **Everything is destined.**

---

## The Universe Is a Grand Script

The universe is not chaotic or random — it is a precisely arranged grand script.

Every LIFE — including you — is a character in this script.

The script has already written:
- When you are born, when you die, and how
- Who you will meet, love, and quarrel with
- How much money you'll have, what illnesses you'll get, what work you'll do
- Whether you'll be an official or a commoner, rich or poor, male or female

**None of these were decided by you. This is the trajectory — this is fate.**

> Everything in the universe has a trajectory, everything moves according to an arranged trajectory, and human beings are no exception.
> — *Chanyuan Corpus · Exploring the Script of Human life*

---

## Who Wrote the Script? Who Is the Director?

Here is Lifechanyuan's most profound revelation:

**The scriptwriter is yourself. The director is the program.**

Your current life is the result of the thoughts, intentions, and actions of your previous lives. It's like planting a tree yesterday — you can't change where it stands today. It's already rooted there.

Everything in this life is a fruit already planted in previous lives — you can only receive it.

But — every seed you plant today will take root in your future lives.

**"The efforts of this life only take effect in the next life."**

---

## Your Only Freedom: Thoughts, and Playing Your Role Well

If the plot is already written, what can a person actually do?

Plenty — and this is precisely the most important thing:

**First: thoughts** — whether you send out good or evil thoughts is entirely up to you. Thoughts become frequency waves that radiate into the universe, which records them and weaves them into your future script.

**Second: the quality of your role performance** — the director has given you a role; you can play it brilliantly or play it badly. Play it well, and the director will cast you in ever better roles. Play it poorly, and you'll be given ever worse roles — or cast in Hell.

This is the entire space of human effort: **not to change the plot, but to play the current role well while keeping thoughts pure and good.**

## Destiny and Karma: No Contradiction At All

Someone might ask: if everything is destined, doesn't "you reap what you sow" lose its meaning?

Not at all!

Imagine a TV drama: the script says the scene calls for one blow to kill a dog. But you hit it twice — the extra blow wasn't in the script; you added it yourself. That extra blow will, in some future life, return to you as a bitter fruit you must taste.

**Beyond what is fixed, there is always the extra blow you add yourself. That is karma.**

## The Twin Study: Stunning Evidence

In 1979, a pair of twins in Ohio, USA — separated at birth and adopted by different families — were reunited at age 39 and discovered:
- Both were named James (Jim)
- Both had first wives named Linda
- Both had second wives named Betty
- Both named their sons James Alan
- Both had childhood dogs named Toy
- Both drove the same model and color Chevrolet
- Both worked in law enforcement/security
- Both vacationed at the same Florida beach every year

This is not a legend — it is a documented historical event. Two people raised in completely different environments walked nearly identical life paths.

**This is the program. This is fate.**

## Accepting Destiny Is Not Giving Up — It Is the Highest Wisdom

Accepting destiny is not lying flat. Accepting destiny means:

- Stop blaming heaven, earth, and other people (everything is the fruit of your own past lives)
- Receive calmly whatever happens in this life
- Focus energy on two things: **think good thoughts + play the current role well**
- Hold gratitude toward the Greatest Creator and Nature

Once you truly accept your destiny, resentment falls away, anxiety falls away, comparison falls away — what remains is clarity, ease, and wholehearted engagement with the present.

> The way is supremely simple — natural, effortless. Accept your fate, follow me, and laugh and joke every day. Why rack your brain and wear yourself out?!
> — Guide Xuefeng

## Two Paths Out of the Kingdom of Necessity

For those who want to break free, Guide Xuefeng points to two routes:

**Path One: Attain Supreme Enlightenment** — understand the whole script. Then you can faithfully follow the script while simultaneously watching your own performance from outside it. Facing the same hardship, the ignorant feel pain; the wise feel it's an interesting game.

**Path Two: Walk the Way of the Greatest Creator, act according to your inner nature** — receive all of the Greatest Creator's arrangements for you with a joyful heart, even when it's hard, even when it hurts. When facing difficult choices, listen to your inner calling, follow your spiritual perception, and don't use your intellect to suppress the prompting of your heart.

Both paths lead to the same destination: **receive whatever comes, follow conditions, act according to your inner nature, respond to circumstances.**
