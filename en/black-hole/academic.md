# Black Hole (Academic Version)

## Abstract

In the Lifechanyuan cosmological system, "black holes" carry a dual significance. At the physical level, they are conscious cosmic guardians created by the Greatest Creator, sustaining the orderly motion of celestial bodies through dynamic equilibrium (absorbing gravity, emitting repulsion). At the life-space level, they are differentiated into two distinct coordinate-world entities: the **Yin-Polar Black Hole Body** (the most energy-dense object in the universe, a prison for divine transgressors, classified among the higher life spaces) and the **Yang-Polar Black Hole Body** (the maternal womb, the reincarnation gateway, classified among the neutral worlds). This entry systematically surveys the definitional framework, Lifechanyuan's distinctive black hole theory, the spatial coordinates of both black hole bodies, and their religio-cosmological significance, drawing on *The Xuefeng Corpus* and the *Chanyuan Corpus*.

## I. Core Propositions of the Lifechanyuan Black Hole Theory

Lifechanyuan's understanding of black holes diverges from mainstream physics in several foundational respects:

**① Black holes are conscious gods**  
Xuefeng defines black holes as "gods created by the Greatest Creator," endowed with consciousness and a designated function: regulating the orbital order of celestial bodies, analogous to traffic officers in a city. This moves entirely beyond the mass-energy framework of physics into the domain of cosmic theology.

**② Black holes are not dark — interior is luminous**  
In contrast to the scientific position that the interior of the event horizon is unknown, Xuefeng asserts unequivocally that the inside of a black hole is "brilliantly radiant." The outer darkness is a perimeter-energy effect; the interior is light. This claim rests on spiritual sensing rather than instrument observation.

**③ Dynamic equilibrium — not "only in, never out"**  
Xuefeng rejects the scientific hypothesis that black holes only absorb and never release. He holds that all things in the universe must maintain dynamic equilibrium. Black holes ingest gravity and expel repulsive force — and it is through this repulsion that they maintain the safe distances between celestial bodies.

**④ The energy-form formula**  
"The greater the energy, the less the form; the lesser the energy, the more the form." This is Xuefeng's original proposition, explaining why black holes are invisible: their extreme energy renders them formless.

## II. Two Black Hole Bodies — Position in the 36-Dimensional Space

The Lifechanyuan system extends the black hole concept into two specific dimensional coordinates among the universe's 20 parallel worlds:

| Name | Coordinate | Life-Space Classification | Functional Definition |
|------|-----------|--------------------------|----------------------|
| Yin-Polar Black Hole Body | (-X)(-Y)Z World | Higher life space | Most energy-dense body; cosmic prison for divine transgressors |
| Yang-Polar Black Hole Body | YZ World | Neutral world | Maternal womb; reincarnating souls' "new abode" |

**The Yin-Polar Black Hole Body** sits within the higher life spaces alongside the Claritas Realm, Heavenly Realm, Elysium World, Ten-Thousand-Year World, and Thousand-Year World — the "heavenly" side of the cosmic symmetry — but plays a unique role as the enforcement mechanism of the heavenly law, specifically for divine beings who have transgressed.

**The Yang-Polar Black Hole Body** belongs to the neutral worlds. It is the critical transit point between the material world (human realm) and the antimatter dimension. A reincarnating soul must pass through the space tunnel and enter the maternal womb (Yang-Polar Black Hole Body) before it fully inhabits a new physical life.

## III. The Galactic Central Black Hole as Cosmic Power System

Qian Kun Cao, writing in the Chanyuan Corpus (Civilization Series), provides a detailed account of the galactic central black hole's dynamic mechanism: matter from the spiral arms is compressed inward to the black hole, ejected from the two poles, reabsorbed by the arms, forming a magnetic-field-like structure encompassing the entire galaxy. This constitutes the Milky Way's power system.

Qian Kun Cao subsequently noted that, upon searching for images online, this inference was confirmed by actual astronomical observation — a rare instance in the Chanyuan Corpus of a spiritually-derived claim receiving post-hoc scientific validation, illustrating the "spiritual precognition, scientific verification" epistemological pattern characteristic of the Lifechanyuan system.

## V. Epistemology — Spiritual Sensing and Scientific Convergence

"I explored the mysteries of the universe with devout religious feeling, while Mr. Hawking explored the secrets of the universe with devout scientific rigor. What is gratifying is that, despite the fundamental difference between religion and science in epistemology and methodology, the conclusions converge — on many points remarkably so."
*(Xuefeng, The Mysteries of the Black Hole)*

Lifechanyuan's knowledge of black holes derives from spiritual sensing (*lingjue*), not instrumental measurement. Xuefeng acknowledges this explicitly and invites scientific verification. This "spiritual sensing first, scientific testing after" stance is a defining epistemological feature of the Lifechanyuan system.
