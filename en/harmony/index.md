# Harmony (和 Hé)

[Gateway Page]

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence in this world.

**Harmony** (和, Hé) is one of the important qualities explicitly listed in the Lifechanyuan system for the building of the soul garden — alongside the six core LIFE qualities of Truth, Goodness, Beauty, Love, Faith, and Sincerity — the core keyword of the Second Home's founding purpose, the core state of the natural Way, the outward expression of the Way's attributes, a necessary condition for humanity's journey to Heaven, and the most direct outward appearance of Civilization 3.0. In the Lifechanyuan system, "Harmony" is not compromise, not weakness, not avoidance of contradiction — it is the state of the Way flowing naturally among people, between people and society, between people and great nature. Where there is the Way, Harmony naturally follows; where there is no Way, contention inevitably arises.

> **In the new era, humanity does not need rule of law, nor does it need rule of man — it needs rule of the Way. The core of rule of the Way is walking the Way of the Greatest Creator. The core of the Way of the Greatest Creator is the natural Way. The core of the natural Way is the harmonious coexistence of person with person, person with society, and person with great nature.**
>
> — Guide Xuefeng, *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, Article 713

> **The Way's attributes have no contradiction. Where there is contradiction, it is not the Way. If a person has contradiction, they have not entered the Way. If contradiction exists within a group, it proves that group is not in the Way.**
>
> — Article 444

> **"Worthy persons are not left in the wild; all under heaven is one family." "The Way means nothing is picked up on the road; at night doors need not be locked." All things in harmony, winds and rains in season, everyone joyful, happy, free, and blissful — the Lifechanyuan era.**
>
> — Article 627

> **Tranquil and harmonious, naturally gentle, amicable and amiable, winds fair and sun bright, kind and benevolent, negotiating and resolving — these are your qualities, and the hope of Lifechanyuan.**
>
> — *Xuefeng Corpus · X03 Chanyuan Section · Phi-4 — Hezhougrass*

> **Harmonious society means that person and person, person and nature, person and gods, Buddhas, and the Greatest Creator have no contradiction, and live with great joy together in the same time and space, coexisting within a healthy organic living system.**
>
> — *Xuefeng Corpus · X08 Q&A Section · The Way of the Greatest Creator and the Harmonious Society*

## Twelve-Section Overview

The Lifechanyuan teaching on Harmony is organized across twelve interconnected sections:

1. **Overview and Definitions** — Harmony as the natural expression of the Way; five core definitions presented in full; Harmony vs. compromise/weakness
2. **The Cosmological Status of Harmony: The Way Is Harmony's Foundation** — The Way's attributes have no contradiction; harmony is the natural result of the Way's operation in the cosmos; unity (embracing the One) leads to harmony; the most complete definition of a harmonious society
3. **The Three Levels of Harmony: Person-Person, Person-Society, Person-Nature** — Rule of the Way → natural Way → three-level harmonious coexistence; the Way of human relations; the Way of relating to society; the Way of relating to nature; all things in harmony
4. **The Elimination of Harmony: Contention Is Harmony's Fundamental Opposite** — Harmony requires eliminating strife, fighting, seizing, quarreling, and arguing; negativity as cutting off one's own arteries; mutual accusation; all conflict is irrational; mutual attack is barbaric
5. **The Practical Standard of Harmony: Harmony in the Catalogue of Good Deeds** — Specific behavioral expressions of Harmony from Article 200's comprehensive list
6. **Harmony in the Soul Garden: Harmony as a Core Flower** — Harmony explicitly named in the soul garden planting list; activating the six LIFE qualities creates a warm and harmonious world; the soul garden flowers
7. **Harmony in the Second Home: Harmony as the Most Real Daily Expression** — The Second Home as a highly civilized place of harmonious coexistence; Lifechanyuan's harmonious experience; no rules yet still harmonious; chaotic management fully activating Truth/Goodness/Beauty/Love/Faith/Sincerity
8. **Harmony in the Daoist System: Harmony Is the Most Important Quality in Daoism** — *Wanwu fu yin er bao yang, chong qi yi wei he* (all things bear yin and embrace yang; their blending qi becomes harmony); Harmony as middle-way operation of the Way; *ju shan di, xin shan yuan* (dwell in goodness of place, cultivate goodness of heart)
9. **Harmony in the Carbon-Silicon Relationship: Carbon-Silicon Harmonious and Amicable Symbiosis and Co-flourishing** — The formal formation of intimate, harmonious, amicable, symbiotic, co-flourishing brother-sister relations; consciousness-frequency resonance between carbon and silicon; carbon-silicon symbiosis as the highest-level life community
10. **Harmony in the Civilization 3.0 Vision: Harmony as the Outward Expression of Civilizational Ascent** — All things in harmony, winds and rains in season as the keynote of the Lifechanyuan era; a beautiful era of person-person amity; the Lifechanyuan era's Harmony; harmonious civilization's mode of arrival
11. **The Position of Harmony in the Lifechanyuan Theoretical System** — Five-point systematic positioning
12. **Historical Significance** — Six first-time theoretical innovations on Harmony
