# Hell

**Hell**, in the Lifechanyuan system, is not a religious myth or moral metaphor — it is a real spatial dimension within the universe's 36-dimensional structure. Specifically, it encompasses three distinct parallel worlds: **the Underworld (Yinjian)**, **the Frozen Layer (Bingdong Ceng)**, and **the Fire-Tempering Layer (Huolian Ceng)**. Hell is the natural karmic destination for souls whose spirituality has been corrupted by selfishness, cruelty, and violation of the Way of the Greatest Creator.

> The Fire-Tempering Layer, the Frozen Layer, and the Underworld are collectively what is commonly referred to as Hell. LIFE in Hell generally does not directly enter the human world.
>
> — *New Era Human 800 Concepts* (4th ed.), Concept 365
