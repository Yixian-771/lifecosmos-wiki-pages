# Life Visa

A Life Visa is the passage credential a soul must carry to enter Heaven — issued in the form of a Chanyuan Celestial name, it is embedded directly into the holder's antimatter life-structure and serves as the essential prerequisite for the soul's arrival in the Millennium World, the Ten-Thousand-Year World, or the Paradise Realm of the Celestial Islands Continent after the body passes away.
