# Life Visa · Internal Edition

## I. Definition and Nature

> "Traveling abroad requires a visa — that is simply how things work. So what about traveling from this world to Heaven? Going to Heaven requires a visa even more — a Life Visa."

> "Lifechanyuan is Heaven's embassy on earth, the institution specifically authorized to issue Life Visas."

> "The visa issued by this 'Heaven's Embassy on Earth' takes the form of stamping a 'Celestial' imprint into the applicant's consciousness. Anyone who receives a Chanyuan Celestial name issued by Lifechanyuan holds a Life Visa to Heaven. Without it, even if you arrive at Heaven's gate, the door will not open for you — you will be denied entry."

(Chanyuan Corpus · Revelation Chapter · Life Visa)

> "The Life Visa is a form of antimatter. It embeds itself directly into the antimatter structure of your life, accelerating your life-structure's evolution toward perfect symmetry, enveloping your life-structure in a halo of seven colors, and guiding your soul along the space tunnel toward the higher realms of LIFE."

(Chanyuan Corpus · Revelation Chapter · Walking Out of Egypt of the Soul)

> "Three piles of firewood symbolize obtaining life itself — but to reach Heaven, a Life Visa is still required."

(Chanyuan Corpus · Revelation Chapter · Life Visa)

> "To achieve a qualitative leap in one's life, a Life Visa is necessary. Those authorized to issue Life Visas are Jesus, Sakyamuni, Muhammad, and Hundun Yuanchu (Xuefeng)."

(Xuefeng Corpus · Chanyuan Chapter · 80 New Concepts of Lifechanyuan)

## III. Conditions for Issuance

> "Where is the soul's true home? The answer is Heaven. And to enter Heaven, one needs a 'Heavenly Visa.'"

(Chanyuan Corpus · Civilization Chapter · The Second Benefit of Soul Purification)

> "A person whose soul is turbid, thinking narrow, and life-quality low is simply incompatible with Heaven. Like a traveler who is entangled in debt, bound by family obligations, and marked by poor conduct — such a person cannot even approach Heaven's gate, let alone obtain a Heavenly Visa."

(Chanyuan Corpus · Civilization Chapter · The Second Benefit of Soul Purification)

> "Through soul purification, a person undergoes fundamental transformation. Spiritual perception gradually awakens — one begins to feel the all-pervasive love of the Greatest Creator and the Buddhas and deities, to understand and resonate with the life-states of Heavenly beings, and to align one's consciousness with Heaven's frequencies.
>
> At this point, the haze of worldly life clears, and the soul's memory stirs. You come to know: you are a being originally from Heaven, temporarily here on earth for cultivation and refinement. You come to know: you are not living for a lifetime of fame, wealth, love, or conflict — but for the elevation of LIFE, for the journey home to Heaven. And you come to know: your mission is to scatter beauty and goodness across the human world. The phrase 'I came from Heaven, and to Heaven I shall return' is no longer a slogan — it becomes a genuine awakening in the depths of your soul.
>
> When you reach this state, you have the standing to apply for a Life Visa."

(Chanyuan Corpus · Civilization Chapter · The Second Benefit of Soul Purification)

> "To obtain a visa to the Millennium World, the Ten-Thousand-Year World, or the Paradise Realm, one must relinquish the self and enter a state of primordial wholeness — not the Confucian suppression of desire, nor the Western ideal of self-actualization, but the release of ego-attachment. If you insist that your own perception is entirely correct and flawless, you will walk confidently toward an ultimate error, fall into illusory thinking, and never find your way out of life's desert."

(New Era Human Eight Hundred Concepts, Fourth Edition · Concept 486)
