# Faith (信 Xìn)

[Gateway Page]

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence in this world.

**Faith** (信, Xìn) is one of the eight core LIFE qualities in the Lifechanyuan system — alongside Truth, Goodness, Beauty, Love, Faith, Sincerity, and Harmony — and is the most essential inner state of cultivation practice. It is the spiritual foundation upon which the entire Lifechanyuan edifice rests, the bridge to the Elysium World, and the most important flower in the soul garden of every Chanyuan Celestial.

> Faith is Truth; Love is LIFE.
>
> — Guide Xuefeng, *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, Article 142

> Faith can accomplish everything. Small faith produces small fruit; great faith produces great fruit. Without faith, one will be lost; without faith, one will accomplish nothing.
>
> — Article 560

---

## Core Positioning

In the Lifechanyuan theoretical framework, **Faith** operates simultaneously on four levels:

- **Ontological level**: An attribute of the Greatest Creator, a property of celestials and saints
- **Epistemological level**: The very definition of Truth ("Faith *is* Truth")
- **Cultivation level**: The core inner state; the first problem every practitioner must resolve
- **Ethical level**: The primary requirement of being a person; keeping one's word is a cosmic law
