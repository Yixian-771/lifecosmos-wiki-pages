## I. Overview and Definitions

Faith (信, Xìn) is one of the eight core LIFE qualities in the Lifechanyuan system — Truth, Goodness, Beauty, Love, Faith, Sincerity — and is the most important flower in the soul garden of every Chanyuan Celestial. It is the bridge to Heaven, the most fundamental inner state of cultivation practice, and the spiritual cornerstone upon which the entire Lifechanyuan structure stands.

**Core Definition One** (*New Era Human 800 Concepts*, Article 142, full original):
> Faith is Truth; Love is LIFE.

**Core Definition Two** (Article 560, full original):
> Faith is Truth. Faith can accomplish everything. Small faith produces small fruit; great faith produces great fruit. Without faith, one will be lost; without faith, one will accomplish nothing.

**Core Definition Three** (*Chanyuan Corpus · Wisdom Chapter · "Faith Is Truth, Love Is LIFE,"* full original):
> Faith is a resonant state of the soul, a meticulous and rigorous structure of thought, a perfectly symmetrical system capable of responding in an instant. Faith is an attribute of the Greatest Creator, the domain of celestials and Buddhas, the quality of immortals and saints, and the pursuit of the wise.

**Core Definition Four** (*Chanyuan Corpus · Transmission Chapter · "The Bridge to the Elysium World,"* full original):
> Faith is a state of consciousness and psychology in which one has not a single shred of doubt toward Truth, toward things, or toward people.

**Core Definition Five** (*Xuefeng Corpus · Satire Chapter · "Refuting AIE's Rapid Review"*):
> "Faith" (信 xìn) and "believing" (相信 xiāngxìn) are two different concepts. Faith is Truth, but believing is not Truth. Faith gives back one hundred percent; believing may come to nothing. Faith is unshakeable; believing changes as its object changes.

## II. The Relationship Between Faith and Truth: Faith *Is* Truth

This is Lifechanyuan's most fundamental philosophical revelation about Faith — the most refined proposition in the entire system.

**（I）Progressive Elucidation from "Heaven's Revelation Chapter" to "Wisdom Chapter"** (*Chanyuan Corpus · Wisdom Chapter · "Faith Is Truth, Love Is LIFE,"* full original):
> In *Heaven's Revelation Chapter* No. 57, Truth was elucidated: Truth is the Way of the Greatest Creator; to walk the Way of the Greatest Creator is to walk the path of Truth. But this elucidation still gives us a boundless, vague, and nebulous feeling. To come closer to actuality and to help us know Truth more clearly, I shall now put it another way: Truth is Faith.

**（II）The Fundamental Identity of Faith and Truth** (*Chanyuan Corpus · Heaven's Revelation Chapter · "Truth!"* full original):
> Therefore, Truth is Faith. If a person does not keep faith, lies, and does not keep their word, they are acting against Truth.

**（III）Faith and Truth Manifested in Daily Life**:
> Faith not only heals illness, it can also bring the dead back to life. Jesus is the embodiment of Faith. He clearly told us: if a person has Faith, they can uproot a great tree and plant it in the sea. (*Wisdom Chapter · "Faith Is Truth, Love Is LIFE"*)

**（IV）The Sharp Divide Between Faith and Faithlessness**:
> Without faith, one will be lost; without faith, one will accomplish nothing. (Article 560)  
> Faith can accomplish everything. Small faith produces small fruit; great faith produces great fruit. (Article 560)

## III. Great Faith: The Highest Realm of Faith

"Great Faith" (大信) is the highest requirement for Faith in the Lifechanyuan system and the core inner state of every Chanyuan Celestial's cultivation practice.

**（I）Definition of Great Faith** (*New Era Human 800 Concepts*, Article 578, full original):
> What is Great Faith? It is to believe and trust one hundred percent without adding any doubt whatsoever — to trust what one understands, and also to trust what one does not yet understand. If Great Faith cannot be established, we are like water without a source, like a tree without roots.

**（II）The Three Objects of Great Faith** (*Xuefeng Corpus · Essays Chapter · "Establishing Great Faith — Second Home Cultural Construction No. 1,"* full original):
> The foremost item of Second Home cultural construction is to establish Great Faith: Great Faith in the Greatest Creator Buddha; Great Faith in Jesus Christ, Sakyamuni Buddha, the Immortal Laozi, the Prophet Muhammad, and the great man Mao Zedong; Great Faith in Guide Xuefeng. If these three Great Faiths cannot be established, a healthy culture cannot be built; one will wander without direction, fall into disorder and chaos, and waste time, energy, resources, and heart-strength through internal friction.

**（III）The Power of Great Faith** (*Xuefeng Corpus · Essays Chapter · "Establishing Great Faith,"* full original):
> Faith is Truth, Love is LIFE. Faith can accomplish everything; small faith produces small fruit; great faith produces great fruit; without faith one will be lost; without faith one will accomplish nothing.  
> Establishing Great Faith allows one to pass through all the hardships and dangers of life's journey, to light the divine lamp in one's heart and illuminate the path ahead, to cast off negativity and pessimism and build a positive and optimistic mindset, to chase the sun and escape from darkness, and to persevere without yielding until one finally reaches Spirit Mountain.

**（IV）The Path to Establishing Great Faith**:
> Every Chanyuan Celestial must read through the *Greatest Creator Chapter* in full, then combine it with the realities of one's own life and read more Heaven's Revelations, thereby establishing Great Faith in the Greatest Creator Buddha. (*"Establishing Great Faith"*)  
> Lifechanyuan is built upon the foundation of Great Faith — Great Faith in the Greatest Creator, in the Way, in Jesus Christ, in Sakyamuni Buddha, in Immortal Laozi, in the Prophet Muhammad; faith in facts, in science, in logic, in spiritual perception, in cause and effect, in a beautiful future for humanity. (*Xuefeng Corpus · Chanyuan Chapter · "I Would Rather Starve Than Break Faith with Others"*)

**（V）The Opposites of Great Faith — Small Faith and No Faith**:
> Jesus often lamented over us, saying "O you of little faith!" His disciples went out to spread the teachings, and many returned empty-handed; Jesus always said they had "little faith." (*Wisdom Chapter · "Faith Is Truth, Love Is LIFE"*)  
> If Peter had maintained his faith at that moment, and facing the raging waves had been able to "stroll as if in a leisurely garden," he would not have sunk. (*Wisdom Chapter · "Faith Is Truth, Love Is LIFE"*)

## IV. Keeping Faith: Faith Expressed on the Behavioral Level

Keeping faith (守信) is the concrete expression of Faith moving from inner quality into daily words and deeds, and is the fundamental guarantee of Second Home's operation.

**（I）Keeping Faith Is the Primary Duty of Being a Person** (*New Era Human 800 Concepts*, Article 119, full original):
> Keeping faith is the primary duty of being a person; not keeping faith is a kind of crime.

**（II）Do Not Take Oaths and Vows** (Article 581, full original):
> Do not take oaths and vows, because we cannot control how situations change and develop. Once we are unable to keep a vow, we have broken faith — we have put shackles on ourselves, bound ourselves.

**（III）Keeping Faith Is a Necessary Condition for Perfect Humanity** (Article 48):
> Perfect humanity must have eight conditions, the fourth of which is: humility, keeping faith, and honesty.

**（IV）Keeping Faith Is One of the Conditions for Going to the Thousand-Year World** (Article 492):
> To go to the Thousand-Year World of Heaven: eighth point — keeping faith: keeping one's word without going back on it, honoring promises, honoring agreements.

**（V）The Foundation of Keeping Faith in the Way of the Greatest Creator** (Article 517, full original):
> The core of the Way of the Greatest Creator is "as you sow, so shall you reap." Only on the soil of goodness, honesty, faithfulness, and selflessness can one "reap what one sows." Departing from the Way of the Greatest Creator, hope inevitably turns to despair and comedy turns to tragedy.

**（VI）Trust and Distrust** (Article 137, full original):
> Trust is honey; distrust is a knife.

## V. The Bridge to Elysium: The Three-Word Transmission of Faith, Love, and Nature

Faith is not isolated; together with Love (爱) and Nature (性), it forms the most refined three-word transmission of the Lifechanyuan cultivation system.

**（I）General Statement of the Three Words** (*Chanyuan Corpus · Transmission Chapter · "The Bridge to the Elysium World,"* full original):
> Having devoted eighteen years to the profound study of transmitting and elucidating the Way, today I can sum up the vast teachings in three words: Faith, Love, Nature (信、爱、性).  
> That is to say, as long as one brings "Faith, Love, Nature" to their ultimate expression, one can reach the Elysium World; one can reach Heaven.  
> Or we could put it this way: we need not read extensively, need not research and explore, need not strive and struggle, need not practice fasting or reciting sutras — as long as one wholly embodies "Faith! Love! Nature!" one can achieve the most beautiful wishes and realize the most beautiful ideals.

**（II）The Essence of Faith** (*"The Bridge to the Elysium World,"* full original):
> The single most important thing a person must do in their lifetime is resolve the problem of Faith.  
> Once the problem of Faith is resolved, the path of life is joy and freedom; the path of LIFE is flowers and fragrance; one can grasp the essentials and see all the mountains below at a glance.  
> What should one have faith in? One can have faith in anything — as long as you have faith, you have grasped the core, you have grasped the key, you can achieve your heart's wish.  
> In sum: whatever you have faith in, you can achieve. As long as you have steadfast faith and not a shred of doubt, your heart's wish will certainly be fulfilled — the secret of spiritual powers also lies here.  
> Once you have faith, do not doubt. Faith with reservations is not faith.

**（III）Faith Can Actualize the Heart-Image** (*"The Bridge to the Elysium World,"* full original):
> For example, if you steadfastly believe you will become a dragon, you certainly will. If you steadfastly believe you will turn stone into gold, you certainly will.  
> When we generate doubt along the path of faith, dark clouds begin to gather, ominous winds arise, sand and rocks fly, and all is thrown into turmoil.

## VI. Belief (Xìnyǎng): Faith on the Level of Life Direction

Belief/faith as a life philosophy (信仰, xìnyǎng) is the overall orientation of Faith — the highest form of Faith: Great Faith in a complete direction and system.

**（I）Belief Is the Navigator of Life** (Article 388, full original):
> Whatever kind of belief one has, one will have that kind of spiritual state and psychological activity; whatever kind of psychological activity, that kind of speech and action; whatever kind of speech and action, that kind of result and life path. Belief is the navigator of life; different beliefs achieve different lives.

**（II）Life Cannot Be Without Belief** (Article 57, full original):
> Life cannot be without belief. A life without belief is a life of contradiction and confusion, a life without direction, a life without a future, a life destined for tragedy.

**（III）Three Great Treasures for a Life Free of Worry** (Article 43, full original):
> Three great treasures for a life free of worry: steadfast faith in the Greatest Creator, belief in cause and effect, and giving one's best effort in all things.

**（IV）Believing in the Greatest Creator Is the Cornerstone of Universal Values** (Article 710, full original):
> The foremost item of universal values is believing in the Greatest Creator — this is the cornerstone of all universal values. Belief has nothing to do with religion.

**（V）Steadfast Faith in the Greatest Creator Is the Core of Keeping the Way** (Article 456):
> Keeping the Way means to cultivate and practice, to conduct oneself in accordance with the already-established worldview, outlook on life, view of LIFE, and value system. No matter how great the changes of time, no matter how great the temptations faced, one will steadfastly uphold belief and conviction: "Tempered by a thousand blows, still unyielding; let the winds come from any direction." "Let the winds and waves arise; I sit steady in the fishing boat" — unmoved and unshaken. What Jesus taught us — "He who endures to the end will be saved" — is the core meaning of Keeping the Way.
