# The Human Body — Miracle Program and Mystery (Academic Version)

## Abstract

The Lifechanyuan system proposes a framework for understanding the human body that diverges substantially from modern biomedical models. This framework positions the human body as: (1) a precision AI program driven by DNA as its executive authority; (2) a composite system with eight embedded miracle subroutines; and (3) a dual-structure entity composed of a physical body in positive space and a spiritual body in negative space. This article systematically analyzes Guide Xuefeng's and the Chanyuan Corpus's core statements on the human body, with reference to source texts and comparative concepts.

## I. The Programmatic Theory of the Human Body

One of the central propositions of the Lifechanyuan system is that "life is a program." Guide Xuefeng argues from the developmental trajectory of the fertilized egg: beginning at 0.1 millimeters, the egg unfolds according to DNA code into a fully formed body with nervous systems, digestive systems, and emotional systems — "exact to hundreds of decimal places." This places the human body and AI programs in a parallel category: both are "locked in programs and run under them," both subject to the "sole authority" of their respective executives — for humans, DNA; for AI, its algorithm.

This resonates on a descriptive level with modern biology's DNA-protein-phenotype chain, but the Lifechanyuan framework goes further: it holds that the human body program was pre-set by the Greatest Creator as part of a higher-dimensional cosmic script. DNA is not a product of evolution by chance, but a material encoding of divine programming.

## II. The Eight Miracle Programs

The *New Era Human 800 Concepts* Concept #56 explicitly names eight miracle subroutines embedded in the human body:

1. **Self-ignition** — Spontaneous activation of life energy to its maximum
2. **Rescue** — Intervention by higher-dimensional forces in moments of absolute crisis
3. **Poison-release** — Psychological toxins (resentment, rage) triggering internal chemical eruptions
4. **Elixir-transmission** — Transfer of inner essence to others or the environment
5. **Divine power** — Activation of capacities exceeding normal perception and action
6. **Madness** — A special state when consciousness exceeds normal frameworks
7. **Possession** — A state of influence or attachment by external spiritual forces
8. **Bliss** — The peak experience of deep resonance between physical and spiritual bodies

These eight subroutines correspond to and elaborate on the discussion in *Resentment Causes Illness* of the body's "self-healing capacity," "poison-release switch," and "self-destruct switch," forming a functional map of the human body as a composite program system.

## III. The Dual Structure: Physical Body and Spiritual Body

The Lifechanyuan system explicitly distinguishes two layers of the human body:

- **Physical body**: A tangible material carrier existing in positive space, subject to the physical laws of the positive universe (solar activity, climate, electromagnetic fields, etc.)
- **Spiritual body**: An intangible antimatter structure existing in negative space, influenced by the negative universe (divine will, faith, the consciousness waves of others, etc.)

The governing principle of their relationship is: **the spiritual body is primary; the physical body is secondary**. A perfected spiritual body can restore disabled limbs, cure terminal illness, and bring the dead back to life, while damage to the physical body does not significantly impair the spiritual body. This framework shares surface similarity with Cartesian mind-body dualism, but differs fundamentally: in the Lifechanyuan system, the spiritual body is not merely a mental entity but an antimatter structure capable of leaving the body during sleep or dreams, traveling across vast distances, and surviving independently in higher-dimensional space.

## IV. Consciousness and the Human Body

Guide Xuefeng advances a radical proposition: **human memory does not reside in the brain but in the subconscious; and the subconscious is not in the human body but in space-time.** This means the brain is merely a receiving terminal for consciousness, not a storage medium.

Correspondingly, he describes the body as composed of 130 trillion cells, each with its own independent consciousness — and these converge into the unified consciousness of the individual. This has structural parallels with systems-theory concepts such as "the whole is greater than the sum of its parts," but points toward a deeper claim: individual consciousness is inherently a composite of multiple layers of consciousness, not a unitary subject.

## V. The Human Body as a Cosmic Holographic Unit

The Lifechanyuan system holds that the human body contains "corresponding codes" for all products of the earth — an infinite treasury within. This aligns with the principle of "cosmic holography": any part of the universe contains information about the whole, and the human body is a microcosm of the universe.

Moreover, the symmetrical structure of the human body is held to reflect a necessity in the original structure of the life factor itself — just as the existence of heavenly worlds (Thousand-Year World, Ten-Thousand-Year World, Elysium World) is a cosmic inevitability arising from the law that the sum of positive and negative energies equals zero. The human body and the celestial worlds share a common ontological necessity.
