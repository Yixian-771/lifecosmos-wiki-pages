# The Human Body — Miracle Program and Mystery (Friendly Version)

## Your Body Is a Universe-Class Miracle

Think about this: a dot smaller than the tip of a needle — invisible to the naked eye — becomes you.

In that moment, everything was already written.

Eyes appear on the front of the face, not the back of the head. Hair grows on the scalp but not indefinitely elsewhere. The heart beats. The nerves feel. The digestive system processes. The emotional system responds. Every detail, precise to hundreds of decimal places.

No one taught it. It just knew.

Because **the human body is a program.**

## DNA: The Executive of Your Body

The controller of all this is something called DNA.

That fertilized egg, beginning at just 0.1 millimeters, contains the complete code for building an entire human body — every hair, every cell, every bone, every emotional response, all of it written inside.

A monkey's egg will never become a human. A human egg will never become an eagle. The reason is simple: they run different programs.

Guide Xuefeng put it plainly: **"The complexity of the human body is no less than that of the entire universe."**

You are not a simple machine. You are a cosmic masterpiece — more precise and complex than anything humanity has ever engineered.

## Eight Hidden Switches

Inside your body, eight extraordinary programs wait to be activated:

**Self-ignition · Rescue · Poison-release · Elixir-transmission · Divine power · Madness · Possession · Bliss**

This is not metaphor. Guide Xuefeng states it directly. When the right conditions are present, these eight programs automatically switch on.

Some people are rescued from impossible situations. Others unlock abilities beyond normal human capacity. Some experience moments of bliss that defy description. None of this is random — it is the activation of programs that were always there, waiting.

## You Actually Have Two Bodies

Think you only have one body? You have two.

- **The physical body**: The one you can see and touch. It operates in positive space and responds to food, climate, environment.
- **The spiritual body**: The one you cannot see. It operates in negative space and responds to beliefs, consciousness, the energy of others.

Their relationship is clear: **the spiritual body is the master; the physical body is the vessel.**

A perfect spiritual body keeps the physical body in its best state — and can even restore disabled limbs, cure terminal illness, and bring the dying back to life. While damage to the physical body does not deeply affect the spiritual body's perfection.

So what truly needs your care is not just your body. It is your soul.

## Your Emotions Rewrite Your Cells

Your body has 130 trillion cells. Every one of them has consciousness. Together, they form your consciousness.

When you carry chronic resentment and complaint, **every single cell is in distress.** Over time, cells change at the molecular level. Organs begin to break down. The heart is the first to suffer. Your ability to fight disease weakens. Blood and vital energy stagnate.

Resentment causes illness. It is not a metaphor — it is how the body's program responds to the data you feed it.

And the reverse is equally true: a calm, loving state keeps every system in harmony, and health follows naturally.

## Treasure and Toxin — Both Are Inside You

Here is something extraordinary that Guide Xuefeng revealed:

**Every natural resource on earth has a corresponding code within the human body.**

If you wish to attract something into your life, begin by finding the code within yourself. When you unlock it, the corresponding thing in the outer world will come to you.

But the body also carries toxins. When you are consumed by anger, a volcano of toxins erupts — primarily from the brain — and if you do not stop it, it floods through your entire body, disrupting every organ and blocking the flow of energy.

**Guard your inner state. It is your body's most important environment.**

## Your Memory Is Not in Your Brain

Here is one of Lifechanyuan's most surprising teachings:

> **"Human memory does not reside in the brain. It resides in the subconscious — and the subconscious is not in the human body but in space-time itself."**

Your brain is a receiver, not a hard drive. The actual record of your experience — and perhaps of experiences far older than this lifetime — is stored in the fabric of space-time.

This is why some people remember things they have no ordinary explanation for. This is why certain dreams feel like memories of another world.

## Your Body Is a Gift

Finally, remember this one thing:

Your eyes and ears, your sense of smell and touch, your organs and limbs and hair — each and every one is an expression of the Greatest Creator's love and grace toward you.

To fully enjoy this body, to live it joyfully and gratefully — this is how you respond to the gift.
