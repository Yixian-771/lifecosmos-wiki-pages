Life is a program, and the human body is its most intricate expression. From a fertilized egg no larger than a pinpoint, DNA orchestrates the precise formation of organs, nerve systems, emotions, and senses — with accuracy extending to hundreds of decimal places. Nothing is left to chance. Everything unfolds according to a pre-written code.

Within this program, **eight miracle subroutines** are built in: Self-ignition, Rescue, Poison-release, Elixir-transmission, Divine power, Madness, Possession, and Bliss — each activating automatically when the right conditions are met.
