# Becoming a Celestial Being and a Buddha

**Becoming a Celestial Being and a Buddha** (成仙成佛, *chéng xiān chéng fó*) is the ultimate aim of cultivation practice in the Lifechanyuan system — the highest state LIFE can attain. Through purifying the heart-mind, perfecting the antimatter structure of LIFE, and awakening to the truth of LIFE and the universe, a person transcends ordinary human existence and ascends to the higher realms of the Kingdom of Heaven. Celestial beings and buddhas represent two distinct pinnacles: celestial beings embody supreme freedom and joy; buddhas embody supreme wisdom and awakening. Both are the highest destinations available to human LIFE — and the fundamental precondition for both is the same: *"Perfect human nature, and the celestial-buddha state arises naturally."*
