## Scientific Proof: The Chladni Figures

German physicist Ernst Chladni did a famous experiment: he spread fine sand on a thin metal plate and drew a bow across it, like a violin. The sand arranged itself into beautiful geometric patterns. Change the frequency — the pattern changed. This is known as Chladni figures.

Xuefeng's conclusion: **sound waves genuinely change the physical world.** This has been experimentally proven.
