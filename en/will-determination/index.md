# Will and Determination (意志)

In Lifechanyuan's framework, *will* (意志, *yìzhì*) is a form of psychic potential energy: the mental and psychological state that arises from a heartfelt wish (*yì*) and sustains itself in pursuit of that wish (*zhì*). Will is latent until action releases it as kinetic energy. A key distinction: will is energy, not structure — no matter how strong one's will, it cannot change LIFE's antimatter structure. Only consciousness can do that. Yet without will as fuel, even the most capable LIFE cannot set sail.
