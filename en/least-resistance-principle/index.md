# Principle of Least Resistance

The **Principle of Least Resistance** is a core action guideline in the Lifechanyuan system, grounded in the Way of the Greatest Creator (the Natural Way). It holds that in all endeavors one should follow the most natural, most frictionless path — never forcing outcomes — embodying the wisdom that "when water finds its level, things complete themselves." It is both a cosmic law governing how the universe helps beings fulfill their wishes and a practical guide for daily life, spiritual cultivation, and building the Home for Soul.

> If you act on the Principle of Least Resistance, your life will encounter no obstacle.
>
> — Chanyuan Corpus · Revelations · "The Principle of Least Resistance"
