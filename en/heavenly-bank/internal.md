## III. How to Make Deposits

**Core Principle:**

"111. Giving and dedication are the finest means of depositing treasures in the Kingdom of Heaven."
*(New Era Human 800 Concepts, Fourth Edition · Concept 111)*

**Eight Categories of Giving:**

"There are many ways of giving; broadly they fall into eight categories: 1. Spreading Lifechanyuan values. 2. Encouraging and supporting brothers, sisters, loved ones, and companions. 3. Creating spiritual, psychological, or material wealth for the community. 4. Dedicating intellectual, financial, emotional, physical, and labor resources to the community. 5. Personally participating in the community's economic development or Second Home construction. 6. Providing spiritual, psychological, or material assistance to any brother, sister, loved one, or companion facing difficulty. 7. Upholding community values and harmony, and rejecting all words and actions that create conflict, spread falsehood, or disrupt peace. 8. Seeking new ideas, new thinking, and new perspectives for Lifechanyuan.

The process of giving is the process of elevating our spirituality, enhancing our wisdom, accumulating our merit, perfecting our consciousness, beautifying the antimatter structure of our lives, and advancing step by step toward the Thousand-Year World, Ten-Thousand-Year World, and Celestial Islands Continent."
*(Xuefeng Corpus · Encouragement Edition · Press Forward Toward the Kingdom of Heaven with Full Force)*

**The Greatest Deposit:**

"How can one repay accumulated debts from past lives? How can one accumulate merit and build savings in the Heavenly Bank? The best way to repay debt is through formless giving. ## IV. Three Conditions for Entering the Kingdom of Heaven

**The Three Conditions:**

"The principle of escaping the cycle of reincarnation and entering the Kingdom of Heaven is simple, not complex at all: first, settle all worldly bonds; second, repay all debts and accumulate sufficient treasure for the Kingdom of Heaven; third, perfect and beautify the soul garden so that one's quality of life meets the standard of a citizen of the Kingdom of Heaven."
*(Xuefeng Corpus · Miscellaneous Essays · Plan a Hundred Years Ahead, Lest the Moment of Truth Leave You Helpless)*

**Zero Balance — Turned Away at the Gate:**

"Imagine reaching the border of the Kingdom of Heaven with no one blocking your path — yet being told there is not a single deposit in your Heavenly Bank account, and being sent back to where you came from."
*(Xuefeng Corpus · Miscellaneous Essays · Plan a Hundred Years Ahead, Lest the Moment of Truth Leave You Helpless)*

**Deposits Alone Are Not Enough — All Three Must Be Met:**

"Imagine you have developed a habit of never finishing your food, of relieving yourself wherever you please, of speaking ill of others behind their backs. Even if all worldly bonds are settled, all debts repaid, and the Heavenly Bank account has sufficient funds — your habits are simply incompatible with life in the Kingdom of Heaven, and the gates will not open for you."
*(Xuefeng Corpus · Miscellaneous Essays · Plan a Hundred Years Ahead, Lest the Moment of Truth Leave You Helpless)*

**Systematic Statement:**

"The Kingdom of Heaven has three tiers, each corresponding to a different standard of life quality. Whichever tier your life quality matches, that is where you will go. Three indispensable conditions apply: first, repay the debts of past lives; second, accumulate sufficient merit; third, cultivate and perfect the quality of life. All three are necessary; none may be absent."
*(Chanyuan Corpus · Civilization Edition · The Fourth Benefit of Purifying the Soul — Opening Spiritual Perception, Locating the Celestial Isle)*

**Xuefeng's Summary:**

"I have stated many times in the Corpus: the essentials of cultivation are — repaying debts and settling worldly bonds; formless giving and accumulating merit; perfecting and beautifying the antimatter structure of life. These three tasks we have completed … We have accumulated sufficient merit; we have sufficient savings in the Heavenly Bank."
*(Xuefeng's Other Writings · 2019 · Mapping the Pitfalls on the Path to Becoming Celestial)*
