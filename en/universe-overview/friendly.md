# The Universe (Overview) · Friendly Version

## What Is the Universe?

We all know the universe is vast — bigger than the Milky Way, bigger than anything we can observe, stretching beyond all edges. But in Lifechanyuan's framework, the definition goes even wider than that:

> "Yu refers to all infinite space; zhou refers to all infinite time; the universe is the composite of matter and antimatter constituted by infinite space and infinite time."

Notice the word *antimatter*. The universe isn't only the material world we can see and touch — it also includes a vast antimatter world that physical senses cannot reach, yet which is equally real.

And then there's this striking claim:

> "The universe is an illusion, and its magnitude is related to consciousness. The stronger the consciousness, the greater the universe; the weaker the consciousness, the smaller the universe; without consciousness, there is no universe."

How much of the universe you can perceive depends on the level of your consciousness. This doesn't mean the universe isn't real — it means your window onto it is shaped by who you are inside.

## How Did the Universe Begin?

Science says: the Big Bang. Lifechanyuan says: not quite.

> "The origin of the universe is not the Big Bang, but Wuji giving birth to Taiji, and chaos transforming into Hundun."

Before the universe existed, there was **Wuji** — the Undifferentiated Absolute. No inside or outside. No large or small. No time, no space, no matter, no spirit. At once a pure clarity and a state of chaos; at once the presence of everything and the absence of everything.

Then, within this chaos, energy arranged itself into order — and from that ordering, consciousness arose. That consciousness is the Greatest Creator. Wuji gave birth to Taiji; Taiji *is* the Greatest Creator. From there, the universe unfolded: yin and yang took shape, 20 parallel worlds and 36 dimensions of space gradually emerged, and time appeared.

Here is the most remarkable claim in all of this:

> "In the universe there is only one contingency, and that is the birth of the Greatest Creator; beyond this, everything is necessity."

Everything you experience — every encounter, every "coincidence," every turn of fate — is the necessary unfolding of a chain that began with one single contingent event: the moment the Greatest Creator came into being.

## What Is the Universe Made Of?

Lifechanyuan gives two kinds of answers.

**Three elements:** Consciousness, structure, and energy. Everything in the universe is some combination of these three.

**Eight forces:** Magnetism, gravity, the strong force, the weak force (known to science) — plus four more that science hasn't yet recognized: the constructive force, the repulsive force, the mind-force, and the spirit-force.

Why can't scientists build a unified theory of the universe? Because they're working with only half the picture. The unified field, Lifechanyuan says, already exists:

> "The unified field of the universe is the Way of the Greatest Creator — the Hundun system formed by consciousness, structure, and energy."

## The Universe's True Nature: Hundun

When most of us look at the world, we see opposites — good and evil, yin and yang, life and death. But Lifechanyuan says this is only the surface.

> "Unity-in-opposition is the appearance of the universe, not its essence; the essence of the universe is Hundun. Within Hundun there is no distinction between right and wrong, good and evil, beautiful and ugly."

Hundun (浑沌) is not disorder — it is a wholeness so complete that opposites dissolve into it. The sages who saw the world this way — Jesus, Laozi, Shakyamuni — were great precisely because they perceived the universe as Hundun rather than as conflict.

The central phenomenon of Hundun is **cosmic holographic resonance**. Every part of the universe is instantly connected to every other part:

- A butterfly's wings in Alaska can trigger a storm at the Cape of Good Hope
- A single thought you have spreads through the entire universe in an instant
- Even a single cough can shake the Heavenly Court

> "A whisper in a darkened room thunders like a clap of lightning."

Nothing is isolated. Nothing goes unregistered.

## How Does the Universe Run?

**The Tao is the blood of the universe.**

> "The Tao is the consciousness of the Greatest Creator, the blood of the universe, the nature of all things, the synthesis of all laws, and the master program governing all things."

The Tao is in everything. Everything is in the Tao. There is nothing outside it.

**The universe's total energy is always zero.**

This sounds abstract, but the implication is simple: every action generates energy that must be balanced somewhere. The mechanism that maintains this balance is **karma** — the Law of Karma and Retribution. As you sow, so shall you reap. The net of heaven is vast; nothing slips through. This isn't moralizing — it's how the universe keeps its accounts.

**Everything in the universe is necessary.**

> "In the universe there are no contingent phenomena; behind every occurrence there must necessarily be corresponding factors at work."

What feels like coincidence is the universe's programming expressing itself.

## How Many Layers Does the Universe Have?

Far more than three dimensions.

- **Positive Universe** — the material world we inhabit
- **Negative Universe** — the antimatter world, which operates at speeds beyond light; dreams are one of its manifestations
- **36 dimensions of space** — from the lowest layers (the Fire-Refining Layer, the Frozen Layer) to the highest (the Cool Realm, the Celestial Islands Continent)
- **20 parallel worlds** — the human world, the ghost world, the divine world, the Thousand-Year World, the Ten-Thousand-Year World, the Celestial Islands Continent… all coexisting simultaneously

All 20 parallel worlds connect to a single zero point — the **Cool Realm**, where the Greatest Creator's essence resides.

## Why Does the Universe Exist?

The answer may surprise you:

> "The universe exists for LIFE, and LIFE exists for the universe. If LIFE perishes, the universe is extinguished; if the universe dies, LIFE is cut off."

The universe isn't an indifferent backdrop. It is a purposeful creation, designed for LIFE. And the Greatest Creator's original intention?

> "To allow all LIFE to live in joy, happiness, freedom, and bliss — this is the Greatest Creator's original intention in creating the universe."

LIFE's ultimate destination in this universe is the **Celestial Islands Continent** — the Greatest Creator's back garden. Eighty billion islands. Each one roughly the size of the Earth. Each home to a single celestial immortal, waiting for those who have cultivated their way from the human world into immortality.

> "To become a celestial immortal and reach the Elysium World is to reach the peak of LIFE."

The universe is the boundless composite of matter and antimatter, created by the Greatest Creator, animated by the Tao, ordered by Hundun's holographic wholeness, and designed entirely for the flourishing and ultimate homecoming of LIFE.
