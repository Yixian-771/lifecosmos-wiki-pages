# Sympathetic Resonance

**Sympathetic Resonance**—all things in the universe have their own vibrational frequency; when frequencies match, resonance occurs and energy is mutually amplified. Consciousness and the heart-mind also have frequencies; resonating with the Way of the Greatest Creator is the direction of cultivation, and those whose hearts resonate with yours are your true kin.
