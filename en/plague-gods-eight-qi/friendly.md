# Plague Gods, Plague King & Eight Qi (Friendly Version)

## Start with a Puzzle

Why do some people live in the hardest-hit epidemic zones, go out every day without a mask, and remain perfectly healthy — while others who lock themselves indoors and take every precaution still fall ill?

Lifechanyuan offers an answer that is entirely different from conventional medicine.

---

## Plague Gods Are Not "Germs" — They Are Cosmic Custodians

In Lifechanyuan's view of the universe, plagues are not random biological disasters. They are deliberate acts by a cosmic management system.

There is a being called the **Plague King**, who commands sixty-four numbered Plague Gods. Their sole duty: **to maintain the moral and spiritual cleanliness of Earth**.

> "When humanity's moral level declines and hearts grow dark, I send out the Plague Gods to intervene."
>
> — The Plague King

Think of it like this: just as a city's sanitation system kicks into high gear when pollution spikes, Plague Gods activate when human hearts fill with "black qi." Minor infractions get a warning; severe accumulation of black qi brings large-scale cleansing.

The Plague King even admits that he himself is just a "chess piece" on the board of the Lord of Divine Kings — not malicious, just faithfully doing his job.

---

## What Are the "Eight Qi"?

In 2020, guide Xuefeng had a conversation with "Plague God 008" — that is, the COVID-19 virus. Plague God 008 explained that it was not transmitted from bats or any animal. It had been **latent in the human body for tens of thousands of years** and only "woke up" when conditions were right.

What conditions? The Eight Qi:

> **Sufficiently dense foul-meat qi, sufficiently fierce violent qi, sufficiently explosive temper qi, sufficiently fearful timid qi, sufficiently corrupt heart qi, sufficiently turbid lung qi, sufficiently weakened kidney qi, sufficiently depleted liver qi — when all Eight Qi are present, I appear on my own.**

In plain language:
- **Foul-meat qi** — eating meat, slaughter, a heavy, "bloody" energy field
- **Violent qi** — anger, hatred, aggression
- **Temper qi** — irritability, rage, emotional explosiveness
- **Timid qi / fear qi** — extreme fear, especially terror of death
- **Heart qi** — corrupt intentions, self-interest, bad faith
- **Lung qi** — turbid accumulation in the lungs
- **Kidney qi** — depleted kidney energy (often seen in older adults)
- **Liver qi** — weakened liver energy

This explains why children and young people were less likely to fall seriously ill in the epidemic — bold timid qi, strong kidney qi, clear lung qi. Older people showed the reverse pattern.

## "Wearing a Mask Does Nothing" — Here's the Logic

Plague God 008 said something startling: it **does not enter through the mouth** — it **enters through the eyes and ears**, via information.

When you see footage of infected patients, when you hear again and again that "cases are surging and people are dying," that information enters your heart — and the Plague God latent in your body stirs awake. The more vividly you hear it, the higher your probability of infection.

This is also why doctors and nurses were so vulnerable — they absorbed the most direct, detailed information about the illness every single day.

> "The more panicked and fearful people are, the more at a loss — the easier it is for me to take advantage. Causing people to fear is one of my effective methods of control."
>
> — Plague God 008

---

## So How Do You Stay Safe?

The answer comes straight from Plague God 008:

> "If a person's heart is pure, they settle the mind in oneness, have no self-interest, and are happy and joyful — I have no way in."

And when the epidemic surged again in 2023, guide Xuefeng offered three practical steps:

**1. Trust in the Greatest Creator and the Tao** — understand that all things are inevitable. The epidemic's rise and fall are not random. What must happen will happen. Trusting this eliminates fear.

**2. Greet the Plague God with a calm heart** — welcome it when it comes, bid it farewell when it goes. Don't resist. Don't worry. Hand your life over to the Greatest Creator and let the Tao decide when your earthly journey ends. This is what it means to be one with the Tao.

**3. Stay happy** — get enough sleep. Laugh every day. In moments of pressure and depression, immunity plummets. In a state of joyful lightness, the body's defenses are strongest.

> "The sky falls — ha ha, wonderful! The ground sinks — heh heh, interesting! No way out — wow, so *that's* how it is! Earth is about to disappear — let's go to the Thousand-Year World! Like this, there is joy at every moment, romance in every place."
>
> — Xuefeng, *Survival Laws Under Raging Epidemics* (2023)

## The Epidemic Is a "Wordless Scripture"

Xuefeng called the epidemic a **wordless scripture** — a teaching sent by the universe without a single written character.

Not a punishment. A mirror.

It shows humanity: *your hearts have accumulated too much darkness*. And for spiritual practitioners, it is something more: a real-time test of how deep the practice goes. Can you hold clarity, fearlessness, and joy when the whole world is panicking?

If yes — the Plague God has no foothold in you. If no — perhaps it is time to look inward.
