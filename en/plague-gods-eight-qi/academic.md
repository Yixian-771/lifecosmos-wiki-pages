# Plague Gods, Plague King & Eight Qi (Academic Version)

## Abstract

"Plague Gods, Plague King & Eight Qi" is a core theoretical framework within Lifechanyuan that reframes epidemic disease as a cosmic purification mechanism rather than a biological phenomenon. Developed primarily by guide Xuefeng through a series of dialogue-form texts written in January–June 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic, the theory posits that disease is triggered by eight internal energetic conditions (the "Eight Qi") inherent to the human body, not by external pathogens. Plague Gods — numbered entities commanded by the Plague King under the authority of the "Lord of Divine Kings" — activate when these Eight Qi reach sufficient thresholds, dissolving the black qi of moral and spiritual corruption that humanity generates. This article examines the framework's theoretical structure, key propositions, information transmission model, three supporting pillars, and its implications for spiritual practice.

## II. Theoretical Framework

### 2.1 The Plague God Hierarchy

The Lifechanyuan cosmology includes a functional tier of "purification beings" responsible for maintaining Earth's moral order:

- **The Plague King (瘟王)**: Commands sixty-four Plague Gods; is himself a "chess piece" of the Lord of Divine Kings, described as "the most diligent in the entire universe" alongside the Demon King
- **Plague Gods (瘟神)**: Numbered entities, with lower numbers indicating greater power and more difficult containment. Known assignments:
  - 006: Airborne — infection through breathing alone
  - 007: Qi-breath transmission — healthy bodies can carry and spread
  - 008: COVID-19 — primarily via infected bodies (the visible, material layer)
  - 009: Bubonic plague
  - 010: Smallpox
  - 011: Influenza
- **Operational logic**: The Plague Gods activate when they observe "black qi" (道德与心灵黑气) covering a region, sourced from the human heart. Their response is proportional to severity: minor incidents = population warning; severe = large-scale population reduction.

Crucially, the Plague King states: "We do not concern ourselves with human affairs. We are only responsible for purifying the Earth." This frames plague not as punishment in any moralistic sense, but as an impersonal systemic correction.

### 2.2 The Eight Qi Theory

The Eight Qi are the specific internal conditions that enable Plague Gods to activate within an individual body. They are entirely endogenous — related to the person's physical and moral state, not to exposure to pathogens:

| Qi | Literal translation | Domain |
|----|---------------------|--------|
| 腥气 (foul-meat qi) | Sufficient density | Dietary/karmic: consumption of meat, slaughter |
| 戾气 (violent qi) | Sufficiently fierce | Emotional: hatred, anger, aggression |
| 脾气 (temper qi) | Sufficiently explosive | Emotional: irritability, rage |
| 胆气 / 怕气 (timid qi / fear qi) | Sufficiently fearful | Psychological: terror, especially fear of death |
| 心气 (heart qi) | Sufficiently corrupt | Moral: self-interest, ill intention |
| 肺气 (lung qi) | Sufficiently turbid | Physical: lung accumulation |
| 肾气 (kidney qi) | Sufficiently weak | Physical: kidney depletion (age/lifestyle related) |
| 肝气 (liver qi) | Sufficiently depleted | Physical/emotional: liver depletion |

Empirical validation cited in the framework: during the COVID-19 outbreak, children and young people (bold timid qi, strong kidney qi, clear lung qi) showed markedly lower infection and death rates; middle-aged and elderly people (dense foul-meat qi, heavy violent qi, depleted kidney qi) showed higher rates — consistent with the Eight Qi prediction.

### 2.3 Information Transmission Model

The framework proposes a counterintuitive transmission mechanism that bypasses conventional germ theory:

- Plague God 008 **does not enter via mouth or wounds** but **through the eyes and ears as information**
- Mechanism: when the eyes observe infected scenes, or the ears receive epidemic information, the latent Plague God within the observer's body is "awakened"
- Corollary: **fear itself accelerates infection** — the more panic and distress, the more vulnerable the body becomes; "a person can be frightened to death"
- Plague God 007 operates at an even more subtle level: transmission via the "ill-fortune qi" (晦气) that clings to all bodies — both sick and healthy — making it entirely uncontainable by quarantine

This model reframes public health communication as a disease vector in its own right: media coverage of epidemic deaths is, in this framework, a mechanism of viral spread.

## III. Three Theoretical Pillars

The text *Everyone Carries the Coronavirus* provides three independent theoretical foundations for the claim that external prevention measures are ineffective:

### 3.1 Cosmic Holography

Basis: "In latent information, the sub-system contains all the information of the system." A single drop of blood contains all the body's information. By extension: once a virus exists anywhere on Earth, every human body already contains its information. Prevention is impossible in principle — it is "self-delusion."

### 3.2 Buddhist Self-Nature Theory

Basis: "Everyone has Buddha-nature." The self-nature "is originally complete with everything" — it generates all dharmas, including all illness and all health. Whether one falls ill depends on one's self-nature, not on whether a mask was worn.

### 3.3 The Law of Cause and Effect

Basis: "Cause and effect alone are not empty." Reality is a projection of one's own consciousness. The conditions for infection must be present internally; without those conditions, even complete immersion in a viral environment produces no illness.

## V. Implications for Spiritual Practice

The framework functions not only as a theory of disease but as a practical evaluation tool for spiritual development:

- Plague God 008's stated conditions for defeat — "a pure heart, settled in oneness, without self-interest, happy and joyful" — map directly onto core Lifechanyuan cultivation targets
- The epidemic becomes a real-time test: can the practitioner maintain equanimity, fearlessness, and joy amid collective panic?
- Xuefeng's *Reflections on Plague God 008* explicitly frames the epidemic as a sorting mechanism — those who are "on board" (cultivators who have arrived at the Second Home) represent those who have passed the test; those still outside represent those who have not yet resolved their worldly ties, accumulated sufficient merit, or internalized the principles

This interpretation links the plague framework directly to the concept of "life visa" (生命签证) — the epidemic as an expression of the cosmic evaluation system deciding who is qualified for higher-dimensional existence.
