## I. The Plague King: Commander of Sixty-Four Plague Gods

*(Xuefeng Corpus · Admonitions · Xuefeng's Dialogue with the King of Plagues, 2020-01-26)*

Xuefeng: Greetings, Plague King! I have many things I do not understand and hope you can enlighten me.

Plague King: Please speak.

Xuefeng: How many Plague Gods do you command?

Plague King: Sixty-four.

Xuefeng: What is the primary role of Plague Gods?

Plague King: To maintain the cleanliness of the Earth.

Xuefeng: What kind of cleanliness?

Plague King: The cleanliness of morality and the soul.

Xuefeng: How do you maintain it?

Plague King: When humanity's moral level declines and hearts grow dark, I send out the Plague Gods to intervene.

Xuefeng: How do they intervene?

Plague King: If it is not severe, we reduce the population by a small amount as a warning. If severe, we reduce the population on a large scale.

## IV. The Eight Qi: Eight Internal Triggers of Disease

*(Xuefeng Corpus · Admonitions · My Dialogue with the Wuhan Coronavirus, 2020-01-24)*

Xuefeng: Hello, Wuhan coronavirus. Where do you come from, and what is your name?

Virus: I am Plague God 008. I have been latent on Earth for tens of thousands of years.

Xuefeng: Why have you appeared now?

Plague God 008: Because the conditions for my appearance are now met.

Xuefeng: What do you mean? What conditions?

Plague God 008: Just as a person who goes without bathing or changing clothes for a long time will grow lice — the conditions are met and the lice appear.

Xuefeng: I still don't quite understand. Are you latent in the human body?

Plague God 008: Yes, I am latent in the human body.

Xuefeng: So you didn't transfer to humans from bats, snakes, or civets?

Plague God 008: Of course not. I awoke from within the human body.

Xuefeng: If you have been latent for tens of thousands of years, why didn't you appear before, and only appear recently?

Plague God 008: The conditions for my appearance were never met until now.

Xuefeng: What conditions must be met for you to appear?

Plague God 008: Sufficiently dense foul-meat qi, sufficiently fierce violent qi, sufficiently explosive temper qi, sufficiently fearful timid qi, sufficiently corrupt heart qi, sufficiently turbid lung qi, sufficiently weakened kidney qi, sufficiently depleted liver qi — when all Eight Qi are present, I appear on my own.

Xuefeng: So if any person possesses the "Eight Qi" you describe, you will appear in them?

Plague God 008: Yes.

Xuefeng: Then there is no such thing as viral transmission?

Plague God 008: Transmission is just one pathway of awakening. When one awakens, it goes on to awaken the others latent in other human bodies. But whether they can be awakened still depends on whether the Eight Qi of the person in question meet the necessary conditions.

Xuefeng: So many people, even in epidemic zones, will not be infected?

Plague God 008: Yes. For example, children and young people — their timid qi is bold, their kidney qi strong, their lung qi clear — they are not easily infected. Middle-aged and older people have dense foul-meat qi, heavy violent qi, depleted kidney qi, turbid lung qi, and weak timid qi, so they are easily infected.

Plague God 008: This is not something I can decide. As long as the foul-meat smell is dense, I will stay. When it fades, I cannot stay. So I may disappear in three months, or I may need three years — it depends on humanity, on the Eight Qi. Once human violent qi subsides, I will have no way to stay.

Plague God 008: I cannot be contained. I exist in the formless. Containment measures are just human self-delusion and self-sedation. My appearance is related to human "Eight Qi" — not to geography, not to population density.

## VII. Three Theoretical Pillars: Holography, Buddhism, Karma

*(Xuefeng Corpus · Admonitions · Everyone Carries the Coronavirus, 2020-06-16)*

**The Holographic Theory** According to cosmic holography: "In latent information, the sub-system contains all the information of the system; the system contains all the information of the universe." We know that a single drop of blood can reveal the full information of the human body. This means every cell contains all of a person's information. Therefore: once the coronavirus was born and existed, every person on Earth was infected — regardless of location, regardless of protective measures. To deny this is to deny cosmic holography.

**Buddhist Teaching** "One flower is one world, one leaf is one Buddha." Buddhism says "everyone has Buddha-nature," "all can become Buddha." The Buddhist teaching tells us that our self-nature "is originally complete with everything," "self-nature gives rise to all dharmas." This means we carry the most beautiful things in existence — and equally, the most ugly. We can logically infer "everyone has demonic nature, animal nature." Whether one becomes Buddha or demon depends on one's self-nature. Therefore: whether one is infected and falls ill depends on "self-nature," not on protective measures. If anyone believes they are infected by another person, they do not yet understand Buddhist teaching.

**The Law of Cause and Effect** "All dharmas are empty, but cause and effect are not." "To know what you did in past lives, look at what you receive in this life; to know what you will receive in future, look at what you do now." Cause and effect tell us that "reality is the projection of one's own consciousness." Suffering arises from negative deeds; blessings from merit. "As you sow, so shall you reap." If a person regularly bathes and changes clothes, lice will not appear. The coronavirus has its own conditions for arising and developing. Whether each person is infected depends on whether the conditions for infection are present in them. Without those conditions, even surrounded by the virus, one will not be infected and will not fall ill. With those conditions, infection and illness are inevitable.
