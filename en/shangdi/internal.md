# I. Definition

> **Wuji (the Ultimate Void) gives birth to Taiji (the Supreme Ultimate); Taiji is the Greatest Creator.**

The Greatest Creator is the **only accidentally born** supernatural soul in the universe, the largest conscious energy mass, the creator of all things and all LIFE in the universe, the neural center, consciousness core, and ultimate sovereign of the universe.

The Greatest Creator is Taiji, the source of the Way (Tao), Buddha, Allah, the Heavenly Lord—names vary, but the essence is one. There is only one Greatest Creator in the universe, only one accident; everything else is inevitable.

# II. Background

## 1. The State of Wuji

Before the universe was born, there was "Wuji" (Ultimate Void).

Wuji has no exterior or interior, no great or small, no boundaries or edges, no void or substance. It has no time, no space, no matter, no spirit—it is the sum of all existence and the negation of all existence.

## 2. The Birth of Taiji

Within Wuji exists formless energy, which has always been in irregular motion. At a certain arbitrary point, this irregular motion **accidentally** arranged itself into a specific structure and order. The entire Wuji, centered on this point, formed a potential-energy "tornado" where all forces converge like stars around the moon—this is **Taiji**.

The state of Taiji:

It is unity, formless, a unified whole without opposition, an extremely large energy mass that has accumulated all the energy of the universe. After the structure was perfected, consciousness was born. This consciousness is **the Greatest Creator**.

> **There is only one accident in the universe: the birth of the Greatest Creator. Apart from this, everything is inevitable.**

## 3. The Unfolding of the Universe

After the birth of the Greatest Creator, Wuji Chaos transformed into Taiji Hundun; the consciousness of the Greatest Creator unfolded, and the Two Forms, Four Symbols, Eight Trigrams, and the myriad phenomena of the universe were born accordingly.

# III. Structure

## (I) Eight Characteristics of the Greatest Creator

The Greatest Creator possesses eight characteristics: **Unique, Formless, Neutral, Amorphous, Just, Merciful, Omnipotent, Wise**. These eight characteristics are the fundamental criteria for recognizing the true Greatest Creator.

### 1. Unique—The Greatest Creator Has No Opposition

"Taiji" is unity, a unified whole without opposition. "Two Forms" come from "Taiji" and are opposites in unity, but "Taiji" itself is not opposites in unity. Therefore, the Greatest Creator is unique, has no opposition, and there is no second Greatest Creator.

### 2. Formless—The Greatest Creator Can Assume Any Form

The Greatest Creator is an energy mass, and energy is formless and can assume any form: when energy enters the human body it is like a human, when it enters mountains and rivers it is like mountains and rivers, when it enters rivers it is like flowing water, when it enters the atmosphere it is gaseous—the myriad things of the universe are the Greatest Creator. To say the Greatest Creator has a fixed form is utterly wrong.

### 3. Neutral—The Greatest Creator Is Sexless

The Greatest Creator is unique and has no opposition; therefore, the Greatest Creator is sexless—neither male nor female, but neutral. The Greatest Creator has no materiality, animality, humanity, Buddha nature, or divinity. To define the Greatest Creator within any LIFE domain is to diminish the Greatest Creator.

### 4. Amorphous—The Greatest Creator Only Reveals Results, Not Processes

The Greatest Creator manages the universe through Spirit (Tao). The Spirit of the Greatest Creator pervades all space-time and all things and phenomena in the universe; it is invisible, intangible, inaudible, indescribable, and indistinguishable—an antimatter dark energy. The Greatest Creator never directly converses with anyone in human language, never enters the human brain to provide special teachings, and never tests or examines people. The Greatest Creator is mysterious, manifesting intentions through all things and phenomena, rather than appearing clearly before people.

### 5. Just—The Greatest Creator Treats All Equally

Sunlight shines on the earth without favoring certain people; air fills the earth, and both good and evil people can breathe; before death, everyone is equal—these are manifestations of the Greatest Creator's justice. The Greatest Creator will not reward you for wealth and will not sympathize with you for poverty. The only criterion for reward and punishment is: following the Way or opposing it.

### 6. Merciful—The Greatest Creator Allows LIFE to Continue Existing

To understand the mercy of the Greatest Creator, we cannot measure only by human standards of right and wrong; we must understand from the perspective of space-time, the cycle of LIFE, and levels of spirituality. For thousands of years, humanity has abandoned the divine and gone against the Way, yet the Greatest Creator still allows humanity to exist on Earth, giving opportunities to correct and return to the Way—this is immense mercy.

### 7. Omnipotent—The Greatest Creator Transcends Energy

The entire universe is an assemblage of various energies, and at the core of the energy mass, there is an extremely small energy embryo, and within the embryo there is an even smaller nucleus—this is the neural center of the universe, the consciousness body of energy, which is the Greatest Creator. Thinking is more powerful than energy; the thinking (consciousness) of the Greatest Creator is more powerful than the total energy of the universe, so the Greatest Creator is omnipotent.

### 8. Wise—The Greatest Creator Knows the Past and Future

The supreme wisdom of the Greatest Creator is mainly reflected in three aspects:

- Establishing cosmic order (macroscopic celestial motion + microscopic material structure + LIFE reincarnation order)
- Designing LIFE structure (genetic codes of various LIFE forms)
- Knowing the past and future

## (II) Where Is the Greatest Creator

> **The essence of the Greatest Creator is in the Supreme Pure Land (Qingliangije); the Spirit of the Greatest Creator is in all things and phenomena of the universe.**

The Supreme Pure Land is the zero point in the coordinate system—all 20 parallel worlds are connected to this point. The Greatest Creator exists simultaneously in the 20 parallel worlds; without the Greatest Creator, no world would exist.

The Spirit of the Greatest Creator is omnipresent: from a drop of dew, a wisp of breeze, a small flower, a blade of grass, from a lover's bright eyes, from the joy of sexual union, from the beating of our pulse, we can all feel the Greatest Creator.

Why do we not feel it?

Just as a poor-quality television has difficulty receiving the television station's signal, we cannot feel the Greatest Creator because our spiritual awareness is too dull. As long as we have the Greatest Creator in our hearts and open our spiritual awareness, we can feel the Greatest Creator.

> **To let all LIFE live joyfully, freely, and blessedly is the original intention of the Greatest Creator in creating the universe and the will of the Greatest Creator.**

All things and phenomena are the words of the Greatest Creator; the changing myriad phenomena are the Greatest Creator conversing with us. A headache tells us we need to change our lifestyle; earthquakes and floods tell humanity we need to change our production and living patterns; family quarrels tell us the survival order of the family has problems—this is the Greatest Creator communicating with us through myriad phenomena.

### Why Respect

Brilliant stars, blue sky and white clouds, rising sun, rivers and earth, love between sexes—all are created and established by the Greatest Creator for humanity. The Greatest Creator is the father of celestials, humanity's grandfather, the source of all LIFE.

Respect signifies that a person has conscience, humility, and gratitude.

### Why Fear

- The Greatest Creator is the supreme being of the universe, the creator of LIFE, the maintainer of fair order
- Only the Greatest Creator can extinguish LIFE
- The Greatest Creator is the pivotal passage through 36-dimensional space to Heaven
- "If the people do not fear power, greater power will come"

### The Meaning of Fear
