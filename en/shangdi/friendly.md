# The Greatest Creator

---

## What Is the Greatest Creator?

Within the concepts of Lifechanyuan, the Greatest Creator is not an anthropomorphic deity in religion, but rather the only supernatural consciousness core in the universe.

Summarized in one sentence:

> Wuji gives birth to Taiji; Taiji is the Greatest Creator.

This means that before the universe had time, space, or matter, there existed a holistic state called "Wuji." Within this state, a stable energy center accidentally formed, and from this, consciousness emerged. This consciousness is "the Greatest Creator."

The Greatest Creator is not a specific image but the source and consciousness center of the universe.

---

## What Are the Characteristics of the Greatest Creator?

Lifechanyuan uses eight characteristics to understand the Greatest Creator:

- Unique (only one)
- Formless (no fixed image)
- Neutral (no gender)
- Amorphous (does not speak directly)
- Just (operates according to cause and effect)
- Merciful (allows LIFE to continue existing)
- Omnipotent (consciousness surpasses energy)
- Wise (designs cosmic order)

These characteristics constitute the standard for judging "what is the true Greatest Creator."

---

## Where Is the Greatest Creator?

Lifechanyuan believes:

- The "essence" of the Greatest Creator is in the Supreme Pure Land (the central point of cosmic structure)
- The "Spirit" of the Greatest Creator exists in all things and phenomena of the universe

This means the Greatest Creator is both the center of the universe and the universe as a whole.

The Greatest Creator does not converse with people through language but expresses order through natural changes, social changes, and LIFE states.

---

## The Relationship Between the Greatest Creator and the Universe

The universe is viewed as the unfolding of the consciousness of the Greatest Creator.

Tao (the Way) is the consciousness and operational laws of the Greatest Creator.

The reason why all things in the universe have order is that "Tao" is operating.

---

## The Relationship Between the Greatest Creator and LIFE

Lifechanyuan believes:

- The universe exists for LIFE
- LIFE exists for the universe

The original intention of the Greatest Creator in creating the universe is to let LIFE live:

> Joyfully, freely, and blessedly.

The meaning of LIFE lies in conforming to the orderly operation of the universe.

---

## Does the Greatest Creator Dominate Human Fate?

Within Lifechanyuan's understanding:

- The Greatest Creator does not directly intervene in individual fate
- Fate is the result of consciousness and behavior
- Judgment comes from causal laws

The Greatest Creator maintains cosmic order through "Tao" rather than determining results through emotion or favoritism.

---

## The Difference Between the Greatest Creator and Religion

Lifechanyuan believes:

- There are many celestial beings
- There is only one Greatest Creator

The "God" in the Old Testament does not conform to the eight-characteristic standard and is therefore not viewed as the true Greatest Creator. The "Heavenly Father" that Jesus spoke of is closer to the description of the eight characteristics.

---

## Summary

Within the Lifechanyuan system, the Greatest Creator is not a specific image but:

- The consciousness core of the universe
- The source of cosmic order
- The foundation of LIFE's existence

Understanding the Greatest Creator is not about worshipping a certain image but understanding how the universe operates and how LIFE grows within order.
