# The Nonmaterial World

【Entry Portal】

The Nonmaterial World — also called the **Negative Universe** (负宇宙, *fù yǔzhòu*) — is one of the two fundamental halves of the cosmos in the Lifechanyuan framework. While the material world (Positive Universe) is the domain of physical existence perceivable by the senses, the Nonmaterial World is the domain of LIFE-bodies, consciousness, and spiritual existence. It is the native realm of the Greatest Creator, divine beings, celestials, and heavenly angels — and the ultimate destination toward which Chanyuan Celestials cultivate.

In this cosmology, the Nonmaterial World is not secondary or derivative. It is the **source**: the material world is the reflection and surface expression of the nonmaterial. Everything that has no mass, no weight, and cannot be detected by any physical instrument — yet actively influences material reality — belongs to the nonmaterial domain. This includes consciousness, the soul, dream-space, spiritual telepathy channels, and the Elysium World.

This entry covers the full theoretical structure of the Nonmaterial World across three reading versions.
