# The Antimatter World

[Entry Gateway]

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological
> essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across
> incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage
> of human existence in this world.

**The Antimatter World** (反物质世界, also called the Nonmaterial World or Negative Universe) is one of the two fundamental halves of the cosmos in the Lifechanyuan framework. While the material world (Positive Universe) is the domain of physical existence perceivable by the senses, the Antimatter World is the domain of LIFE-bodies, consciousness, and spiritual existence — the native realm of the Greatest Creator, divine beings, celestials, and heavenly angels, and the ultimate destination toward which Chanyuan Celestials cultivate.

> The Antimatter World is more real than the material world — it is the source from which all material forms arise and to which all LIFE returns.
>
> — Guide Xuefeng

---

## Core Positioning

In the Lifechanyuan system, the Antimatter World is not a mystical metaphor — it is the cosmological structure that contains all post-death LIFE destinations: the Thousand-Year World, Ten-Thousand-Year World, Elysium World, Celestial Islands Continent, and the Kingdom of Heaven all exist within the Antimatter World.
