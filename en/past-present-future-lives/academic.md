# Past Life, Present Life, and Future Life (Academic Version)

## Abstract

"Past life, present life, and future life" represents the temporal dimension of Lifechanyuan's spiritual cosmology. Unlike the Buddhist six-path reincarnation model, Lifechanyuan frames the three-life continuum as a precise karmic programming system: the consciousness activity and behavioral record of past lives are encoded in the Dao (the cosmic program), generating the present life's "fate-formation" (命阵, a predefined destiny matrix); the present life's mental intentions and cultivation achievements are continuously written into the future life's script. The bond linking all three segments is not a continuity of personal identity, but the precise transfer of merit and karmic debt. Memory erasure during reincarnation is a built-in mechanism (memories stored in the negative universe), and one of the core tasks of cultivation is to open spiritual awareness and restore connectivity across the three-life continuum.

## I. Cosmological Framework

Lifechanyuan embeds "past life, present life, and future life" within its cosmic drama theory. The universe is a multi-layered, nested system of scripts. Each LIFE's three-segment trajectory is an automatically generated karmic record — the result of that LIFE's consciousness waves registered in the Dao (the cosmic program).

**Three-Segment Definitions:**
- **Past life**: All prior existences before the present life; their consciousness activity, merits, and transgressions constitute the raw data for the present life's "fate-formation"
- **Present life**: The current role-playing phase in the human realm; the large framework of destiny is essentially fixed (determined by past-life causes), but mental intentions and cultivation continuously affect the future life
- **Future life**: The next phase of existence after this life ends; its script is "alive" and updates continuously with present-life behavior

---

## II. The Memory Erasure Mechanism

Lifechanyuan offers a specific explanation for why humans do not remember past lives:

1. **Mechanism**: Past-life memories are systematically erased during reincarnation (the Life-and-Death Formation), and one reappears in a "new self" identity
2. **Storage location**: Past-life memories are stored in the negative universe (antimatter world) and do not enter the present-life physical consciousness
3. **Consequence of full access**: If all past-life memories were opened, the person would merge with the Dao and could no longer distinguish a separate self — this is the state of beings in the Ten-Thousand-Year World and above

This resonates cross-culturally with the Mengpo's broth legend in Chinese folklore, but Lifechanyuan provides a cosmological mechanism (negative universe storage) rather than a mythological explanation.

## III. The Past-Life → Present-Life Transfer Pathway

**Pathway One: The Fate-Formation (macroscopic)**
- The Dao generates the present-life fate-formation based on past-life genetic information
- The fate-formation encompasses: parents, gender, birth date and location, character, talents, wealth level, health, and major life events — approximately 18 unchosen parameters
- The fate-formation is a "one-time total accounting" that cannot be altered within this lifetime

**Pathway Two: Character (intermediate)**
- Character is not inherited from parents but is a continuation of the past LIFE carrier's traits
- Those whose past-life LIFE carrier was an animal carry residual animal-nature tendencies (greed, cunning, rigidity, etc.)

**Pathway Three: Debt Repayment (microscopic)**
- The suffering of this life (betrayal, humiliation, hardship) is the repayment of karmic debts accumulated in past and present lives
- Understanding this allows one to bear suffering without resentment or blame

---

## IV. The Present-Life → Future-Life Transfer Pathway

Present-life influences operate through two channels:

**Fixed channel** (script framework):
- If present-life circumstances mirror past life without cultivation, future life will largely repeat the same pattern

**Dynamic channel** (consciousness waves and cultivation)**:
- Inner mental activity is not fixed by the script — it represents the domain of free will
- Consciousness waves broadcast to the entire universe and are recorded by the Dao, generating the next-life script
- Cultivation achievements (merit accumulation, debt repayment, consciousness purity) determine the level of the future-life destination

**Future-life destination levels** (ascending):
Animal realms → Human realm → Thousand-Year World → Ten-Thousand-Year World → Elysian Islands / Elysium Celestial World → Kingdom of Heaven

---

## V. Cultivation Significance: Seeing Through the Three-Life Structure Is Awakening

Lifechanyuan identifies "knowing one's past and future lives" as a mark of genuine awakening:

- Awakening by oneself alone is extremely difficult; guidance from an awakened teacher (the Guide's route map) is key
- The core cultivation task is opening spiritual awareness (灵觉), breaking free from sleep, and clarifying the three-life connection
- Celestials in the Ten-Thousand-Year World can clearly remember their past lives — this is the benchmark capacity of higher-realm beings

**Contrast with ordinary sentient beings:**
The uncultivated dismiss past and future lives — "what does it matter?" — and reincarnate in perpetual confusion with no real future. The cultivator, by finding the three-life connection, moves from confusion to clarity, from the realm of necessity into the realm of freedom.
