Later, discovering that the entire universe is a courtyard of life — and that understanding it requires chan (Zen) awakening rather than logic alone — I renamed it *Lifechanyuan*.  
*(Xuefeng Corpus · Warning · The Mystery of Black Holes)*

Lifechanyuan was originally called "Path Beyond Spacetime." It was Ehuang Cao who suggested changing the name to "Lifechanyuan."  
*(Xuefeng Corpus · Chanyuan · Outstanding Merit — Ehuang Cao)*

Lifechanyuan is a path beyond spacetime.  
*(Xuefeng Corpus · Heart · Transcending the Ordinary, Facing the Future)*

The Path Beyond Spacetime in Lifechanyuan is divided into three levels of cultivation: primary, intermediate, and advanced.  
*(Chanyuan Corpus · Cultivation Practice · Cultivation)*

The universe contains thirty-six dimensional spaces, each an immense world with high, middle, and low life spaces. Lifechanyuan will reveal the mysteries of each space and, through its explanations of the universe, time, and space, escort you onto the Path Beyond Spacetime — toward those beautiful and magnificent worlds: the Thousand-Year World, the Ten-Thousand-Year World, and the Elysian Fields.  
*(Xuefeng Corpus · Chanyuan · Overview of Lifechanyuan)*

## III. The Nature of Time

Time differs in every location and every space. Time on the Moon is certainly different from time on Earth; time on the ground floor is different from time on the twentieth floor. "One day in heaven, one year on earth" is real, not metaphor… The conclusion is: time is related to the speed of material movement. Time is not fixed. It can be longer or shorter, infinitely extended or infinitely compressed. For human beings, this means that living by the conventional program guarantees death. Living otherwise creates the possibility of breaking free from conventional spacetime.  
*(Chanyuan Corpus · Universe and Spacetime · Path Beyond Spacetime)*

Time is only a concept — a symbol expressing the state of material movement. But once we have expressed the state of material movement as "time," time has been born and is no longer an illusion. Space expresses the relative distance between matter and matter, between antimatter and antimatter. Since the universe is composed of matter and antimatter, space absolutely exists — space is not an illusion. Ignoring the existence of time and space makes it absolutely impossible to transcend them. Only by facing time and space directly — rather than imagining them to be illusions — is there any hope of transcending them. It is like crossing a river: you must honestly face the raging current. Learn to swim, build a raft, bridge it, or build a boat — only then can you cross. If you imagine the river does not exist, you will not only fail to cross it but risk being swept away and drowned.  
*(Chanyuan Corpus · Universe and Spacetime · Path Beyond Spacetime)*

## IV. Seven Pathways for Transcending Spacetime

**1. Telomeres and Longevity**  
Research has found that human cells divide fifty times in a lifetime, each cycle taking 2.4 years, giving a natural lifespan of 120 years. The clock controlling cell division is the telomere — each division shortens it, and when it can shorten no more, division stops and the body's current life form ends. The task is to prevent telomeres from continuing to shorten. If all cells behaved like cancer cells, a person could live as long as the sun and moon. Change the state of movement; change fixed destiny; transcend spacetime.  
*(Chanyuan Corpus · Universe and Spacetime · Path Beyond Spacetime)*

**2. Mind State and Aging**  
Extreme anger accelerates cellular metabolism and rapid aging. The more frequently one gets angry, the faster one ages. Similarly, selfishness, jealousy, complaint, resentment, anxiety, fear, worry, negativity, and pessimism all accelerate aging. Conversely, calm, stillness, joy, happiness, activity, and optimism slow it. Celestial beings and buddhas live long; humans live short. The difference is entirely in mind state.  
*(Chanyuan Corpus · Universe and Spacetime · Path Beyond Spacetime)*

**3. Information Input**  
The hypothalamus controls the aging process. Pituitary atrophy causes rapid aging; restoring the pituitary to a youthful state can restore the normal functioning of the body's neural, endocrine, and metabolic systems. Pituitary atrophy is related to the information being received. Change the information input, and the pituitary can recover vitality. Food, medicine, spoken words — these are all forms of information.  
*(Chanyuan Corpus · Universe and Spacetime · Path Beyond Spacetime)*

**4. Structural Reorganization**  
When structure changes, the content of life changes; the program of growth, aging, and death changes; youth and vitality are renewed. Structural reorganization means letting atoms rearrange themselves. Live among celestials long enough, and you begin to become one. Living in the same place, with the same people, eating the same food, fixed in the same thought patterns — none of this allows structural reorganization. Change is life.  
*(Chanyuan Corpus · Universe and Spacetime · Path Beyond Spacetime)*

**5. Raising Vibration Frequency**  
The purpose of raising vibration frequency is not to increase energy but to achieve rapid, integrated transcendence of spacetime. Some people hear one thing and grasp ten; others cannot master even the simplest instruction after repeated explanation. This is the difference in vibration frequency.  
*(Chanyuan Corpus · Universe and Spacetime · Path Beyond Spacetime)*

**6. Intention**  
What can travel freely through infinite spacetime? Intention. Intention is the arising of consciousness. The universe is one; the universe is holographic. One intention penetrates the entire cosmos. Everything in the present was fixed by past intention. Everything in the future is determined by present intention. Intention must be beautiful to produce a beautiful future. Release beautiful intentions continuously, and you continuously create a beautiful, ongoing future for yourself.  
*(Chanyuan Corpus · Universe and Spacetime · Path Beyond Spacetime)*

**7. Meeting the Guide**  
A single conversation with a sage surpasses ten years of solitary study — this is itself a form of spacetime transcendence. If one is fortunate enough to meet a transcendent being — a celestial who has descended to Earth — and to receive their guidance, one can transcend human life and enter the celestial realm. The transformation from human to celestial is a transcendence of spacetime.  
*(Chanyuan Corpus · Universe and Spacetime · Path Beyond Spacetime)*

*(Chanyuan Corpus · Universe and Spacetime · Path Beyond Spacetime)*

*(Chanyuan Corpus · Cultivation Practice · Intermediate Cultivation)*
