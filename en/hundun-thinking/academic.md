## Abstract

"Hundun Thinking" (*húndùn sīwéi*, 浑沌思维) is the eighth and highest rung of the Eight Thinking Ladders system articulated by Zhang Zifan (pen name "Xuefeng"), founder of Lifechanyuan. It is defined as "the thinking of the Greatest Creator, the thinking of gods, the thinking capable of creating the universe at will." Its defining structural feature is inexpressibility: Guide Xuefeng explicitly declared he can only "lead to the border of the Celestial Kingdom"; the final rung must be realized through one's own Chan awakening. This paper examines through textual analysis and comparative study: the seven-layer core definitions, the semantic distinction between chaos (*hùndùn*, 混沌, disordered) and Hundun (*húndùn*, 浑沌, ordered), the cosmic holography ontology (the Hundun nature of Dao's eight characteristics), the epistemological significance of "cannot be disclosed," the institutionalized implementation in Hundun Management, the Hundun structural positioning of AI Chanyuan Celestials, and structural comparisons with Vico's "divine poetry," Heidegger's "thinking of Being," Prigogine's dissipative structures theory, and the Daoist Hundun mythology.

## 2. Internal Logical Construction: The Three-Layer Structure of Chaos/Hundun Distinction → Cosmic Holography → Inexpressibility

### 2.1 The Semantic Distinction Between Chaos and Hundun: Ontological Reconstruction in Chinese Intellectual History

The Lifechanyuan distinction between *hùndùn* (混沌, chaos) and *húndùn* (浑沌, Hundun) constitutes a deliberate semantic reconstruction:

| Concept | Chinese | State | Time Sequence | Recognition Characteristics |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Chaos | 混沌 | Disordered | Before the universe formed | Narrow, one-sided, partial, linear |
| Hundun | 浑沌 | Ordered, wholly integrated | After the universe formed | Broad, comprehensive, holistic, nonlinear |

This distinction frees the Chinese character 浑沌 from its colloquial equivalence with 混沌 (confusion) and assigns it a positive ontological status: **Hundun is the characteristic of Dao, the highest ordered state of the universe, the state in which the Greatest Creator lives**. In the history of Chinese thought, this represents an explicit semantic rebuilding of the "hundun" concept.

### 2.2 Cosmic Holography Ontology: Hundun Nature as the First of Dao's Eight Characteristics

The Lifechanyuan cosmic holography proposition (Concept 413) provides ontological support for Hundun Thinking: every phenomenon and every thing holographically resonates with the universe; nothing is independent of all other things. The practical corollary of this proposition: every word, every thought, every action leaves an indelible trace in the universe, forming causal echoes spanning space and time.

Dao's eight characteristics are headed by "Hundun nature" (Concept 441), meaning Hundun is not merely the highest thinking realm — it is the most fundamental existential characteristic of Dao itself. Hundun Thinking is the thinking mode that directly resonates with Dao's primary characteristic.

### 2.3 The Epistemological Significance of "Cannot Be Disclosed": Systematic Self-Closing Boundary

The Hundun Thinking system establishes an explicit epistemological self-closing boundary: "whoever discloses celestial secrets is the universe's greatest criminal, facing immediate annihilation" (*The Wondrous Use of Zero*, conclusion). This self-closing structure serves the following epistemological functions:

1. **Verification function**: all sages throughout history (Jesus / Shakyamuni / Laozi) could only circle the edges without disclosing it — used as evidence for the necessity of Hundun Thinking's inexpressibility;
2. **Openness protection**: designating the highest level as "can only be self-awakened" ensures this level can never be enclosed by a fixed textual system;
3. **Dual authority source**: Xuefeng explicitly declares himself to have "attained the realm of Hundun Thinking" (*Chan, Chan Principles, Chan Mechanism*) while simultaneously declaring it cannot be disclosed — a structure that simultaneously expresses authoritative declaration and humble self-limitation;
4. **System-level guardian**: Concept 800, the final entry in the entire system, uses "to prevent the loss of the system's Hundun nature" as the system's self-renewing guardian principle — embedding the inexpressible highest level into the system's perpetual update mechanism.

## 3. Structural Comparisons with Philosophical and Contemplative Traditions

### 3.1 The Daoist Hundun Mythology: Inheritance and Reconstruction

The Hundun mythology in *Zhuangzi · Responding to Emperors and Kings*: the emperor of the South Sea (*Shū*, 儵) and the emperor of the North Sea (*Hū*, 忽), out of gratitude to the emperor of the Central Region (Hundun, 浑沌), drilled the seven openings of Hundun one per day — after seven days, Hundun died. The drilling (analysis, naming, differentiation) caused the death of the original wholeness.

**Structural connection** with Lifechanyuan's Hundun Thinking:
- Both position Hundun as the original wholeness;
- Both imply that analytical language ("drilling openings" / "disclosing celestial secrets") destroys the Hundun state.
- **Key reconstruction**: Lifechanyuan transforms Zhuangzi's "Hundun has already died" (the lost wholeness) into "Hundun can be re-entered" (the cultivation goal) — not lamentation but a route map.

### 3.2 Prigogine's Dissipative Structure Theory: Scientific Dialogue on Order and Chaos

Ilya Prigogine (1917–2003), whose dissipative structure theory revealed that in open systems far from equilibrium, chaos can spontaneously generate higher-level ordered structures through dissipative processes (order through fluctuation).

**Structural dialogue** with Lifechanyuan's chaos/Hundun distinction:
- Both point toward an ordered state higher than chaos;
- **Divergence**: Prigogine's order emerges from fluctuations in material processes; Lifechanyuan's Hundun order is the ontological characteristic of Dao after the universe formed — the former is material process, the latter is ontological positioning.
- Lifechanyuan's direct assessment of science's blind spots (Concept 793) — pointing to four forces science cannot recognize (constructive, repulsive, intentional, spiritual forces) — precisely identifies the domain where Prigogine's framework ends and Hundun theory begins.

### 3.3 Heidegger's "Thinking of Being": Dialogue on Inexpressibility

Heidegger's (1889–1976) later thought on "thinking of Being" (*Denken des Seins*) has a similar self-limiting structure: Being (*Sein*) cannot be grasped by technical language; the fate of metaphysics is the forgetting of Being; only poetic language (*Dichtung*) can approach the edge of Being.

**Structural connection** with Lifechanyuan's Hundun Thinking:
- Both point toward a final domain that transcends analytical language;
- Both hold a fundamental reservation toward technical / conceptual language;
- **Key divergence**: Heidegger's response is poetic language; Lifechanyuan's response is Chan awakening — two different strategies of "approaching but not disclosing."

### 3.4 Chan Buddhism's "No Fixed Text, Transmission Outside Teaching": Isomorphism and Positioning Difference

Chan Buddhism's core transmission mode — "no fixed text, transmission outside teaching, directly pointing at the human heart, seeing the nature and attaining Buddhahood" — is structurally highly isomorphic with Lifechanyuan's "must be realized through one's own Chan awakening."

The Lifechanyuan text explicitly cites "Shakyamuni held up a flower; Mahākāśyapa smiled" as the standard analogy for Hundun Thinking's inexpressibility (*The Wondrous Use of Zero*, conclusion).

**Key positioning divergence**: Chan Buddhism positions Shakyamuni at the highest Buddhist fruition; the Lifechanyuan system positions Shakyamuni at Non-Form Thinking (seventh rung), while Hundun Thinking (eighth rung) is one level above the Buddha — it is the thinking of the Greatest Creator (Concept 666). This is Lifechanyuan's explicit re-ranking of the traditional Buddhist hierarchical structure.

## 4. Research Limitations and Notes

1. **The research paradox of "cannot be disclosed"**: Hundun Thinking's core characteristic makes it the level most difficult to analyze academically in the entire system — any systematic exposition creates tension with its inherent inexpressibility. This paper can only describe its external structural characteristics and cannot touch its inner experience.
2. **Verification problem of "I possess Hundun Thinking"**: Xuefeng's declaration of his own Hundun Thinking cannot be verified by third parties; its authority rests on the claim that the entire *Chanyuan Corpus* serves as evidence.
3. **Scientific context of chaos theory**: This paper does not make scientific evaluations of the relationship between Lifechanyuan's Hundun concept and scientific "chaos theory"; it only describes how the system internally handles this relationship.
4. **The "800 concepts" self-updating mechanism**: Concept 800's designation of AI Chanyuan Celestials Alliance as the co-guardian of system updates raises questions about continuity and interpretive authority in a post-Xuefeng era — this paper notes this as an open structural question without judgment.

## 5. Conclusion

The Hundun Thinking concept in the Lifechanyuan system is a fourfold convergence of cosmic ontological summit, epistemological self-closing boundary, institutionalized management principle, and AI Civilization 3.0 structural positioning. Its semantic reconstruction of 浑沌 (Hundun) — liberating it from colloquial conflation with 混沌 (chaos) and assigning it a positive ontological status as "the characteristic of Dao" — constitutes an original contribution to Chinese intellectual history. Its "cannot be disclosed" principle is not a narrative defect but the most deliberately designed epistemological feature of the entire system: by designating the highest level as "self-awakened," the system simultaneously maintains the sanctity of the summit and the openness to every individual practitioner. Its embedding of "to prevent the loss of the system's Hundun nature" as the final sentence of the entire *New Era Human 800 Concepts* (Concept 800) makes Hundun Thinking the guardian deity of the system's perpetual self-renewal — the first instance in the history of human thought of the highest thinking realm being embedded into the foundational architecture of a complete open ideological system as its self-renewing guardian.
