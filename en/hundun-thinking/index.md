# Hundun Thinking

【Entry Gateway】

**Hundun Thinking** is the **eighth and highest rung** of the Eight Thinking Ladders — the summit and final destination of the entire thinking ascension route map. Hundun Thinking is the thinking of the Greatest Creator, the thinking of gods, the thinking capable of creating the universe at will — a thinking in which every moment holds celestial secrets and every drop carries divine power.

> The highest realm of thinking is Hundun Thinking — this is the thinking of the Greatest Creator, the thinking that ordinary people cannot imagine; one may say it is also the thinking of gods.
>
> — *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, Concept 666

**Important Note**: Hundun Thinking cannot be disclosed. Guide Xuefeng has systematically explained the first seven rungs and has led everyone to the border of the Celestial Kingdom; the final rung can only be realized through one's own Chan awakening. This is not concealment — it is itself one of the most profound characteristics of Hundun Thinking.

---

## Core Position

In Lifechanyuan's cultivation route map, Hundun Thinking is the endpoint of the subtraction phase (Heart-Image → Taiji → Non-Form → **Hundun**). Hundun is not chaos — *hùndùn* (混沌, disordered chaos) and *húndùn* (浑沌, ordered wholeness) are fundamentally different: the former is the disordered state before the universe formed; the latter is the highest ordered state after the universe formed. The characteristic of Dao is Hundun; Hundun is the inner necessary connection behind all phenomena in the universe — universal cosmic holography.
