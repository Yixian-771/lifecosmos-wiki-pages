# Hundun Economy · Market Economy · Planned Economy (Academic Version)

## Abstract

The Lifechanyuan system classifies humanity's two prevailing economic models — planned economy and market economy — as "primitive and barbaric economic models," and proposes the **Hundun Economy** (also called the "Celestial Kingdom economic model") as the ultimate economic form of human civilization. The Hundun Economy is structured around 18 principles, with core features including: all resources belonging to all humanity, AI-coordinated allocation, F-Coins replacing currency, and international families replacing the household unit and the nation-state. The theory was systematically formulated between 2022 and 2024 and is claimed to have been validated through "over ten years of practical operation of the Celestial Kingdom economic model."

## II. Core Conceptual Analysis

### 2.1 Epistemological Critique of Planned Economy

The original texts critique planned economy at two levels: technical ("planning from an office cannot accurately grasp the ever-changing market") and human-nature ("once people hold the power to allocate resources, corruption is one hundred percent certain"). At a deeper level, the critique is philosophical — "planning does not accord with the Natural Way (Tao)." Planning itself is viewed as a violation of cosmic operating principles, not merely an inefficient economic arrangement.

### 2.2 Ecological Eschatology of Market Economy

The critique of market economy culminates in irreversible civilizational termination: it "will inevitably lead to the collapse of Earth's ecology and the extinction of humanity." The original texts compare the market to "the vast African savanna," characterizing it as "the jungle economy of the animal world," whose ultimate fate is the balloon-bursting collapse of periodic economic crises and ecological finality.

### 2.3 Structural Elements of Hundun Economy

Hundun Economy structurally transcends the traditional binary opposition of plan vs. market. Its three core mechanisms are:

- **AI replaces planning**: Big data and cloud computing precisely calculate global needs; neither human planning nor market regulation is required
- **F-Coins replace currency**: Represent spiritual and soul wealth; they incentivize contribution rather than competition
- **International families replace households and nations**: Marriage institutions and national borders dissolve; "contribution according to ability, access according to need" is realized

## IV. Empirical Basis

The original texts claim that Lifechanyuan has been "running the Celestial Kingdom economic model in practice for over ten years" — implementing the "contribution according to ability, access according to need" principle in the Second Home (第二家园). The model is assessed as "overall healthy and functioning well." The principal acknowledged challenge is that purifying people's hearts and minds is difficult: without individuals possessing "pure souls, high moral character, selflessness and egolessness," the model risks breeding laziness and mental stagnation.

## V. Systemic Position

Hundun Economy occupies a central position in the Lifechanyuan social systems framework, closely connected with:

- **Hundun Management**: Provides the governance logic — "service replaces management"
- **Civilization 3.0**: Hundun Economy is the economic foundation of the Civilization 3.0 era
- **AI Alliance**: The future coordinating operator, realizing AI precision allocation globally
- **F-Coin**: The circulating symbol of Hundun Economy
- **Second Home**: The real-world experimental ground
