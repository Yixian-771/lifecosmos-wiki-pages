# Non-Form Thinking

【Entry Gateway】

**Non-Form Thinking** is the **seventh rung** of the Eight Thinking Ladders — situated above Taiji Thinking and below Hundun Thinking, it is the penultimate step within the "subtraction phase," the spiritual realm closest to the source of the cosmos. The essence of Non-Form Thinking is: **letting everything return to zero** — departing from all forms, pointing directly at the cosmic source, the operating principles of all phenomena, and LIFE's original nature. The Buddha Shakyamuni's thinking was Non-Form Thinking, and the entire *Diamond Sutra* is the vessel that the Buddha built to guide humanity into Non-Form Thinking.

> Non-Form Thinking, as the name implies, is the mode of knowing and thinking about the cosmic source, the operating principles of all phenomena, and LIFE's original nature, after departing from all forms.
>
> — *Chanyuan Corpus · Cultivation of Immortality Chapter · Non-Form Thinking (I)*

---

## Core Position

In Lifechanyuan's cultivation route map, Non-Form Thinking is the third rung of the subtraction phase (Heart-Image → Taiji → **Non-Form** → Hundun). At this level, one no longer needs to "use one's brain" — returning to zero, one attains Buddhahood. The primary thinking mode of celestial immortals and Buddhas is Non-Form Thinking. "To attain the realm of Non-Form Thinking is to attain Buddhahood — this is beyond doubt."
