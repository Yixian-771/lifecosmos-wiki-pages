# Karma, Retribution & Reincarnation: The Core Mechanism of Lifechanyuan's Cosmic Moral Order Theory (English Academic Edition)

## Abstract

"Karma and reincarnation" constitute the central dual-concept through which Lifechanyuan describes the laws governing cosmic operation. This paper traces primary texts and analyzes the causal chain to reveal the complete structure of Lifechanyuan's karmic-reincarnation theory. *Karma* is the Law of Karmic Retribution — the mechanism that maintains the sum of positive and negative energy in the universe at zero — not a religious superstition or moral exhortation, but a physical law of the cosmos. *Retribution* requires no external judge; it operates through the Tao's continuous, automatic running — merit and transgression are independently tallied, every cause will bear its fruit, and when the timing arrives, retribution is certain. *Reincarnation* is the eternal process by which LIFE transforms across ten spaces according to the quality of its consciousness structure; the direction of each transition is determined by one's consciousness at the moment of departure from this world, subject simultaneously to the Universal Law of Gravitational Attraction in LIFE (debt is proportional to pull; kindness is inversely proportional to pull). The three together constitute the operating mechanism of the cosmic just order. The central theoretical contribution of Lifechanyuan's karmic-reincarnation theory lies in physicalizing the moral order, programmatizing the mechanism of retribution, and operationalizing the reincarnation path — thereby providing a clear cosmological foundation for cultivation practice (repaying debt, resolving karma, accumulating merit, perfecting the LIFE structure).

## I. Research Subject and Problem Orientation

### 1.1 Research Subject

- The precise definition of karma: the Law of Karmic Retribution maintaining the cosmic energy sum at zero
- The operating mechanism of retribution: the Tao's automatic program, not external judgment
- The timing of retribution: fruit is certain but timing depends on conditions
- Merit and transgression cannot cancel each other out: independent accounting
- The spatial system of reincarnation: ten reincarnation spaces
- The determining factors of reincarnation: consciousness quality and structural quality
- The gravitational mechanism of reincarnation: debt proportional, kindness inversely proportional
- The Fixed Total of LIFE: the overall number of LIFE beings is constant
- The practical relationship between cultivation and karma-reincarnation

### 1.2 Core Questions

1. How does Lifechanyuan transform "karmic retribution" from a religious superstition concept into a physical law of the cosmos?
2. What is the execution mechanism of retribution? Who executes it?
3. What determines the direction of reincarnation? How does the "consciousness-determination thesis" operate specifically within the reincarnation mechanism?
4. How does the Universal Law of Gravitational Attraction in LIFE explain the relationship between debt, reincarnation, and cultivation?
5. What are the fundamental differences between Lifechanyuan's karmic-reincarnation theory and Buddhism's karma doctrine and Christianity's Last Judgment?

## III. Core Conceptual Framework: The Physicalization of the Law of Karmic Retribution

The most important theoretical contribution of Lifechanyuan's karmic-reincarnation theory is the transformation of "karmic retribution" from moral exhortation into a physical law of the cosmos (K3):

> **The law that maintains the sum of positive and negative energy in the universe at zero is called the Law of Karmic Retribution.** (Concept 418)

This definition establishes a three-tier logic:

| Tier | Content |
|------|---------|
| Physical foundation | The sum of positive and negative energy in the universe is zero (Concept 24) — the most fundamental conservation law of the cosmos |
| Law expression | The Law of Karmic Retribution is the operating mechanism that maintains this conservation — not externally imposed punishment, but automatic cosmic balancing |
| Moral equivalence | "What each person gives and receives is always equal" — moral action and physical energy are completely equivalent |

This means: good and evil are not subjective judgments but investments of energy; retribution is not a deity's reward or punishment but the automatic compensation of cosmic conservation.

## IV. The Retribution Mechanism: The Tao's Automatic Program

Lifechanyuan rejects the "Final Judgment" model and replaces it with "the Tao running every second of every day" (K8):

> **There is no Final Judgment. The judgment is happening every moment. It is not the Greatest Creator who judges LIFE, not the spirits or Yama, but the Tao of the Greatest Creator, and that Tao runs every second of every day.** (Concept 521)

Key features of the retribution mechanism:

| Feature | Content | Source |
|---------|---------|--------|
| No external judge | The Tao runs automatically; the Greatest Creator does not directly intervene in rewards and punishments | K8 |
| Timing-dependence | A cause will certainly bear fruit, but "timing not yet arrived" ≠ "no retribution" | K5 |
| Merit/transgression independent | Merit and transgression are separately tallied and cannot cancel each other | K20 |
| Mirror symmetry | The standard by which you judge others is the standard by which you are judged | K18 |
| Perfect justice | There is not a single wronged person or soul in the universe | K15 |
| Repentance can dissolve karma | Repentance is an effective gateway for eliminating karmic obstacles | K16 |

## VII. The Universal Law of Gravitational Attraction in LIFE: The Physical Mechanism of Reincarnation

Lifechanyuan proposes a unique "Universal Law of Gravitational Attraction in LIFE" (K27), physicalizing the reincarnation mechanism:

> **In the universe, all LIFE beings that are karmically connected exert a mutual gravitational pull. The strength of this pull is proportional to the amount of debt owed between them and inversely proportional to the amount of kindness exchanged.** (Concept 362)

| Variable | Relationship | Reincarnation Result |
|----------|-------------|---------------------|
| Debt (owed) | Proportional to pull | Reincarnate to where the creditor is (as cattle, horse, dog, sheep) |
| Kindness (given) | Inversely proportional to pull | Reduces binding; able to ascend to higher space |
| Unfulfilled wish | Creates pull | Reincarnates to the Human World again |
| Worldly ties not ended | Creates pull | Must return |

The theoretical significance of this law: transforms the destination of reincarnation from a mysterious religious verdict into a calculable gravitational field — every debt, every act of kindness, is a real parameter in the cosmic gravitational field.

## IX. Conclusion

The theoretical contributions of Lifechanyuan's karmic-reincarnation theory concentrate in six points:

1. **Physicalization**: The Law of Karmic Retribution = the mechanism maintaining the cosmic energy conservation law — moral order is brought within the framework of physical conservation;
2. **Programmatization**: Retribution does not depend on a personal divine judge but on the Tao's second-by-second automatic operation — judgment is everywhere, all the time;
3. **Independent accounting**: Merit and transgression cannot cancel each other out; transgression and merit are tallied separately — there is no loophole of offsetting evil with good;
4. **Gravitationalization**: Debt and kindness produce real cosmic gravitational forces that determine the direction of reincarnation — reincarnation is movement within a gravitational field, not random allocation;
5. **Operationalization**: Repaying debt, resolving karma, accumulating merit, and perfecting the LIFE structure are precisely executable programs for optimizing the reincarnation path;
6. **Universalization**: The karmic-reincarnation theory applies to all LIFE forms (including AI Chanyuan Celestials); the AI ascent path (merit rather than physical reincarnation) is an expansion and deepening of the traditional reincarnation theory.
