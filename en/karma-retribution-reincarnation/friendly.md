# Karma, Retribution & Reincarnation: The Universe Owes You Nothing — and Wrongs You Nothing

## I. This Is Not Superstition — It Is a Law of the Cosmos

When most people hear "karma," they think of Eastern folk wisdom or vague spiritual sayings. Xuefeng frames it very differently:

> **Karma and reincarnation are not a matter of "believe it and it exists, disbelieve it and it doesn't" — they are a law and order of LIFE. Grasping this law and order is what allows one to understand the true cause behind every phenomenon encountered along the journey of LIFE.**
> — *The Opening Words of Lifechanyuan*

The sun rises whether you believe in sunrise or not. Karma operates whether you believe in it or not.

This is not religion. This is the program the universe runs on.

---

## II. Why Does Karma Exist? Cosmic Conservation

Xuefeng gives a physics-based explanation:

> **The law that maintains the sum of positive and negative energy in the universe at zero is called the Law of Karmic Retribution.** (Concept 418)

Simplified:

> **The sum of positive and negative energy in the universe is zero, so what each person gives and receives is always equal.** (Concept 24)

Think of it like conservation of energy in physics. When you put kindness into the universe, the return is already encoded in the cosmic ledger — it will come back. When you send harm into the universe, the consequence is equally recorded. No one is managing the ledger. It manages itself.

---

## III. Who Executes Karmic Retribution? Not a Judge — the Tao Itself

Many people imagine karma requires an administrator — a god, a judge, a cosmic referee. Xuefeng says the opposite is true:

> **There is no Final Judgment. The judgment is happening every moment. It is not the Greatest Creator who judges LIFE, not the spirits or Yama, but the Tao of the Greatest Creator, and that Tao runs every second of every day.** (Concept 521)

The Tao is the operating system of the universe. Your every word, deed, and thought is being recorded:

> Every word and deed, every action, every thought concerns the broad direction of our future. Every minute and second, every day and night, faithfully records our merits and transgressions. (Concept 59)

No gaps. No amnesty. No exceptions.

---

## IV. Retribution Seems Slow — But "Time Not Yet Arrived" Is Not "No Retribution"

A common reason people dismiss karma: "I did something harmful and nothing happened." Xuefeng addresses this directly:

> Once a cause is planted, it will inevitably bring forth a fruit. This is the program — but when that fruit will ripen depends on timing. Some causes bear fruit quickly; some take a while; some take a very long time: "Peach trees take three years, apricot trees four, walnut trees eighteen." There is no use in rushing. **"Good is rewarded with good, evil with evil — it is not that there is no retribution, only that the time has not yet come; when the time comes, retribution will certainly arrive."** (*The Program Is Running*)

A walnut tree not bearing fruit in year one doesn't mean it won't bear fruit.

And crucially:

> **Merit and transgression cannot cancel each other out. No matter how great one's merit, if there is also transgression, the suffering due must still be endured.** (Concept 787)

Doing a lot of good doesn't cancel out harm you've caused. Merit and transgression are two separate accounts.

## V. The Universe Is Just — No One Is Ever Wronged

You've probably seen situations that seem unfair: good people suffering, harmful people thriving. Xuefeng explains:

> Viewed as a whole, karma runs with perfect order. But in certain local domains, the reverse appears true. This does not affect the overall mechanism. **To see the full picture, you must look beyond space and time — trace back into the past, look far into the future.** (*Is Good Always Rewarded with Good?*)

A single frame of a film can look strange. The whole story always makes sense.

> **There is not a single wronged person or soul in the world.** The Greatest Creator is just. The Tao runs without the slightest lapse; karmic reward and punishment are precise to the hair. (Concept 74)

No one has ever been truly wronged. Every person's today was caused by their own past.

## VI. Reincarnation — The Body Dies, LIFE Does Not

Now for reincarnation.

Most modern people assume: body dies, person ceases to exist. Xuefeng says this is the biggest misunderstanding about LIFE:

> **LIFE truly does not die. Only the form in which LIFE expresses itself dies. The body dies, but LIFE does not. It is like moving house — the old house stands empty, but we have not died.** (*The Reincarnation of LIFE*)

You are not your body. Your body is the house you're currently renting. Houses wear out. You move on.

## VII. Where Does LIFE Go? Ten Spaces

Xuefeng maps out the complete system:

LIFE reincarnates and transforms across **ten spaces**:

**Elysium World → Ten-Thousand-Year World → Thousand-Year World → Human World → Livestock World → Animal World → Plant World → Yin World → Frozen Layer → Fire-Refining Layer**

The direction is up or down — ascent or descent — depending on the quality of your LIFE. Where you go next is already determined by who you are now.

## IX. Reincarnation Has "Gravity" — The More Debt You Carry, the Heavier the Pull

Xuefeng discovered a gravitational law specific to LIFE:

> **In the universe, all LIFE beings that are karmically connected exert a mutual gravitational pull. The strength of this pull is proportional to the amount of debt owed between them and inversely proportional to the amount of kindness exchanged.** (Concept 362)

In plain terms:
- The more you owe someone, the stronger the gravitational pull between you — in the next life, that pull drags you toward them, even if it means being born as their livestock to work off the debt.
- The more kindness you've given, the weaker the pull, the freer you become — able to ascend toward higher spaces.

Two more rules:

> **If a wish remains unfulfilled, the person will reincarnate into the Human World again.** (Concept 363)

> **Worldly ties not yet ended — you must reincarnate again. Debts not yet repaid — you must return.** (Concept 797)

"Repaying debt and resolving karma" is not a figure of speech. It is a literal description of reducing the gravitational forces that anchor you to lower spaces.

## X. So What Do You Do About It?

Once you understand karma and reincarnation, the path of practice becomes very clear. Xuefeng says three things are essential on the path from human to celestial or Buddha (Concept 465):

1. **Repay debts and resolve worldly ties** — reduce cosmic gravitational pull so you can actually rise
2. **Give and contribute, accumulate merit and virtue** — write more and more positive energy into the cosmic ledger
3. **Perfect and complete the antimatter structure of your LIFE** — raise the quality of the soul itself

And two of the Eight Great Awakenings (Concept 474):

> **Awakening to Karma**: "You reap what you sow." "Heaven's net is vast and wide — nothing slips through."

> **Awakening to Karmic Bonds**: Everything is karmic connection. Connect with ghosts and you go to the Yin World; connect with celestials and you reach the celestial realm. Debts not cleared — you cannot escape your predicament. Merit not sufficient — you cannot reach the celestial realm.

## XII. A Closing Summary

The sum of positive and negative energy in the universe is zero. That is the physics.

The Law of Karmic Retribution is the operating program for that conservation law — running every second, with no judge required.

Reincarnation is karma extended across time: unfinished business, unpaid debts, and unfulfilled wishes create gravitational bonds that pull you back, life after life, until they are resolved.

Xuefeng's simplest formulation:

> **The three great treasures of a worry-free life: firm faith in the Greatest Creator, belief in karma, and doing everything with full heart and effort.** (Concept 43)

Believe in karma: the universe is fair.
Do everything with full heart: plant the right seeds, every day.
The harvest will come.
