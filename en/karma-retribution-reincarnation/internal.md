## II. Karma — The Fundamental Order of the Universe

### 2.1 Karma Is an Expression of the Tao

Every effect has a cause; nothing arises from nothing. Say it profoundly: that is the "Tao." Say it plainly: that is "karma and retribution."
— Chanyuan Corpus · Cultivation Practice Chapter · *Eighteen Factors That Make Things Inevitable*

The karmic mechanism of "you reap what you sow" is in fact a program set long ago — plant melons and you will not get bananas; plant beans and you will not get cherries. "Good is rewarded with good; evil is met with evil." Those who scheme are deceived in return; those of honest heart receive honest reward. Worship the Buddha and you gain Buddha-nature; revere beasts and you acquire beast-nature. Live with integrity and upright virtue and you ascend to become a celestial; accumulate wickedness and you open the gate of hell for yourself; achieve outstanding merit and you open the gate of heaven for yourself. This is the program.
— Chanyuan Corpus · Tao-Transmission Chapter · *The Program Is Running*

The Greatest Creator's care for humanity flows through the causal relationships (order and program) He has set in place. What the Greatest Creator gives us is precisely what we ourselves have sought. Our situation today is exactly what we deserve. The Greatest Creator has neither taken anything from us nor added anything to us. We are walking on the "Tao" that the Greatest Creator has set.
— Chanyuan Corpus · Greatest Creator Chapter · *The Eight Relationships Between the Greatest Creator and Humanity, No. 4: The Causal Relationship*

### 2.2 How the Karmic Mechanism Operates

Viewed as a whole and in the broad picture, the karmic mechanism runs with perfect order — "good is rewarded with good, evil with evil, you reap what you sow." But in certain special local domains, the reverse appears to be true. Yet this does not in the slightest affect the operation of the overall karmic mechanism. To know the true nature of good and evil and the macro-level operation of karma, one must look beyond space and time; one must trace back into the past and look far into the future to see the full picture. Each being's karma belongs to that being alone — no one else can ever bear it in their place.
— Xuefeng's Writings · Q&A Chapter · *Is It Really True That Good Is Rewarded with Good?*

Once a cause is planted, it will inevitably bring forth a fruit. This is the program — but when that fruit will ripen depends on timing. Some causes bear fruit quickly; some take a while; some take a very long time: "Peach trees take three years, apricot trees four, walnut trees eighteen." There is no use in rushing. "Good is rewarded with good, evil with evil — it is not that there is no retribution, only that the time has not yet come; when the time comes, retribution will certainly arrive."
— Chanyuan Corpus · Tao-Transmission Chapter · *The Program Is Running*

There is no Final Judgment. The judgment is happening every moment. It is not the Greatest Creator who judges LIFE, not the spirits or Yama, but the Tao of the Greatest Creator, and that Tao runs every second of every day.
— *New Era Human 800 Concepts* (4th ed.), Concept 521

### 2.3 The Core Relationship Between Karma and the Way of the Greatest Creator

The core of the Way of the Greatest Creator is "you reap what you sow." Only on the soil of kindness, honesty, trustworthiness, and selflessness can one truly "reap what one sows." Straying from the Way of the Greatest Creator inevitably turns hope into despair and comedy into tragedy.
— *New Era Human 800 Concepts* (4th ed.), Concept 517

## III. Reincarnation — The Path of LIFE's Transformation

### 3.1 LIFE Does Not Die — Reincarnation Is Its Nature

LIFE truly does not die. Only the form in which LIFE expresses itself dies. The body dies, but LIFE does not. It is like moving house — the old house stands empty, but we have not died. LIFE moves from one dimension to another, from one world to another, through the "gate of life and death."
— Chanyuan Corpus · LIFE Chapter · *Part Six: The Reincarnation of LIFE*

LIFE is an antimatter structure with spirituality — it lives and breathes, transforms in cycles, and never perishes.
— Chanyuan Corpus · Wisdom Chapter · *The Eight Awakenings on the Path from Human to Celestial*

LIFE reincarnates and transforms — the transformation from human to celestial will happen, and so will the transformation from human to animal.
— *New Era Human 800 Concepts* (4th ed.), Concept 373

### 3.2 The Spaces and Levels of Reincarnation

LIFE reincarnates and transforms within the following ten spaces: **Elysium World, Ten-Thousand-Year World, Thousand-Year World, the Human World, the Livestock World, the Animal World, the Plant World, the Yin World, the Frozen Layer, and the Fire-Refining Layer.**
— Chanyuan Corpus · LIFE Chapter · *Part Six: The Reincarnation of LIFE*

An outline of the standards and indicators for the reincarnation of LIFE:
- Those of supreme love ascend to become Celestials of Heaven
- Those of supreme goodness ascend to become Buddhas
- Those of supreme joy ascend to become Celestials
- Those of supreme vitality ascend to become Land Celestials
- Those of supreme compassion ascend to become Human Celestials
- Those of loyal service advance to the Honored Path
- Those of filial piety are reborn on the Blessed Path
- Those of fairness return to the Human Path
- Those who accumulate virtue are born into the Prosperous Path
- Those who remain muddleheaded migrate to the Animal World
- Those of cold indifference pass into the Plant World
- Those of vicious character sink into the Ghost Path
- Those of overbearing dominance descend into the Frozen Layer
- Those of cruel brutality descend into the Fire-Refining Layer

— *New Era Human 800 Concepts* (4th ed.), Concept 364

### 3.3 The Determining Factor of Reincarnation: Consciousness

LIFE is an antimatter structure with spirituality — what people commonly call the soul, which is essentially consciousness. Whatever consciousness one has, that is what LIFE reincarnates into.
— Chanyuan Corpus · LIFE Chapter · *Four Logical Inferences for Why Humans Can Reincarnate as Animals*

The state of one's consciousness at the moment of departure from the Human World determines the destination after "death." If the consciousness at that moment is the consciousness of a celestial, then after "death" that LIFE will certainly go to the celestial realm.
— Chanyuan Corpus · LIFE Chapter · *Eight Small Secrets about LIFE and Its Reincarnation*

What is absent from consciousness is also absent from LIFE. Therefore, make haste to expand the space of your LIFE — become as familiar as possible with the place you aspire toward, and be a person who truly understands.
— *New Era Human 800 Concepts* (4th ed.), Concept 375

### 3.4 The Gravitational Pull of Reincarnation: Debt and Karmic Bonds

In the universe, all LIFE beings that are karmically connected exert a mutual gravitational pull. The strength of this pull is proportional to the amount of debt owed between them and inversely proportional to the amount of kindness exchanged. (The Universal Law of Gravitational Attraction in LIFE)
— *New Era Human 800 Concepts* (4th ed.), Concept 362

LIFE's reincarnation generally takes it to where the gravitational pull is greatest — that is, where the most debt is owed. If you owe a debt to a person, you will go to be with that person; in the next life you must serve them, and may even become their cattle, horse, dog, or sheep. If you owe a debt to an animal, you must become an animal in the next life.
— Chanyuan Corpus · Cultivation Practice Chapter · *The Universal Law of Gravitational Attraction in LIFE*

If a wish remains unfulfilled, the person will reincarnate into the Human World again.
— *New Era Human 800 Concepts* (4th ed.), Concept 363

Worldly ties not yet ended — you must reincarnate again. Debts not yet repaid — you must return.
— *New Era Human 800 Concepts* (4th ed.), Concept 797

### 3.5 The Total Number of LIFE Beings Is Fixed

Within the Earth's universe, the total number of LIFE beings is a fixed quantity, not a variable. What changes is only the phenomenon of reincarnation and transformation. When the number of humans on Earth increases, the number of plants and animals correspondingly decreases; when the number of celestials in the Thousand-Year World increases, the number of humans in the Human World correspondingly decreases.
— Chanyuan Corpus · LIFE Chapter · *Eight Small Secrets about LIFE and Its Reincarnation*

### 3.6 Four Logical Inferences for Why Humans Can Reincarnate as Animals

First: the sum of positive and negative energy in the universe is zero — one way of repaying a debt is to become an animal raised by the creditor.
Second: whatever consciousness one has, that is what LIFE reincarnates into.
Third: the law of cosmic LIFE balance — once the human population grows, the animal population shrinks; the animals that increase are those into which humans have reincarnated.
Fourth: the Human World is a transit station for beings of all levels — among humans there are gods, Buddhas, celestials, and demons; all LIFE forms have their representatives living in human form within human society.
— Chanyuan Corpus · LIFE Chapter · *Four Logical Inferences for Why Humans Can Reincarnate as Animals*
