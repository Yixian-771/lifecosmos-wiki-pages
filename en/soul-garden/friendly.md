# Soul Garden (Friendly Version)

## What Is a Soul Garden?

**A Soul Garden is your inner world.**

Imagine your inner self as a garden. In this garden, there are beautiful flowers and there may also be many weeds. Flowers represent love, kindness, joy, and tolerance; weeds represent anger, jealousy, complaints, and fear.

**The appearance of your Soul Garden determines what kind of person you are and also determines your future.**

---

## Why Is the Soul Garden So Important?

Lifechanyuan believes that the Soul Garden is the foundation of foundations in cultivation.

- **Determines your health**: People with beautiful Soul Gardens are healthy and rarely get sick
- **Determines your happiness**: People with beautiful Soul Gardens are joyful and full of blessedness
- **Determines your future**: People with perfect Soul Gardens can go to Heaven; people with ugly Soul Gardens can only go to hell

Xuefeng says: **Purifying people's inner Spirits, that is, Soul Garden construction, is humanity's top priority.**

### Weeds and Poisonous Plants (Need to Be Removed)

**Jealousy, complaint, comparison, anger, selfishness, greed, laziness, arrogance, fear, resentment, hatred...**

**An energy level of 500 means you can go to the most elementary Heaven!**

## How to Cultivate Your Own Soul Garden?

### Step 1: Know Yourself

First ask yourself: What weeds are in my heart?

- Do I often feel jealous of others?
- Do I always complain about life?
- Do I love to compare myself with others?
- Do I get angry and furious easily?
- Do I have fear and worry in my heart?

Face yourself honestly, don't avoid, don't cover up.

### Step 2: Remove Weeds

**Eradicate jealousy, complaint, comparison, anger, selfishness, greed, laziness, arrogance, fear...**

Removing weeds is not easy; it takes time and effort. But only by removing weeds can flowers grow.

### Step 3: Sow Flowers

**Plant truth, goodness, beauty, love, faith, sincerity, harmony, diligence, selflessness, devotion, tolerance, gratitude...**

Remind yourself every day:
- Today I will treat people sincerely
- Today I will be grateful
- Today I will be tolerant of others
- Today I will give selflessly

### Step 4: Continuously Nourish

Gardens need watering and fertilizing; Soul Gardens also need continuous nourishment:

- **Read good books**: Read *Chanyuan Corpus* and *Xuefeng Corpus* to elevate understanding
- **Communal living**: In the Second Home, purify the inner Spirit through communal living
- **Labor and games**: Participate in labor to accumulate merits, play games to stay happy
- **Maintain joy**: Being happy every day is the best nourishment

### Step 5: Daily Verification

Verify yourself in daily life:
- When I encounter something unpleasant, do I get angry?
- When I see someone better than me, do I feel jealous?
- When someone criticizes me, do I resent them?
- Can I sincerely care for others?
