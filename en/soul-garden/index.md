# Soul Garden

[Gateway Page]

Soul Garden is one of the core cultivation concepts in the Lifechanyuan ideological system and the foundation of Chanyuan Celestials' cultivation practice.

---

## Core Definition

**Soul Garden is one's consciousness.**

The components within consciousness determine whether the Soul Garden is beautiful or ugly. The degree of perfection of the Soul Garden determines what kind of LIFE a person can achieve—a perfect Soul Garden enables one to become a Celestial Being or attain Buddhahood; an ugly Soul Garden leads to becoming a ghost or demon.

---

## Key Points

### Essence
- Soul Garden is consciousness
- Consciousness is the core of LIFE's antimatter structure  
- The state of the Soul Garden determines LIFE's destination and future

### Composition
- **Weeds and Poisonous Plants**: jealousy, complaint, comparison, anger, selfishness, greed, laziness, arrogance, fear, etc. (energy level <200)
- **Flowers and Crops**: truth, goodness, beauty, love, faith, sincerity, harmony, diligence, selflessness, devotion, tolerance, gratitude, etc. (energy level ≥200)
- **Watershed**: 200 is the watershed of consciousness energy level; 500 is the minimum standard for primary Heaven

### Importance
- The foremost gateway in cultivation, the foundation of foundations
- The basis of all construction in the Second Home
- The prerequisite for formatting human consciousness into Celestial Being consciousness
- Determines whether one can enter Heaven

### Construction Methods
1. Recognize and perceive one's own weeds
2. Remove weeds and poisonous plants
3. Sow flowers and crops
4. Continuously nourish (read Corpus, communal living, labor, games)
5. Daily comparison and verification
