# The Twenty Parallel Worlds (Friendly Version)

## The Universe Has More Than One World

We grow up thinking there is one world: Earth, the solar system, outer space.

Guide Xuefeng says: that's not quite right. The universe contains **twenty worlds existing simultaneously**, and the human realm where you and I live is only one of them.

This is not science fiction. It is a picture of the cosmos derived from mathematics.

---

## Drawing the Universe with Math

**Step 1**: Picture a flat coordinate system with an x-axis and a y-axis. Each axis runs positive and negative. That gives you four quadrants.

**Step 2**: Now add a z-axis — representing the imaginary dimension, the doorway to the antimatter world. The coordinate system becomes three-dimensional.

Run the factorial calculation: (5×4/2) + (5×4×3/6) = 10 + 10 = **20 quadrants**.

Each quadrant is a world.

> Mathematics is the abstract world; the world is concrete mathematics. There is no world outside mathematics, and no mathematics outside the world.
> *(Chanyuan Corpus · Antimatter World Chapter · The Twenty Parallel Worlds and Their Names)*

## Why Can't We See the Other Nineteen?

Simple: our five senses — eyes, ears, nose, tongue, skin — are only tuned to the human world.

The other nineteen worlds are not absent. Our spiritual sight (灵觉) has not been opened; our thinking is too constrained.

Xuefeng offers an analogy: when you dream, the dream world feels real. When you wake up, you can't see it anymore — but would you say it never existed?

> We regard them as illusory because our five senses cannot perceive them and our spiritual sight has not been opened. If spiritual sight opens and our thinking reaches that level, we will be able to "see" the other nineteen worlds.
> *(Chanyuan Corpus · Antimatter World Chapter · Space-Time Tunnels and the Twenty Parallel Worlds)*

## The Center of It All: The O-Point

The twenty worlds are not isolated from one another. They all converge at a single point — the **Cool Realm O-point**.

Think of it like a three-dimensional coordinate system: every axis passes through the origin "0." The twenty worlds are like twenty spokes meeting at the hub of a wheel.

**The essence of the Greatest Creator resides at this O-point.**

This is why Lifechanyuan keeps emphasizing "walk the path of the Greatest Creator" — only by passing through the O-point can you cross from one world to another. Without the Greatest Creator, you can only go in circles within a single world.

> Staying close to the Greatest Creator enables one to avoid extreme calamities.
> *(Chanyuan Corpus · Revelation Chapter · Extreme Heat Must Bring Extreme Cold)*
