# Spiritual Rebirth & Abortion

> Humanity has long assumed that a fetus in the womb is a living being — but that is not so. The fetus is merely a "dwelling place," not a life. Only when a reincarnating soul travels through the space tunnels of the 36-dimensional universe and moves into this dwelling does the fetus become a life. Generally speaking, the fetus only carries a living soul a few days before birth.
>
> — Chanyuan Corpus · Life Chapter · The Evolution of LIFE

**Spiritual rebirth** (投胎, literally "entering the womb") describes, in the Lifechanyuan framework, the specific mechanism by which a soul — a spiritualized antimatter structure — travels through the space tunnels of the 36-dimensional universe and enters the mother's womb (called the Positive Extreme Black Hole Body in the cosmic map) to begin a new life cycle. **Abortion** is an act that Guide Xuefeng explicitly expresses his hatred and strong opposition toward.
