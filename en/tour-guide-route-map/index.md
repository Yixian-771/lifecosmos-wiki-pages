# Tour Guide Route Map

[Gateway Page]

The Tour Guide Route Map is the central practical guideline of the Lifechanyuan system — the complete path that Guide Xuefeng has carefully charted to lead Chanyuan Celestials from the human world to the Thousand-Year World, Ten-Thousand-Year World, Elysium World, and Celestial Islands Continent.

It is not a philosophical abstraction. It is a concrete, operational, and verifiable path.

---

## Core Definition

> The Tour Guide Route Map of Lifechanyuan is: a person of virtue who does not contend over personal interests and is not entangled in worldly feelings, desires, and lusts — who studies Lifechanyuan concepts, concludes worldly affinities, enters the Life Oasis, performs meritorious work within it to repay karmic debts, accumulates treasures for Heaven, perfects the Soul Garden, and gradually uses the consciousness of celestials to reformat the consciousness of an ordinary person — in three steps:
> 1. Replace ordinary human consciousness with the consciousness of a Thousand-Year World celestial;
> 2. Replace Thousand-Year World celestial consciousness with Ten-Thousand-Year World celestial consciousness;
> 3. Replace Ten-Thousand-Year World celestial consciousness with Elysium–Celestial Islands Continent celestial consciousness — ultimately achieving the fruit of a celestial and proceeding to the Celestial Islands Continent.

*(Xuefeng Corpus · Heart Chapter · "Holding Constancy to Become the Perfected One")*

---

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence, while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence in this world.
