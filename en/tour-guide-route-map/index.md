# Tour Guide Route Map

[Entry Gateway]

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological
> essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across
> incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage
> of human existence in this world.

**The Tour Guide Route Map** (导游路线图) is the central practical guideline of the Lifechanyuan system — the complete, concrete, and operational path that Guide Xuefeng has charted to lead Chanyuan Celestials from the human world to the Thousand-Year World, Ten-Thousand-Year World, Elysium World, and Celestial Islands Continent. It is not a philosophical abstraction; it is a step-by-step map with verifiable milestones.

> The route map is not theory. It is the road itself.
>
> — Guide Xuefeng

---

## Core Positioning

In the Lifechanyuan system, the Tour Guide Route Map integrates soul garden construction, thinking elevation (the Eight Thinking Ladders), understanding of LIFE's mysteries, and the deepening of the six LIFE qualities into a single progression. Each milestone corresponds to a specific LIFE destination — understanding the eight types of thinking leads toward the Thousand-Year World; understanding the eight LIFE mysteries leads toward the Elysium–Celestial Islands Continent.
