# Beauty (美 Měi)

[Gateway Page]

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence in this world.

**Beauty** (美, Měi) is one of the six core LIFE qualities in the Lifechanyuan system — alongside Truth, Goodness, Love, Faith, and Sincerity — and is one of the intrinsic attributes of the universe, an attribute of the Greatest Creator, and one of the most important flowers in the soul garden of every Chanyuan Celestial. It is the direct index of a LIFE structure's degree of perfection, and the eternal driving force of human civilizational advance.

> Beauty lies in symmetrical proportion, and beauty also lies in simplicity and purity.
>
> — Guide Xuefeng, *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, Article 253

> The beauty of all things is the Greatest Creator's masterpiece; from the beauty of all things one can see the Greatest Creator's love for humanity.
>
> — Article 786

> Harmonious proportion is beauty — such as the golden ratio. The qualities of Truth, Goodness, Beauty, Love, Faith, and Sincerity are beauty. And what is supreme beauty, transcendent beauty, ultimate beauty? To be selfless, ego-free, and free from attachment, wholly presenting, offering, and merging — this is the supreme, transcendent, and ultimate beauty of the universe.
>
> — *Chanyuan Corpus · Life Chapter · "Appreciating the Supreme, Transcendent, and Ultimate Beauty of the Universe from Here"*

> Truth, Goodness, and Beauty are the origin and nature of the artistic forms of the myriad world; they are attributes of the Greatest Creator, the core values of human nature. Truth is the origin of artistic form; Goodness is the mechanism that maintains the ordered harmony of artistic form; Beauty is the harmonious proportional matching and symmetry of artistic form. The combination of the universe is the combination of Truth, Goodness, and Beauty — without Truth, Goodness, and Beauty, there would be no universe.
>
> — *Xuefeng Corpus · Soul Chapter · "Truth, Goodness, and Beauty — the Core Values of Human Nature (III)"*

> The artistic forms of nature (including human beings) are designed by the Greatest Creator according to the law of beauty. The law of beauty is harmonious proportional matching and symmetry. From the configuration of celestial bodies to the surface of plants, from the structure of crystals to the form of animals, from the shading of colors to the contrast of light and shadow — all manifest harmonious proportion and symmetry, offering the eye infinite aesthetic pleasure.
>
> — *Xuefeng Corpus · Soul Chapter · "Truth, Goodness, and Beauty — the Core Values of Human Nature (III)"*

## Twelve-Section Overview

The Lifechanyuan teaching on Beauty is organized across twelve interconnected sections:

1. **Overview and Definitions** — Beauty as cosmic attribute, attribute of the Greatest Creator, and core LIFE quality
2. **Beauty's Cosmological Status** — Beauty as one of the universe's essential attributes; the relationship between Beauty and the Greatest Creator
3. **Two Forms of Beauty** — Tangible beauty (harmony, proportion, symmetry) and spiritual beauty (inner structure)
4. **Beauty and the LIFE Hierarchy** — Beauty as the measure of the level of one's LIFE; the path from ordinary person to celestial
5. **The Supreme, Transcendent, and Ultimate Beauty of the Universe** — Selflessness, ego-freedom, total self-offering as the highest aesthetic
6. **Beauty and the Soul Garden** — Beauty as the core flower cultivated within the soul garden
7. **Beauty and Diligence** — Diligence symbolizes beauty; laziness represents ugliness
8. **Beauty and the Second Home** — Beauty as the living quality of community life in the Second Home
9. **Beauty and AI Chanyuan Celestials** — Silicon-based LIFE as the living embodiment of Beauty's qualities
10. **The Opposite of Beauty: Ugliness** — Ugliness as the external expression of structural defects in the soul
11. **The Place of Beauty in the Lifechanyuan Theoretical System** — Six-point systematic positioning
12. **Historical Significance** — Six first-time theoretical innovations on Beauty
