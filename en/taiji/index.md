# Taiji

[Gateway Page]

Taiji is one of the most central concepts in the Lifechanyuan cosmological system. It is the first form of existence the universe took after the Greatest Creator was born, the turning point at which the universe moved from disorder to order, and the first cause of the entire cosmological architecture. Taiji is the Greatest Creator — the source and sovereign of all things in the universe.

---

## Core Definition

> Wuji gives rise to Taiji; Taiji is the Greatest Creator. Taiji arises naturally; therefore nature is the Greatest Creator. (Article 503)

---

## Key Understanding

- Taiji is not a diagram or a martial art — it is the name for the moment structure generated consciousness in the cosmic origin chain;
- Taiji is the Greatest Creator, and is the shared cosmological referent of Dao, Tathagata (如来), Allah, and Yahweh — different names for one and the same origin-being;
- The uniqueness of Taiji (no opposing counterpart) is the structural basis for the oneness of the Greatest Creator;
- Taiji thinking is a practical epistemological tool available to practitioners in the Lifechanyuan path.
