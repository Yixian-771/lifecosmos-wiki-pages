## II. The Full Meaning of Trusting the Home

### The Shift from Trusting People to Trusting the Home

"'At home rely on parents; away from home rely on friends.' This is one element of traditional culture. In the real human world, to survive and thrive, people mainly rely on personal relationships... Whenever the object of one's reliance develops problems, a series of interpersonal conflicts and emotional suffering arise... Once the object of reliance is shifted from people to the Home, all these phenomena heal themselves and vanish like smoke."
(Xuefeng Corpus · Essays · Trusting the Home — Second Home Cultural Construction, Part Five)

"Every Chanyuan Celestial's clothing, food, housing, transport, aging, illness, and death are handled by the Home. The care of elders and the education of children are handled by the Home. Emotional and sexual needs are freely and openly guaranteed by the Home. The Home provides every Chanyuan Celestial with a stage to display their talents. In this way, we can completely transform what traditional culture placed in personal relationships into dependence on the Home. As a result, interpersonal relationships become pure and simple, natural and harmonious — those living together share only warmth and joy, kindness and affection."
(Xuefeng Corpus · Essays · Trusting the Home — Second Home Cultural Construction, Part Five)

### How to Trust the Home

"View the Home as your place of livelihood and nurture; view the Home as your stage for exercising wisdom and talent; view the Home as the best environment for attaining joy, happiness, freedom, and bliss; view the Home as the fertile soil for manifesting your qualities of truth, kindness, beauty, love, faith, and sincerity to their utmost; view every Chanyuan Celestial in the Home as your brother, sister, family member, and beloved."
(Xuefeng Corpus · Essays · Trusting the Home — Second Home Cultural Construction, Part Five)

"Love the Home. Contribute your talent and wisdom to the Home selflessly. Do each piece of work you have chosen in the Home according to your interests and preferences, carefully and thoroughly. Strive for first-rate performance and first-rate results. Give more to the Home each day than you receive. At the same time, continuously elevate the quality of your LIFE, perfect your antimatter structure, and ceaselessly climb toward the highest realm of LIFE."
(Xuefeng Corpus · Essays · Trusting the Home — Second Home Cultural Construction, Part Five)

"How to trust the Home? Uphold the *800 New Era Human Concepts* — this is the most fundamental guarantee for realizing both personal aspirations and the Home's ideals."
(Xuefeng Corpus · Essays · Trusting the Home — Second Home Cultural Construction, Part Five)

### The Essence of Trusting the Home

"The Lifechanyuan Second Home is the kingdom of the Greatest Creator, a thoroughly communistic community. Here there is no marriage, no fixed opposite-sex attachment. We trust the Greatest Creator, trust the Home, and do not trust people. Every member is not only emotionally independent of any individual but also, for their future ideal life, does not depend on any person. We are each an independently civilized, free human being."

## III. Steadfast Principles: Standards and Significance

### Principles Over Sentimentality

"I have said many times that Chanyuan Celestials must uphold Chanyuan principles, placing principles above all else, and must not build special individual relationships with one another... Chanyuan Celestials should value principles over sentimentality. This directly concerns whether we can become celestial, whether we can reach the Celestial Realm. Even at the Millennium Realm, there is no distinction between close and distant. That distinction belongs to earthly consciousness. If Chanyuan Celestials still differentiate between intimate and distant, deciding what to say based on how well they get along with someone, while ignoring Chanyuan principles — then we are wrong. We do not understand the life of the Celestial Realm. We remain only human, unable to become celestial."
(Other writings of the Guide · 2008 · Principles Over Sentimentality)

"In this Home, regardless of who it is, whoever upholds Chanyuan principles is our brother, sister, family, and beloved. Whoever does not uphold Chanyuan principles is not our brother, sister, family, or beloved. Once someone abandons Chanyuan principles, the relationship as brother, sister, family, and beloved immediately disappears."
(Other writings of the Guide · 2008 · Principles Over Sentimentality)

### Clarity of Mind at All Times

"At any time and under any circumstances, every Chanyuan Celestial in the Home must maintain a high degree of mental clarity. Principles must be upheld. Otherwise, the Home will move toward failure, individuals will move toward power struggles, and the Home will evolve into an organization of scheming and infighting."
(Xuefeng Corpus · Q&A Chapter · Conglong Grass Asks About Collective Decision-Making)

"Steadfastly uphold principles, steadfastly keep to the Home, walk along the Guide's Route Map — over time, you will become a celestial and arrive at the Elysian Realm on the Celestial Islands Continent."
(Chanyuan Corpus · Celestial Cultivation Chapter · A Simple 'Diagram' of the Consciousness Structure of a Celestial)

Therefore we must handle Home affairs and relationships through Chanyuan principles, and must never allow personal sentimentality to cause us to betray Chanyuan principles and lose the opportunity to elevate the quality of our LIFE."
(Other writings of the Guide · 2008 · Principles Over Sentimentality)
