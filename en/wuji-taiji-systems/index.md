# Wuji System and Taiji System

The **Wuji System** (*wújí tǐzhì* 无极体制) and **Taiji System** (*tàijí tǐzhì* 太极体制) form the methodological core of Lifechanyuan's blueprint for a new civilizational era. The Wuji System corresponds to the universe's holistic resonance property — in society, this manifests as information transparency, the functioning of conscience, and an immediate-accountability ("present karma") mechanism. The Taiji System corresponds to the universe's ordered creative laws — in society, this manifests as the coordinated construction of a *support structure* and a *manifestation structure*. Together they represent the complete unfolding of the mind-matter relationship in social governance and cultivation practice.
