# Cosmic Origin

[Entry Gateway]

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological
> essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across
> incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage
> of human existence in this world.

**Cosmic Origin** (宇宙起源) is a foundational entry in the Lifechanyuan cosmology. The universe is not a product of chance — it is the product of the Greatest Creator's consciousness. The cosmological sequence: Wuji (pre-structured potential) → Taiji (the Greatest Creator) → structured energy unfolds into the thirty-six-dimensional universe. The universe is consciousness made manifest.

> Wuji gives rise to Taiji; Taiji is the Greatest Creator. Taiji arises naturally; therefore nature is the Greatest Creator.
>
> — Guide Xuefeng, *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, Article 503

---

## Core Positioning

In the Lifechanyuan system, cosmic origin is not a Big Bang event but a consciousness-genesis sequence: from unordered energy (Wuji) to ordered structure generating consciousness (Taiji / the Greatest Creator), to the unfolding of the thirty-six-dimensional universe as the Greatest Creator's artistic expression of Truth, Goodness, and Beauty.
