# Cosmic Origin

[Gateway Page]

Cosmic Origin is a foundational entry in the Lifechanyuan cosmology. It addresses three central questions: where the universe comes from, how it unfolds, and why it runs in ordered patterns.

This framework is summarized by: **Wuji gives rise to Taiji; chaos transforms into hundun order.**

---

## Core Definition

**The universe is a product of the Greatest Creator’s consciousness. Cosmic origin is not framed as a pure Big Bang event, but as a sequence from unordered energy to ordered structure, from structure to consciousness, and from consciousness to universal unfolding.**

---

## Key Points

### Core Generation Chain
1. Wuji state (pre-structured state)
2. Unordered energy movement
3. Accidental ordering into structure
4. Consciousness emergence (the Greatest Creator)
5. Dao-governed expansion of universe, time, and space

### Theoretical Axes
- Three elements: consciousness, structure, energy
- Balance principle: sum of positive and negative energy is zero
- Single cosmic contingency: birth of the Greatest Creator; after that, process is necessity-driven

### System Significance
- Connects the origin of the Greatest Creator, Dao, time, and space
- Grounds the emergence logic of 36-dimensional space and parallel worlds
- Provides cosmological coordinates for LIFE elevation discourse
