# The Lifechanyuan Way of Cooperation (Internal Edition)

## I. The General Position: Lifechanyuan's Stance on Cooperation

Lifechanyuan is not a political organization, not a religious organization, not any form of organization whatsoever, and certainly not an economic institution set up to make money or turn a profit.

In theory, Lifechanyuan has no basis for cooperation with the outside world. Lifechanyuan's position is clear: if you think Lifechanyuan's concepts are good and the Second Home program is good, then enter Lifechanyuan wholeheartedly; if you have your own values and your own theory and path different from Lifechanyuan's, then follow your own path. Like the stars in the ocean of the cosmos, Lifechanyuan is a shining star, and you yourself are a shining star too — how wonderful is that.

Lifechanyuan's concepts and the Second Home program are entirely oriented toward enabling Chanyuan Celestials to eventually enter Heaven. Anything unrelated to Heaven holds little value for Lifechanyuan.

Lifechanyuan has no cooperative relationship with the United Nations, any nation, any political party, any religious group, any organization, any community of any kind, or any individual. Lifechanyuan also does not participate in any activity of the United Nations, any nation, any political party, any religion, any organization, any community, or any individual.

— *Lifechanyuan Is a Self-Coherent Open System*; *The Lifechanyuan Way of Cooperation*, Xuefeng

---

## II. The Core Reasons for Non-Cooperation

Lifechanyuan walks the path of the natural Tao. Within the natural Tao, all gestation, emergence, growth, and decline are determined — not something human effort can change or resist. That is why Lifechanyuan has remained quietly obscure for more than twenty years: this is all arranged and governed by the program of the Tao.

As your guide, I am neither impatient nor anxious, neither rushed nor flustered. Each day I move spontaneously, driven by the greatest impulse of my inner being. Whether Lifechanyuan grows and flourishes, whether it catches fire, whether it can bring the greatest number of people into the paradise on earth — these are matters for the Tao to consider, not for me to fret over.

The guide enjoys "autocracy," embraces the Superman Philosophy — that is: **either I follow you, or you follow me — no negotiation whatsoever.** I do not like and am not willing to discuss, argue, or debate with others, and cooperation is precisely the program most likely to give rise to deliberation, discussion, and dispute.

— *The Lifechanyuan Way of Cooperation*; *The Reasons Lifechanyuan Does Not Cooperate with Any Social Organization*, Xuefeng

## III. Self-Coherence — The Inner Root of the Way of Cooperation

Lifechanyuan's non-cooperation is rooted in the "Self-Coherence" of its system.

**"Where you seek no favors, all places welcome you; where you pour no drinks, let the wine price rise however it will." The hallmark of a Celestial Being is "Self-Coherence," and that is what Lifechanyuan strives to realize.**

"Self-Coherence" means that one's inner system has formed a complete whole — lacking nothing, running in harmonious coordination internally, and needing nothing from the outside world to meet any of its needs.

From theory to practice, Lifechanyuan has built its own complete system: from the origins of the Greatest Creator, the universe, LIFE, and humanity, to space-time, LIFE, human experience, and thinking — it has its own understanding and interpretation of all of these; from revealing the nature of the Tao and Heaven to explaining how to enter a higher civilization, it has detailed analysis and roadmaps; especially regarding how a human being can repay karmic debts, settle worldly entanglements, accumulate merit, perfect the antimatter structure of LIFE, and ultimately reach Heaven and climb to the summit of LIFE — Lifechanyuan has its own body of doctrine and practical experience. Lifechanyuan is already a self-contained system.

This system is unprecedented and will not be surpassed — it is the pinnacle of human thought and practice, transcending existing religions, political parties, and national doctrines, and going far beyond existing science, philosophy, and education.

— *Lifechanyuan Is a Self-Coherent Open System*; *Self-Coherence: One of the Essential Qualities of Celestials on the Celestial Islands*, Xuefeng / Chanyuan Corpus

## IV. Open — Self-Coherence Does Not Mean Closed

This "Self-Coherence" is not an absolutely closed system; it is, rather, an open system — open to LIFE in all layers of the cosmos, open to all nations, ethnicities, religions, and cultures in the world, open to every citizen of the earth.

However, this openness does not mean absorbing everything indiscriminately into the Lifechanyuan system. Rather, it means drawing in only those new discoveries and essential elements that accord with Lifechanyuan's cosmology, worldview on LIFE, philosophy of human experience, and value system — discoveries grounded in fact, science, logic, and spiritual sensing — so as to continuously refine and enrich the depth and breadth of Lifechanyuan, and to further civilize Lifechanyuan's practical activities.

Concept 800 of *New Era Human 800 Concepts* states clearly:
**"The New Era Human 800 Concepts is an open system. As time and space shift, new thoughts, new ideas, and new methods will emerge in an endless stream. These 800 concepts must be continually updated and refined. As the era develops, what should be removed will be removed, and what should be added will be added. Never cling to convention, never impose a ceiling."**

— *Lifechanyuan Is a Self-Coherent Open System*; *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, Concept 800

## V. The Internal Way of Cooperation: Superman Philosophy and Hundun Management

Non-cooperation externally does not mean disorder internally. Lifechanyuan has a clear internal order of cooperation.

Lifechanyuan's internal operations follow two parallel principles:

**（I) Macro Level: Hundun Management**

Hundun Management means no management at all. Everyone is master; everyone is servant. There is only a difference in division of labor — no relationship of leading and being led. No one may impose their will on another. Everyone strives to express the qualities of truth, goodness, beauty, love, faith, and sincerity to the utmost. Each speaks their own words, does their own work, takes their own responsibility, cultivates their own path, and walks their own road. Do not worry about others' affairs, do not manage others' matters; be responsible only to the Greatest Creator and to yourself, not to others.

**Whoever makes a suggestion bears the responsibility. Do not help those who do not ask; help at once those who do.**

— *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, Concepts 302 and 76; *Second Home Program*, Clause 639

**（II) Micro Level: Superman Philosophy**

The Second Home does not practice democracy. Instead it employs the Superman Philosophy: all affairs of the Home are divided and assigned by responsibility, and each person has the sole say in the domain they are responsible for.

**Whoever is responsible makes the call; whoever is responsible bears all the consequences.**

The Home's management is Hundun Management at the macro level, but in concrete matters it implements the Superman Philosophy — whoever is responsible makes the call. In the production and daily life affairs of each Branch Home, whatever arrangement the Branch Home Director makes is what gets done — do not analyze whether the arrangement is right or wrong. Any opposition to the Branch Home Director's arrangements is wrong.

— *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, Concepts 10, 302, and 647; *Production, Life in Each Branch Home, and Embracing the One with the Branch Director*, Xuefeng

## VI. The Exception: Cooperation with Farm Owners

In the general sense, Lifechanyuan declines cooperation — but there is one form of cooperation it is willing to accept:

There are only two purposes in becoming a Chanyuan Celestial: one is to gain a joyful, happy, free, and blissful human life; the other is to extend one's LIFE into the celestial realm. To become a Celestial, the external environment one seeks is only intimate union with nature — hence the willingness to cooperate with farm owners. The conditions and purpose of such cooperation are not to make money, but to build the farm into an ecological paradise.

If a farm owner is willing to cooperate with Lifechanyuan, the condition is that the farm be fully entrusted to Lifechanyuan to manage. Lifechanyuan takes on all responsibilities for the farm owner's family — food, clothing, housing, transportation, birth, aging, illness, and death. Once the terms of cooperation are established, they are never altered.

— *The Lifechanyuan Way of Cooperation*, Xuefeng, 2026-04-14

## VII. A Brotherly-Sisterly Relationship with Similar Communities

Lifechanyuan can form a brotherly-sisterly relationship with communities that share similar values. This relationship means mutual non-interference in each other's management affairs, with only mutual dispatch of members for exchange visits, and mutual exchange of experience in cultural matters and the techniques of ecological construction.

— *The Lifechanyuan Way of Cooperation*, Xuefeng, 2026-04-14
