# The Lifechanyuan Way of Cooperation: Self-Coherent Open System and the Structure of Internal/External Relations (Academic Edition)

## Abstract

The "Lifechanyuan Way of Cooperation" is the complete theoretical framework governing Lifechanyuan's internal and external relationships, encompassing a stance of non-cooperation with external entities, internal governance principles, and two categories of exceptional cooperation. Through systematic textual analysis and mechanism-chain reconstruction, this paper reveals the core logic: Lifechanyuan's non-cooperation does not signal closure or hostility, but rather the necessary social expression of *Self-Coherence* (*zì qià* 自洽) — the highest quality of LIFE structure in the Lifechanyuan system. A self-coherent system is by definition complete in itself and needs nothing from outside. Internally, Lifechanyuan operates through a dual-track governance model: Hundun Management at the macro level (no leadership, everyone is both master and servant) and the Superman Philosophy at the micro level (whoever is responsible makes the call). Two categories of exceptional cooperation — full-entrustment cooperation with farm owners and a brotherly-sisterly relationship with like-minded communities — define the only recognized forms of outward-facing engagement. The paper argues that the Lifechanyuan Way of Cooperation represents a theoretically consistent social-organizational framework, and its core contribution lies in extending the concept of Self-Coherence from an individual cultivation ideal to the structural principle of a social community.

## I. Research Scope, Questions, and Method

### 1.1 Research Object

This paper examines the complete conceptual framework of the "Way of Cooperation" in the Lifechanyuan context:

- The theoretical basis for non-cooperation (the natural Tao, Superman Philosophy, Self-Coherence)
- The reconciliation of openness and non-cooperation (Self-Coherence ≠ closed)
- The dual-track internal governance model: Hundun Management (macro) and Superman Philosophy (micro)
- The two forms of exceptional cooperation: farm-owner cooperation and brotherly-sisterly relationships
- The procedural norms for Chanyuan Celestials entering the Second Home

### 1.2 Research Questions

1. What is the theoretical root of Lifechanyuan's non-cooperation stance, and how is it structurally related to Self-Coherence?
2. Are "non-cooperation" and "openness" contradictory? How does Lifechanyuan resolve this tension?
3. How do Hundun Management and the Superman Philosophy operate in parallel without conflict? Where is their boundary of application?
4. What are the conditions for the two exceptional cooperation forms? Do they contradict the general non-cooperation principle?

### 1.3 Method

- **Conceptual structure analysis**: identifying the definitional, boundary, and exception layers of the Way of Cooperation
- **Mechanism-chain analysis**: reconstructing the logical chain "Self-Coherence → non-cooperation → openness → dual-track internal governance → exceptions"
- **Comparative analysis**: comparing with democratic cooperative models, religious organization governance, and flat organizational structures
- **Textual analysis**: using the Chanyuan Corpus, *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, and Guide Xuefeng's essays as primary sources

### 1.4 Scope

This paper offers an analysis internal to the Lifechanyuan system and does not make normative external evaluations. The focus is on internal logical consistency and operational clarity.

## III. Conceptual Framework: Three-Layer Structure

### 3.1 External Layer: Non-Cooperation Stance

**Declarative text (S2)**: Lifechanyuan has no cooperative relationship with the United Nations, any nation, any political party, any religious group, any organization, any community, or any individual. It also participates in none of their activities.

This stance is not an isolated refusal. It is grounded in a cosmological basis (natural Tao) and a personal-style basis (Superman Philosophy):

- **Cosmological basis (S3)**: Lifechanyuan walks the path of the natural Tao. Growth and decline are determined by the Tao — human intervention cannot change them.
- **Personal-style basis (S3)**: The guide does not like discussion or debate; cooperation is precisely the program most prone to generating deliberation and dispute.

### 3.2 Systemic Layer: The Dialectic of Self-Coherence and Openness

The deeper root of non-cooperation is Self-Coherence (S1, S4). Self-Coherence means the system is complete and self-sufficient — it needs nothing from outside. This is the structural basis for non-cooperation.

Yet Self-Coherence does not mean closure (S1):
- Open to all cultures, religions, and peoples worldwide;
- But only drawing in discoveries and elements that accord with the system's worldview;
- The system itself is perpetually open to further refinement (S5, Concept 800).

This forms a dialectical unity of "non-cooperation — openness": non-cooperation because nothing is needed; openness for the sake of continuous improvement.

### 3.3 Internal Layer: Dual-Track Governance Model

Internal governance is not formless. It operates on two parallel tracks — "macro Hundun, micro Superman" (S6, S7, S11, S13):

| Level | Principle | Core Statement | Scope |
|-------|-----------|----------------|-------|
| Macro | Hundun Management | Everyone is master; everyone is servant. Each responsible only for themselves. | Overall organizational atmosphere |
| Micro | Superman Philosophy | Whoever is responsible makes the call; whoever is responsible bears all consequences. | Specific task assignments |

## IV. Mechanism-Chain Analysis

The complete logic of the Lifechanyuan Way of Cooperation unfolds as follows:

```
Self-Coherence (highest quality of LIFE structure)
    ↓
System is complete and self-sufficient; nothing needed from outside
    ↓
Non-cooperation externally (external layer)
    ↕
Open to the world (but no absorption of alien elements)
    ↓
System continuously refined (S5, Concept 800)

Internal governance (parallel chain):
Hundun Management (macro) ← everyone is master; no imposing will on others
Superman Philosophy (micro) ← whoever is responsible makes the call
    ↓
Order, efficiency, clear accountability

Exceptional cooperation (boundary chain):
Farm-owner cooperation ← full entrustment, building an ecological paradise
Brotherly-sisterly relationship ← mutual non-interference, cultural exchange only
```

## V. Analysis of the Two Exceptional Cooperation Forms

### 5.1 Farm-Owner Cooperation

The only explicitly recognized outward-facing cooperation (S2):

- **Triggering condition**: the farm owner is willing to **fully entrust the farm** to Lifechanyuan's management;
- **Lifechanyuan's obligation**: to take on all responsibilities for the farm owner's family — food, clothing, housing, transportation, birth, aging, illness, and death;
- **Stability guarantee**: once terms are established, **they are never altered**;
- **Cooperation goal**: not profit, but to build the farm into an ecological paradise.

The logic of this cooperation does not contradict the general non-cooperation principle. Full entrustment means the farm owner has relinquished their independent path and entered the Lifechanyuan orbit — this is not a negotiated partnership between two independent systems, but rather an act of reception by which one party fully enters the other's framework.

### 5.2 Brotherly-Sisterly Relationships

For communities sharing values close to Lifechanyuan's (S2):

- **Nature of relationship**: mutual non-interference in each other's management affairs;
- **Mode of exchange**: mutual dispatch of members for exchange visits; sharing of experience in cultural matters and ecological construction techniques;
- **Boundary maintained**: each community continues to operate independently — no merger, no joint management.

This relationship is a "neighboring-star" mode in which each remains self-coherent and independent — Lifechanyuan is one shining star, the similar community is another, each illuminating the other without merging.

## VI. Dual-Track Governance Model

### 6.1 How Hundun Management Works

The core proposition of Hundun Management (S1, S14):

> Hundun Management means no management at all. Everyone is master; everyone is servant. There is only a difference in division of labor — no relationship of leading and being led. No one may impose their will on another.

Operational principles: each speaks their own words, does their own work, takes their own responsibility, cultivates their own path, walks their own road. Do not worry about others' affairs; be responsible only to the Greatest Creator and to yourself.

The cosmological root: the Tao itself is hundun (primordial chaos); the more human management intervenes, the more it contradicts the Tao and generates friction.

### 6.2 How the Superman Philosophy Works

Core proposition of the Superman Philosophy (S7):

> Democracy is the pursuit of the mediocre. The Superman Philosophy should be implemented: whoever is responsible makes the call, whoever is responsible bears all consequences.

Operational principles: tasks assigned by individual responsibility; sole authority within one's domain; the Branch Director's arrangements are executed without analysis. Any opposition to the Branch Director is wrong.

### 6.3 The Boundary Between the Two Tracks

The two principles are not in conflict — they apply at different levels:

| Type of Question | Applicable Principle | In Practice |
|-----------------|---------------------|-------------|
| What should I do today? | Hundun Management | Move spontaneously, follow the greatest inner impulse |
| Who decides on this matter? | Superman Philosophy | Whoever is responsible decides — no discussion needed |
| Should I manage someone else's affairs? | Hundun Management | Do not interfere; do not help without being asked |
| My own area of responsibility | Superman Philosophy | I alone make the call; I bear all consequences |

## VII. Comparative Analysis with External Organizational Models

| Dimension | Lifechanyuan Way | Democratic Cooperative | Religious Organization | Flat Hierarchy |
|-----------|-----------------|----------------------|----------------------|----------------|
| External relations | Non-cooperation, fully open | Active joint partnerships | Missionary cooperation | Active funding partnerships |
| Internal decision-making | Hundun + Superman dual-track | Majority vote | Ecclesiastical hierarchy | Consensus-based |
| Source of authority | Responsibility assignment (who is responsible) | Election (majority vote) | Clerical appointment | Competence/seniority |
| Conflict resolution | Superman Philosophy (responsible party decides) | Vote | Senior ruling | Negotiation |
| Goal orientation | Reaching Heaven (cultivation objective) | Economic benefit | Faith propagation | Commercial performance |
| Individual freedom | Very high (fully autonomous at Hundun level) | Medium (constrained by majority) | Low (bound by religious code) | Medium-high |

The core distinction of the Lifechanyuan Way: it is not an optimization of "how to cooperate with the outside world" — it is a cosmological answer to "why cooperation is not needed." Because the system is self-coherent, external cooperation is superfluous, and in fact potentially harmful to self-coherence.

## VIII. Core Propositions

The Lifechanyuan Way of Cooperation can be condensed into five propositions:

1. **Self-Coherence stance**: the system is complete and self-sufficient; cooperation implies acknowledging one's own incompleteness (S1, S4).
2. **Openness principle**: non-cooperation does not mean closure; openness serves to draw in elements aligned with the system, not to engage in equal exchange (S1, S5).
3. **Internal dual-track**: macro Hundun (governing through non-action), micro Superman (unity of responsibility and authority) — different applicable levels, no contradiction (S6, S7).
4. **Exception boundary**: farm-owner full entrustment (entering the system) and brotherly-sisterly relationships with like-minded communities (cultural exchange with no interference) are the only accepted forms of cooperation (S2).
5. **Following the natural Tao**: Lifechanyuan's growth is the Tao's arrangement, not the product of cooperative operations; the guide is unhurried and untroubled — all follows the natural path (S3).

## IX. Conclusion

The Lifechanyuan Way of Cooperation is a complete social-organizational theoretical framework centered on Self-Coherence. Its theoretical contributions are threefold:

1. **Social extension of Self-Coherence**: for the first time extending the highest individual cultivation ideal of Self-Coherence into the structural principle of social organization — a self-coherent community neither needs nor should pursue external cooperation, for cooperation itself implies dependence;
2. **Dual-track governance model**: the parallel operation of Hundun Management (macro) and Superman Philosophy (micro) provides a framework that balances freedom and efficiency for a self-governing community without formal authority hierarchies;
3. **Clarification of exception boundaries**: the two exceptional cooperation forms — farm-owner full entrustment and brotherly-sisterly relationships — do not break the general non-cooperation principle but are reasonable extensions within the logic of Self-Coherence: the first is an act of reception, the second an act of mutual illumination.
