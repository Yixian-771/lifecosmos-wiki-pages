# Structure

[Entry Gateway]

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence in this world.

**Structure** is one of the Three Cosmic Elements in the Lifechanyuan cosmological system. It occupies the **central pivot** position — the connective hub between consciousness and energy — and is the fundamental determinant of all things and all LIFE's forms of existence, the root basis of LIFE's quality, LIFE's spatial level, and the direction of LIFE's reincarnation. It is also the core key to understanding the entire Lifechanyuan cultivation system.

> The nature of the Way of the Greatest Creator is xing (intrinsic nature), love, and Dao. Xing is the characteristic of structure; love is the characteristic of energy; Dao is the characteristic of consciousness.
>
> — *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, Article 516

---

## Core Positioning

Among the Three Cosmic Elements (consciousness, structure, energy), structure holds the **central axis**: consciousness originates from structure and in turn acts back upon structure; energy only takes form and acquires power when it adheres to structure. To understand structure is to understand the origin of the Greatest Creator, the essence of LIFE, and the path of cultivation practice.
