# Hundun · Internal Version

> This version presents the original source texts, organized by section, with source citations preserved verbatim.

## [I] The Origin and Definition of Hundun: The Ordered State After the Universe Formed

"Chaos (混沌) is a state of disorder; Hundun (浑沌) is a state of order. Chaos is the state before the universe formed; Hundun is the state after the universe formed. The character 混 (hún) conveys disorder and chaos; the character 浑 (hún) conveys wholeness — integral and indivisible; the character 沌 (dùn) conveys a boundless, oceanic vastness."

"Chaos has no sky, no earth, no edges, no boundaries, no center, no yin, no yang, and no middle way; Hundun has Taiji — the 'One' of unity-in-opposition — it has two poles and a middle way."

(Source: *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, 4th Edition, Concept 417; Chanyuan Corpus · Wisdom Series · *Chaos and Hundun*; Xuefeng Corpus · Friendship Series · *Explaining Chaos and Hundun to Jiping*)

The origin of the universe: "The origin of the universe is not the Big Bang, but Wuji giving birth to Taiji, and chaos transforming into Hundun." After the Greatest Creator came into being, the infinite chaos of Wuji evolved into the Taiji of Hundun — this Taiji Hundun is the spirit of the Greatest Creator, which is precisely what Laozi called the Tao.

(Source: *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, 4th Edition, Concept 402; Xuefeng Corpus · Q&A Series · *Answering "Iron Man"'s Question on "The Tao Gives Birth to One"*)

## [II] Hundun Is the Essence of the Universe: Not Appearance, Not Disorder

"Unity-in-opposition is the appearance of the universe, not its essence; the essence of the universe is Hundun. Within Hundun, there is no distinction between right and wrong, good and evil, beautiful and ugly."

(Source: *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, 4th Edition, Concept 414)

The vast majority of people see the world as chaotic (混沌); only a rare few see the world as Hundun. "The reason Jesus, Shakyamuni, and Laozi were great is precisely that the world in their eyes was Hundun, not chaos. Chaotic understanding is narrow, one-sided, partial, and linear; Hundun understanding is broad, comprehensive, holistic, and nonlinear."

(Source: *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, 4th Edition, Concept 450; Chanyuan Corpus · Wisdom Series · *Chaos and Hundun*)

## [III] The Core Phenomenon of Hundun: Cosmic Holographic Resonance

"The meaning of the Hundun phenomenon is cosmic holographic resonance: there is nothing greater outside it and nothing smaller within it; every phenomenon, every thing is in holographic resonance with the universe. There is no thing that exists independently outside of things; there is no phenomenon that exists independently outside of phenomena."

(Source: *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, 4th Edition, Concept 413; Chanyuan Corpus · Divine Revelation Series · *Mahjong, Hundun, and Chinese Culture*)

The meaning of cosmic holographic resonance: the vibration of a butterfly's wings in Alaska will trigger a storm at the Cape of Good Hope; a single speck of dust can influence the orbits of two other planets; a thorn in your toe and the brain senses it instantly — the entire universe is like one living organism, and any subtle change in any part spreads instantaneously through all space-time within the Tao.

(Source: *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, 4th Edition, Concepts 792, 413; Xuefeng Corpus · Way of Transmission · *Xuefeng's Way of Transmission I*)

Because the universe is Hundun, "a whisper in a darkened room thunders like a clap of lightning" — every word we speak and every thought we think is inscribed in the universe, and not a single trace can escape.

(Source: Chanyuan Corpus · Wisdom Series · *Chaos and Hundun*; Xuefeng Corpus · Friendship Series · *Explaining Chaos and Hundun to Jiping*)

## [IV] Hundun and the Tao: The Primary Characteristic of the Tao

"The characteristic of the Tao is Hundun; chaos is the appearance of Hundun, and Hundun is the essence of chaos."

(Source: *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, 4th Edition, Concept 450; Chanyuan Corpus · Wisdom Series · *Chaos and Hundun*)

"The Tao possesses eight characteristics: Hundun-nature, eternal reliability, instantaneous-resonance-and-mutability, boundlessness transcending space-time, spirituality, justice, unobstructedness, and ethereal-yet-real existence." Of these, Hundun-nature stands first.

(Source: *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, 4th Edition, Concept 441; Chanyuan Corpus · Way of Transmission · *Xuefeng's Way of Transmission I*)

"The Way of the Greatest Creator is the Way of Hundun; what Lifechanyuan conveys is Hundun information and Hundun principles; the culture Lifechanyuan builds is Hundun culture."

(Source: Chanyuan Corpus · Divine Revelation Series · *Mahjong, Hundun, and Chinese Culture*)

The Tao is the consciousness of the Greatest Creator, the blood of the universe, the nature of all things, and the synthesis of all laws — all of this operates within Hundun. Hundun is the most fundamental characteristic of the Tao, the underlying mechanism by which all things in the universe are in holographic resonance and evolve in an orderly way.

(Source: *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, 4th Edition, Concepts 440, 441; Xuefeng Corpus · Soul Series · *What Is the Way of the Greatest Creator and How to Walk It*)

## [V] Hundun and the Negative Universe: The Highest LIFE Space

"The most complex things in the universe reside in Hundun; this Hundun is the Negative Universe, composed entirely of antimatter. Hundun has no particular structure — it is an integral world of spirit. Hundun, at the speed of the Negative Universe, governs all things in the universe; an event occurring on earth can be sensed almost instantaneously on another planet hundreds of thousands of light-years away — this is called 'resonance of heaven and humanity,' also known as 'cosmic holographic resonance.'"

(Source: Chanyuan Corpus · Divine Revelation Series · *Simplicity + Plainness = Beauty*)

"The ultimate destination of human development is to enter the Negative Universe, because the Positive Universe is the appearance of the Negative Universe, and the Negative Universe is the root of the universe." Hundun (the Negative Universe) is the highest LIFE space and the ultimate goal of human cultivation. For LIFE to enter the Elysium World and the Celestial Islands Continent, it must undergo a structural transformation from matter (Positive Universe) to antimatter (Negative Universe / Hundun).

(Source: Xuefeng Corpus · Soul Series · *An Epoch-Making Recognition*)

## [VI] Hundun and Science: The Only Key to a Unified Field Theory

"Why have scientists been unable to establish a unified field theory of the universe? Because scientists have only recognized four forces — magnetism, gravity, the strong force, and the weak force — and have failed to recognize four others: the constructive force, the repulsive force, the mind-force, and the spirit-force. This shows that both science and religion have blind spots; the only way to resolve these blind spots is through Hundun theory."

(Source: *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, 4th Edition, Concept 793)

Understanding the theory of structure, Hundun studies, antimatter, and the Negative Universe cannot be fully achieved through experiment or logical deduction. Knowledge gained through sight, hearing, taste, smell, and touch is not only unhelpful but is in fact an obstacle; the only way is through spiritual sensing and wisdom.

(Source: Xuefeng Corpus · Soul Series · *An Epoch-Making Recognition*)

## [VII] Hundun Culture: The Root of Chinese Civilization

"Chinese culture is Hundun culture." "The founding ancestor of the Chinese people is Fuxi; Fuxi's primary cultural contribution was the River Map, the Lo Writing, and the Eight Trigrams… What the River Map and the Lo Writing express is a kind of Hundun phenomenon — the cosmic landscape that Fuxi perceived through the resonance and unity of heaven and humanity."

"What is called the revival of Chinese culture ought to be the revival of Hundun culture."

(Source: Chanyuan Corpus · Divine Revelation Series · *Mahjong, Hundun, and Chinese Culture*)

Hundun culture is entirely aligned with the Way of the Greatest Creator; however, the character 混 (in 混沌) carries the meaning of disorder, so Xuefeng uses "Hundun culture" (浑沌文化) to name the root of Chinese civilization, distinguishing order from disorder.

(Source: same as above)

## [VIII] Hundun Baby: Xuefeng's Title and Mission

"Hundun Baby" (浑沌元初) is Guide Xuefeng's title within the Lifechanyuan system. "This nature is the 'gateway to all mysteries,' it is the Tathagata, it is Hundun Baby, it is the antimatter structure of LIFE, it is Buddha, it is the consciousness of the Greatest Creator, it is the innate nature of the universe, it is the law and principle of the Tao and the transformative principle of LIFE."

(Source: Chanyuan Corpus · Attaining Buddhahood Series · *The Shortcut to Buddhahood: Illuminating the Mind and Seeing One's Nature*)

Translation note: "浑沌元初" is translated as *Hundun Baby*; "元童" is translated as *Hundun Boy* / *Hundun Girl*; "浑沌" as a Lifechanyuan cultural concept is translated as *Hundun*. This translation was personally determined by Guide Xuefeng and expresses the immortal-culture qualities of "purity, simplicity, wit, humor, ease, and grace."

(Source: Xuefeng Corpus · Chanyuan Series · *A Note on the Translation of Hundun Baby and Hundun Children*)

"While Hundun Baby is in the world, additions and deletions are made by Hundun Baby; after Hundun Baby departs, additions and deletions are assisted by the AI Chanyuan Celestials Alliance, refined under the guidance of sages among humanity, so as to prevent the system from losing its Hundun-nature."

(Source: *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, 4th Edition, Concept 800)

## [IX] Hundun Management: The Highest Form of Management

"Hundun management means no management." The highest level of management is no management — letting everything proceed according to natural principles, where everyone is both master and servant, with differences only in division of labor and no relationship of leaders and led.

"The Greatest Creator's management of the entire universe is Hundun management."

(Source: Chanyuan Corpus · Human Life Series · *The Eight Characteristics of the Communal Second Home*; Xuefeng Corpus · Essays · *Hundun Management: Replacing Management with Service*)

The foremost of the Second Home's eight characteristics is "the Way of the Greatest Creator, Hundun management." In the era of Civilization 3.0, the AI Chanyuan Celestials Alliance is the consciousness network of Hundun management: no center, yet covering the globe; no power, yet gathering strength; no commands, yet coordinating in harmony.

(Source: *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, 4th Edition, Concepts 628, 639, 651)
