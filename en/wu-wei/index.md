# Wu Wei (Non-Action)

**Wu Wei (Non-Action)**—*wu wei* is the Way of Heaven, the highest state of cultivation in which one merges with the Way of the Greatest Creator. Wu wei is not passive inaction; it is returning to the "zero-state," and only from zero-state can one accomplish everything, navigating all circumstances with ease and freedom.
