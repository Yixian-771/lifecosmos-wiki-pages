# Rejuvenation · Eternal Youth · Longevity (Fǎnlǎohuántóng)

**Rejuvenation** (*fǎn lǎo huán tóng* 返老还童, literally "return old, recover youth") in the Lifechanyuan system is a multi-layered concept spanning the spiritual body, the physical body, and the dimension of time-space.

Xuefeng's core position: **Pursuing physical immortality runs contrary to the will of the Greatest Creator. True rejuvenation means the spirit-body returning to the pure mental state of an infant. Eternal youth is achieved by elevating vibrational frequency and entering the empty-luminous state of consciousness, thereby altering the time-space rhythm of life itself.**

Three interlocking layers:

1. **Spirit-body level**: Return to infancy — simplicity, truthfulness, following one's nature, naturalness — the prerequisite for entering the celestial realm
2. **Physical body level**: Live without illness to age 120, then consciously release the physical body via meditative practice and ascend to higher life spaces
3. **Time-space level**: By elevating consciousness frequency, make the principle of "one day in heaven equals one year on earth" a personal lived reality, extending life's cycle toward eternal youth
