# Masculine Beauty in Strength

Masculine beauty in strength is a core principle in the Lifechanyuan system regarding male virtue and aesthetics. True masculine strength (*yang gang*) is not aggression or domination — it is an inner power expressed through courage, steadiness, and a spirit of adventure, rooted in deep appreciation for feminine softness.

> "Males find beauty in strength, females find beauty in softness. A male who is overly soft is ugly; a female who is overly rigid is ugly."
>
> *(New Era Human 800 Concepts, 4th Edition, Concept 178)*
