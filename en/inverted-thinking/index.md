# Inverted Thinking

> Nothing is something; something is nothing. Up is down; down is up. Success is failure; failure is success.
>
> — Xuefeng, *Chanyuan Corpus · Chapter on Anti-Conventional Thinking · XV. Inverted Thinking*

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence in this world.

**Inverted Thinking** is the core operational tool of Lifechanyuan's *Chapter on Anti-Conventional Thinking*. It is the practice of deliberately rotating the "front face" of conventional thought by 180 degrees to simultaneously perceive the concealed "reverse face" — until front and back merge beyond distinction, entering the state of Hundun (primordial chaos). Its cosmological foundation is the **Möbius Strip**: opposition is the surface appearance of the universe; unity is its true substance.

Inverted Thinking was the first form of anti-conventional thinking that guide Xuefeng awakened to during his high-altitude cultivation in the Qilian Mountains. It is the opening gateway of the entire *Chanyuan Corpus*, and the first key by which Chanyuan Grasses break free from unilateral thinking on the path toward the wise and the saintly.
