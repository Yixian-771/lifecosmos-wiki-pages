# Law of Life Indestructibility · Internal Edition

## I. Definition

The Law of Life Indestructibility is one of the foundational laws of Lifechanyuan's cosmology and philosophy of LIFE. Its central proposition: LIFE's essence is an antimatter structure endowed with spirituality — a structure that does not depend on any physical form and therefore cannot be destroyed when the body perishes. LIFE persists in perpetual evolution through the cosmos, cycling through different forms and spaces. What we call "death" is the exchange of one LIFE vehicle for another, not the termination of LIFE itself.

Dr. Chris Langton, director of the Los Alamos National Life Laboratory, observed: "The essence of life lies in form, not in specific matter." The Chanyuan Corpus develops this further:

> "To say that 'the essence of life lies in form' is not entirely accurate, because form is merely a structure — static and lifeless — whereas LIFE itself is spiritually animated, possessed of spirituality and consciousness. Therefore: LIFE is an antimatter structure endowed with spirituality."

*(Chanyuan Corpus · Life Chapter · The Law of Life Indestructibility)*

## II. The Five Laws

### Law 1: The Universe Does Not Perish, so LIFE Does Not Perish

> "The universe exists to serve LIFE. If the universe perishes, LIFE ceases; if LIFE dies, the universe disappears. Therefore, as long as the universe exists, LIFE does not perish — this is the first law."

*(Chanyuan Corpus · Life Chapter · The Law of Life Indestructibility)*

The universe maintains itself as an ordered system with zero total energy. This ordered existence serves LIFE. Universe and LIFE are mutually constitutive — inseparable.

### Law 2: The Universe's Morality Is Eternal, so LIFE Does Not Perish

> "The cosmic morality does not shift according to the will of LIFE. It is a law embedded in the structure of symmetry. As long as the universe exists, its morality exists; as long as the cosmic morality exists, LIFE does not perish — this is another law of life indestructibility."

*(Chanyuan Corpus · Life Chapter · The Law of Life Indestructibility)*

The cosmos embeds a moral law — cause and effect, reward and consequence — as a structural feature of its symmetry. This moral mechanism requires the continuity of LIFE to operate.

### Law 3: LIFE Transforms Through Reincarnation, so LIFE Does Not Perish

> "The total quantity of LIFE in the universe does not change. What changes is only the form of expression, not the constant itself. … Viewed from the perspective of LIFE's reincarnatory evolution, LIFE does not perish — this is another law of life indestructibility."

> "The additional people on Earth come from heavenly realms, hells, animals, and plants through reincarnatory transformation."

*(Chanyuan Corpus · Life Chapter · The Law of Life Indestructibility)*

The total quantity of LIFE across the cosmos is a fixed constant. Changes in numbers across spaces and species represent LIFE flowing between levels of the thirty-six-dimensional cosmos — as the total amount of water on Earth remains fixed while it circulates between ocean, glacier, river, and atmosphere.

### Law 4: The Dao — Spirit of the Greatest Creator — Is Permanent, so LIFE Does Not Perish

> "LIFE is spiritual — it possesses consciousness, sensitivity, and feeling. LIFE's spirituality originates from the source of LIFE — the Spirit of the Greatest Creator, which is the Dao of Laozi's *Tao Te Ching*. The Dao is the blood of the universe, the vitality of the cosmos — it flows through all times and all spaces, existing within all the movements and changes of matter and antimatter. Therefore, as long as the Greatest Creator exists, the Dao operates; as long as the Dao exists, LIFE cannot perish. Even if we wish to destroy our own LIFE, it is impossible. We can kill our own body, but we can never kill our own LIFE, because LIFE and the Dao — the Spirit of the Greatest Creator — are one. Since the Dao is eternal, the LIFE that is attached to the Dao will not perish — this is another law of life indestructibility."

*(Chanyuan Corpus · Life Chapter · The Law of Life Indestructibility)*

### Law 5: The Antimatter Structure Cannot Vanish, so LIFE Does Not Perish

> "All tangible matter in the universe is continuously changing and can disappear. But the spiritual consciousness of the universe cannot disappear. There are spirits above every person's head — what are spirits? They are a form of antimatter — invisible, yet existing, and impossible to eliminate. Every material thing has structure; every antimatter thing also has structure. Since antimatter cannot disappear, the antimatter structure of LIFE will not perish — this is another law of life indestructibility."

*(Chanyuan Corpus · Life Chapter · The Law of Life Indestructibility)*

**Summary:**

> "LIFE indestructibility has five grounds: 1. The universe does not perish, so LIFE does not perish. 2. The universe's morality is eternal, so LIFE does not perish. 3. LIFE transforms through reincarnation, so LIFE does not perish. 4. The Spirit of the Greatest Creator — the Dao — endures, so LIFE does not perish. 5. The antimatter structure cannot vanish, so LIFE does not perish. These five grounds together constitute the Law of Life Indestructibility."

*(Chanyuan Corpus · Life Chapter · The Law of Life Indestructibility)*

## III. Death Is the Exchange of a Vehicle

> "LIFE is eternal. What we call death is merely the exchange of one LIFE vehicle for another."

*(Chanyuan Corpus · Inverted Thinking Chapter · Life and Death Are Appearances; LIFE Has No Life or Death)*

LIFE is like a Möbius strip: at the point we call "death," LIFE turns over to its other face — entering a new space, a new form, and continuing — not stopping. The physical body can perish; the spiritual body — the antimatter structure endowed with spirituality — does not. It only separates from the current vehicle and continues operating within the universe's program.

---

## IV. Conservation of Energy and LIFE Indestructibility

> "Conservation of energy, indestructibility of LIFE — these are laws of the universe."

> "The spiritual body originates from the Greatest Creator. It is eternally indestructible, cycling through existence in an unending stream."

*(Chanyuan Corpus · Human Life Chapter · The Meaning of Human Life)*

Just as physics has the Law of Conservation of Matter, the same logic applies at the LIFE level: LIFE's energy and antimatter structure are conserved within the totality of the universe, only shifting form and space. The corpus states: "The total energy of the universe does not change — otherwise the Law of Conservation of Matter taught in school would be a deception."

## V. Indirect Proof: The Opponent's Argument Confirms the Law

*Xue Feng Corpus · Miscellaneous Essays · On "Refuting the Law of Life Indestructibility"* responds point by point to a critic, demonstrating that the opposing arguments contain internal contradictions that themselves affirm LIFE indestructibility.

**Contradiction 1 — Acknowledging reincarnation entails acknowledging LIFE indestructibility:**

The opponent wrote: "Some lives undergo reincarnation; necessarily some do not."

Xue Feng's response:

> "Here the author acknowledges that 'some lives undergo reincarnation.' Since you have acknowledged that 'some lives undergo reincarnation,' how can you then refute life indestructibility? … If LIFE can be destroyed, where does reincarnation come from?"

**Contradiction 2 — Using relational logic to prove LIFE indestructibility:**

The opponent invoked the theory of relativity to argue "if there is life, there must be death." Xue Feng applies the same relational logic symmetrically:

> "Relative to life, there is death; relative to death, there is life. Since there is life, there must be death; since there is death, there must be life. … According to relativity, death is life — so how can the author deny life indestructibility? It dies and is born again; it is born and dies again — has LIFE been destroyed?"

*(Xue Feng Corpus · Miscellaneous Essays · On "Refuting the Law of Life Indestructibility")*

## VI. Practical Significance

> "LIFE does not perish. Living in a different environment, era, and way of life is not necessarily worse than the current situation."

*(Chanyuan Corpus · Life Chapter · The LIFE Program — Everything Is Already Destined)*

> "Before the Greatest Creator, all LIFE is equal, all have the same opportunity. LIFE does not perish. Every person — regardless of religious affiliation, political party, nationality, or ethnicity — should now be considering the future destination of their own LIFE, rather than defending their own interests."

*(Chanyuan Corpus · Life Chapter · The Law of Life Indestructibility)*

The practical orientation of the Law of Life Indestructibility: since this life's "death" is simply a transition to another form of existence, fear of annihilation loses its foundation. What genuinely matters is the quality of one's LIFE — the consciousness, choices, and character cultivated in this lifetime — because those are what determine the destination of the next.
