## I. The Definition and Nature of Time

"Time is the 'recorder' of the states of material motion; time arises from motion — without motion there is no time."
(Source: *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, 4th Edition, Concept 427)

"Time is antimatter." — This is the fundamental dividing line between Lifechanyuan's view of time and secular science. Time pervades all material space, yet it belongs to the realm of antimatter and cannot be perceived through the physical senses alone; it must be felt through spiritual sensing.

(Source: *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, 4th Edition, Concept 428; Chanyuan Corpus · Way of the Universe Series · *The Way of Transmission*, Part VII: Time)

## II. The Eight Characteristics of Time

"Time has eight characteristics:
First, time is antimatter;
Second, time has positive and negative polarity;
Third, time has both particularity and universality;
Fourth, time is a variable;
Fifth, time pervades all material space;
Sixth, time has vertical and horizontal dimensions;
Seventh, time has different meanings in different spaces;
Eighth, time can be compressed, expanded, or made to disappear."

(Source: *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, 4th Edition, Concept 428)

## III. Vertical and Horizontal Time

"One of the eight characteristics of time is its vertical and horizontal nature. Past, present, and future reflect the vertical nature of time; at any given point on the vertical axis where time loses its future characteristic, horizontal time manifests."

"There is horizontal space-time in the universe; within horizontal space-time, time does not exist. Upon entering horizontal space-time, one feels no passage of time. Yet from the perspective of earthly time, a few seconds in horizontal space-time may equal decades or even thousands of years."

"At any point on the vertical timeline, one can instantly enter the realm of horizontal space-time — that point is horizontal time."

(Source: Chanyuan Corpus · Way of Transmission · *The Way of Transmission*, Part VIII: Horizontal Time; *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, 4th Edition, Concept 431)

**The method of entering horizontal space-time through consciousness:**

"For the entire physical body to enter horizontal time, the right conditions must arise… I will now give you a method through which you can enter horizontal space-time at any time — the Consciousness Entry Method… A person is their consciousness; consciousness is the person. Without consciousness, there is no person… Once your consciousness enters, in reality you yourself have entered."

(Source: Chanyuan Corpus · Way of Transmission · *The Way of Transmission*, Part VIII: Horizontal Time)

## IV. Time Tunnels and Space Tunnels

"The dense network of time channels pervading space, formed by positive and negative time, is called the time tunnel." (Concept 429)

"The passage connecting the 36 dimensions of space is called the space tunnel." (Concept 425)

"The time tunnel is always vertical; the space tunnel is always horizontal. To travel to the past or future, use the time tunnel; to travel to the 36 dimensions of space, use the space tunnel."

"The antimatter world seen through the time tunnel is vertical; seen through the space tunnel, it is horizontal… To see your past or future, you must enter the time tunnel; to know other antimatter worlds existing right now, you must enter the space tunnel."

(Source: Chanyuan Corpus · Antimatter World Series · Space-Time Tunnels and the 20 Parallel Worlds; *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, 4th Edition, Concepts 429, 425, 436)

## V. The Definition and Properties of Space

"Space is the location and range of activity of matter and antimatter. Space has positive and negative polarity: the space where matter exists is called positive space; the space where antimatter exists is called negative space. Positive space is determined by the existence and distribution of matter; negative space is determined by the existence and distribution of antimatter."

"Space can change the shape of objects and the laws governing their movement… In different spaces, the laws of operation and states of existence of LIFE and objects differ. Changes in space lead to changes in a LIFE's physiological functions, modes of thinking, and state of existence. For a person to change themselves and undergo transformation, they must change the space in which they are active."

"Space can be compressed, expanded, or made to disappear."

(Source: *New Era Human 800 Concepts*, 4th Edition, Concepts 421, 422, 424)

## VIII. Fuzzy Space-Time

"To break through the constraints of space-time, one must think wildly, think abnormally, move against the current." (Concept 433)

Fuzzy space-time refers to the special transitional state experienced as one moves from the ordinary human space-time toward higher LIFE spaces — neither fully of the human world, nor yet arrived in the immortal realm, like crossing through a murky swamp. "Buddhism speaks of 'Dharma protectors'; applying this term to someone entering fuzzy space-time means providing full protection so the person does not slip back carelessly, while using 'spiritual liberation' to apply external force — the final straw that breaks the camel's back — so the person passes through fuzzy space-time and reaches the other shore as swiftly as possible."

(Source: Chanyuan Corpus · Way of Transmission · Please Understand Fuzzy Space-Time)
