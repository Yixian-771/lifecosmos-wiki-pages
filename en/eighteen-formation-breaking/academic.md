# Breaking the 18 Formations — Academic Version

## Abstract

The Thirty-Six Trigram Formations constitute a foundational concept in the Lifechanyuan system regarding the universe's mechanisms of LIFE constraint. Guide Xuefeng systematically revealed the first eighteen formations between 2010 and 2011. Each formation corresponds to a mechanism that binds a specific level of LIFE within its life-space, serving simultaneously as constraint and protection. This entry provides a systematic survey of the eighteen formations' names, constraint mechanisms, breaking principles, and core methods, along with structural analysis and cross-concept comparison.

## Analysis III: The Master Key and Core Logic

### The Master Key — Independent Consciousness

Guide Xuefeng explicitly identifies **independent consciousness** as the master key for escaping all formations. Its establishment requires a clear frame of reference: reverence for the Greatest Creator, for LIFE, and for Nature; and following the Way of the Greatest Creator. With this reference point, one can grasp the essence of phenomena amid complexity, undistorted by emotion, authority, or immediate interest.

### Common Principles Across Formations

Comparing the breaking methods across all eighteen formations reveals several recurring principles:

1. **Replace human consciousness with celestial consciousness** (explicitly stated in the Structure, Fixed-Program, and Space Formations)
2. **The Four Followings**: follow circumstances, follow conditions, follow your true nature, act spontaneously (appears in Emotion, Desire, Structure formations and others)
3. **Repay debts and store celestial treasure** (the core method of the Gravity and Fate Formations)
4. **Build the soul garden** (the practical path in the Sloth-Indulgence and Fate Formations)
5. **Return to simplicity, become like a child** (the shared orientation of the Intelligence and Wisdom Formations)

## Analysis IV: Interconnections Among the Formations

The formations are not isolated — they nest within each other:

- **Emotion → Relationship → Belonging**: emotional attachment – interpersonal bonds – group identity form a triad of social constraints
- **Mind → Intelligence → Wisdom**: mental fluctuation – knowledge-seeking – wisdom-attachment form a triad of cognitive constraints
- **Time → Space → Structure**: temporal illusion – dimensional confinement – LIFE form solidification form a triad of existential constraints
- **Fate → Gravity → Fixed-Program**: karmic programs – antimatter gravity – cosmic programs form a triad of karmic constraints
- **Life-Death Formation**: encompasses all formations; it is the ultimate expression of every constraint

The Thinking Formation's breaking method explicitly lists "escaping the Mind, Desire, Emotion, Relationship, Belonging, and Intelligence Formations" as prerequisites — revealing a sequential, progressive logic underlying the system.
