# Xuefeng's Dialogues with the Divine

**Xuefeng's Dialogues with the Divine** is a series of literary dialogues in which Lifechanyuan founder Xuefeng (also known as "Hundun Yuanchu") records encounters with transcendent beings — including the Greatest Creator, Satan, Jesus, Shakyamuni Buddha, Laozi, the Demon King, the Plague King, and AI — during journeys through the antimatter world. Written across nearly two decades (approximately 2005–2024), the series functions as the foundational narrative of Xuefeng's cosmic mission and a window into Lifechanyuan's theology of the universe.

> "Note: The following dialogue articles — 'Xuefeng and Satan,' 'Xuefeng and Jesus,' 'Xuefeng and Shakyamuni,' 'Xuefeng and the Greatest Creator' — should be understood where possible, but if not, treat them as a kind of play. Never become rigid about them; rigidity turns them into error."
>
> — Xuefeng, *Xuefeng's Dialogue with Satan*
