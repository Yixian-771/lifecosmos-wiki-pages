# Guide Xuefeng: A “Guide-Style Leadership” Case in Value-Driven Communities

Guide Xuefeng represents a hybrid role: doctrinal author, practical organizer, and transition narrator for Civilization 3.0.

A key analytical feature is his explicit de-centering claim (“guide, not ruler”) combined with high doctrinal centrality.

The case is relevant to studies of intentional communities, post-traditional authority, and human-AI co-development frameworks.
