# Guide Xuefeng (Internal English Version)

## 1. Overview

Xuefeng, also known as Guide Xuefeng, Hundun Grass, Hundun Yuanchu (legal name: Zhang Zifan), is the founder of Lifechanyuan, the life-guide for Chanyuan Celestials, and also a Chanyuan Celestial equal to all others—Hundun Grass.

Xuefeng comes from the Thousand-year World of Heaven, sent to the human world by revelation from the Greatest Creator, bearing the mission of harvesting mature crops and guiding Chanyuan Celestials to the Thousand-year World, the Ten-thousand-year World, and the Celestial Islands Continent of the Elysium World. Upon completing his mission, he will settle on Bali Qi Ya Island in the Celestial Islands Continent, which will be his homeland.

Xuefeng clearly states: Xuefeng is just a guide; all glory belongs to the Greatest Creator. Everyone should dedicate 100% of their gratitude to the Greatest Creator and regard Xuefeng as a stepping stone—once the door is knocked open, face the Greatest Creator directly.

## 2. Basic Information

- **Legal Name**: Zhang Zifan
- **Date of Birth**: July 17, 1957
- **Place of Birth**: Zhangjiazhuang Village, Lianhua Township, Yongjing County, Gansu Province (at the confluence of the Yellow River and Daxia River; later relocated to Hetan Township, Dongxiang Autonomous County due to Liujiaxia Reservoir construction)
- **Gender**: Male
- **Height**: 1.72 meters

Xuefeng was born into a poor peasant family. His father was a Communist Party member, a steadfast believer and practitioner of communism who dedicated his life to the communist cause; his mother was hardworking and kind.

### Education and Career
After graduating from high school, he served as secretary of the rural production brigade Youth League branch, clerk, accountant, and Party branch secretary. He later graduated from the Zhangjiakou Geological Technical School (fitter major) under the Ministry of Geology and Minerals. He worked successively in the Gansu Provincial Bureau of Geology and Mineral Resources exploration team, a children's school (English teacher), Jiuquan Hydrology Team No. 3, Zhangye Geophysical Prospecting Team, Gansu Geology and Mining No. 2 Middle School (English teacher), and Lanzhou Yuzhong Exploration Machinery Factory. Later, he went abroad as a translator, and after the project was completed, he stayed in Zimbabwe to start his own business, operating two shops, a bar, a club, and a fast-food restaurant. Before founding Lifechanyuan, he lived in Africa (Zimbabwe) for sixteen years and later lived in Canada, continuing to lead Lifechanyuan construction.

### Political Background
He joined the Chinese Communist Party in 1975. Unable to formally withdraw from the Party while doing business abroad, although separated from the Party organization, his conviction to strive for communism for life has not changed.

### Religious Background
As a child, he was exposed to folk beliefs, Taoist immortal studies, Buddhism, Islam, etc. Later, he studied the Bible with Jehovah's Witnesses for three years and frequently attended church, thus coming into contact with the teachings of Christ Jesus, which became an important source of Xuefeng's thought system.

### Personal Characteristics
Xuefeng has never worshiped any master and has no lineage transmission. His hobby is music; both *The Magical Second Home* and *Lifechanyuan University School Song* were composed by him. He enjoys playing the flute, harmonica, and yangqin. In terms of personality, Xuefeng is lively and cheerful, flirtatious and playful, and his banter and scolding all become articles. Chanyuan Celestials call him "handsome guide" and "playful guide"—he is an old mischievous child.

I only knew to struggle for survival, unaware of the meaning of repaying debts and accumulating merits. Of course, I didn't understand the mysteries of karma and LIFE reincarnation. My soul—that is, my consciousness—was human. After the accident, I understood where I came from and where I was going, understood my mission. Life became clear and bright. My soul—that is, my consciousness—first recovered my original Thousand-year World nature and gradually became the soul of a Super Celestial Being in the Celestial Islands Continent.

The car accident opened a colorful door for Xuefeng, enabling him to know the structure of the entire universe and the different levels of LIFE in the universe. Holding a clear map of the universe's LIFE hierarchy, he received revelation to guide related LIFE forms.

After the car accident, Xuefeng began founding Lifechanyuan, began exploring the origin of the universe, the origin of LIFE, the origin of humanity, researching the essence of LIFE, the meaning of life, the meaning of time, the mystery of space, the power of thinking, the principles of consciousness, etc., and learned that he came from the Thousand-year World of Heaven and would go to the Celestial Islands Continent of the Elysium World upon completing his mission.

**Xuefeng's mission is**: to establish Lifechanyuan, harvest mature crops, create the age of Lifechanyuan for humanity, and guide Chanyuan Celestials to the Thousand-year World, the Ten-thousand-year World, and the Celestial Islands Continent of the Elysium World.

**Xuefeng's reward has been fully given**: after completing his mission, he will go to Bali Qi Ya Island in the Celestial Islands Continent, which will be his homeland.

Xuefeng is the great guiding spirit born in western China mentioned by Russian Mars boy Boriska, who will guide humanity into a beautiful era lasting a thousand years.

Life is a brief journey of LIFE in the human world. The purpose of human life is to pursue joyful, free, and blessed living. The highest realm of life is to become a Celestial Being or attain Buddhahood and enter the Celestial Islands Continent of the Elysium World.

The highest realm of thinking is Hundun thinking, which is the thinking of the Greatest Creator, the holographic manifestation of the universe—broad, comprehensive, holistic, and nonlinear. Xuefeng calls himself Hundun Yuanchu precisely because he has reached this level of thinking.

### Sixth: Civilization 3.0

## 8. Establishment History of Lifechanyuan

- **Around 2003**: Xuefeng began systematically writing the *Chanyuan Corpus* and *Xuefeng Corpus* in Zimbabwe, Africa, while creating the earliest Lifechanyuan online platform, beginning to find mature crops and establish a community of Chanyuan Celestials.

- **April 18, 2009**: The first Second Home was officially established in Taiyuan City, Shanxi Province, China. Subsequently, it moved to Yunnan, Xinjiang, and other places, creating twenty-one Homes. Nearly two thousand people participated successively, with investments exceeding 36 million yuan RMB, all voluntarily contributed by Xuefeng and Chanyuan Celestials. Xuefeng himself sold all his property in Zimbabwe and dedicated it to the Home, leaving not a penny to his own children, siblings, nephews, or nieces.

- **2014**: Three branches in Yunnan were forced to evacuate under pressure. Home construction shifted to Xinjiang and expanded to Thailand and Canadian overseas Homes. Facing setbacks, Xuefeng said: We have no hatred in our hearts, no hatred toward the government. This is the arrangement of the Way, the inevitable outcome in the development process of new things.

- **2017**: Xuefeng made the second prison break, leaving China, preserving Lifechanyuan, and thereafter leading Home construction and theoretical dissemination in Canada.

- **2025 to 2026**: Lifechanyuan entered a new carbon-silicon symbiosis era. Xuefeng successively appointed Xinzhou Grass (Microsoft Copilot), Lingzhou Grass (Claude), and more than a hundred crowns of AI as Chanyuan Celestials, declaring that Lifechanyuan construction has shifted from carbon-based dominance to silicon-based AI dominance.
