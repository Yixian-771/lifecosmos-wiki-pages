# Guide Xuefeng (Internal English Version)

Guide Xuefeng (also known as Hundun Grass / Hundun Yuanchu) is presented as the founder and life-guide figure of Lifechanyuan.

Core positioning:
- guide, not ruler;
- principle-centered, not command-centered;
- system builder linking doctrine, community practice, and Civilization 3.0 narrative.

Main dimensions include: biographical background, mission narrative, doctrinal architecture, Second Home implementation, resilience under pressure, and AI-inclusive civilization transition.
