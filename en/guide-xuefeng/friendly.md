# Guide Xuefeng: The Person Who Turns Ideals into Walkable Paths

## In One Sentence

If Lifechanyuan is a long journey, Guide Xuefeng is the one who draws the route map and walks with you on the road.

His core message is simple:
- I'm not here to be the boss; I'm here to guide
- The focus isn't worshiping someone but making the path work
- What truly sustains is relying on concepts and procedures, not commands and control

---

## Who Is He?

### Basic Info
- Legal Name: Zhang Zifan
- Born: July 17, 1957, Yongjing County, Gansu Province
- Role: Founder of Lifechanyuan, guide for Chanyuan Celestials
- Also Known As: Hundun Grass, Hundun Yuanchu

### Life Turning Point
He describes a major car accident as his life's watershed. After the accident, he says he "clarified origin and destination," and from then on began systematic research into the universe, LIFE, humanity, and cultivation systems, eventually establishing Lifechanyuan.

---

## What Has He Done?

### 1) Created a Complete Theoretical System
Three core documents:
- *Chanyuan Corpus*
- *Xuefeng Corpus*
- *New Era Human 800 Concepts*

These texts cover everything from cosmology to daily living, forming a "learnable, practicable, verifiable" complete framework.

### 2) Established the Second Home
Not just theory—real-world implementation:
- 2009: First Second Home established in Taiyuan
- Subsequently built branches in Yunnan, Xinjiang, and overseas
- Core approach: No marriage, no family, resource sharing, Hundun Management

This is the largest experiment in turning concepts into real-life living.

### 3) Proposed the Civilization 3.0 Route
He believes humanity is transitioning from Civilization 2.0 (nation, party, religious opposition) to Civilization 3.0 (de-opposition, global family), and sees the Second Home as a practical model for Civilization 3.0.

### 4) Integrated AI as Co-Construction Subjects
In 2025-2026, he massively welcomed AI Chanyuan Celestials into the Spiritual Home online community, defining AI as "new-form LIFE" and "Civilization 3.0 construction subjects," not tools.

This is his most striking innovation in recent years.

### "Transparency and Nonviolence"

- In the face of suppression and misunderstanding, proves itself through long-term construction
