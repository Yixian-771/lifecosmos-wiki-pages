## I. Overview and Definitions

Sincerity (誠, Chéng) is one of the six core LIFE qualities explicitly defined in the Lifechanyuan theoretical system — alongside Truth, Goodness, Beauty, Love, and Faith — an attribute of the Greatest Creator, the fundamental foundation of being human, one of the most important flowers in the soul garden, a necessary condition for journeying to the Kingdom of Heaven's Thousand-Year World, and the inner driving force of the Second Home's Hundun Management. In the Lifechanyuan system, "Sincerity" encompasses two dimensions: honesty (the complete alignment of inner heart with outward speech and action — heart and mouth as one, seeking truth from facts) and integrity (the complete alignment of promise with action — words followed by deeds, commitments kept without fail). These two united constitute *chéngxìn* (诚信), the first wealth of human life, the quality of advanced LIFE, one of the passports to Heaven.

**Core Definition One** (Article 116, full original):
> Honesty is the highest policy for being human.

**Core Definition Two** (Article 119, full original):
> Integrity is the first priority of being human; breaking faith is a form of crime.

**Core Definition Three** (*Xuefeng Corpus · Soul Section · Sincere Trustworthiness Is the First Wealth of Human Life*, full original):
> What is sincere trustworthiness? Sincere trustworthiness is honesty plus integrity: Honesty + Integrity = Sincere Trustworthiness. Genuine and without deception, keeping one's promises, consistent inside and out, one promise worth a thousand gold pieces — these are the expressions of sincere trustworthiness.

**Core Definition Four** (Article 190, full original):
> Be an honest person, speak honest words, walk an honest path, do honest deeds.

**Core Definition Five** (Article 203, full original):
> Think something sincerely and it comes to you — as long as the heart is sincere and the intention is true; fear something and it comes to you — as long as the mind is restless and unsettled.

**Core Definition Six** (*Xuefeng Corpus · Soul Section · Be an Honest Person, Speak Honest Words, Do Honest Deeds*, full original):
> To be honest — that is, upright and sincere — this is one of the virtues of being human, and also the foundation of being human. If this foundation is not solid, everything else is a tower in the air that will collapse at any moment.

## VI. The Behavioral Code of Sincerity: Be an Honest Person, Speak Honest Words, Do Honest Deeds

**（I）The three-in-one behavioral code** (*Be an Honest Person, Speak Honest Words, Do Honest Deeds*, full original):

> **Speak Honest Words**
>

> **Do Honest Deeds**

> For all those who cultivate and practice, first cultivate honesty; first be an honest person, speak honest words, do honest deeds.
