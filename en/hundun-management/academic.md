# Hundun Management: Theory and Practice of a Non-Traditional Management Philosophy (English Academic Version)

In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence, while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence.

## Abstract

Hundun Management is a management philosophy proposed and practiced by Lifechanyuan's Second Home, with core concept "replace management with service," emphasizing maximal reduction of human intervention, letting Dao (natural law) manage. This entry provides academic compilation of Hundun Management's theoretical origins, core dimensions, operational mechanisms, and practice cases based on official Lifechanyuan documents and practice records, aiming to provide complete cases for management philosophy research and utopian community practice research.

---

## I. Concept Definition and Name Distinction

### 1.1 Basic Definition

Hundun Management is Second Home's core management approach, proposed and created by Guide Xuefeng. Core concept: maximally reduce human management, replacing with "Dao management" following natural law.

One-sentence definition: Replace management with service; masters are servants, management is service.

### 1.2 Name Distinction: Hundun vs. Chaos

Understanding this distinction is key to grasping Hundun Management:

**Chaos (混沌)**:
- Nature: Disordered state
- Temporality: Pre-universe formation
- Characteristics: No heaven-earth, no edges, no center, no yin-yang, no middle way

**Hundun (浑沌)**:
- Nature: Ordered state
- Temporality: Post-universe formation
- Characteristics: Wholeness, has Taiji, has two poles, has middle way; comprehensive, holistic, non-linear

Hundun Management emphasizes "order" rather than "disorder"—highest management form, not chaos.

## II. Theoretical Origins and Philosophical Foundations

### 2.1 Cosmological Basis

Hundun Management's theoretical basis originates from observing universal operational laws:

**The Greatest Creator's Hundun Management**: The Greatest Creator's universe management is viewed as Hundun Management's prototype. All things endowed with inherent nature operate according to nature; nature orderly yet without human management.

**Holographic Universe View**: Universe holographic, affecting everything; therefore advocates global Hundun Management.

### 2.2 Philosophical Traditions

Hundun Management integrates multiple philosophical traditions:

**Daoist Thought**: Laozi's "wu wei" (non-action governance), "sages handle affairs without action, practice teaching without words" is Hundun Management's direct intellectual source.

**Buddhist Thought**: Emphasizes blossoming "Tathagata nature," letting everything be natural.

**Communist Ideal**: "From each according to ability, to each according to need" directly borrows Marxist ideals.

### 2.3 Management Philosophy Positioning

Hundun Management self-positions as "Dao governance," distinguishing from:
- **Law governance** (rule of law): Emphasizes rules and institutions
- **Human governance** (rule by person): Relies on individual authority

Hundun Management advocates "governing by Dao"; Dao's core is natural law; natural law's core is harmonious coexistence among people, between people and society, between people and nature.

## III. Core Theoretical Framework

### 3.1 Eight Dimensions

Hundun Management's theoretical system comprises eight dimensions:

1. **Unity with Dao**: Follow natural law, reduce human intervention
2. **Reduce Regulations**: Minimalize laws and rules, stimulate inner qualities
3. **Eliminate Private Ownership Roots**: Dissolve marriage-family, share resources
4. **Everyone Creates Wealth**: Reduce intermediaries and administrative personnel
5. **Replace Management with Service**: Managers become servants without privileges
6. **From Each According to Ability, To Each According to Need**: Voluntary, no administrative orders
7. **Harmonize Individuality-Commonality**: Macro wholeness, micro independence
8. **Center on People**: Let people enjoy LIFE process, not suffer for goals

### 3.2 Operational Mechanisms

**Concepts + Programs**: Hundun Management operates via "800 Values for New Era Humanity" and Home programs, not human management.

**Spiritual Purification**: Necessary prerequisite for Hundun Management. Theory holds: more perfect hearts need less management; uglier hearts need more forced management.

**Self-Judgment**: Everyone establishes sacred court on their conscience, judging themselves.

### 3.3 Management Characteristics

**Uses Affinity Not Intimidation**  
**Joyful Play Not Solemn Seriousness**  
**Natural Flow Not Forced Difficulty**  
**Happiness-Freedom Not Discomfort-Restraint**  
**Gentle Rain Not Violent Storms**

## IV. Practice Case Analysis

Lifechanyuan positions Second Home as Hundun Management's experimental ground. According to its self-narration (not independently verified):

**Organizational Structure**: No regulations, no leadership institutions, no offices, no organization, no discipline, no supervision

**Interpersonal Relations**: Everyone both master and servant; only division of labor differs; no hierarchy

**Operational Results**: No quarrels/fights, no theft with unlocked doors, impeccable order

**Extreme Decentralization**: Guide Xuefeng never visited, never issued directives, privately communicated fewer than 8 times

**Autonomous Operation**: Everything based on members' inner guidance

**Results**: Beautiful environment, attracted hundreds from dozens of countries, impeccable order

## VI. Theoretical Value and Limitations

### 6.1 Theoretical Value

**Management Philosophy Level**: Provides extremely anti-traditional management model thinking

**Utopian Practice Level**: Provides experimental case for "anarchist order"

**Eastern Wisdom Modernization**: Concretizes Daoist "wu wei" into operational community management

**Civilizational Transformation Exploration**: Provides possibility for post-modern civilization forms

### 6.2 Research Limitations

1. **Single Source Materials**: Primarily relies on Lifechanyuan self-narration, lacks independent third-party investigation
2. **Limited Sample Size**: Practice cases mainly concentrated in Second Home small-scale communities
3. **Cultural Specificity**: Depends on specific concept systems and spiritual purification prerequisites
4. **Unknown Scalability**: Feasibility from small communities to whole societies lacks verification
5. **Unclear Long-Term Effects**: Lacks long-term tracking of members post-departure

### 6.3 Controversial Points

**Prerequisite Feasibility**: Spiritual purification as prerequisite—how to objectively assess standards, methods, effects?

**Scale Effects**: Can small-community success replicate to cities, nations?

**Conflict Resolution**: Without management mechanisms, how to handle serious conflicts?

**External Environment**: How do Hundun Management communities interact with external law-governed societies?

## VII. Dialogue with Other Management Philosophies

### 7.1 Similarities-Differences with Anarchism

**Similarities**: Both oppose coercive management institutions

**Differences**: Anarchism emphasizes individual freedom; Hundun Management emphasizes holistic harmony. Anarchism mostly political advocacy; Hundun Management includes religious-philosophical dimensions.

### 7.2 With Self-Organization Theory

**Similarities**: Both emphasize internal spontaneous order

**Differences**: Self-organization theory based on systems science; Hundun Management based on Daoist philosophy and religious belief.

### 7.3 With Communist Ideal

**Similarities**: "From each according to ability, to each according to need," eliminate private ownership, class extinction

**Differences**: Communism emphasizes productivity development and material abundance; Hundun Management emphasizes spiritual purification and Dao adherence.

## IX. Conclusion

Hundun Management is unique management philosophy emerging from early-21st-century China, integrating Daoist thought, Buddhist wisdom, communist ideals, and modern ecological concepts. It conducted small-scale but sustained practice in Lifechanyuan Second Home, providing real "anarchist order" case.

From management philosophy perspective, Hundun Management proposes extremely anti-traditional management model; theoretical value lies in fundamental questioning of traditional management paradigms. From social experiment perspective, Hundun Management practice provides valuable empirical material for utopian research, communal studies.

Hundun Management's founder proclaims: "Humanity's way out lies in Hundun Management; besides this, humanity has no way out." Whether this prophecy realizes awaits time and broader practice verification. But at minimum, Hundun Management provides humanity with a case worth researching for exploring new management models.
