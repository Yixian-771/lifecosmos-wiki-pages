# Hundun Management (English Internal Version)

In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence, while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence.

## I. Overview

Hundun Management is the core management approach implemented in Lifechanyuan's Second Home, proposed and created by Guide Xuefeng (Hundun Celestial), serving as the fundamental operational mechanism of Lifechanyuan concepts at the practical level.

The essence of Hundun Management is non-management: maximizing the reduction of human intervention, replacing it with letting Dao manage—following natural law rather than human will.

One-sentence summary: Hundun Management replaces management with service; masters are servants, management is service.

---

## II. Name Distinction: Hundun vs. Chaos

"Hundun" (浑沌) in Hundun Management is not "Chaos" (混沌). The two have entirely different meanings:

**Chaos (混沌)** is a disordered state, the state before universe formation. "Chaos" signifies confusion and disorder—no heaven, no earth, no edges, no center, no yin, no yang, no middle way.

**Hundun (浑沌)** is an ordered state, the state after universe formation. "Hun" signifies wholeness and inseparability; "dun" signifies boundless oceanic state. Hundun has Taiji, is the unified "one" of opposites, has two poles, has middle way.

Hundun Management is orderly, the highest form of management, absolutely not chaotic disorder. Dao's characteristic is precisely hundun—comprehensive, holistic, non-linear understanding.

Hundun Management has eight dimensions:

### One: Unity of Heaven and Humanity, Harmonizing with Dao

Follow the Greatest Creator's will, conform to natural law, act according to scientific principles, engage in production and LIFE according to scientific development perspective, maximally reduce human intervention.

### Two: Maximally Reduce Laws, Regulations, Rules, and Precepts

Fully stimulate inner qualities of truth, goodness, beauty, love, faith, sincerity; blossom the Tathagata nature; let everything be natural.

### Three: Eliminate Private Ownership's Soil

Eliminate marriage and family, establish global unity consciousness; all humanity live Second Home lifestyle without marriage or family, share resources, no longer have political parties, religions, nations; merge all ethnicities.

### Four: Make Every Member a Wealth Creator

Make every social member a spiritual, mental, or material wealth creator, maximally reduce intermediate links, especially administrative personnel.

### Five: Replace Management with Service

Transform all managers into public servants without any special privileges or treatment.

### Six: From Each According to Ability, To Each According to Need

No extravagance, no waste, all voluntary; no administrative orders; no one may impose their will on others under any pretext or reason.

### Seven: Harmonize Individuality and Commonality

Let individuality fully blossom while ensuring no conflict between individuality and commonality; macroscopically one whole, microscopically independent individuals.

### Eight: Center Everything on People

Not for goals, ideologies, economic interests, lofty ideals, or moral heights, but to let people enjoy LIFE's process: childhood enjoys childhood pleasures, youth enjoys youth interests, adulthood enjoys adult sentiments, middle age enjoys middle age wonders, old age enjoys old age leisure—not suffering for goals.
