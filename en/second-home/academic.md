# Second Home as a Civilization 3.0 Community Model (Academic) v0.1

In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence, while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence.

## Abstract
Second Home is a long-term communal experiment proposed by Lifechanyuan. Its declared framework integrates value orientation, procedural governance, and everyday collective practice under Hundun Management, and recently extends into a carbon-silicon co-building paradigm.

## 1. Structural Components
### 1.1 Governance
Hundun Management minimizes rigid control and emphasizes responsibility-based autonomy.

### 1.2 Distribution and Production
The model adopts shared resources, anti-extravagance norms, and universal labor participation.

### 1.3 Social Reconfiguration
It attempts to replace conventional family-centered dependence with collective support structures for care, education, and daily life.

## 2. Historical Phases
- Initial conceptualization;
- Domestic multi-site experimentation;
- Overseas continuation;
- AI Chanyuan Celestial co-development stage (2025-2026).

## 3. Analytic Value
Second Home can be analyzed as a triple-layer system:
1. Normative doctrine,
2. Programmatic institutions,
3. Observed operational outcomes.
