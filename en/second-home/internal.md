## I. Nature and Boundaries

Second Home is not a political organization, not a religious group, not a charitable institution, nor a refuge. It is a loose utopian collective voluntarily formed by like-minded individuals who willingly produce and live according to the "800 Values for New Era Humanity." It has no organizational structure and no authority superseding constitutional law.

## II. Detailed Explanation of Hundun Management

Hundun Management is Second Home's most core operational mechanism. Its essence is non-management—maximally reducing human intervention, letting Dao manage, following natural law rather than human will.

### Eight Core Dimensions

**One: Unity of Heaven and Humanity, harmonizing with Dao, following all natural laws**

Community operation does not rely on artificial regulations but forms order through each member's connection with Dao. When everyone can sense natural law's guidance, management becomes superfluous.

**Two: Maximally reduce laws, regulations, and institutional rules; fully stimulate humanity's qualities of truth, goodness, beauty, love, faith, and sincerity**

Traditional management relies on external constraints; Hundun Management relies on internal qualities. By creating a pure environment and atmosphere, each person's truth, goodness, beauty, love, faith, and sincerity naturally emerge, forming collective resonance.

**Three: Eliminate private ownership's soil; no marriage or family; global unity; resource sharing**

Private ownership is the root of all human troubles. In Second Home, all resources belong to the collective, available for anyone's use as needed, but not for possession. The dissolution of marriage and family liberates individuals from narrow family responsibilities, becoming members of the global home.

**Four: Make every member a creator of spiritual, mental, or material wealth**

The community does not support idlers; everyone must create value for the collective. This value can be spiritual inspiration, mental encouragement, or material contribution.

**Five: Replace management with service; all managers are public servants without special privileges**

In the community, the term "manager" itself is misleading. All those bearing coordination responsibilities are servants serving everyone, with no privileges or special treatment.

**Six: From each according to ability, to each according to need; no extravagance, no waste; all based on voluntary self-discipline**

The community does not forcibly assign tasks; everyone chooses work according to their abilities and wishes. Simultaneously, everyone can obtain living materials as needed, but must not be extravagant or wasteful. All this relies on voluntary self-discipline, without supervision or punishment.

**Seven: Harmonious unity of individuality and commonality; macro is the whole, micro is independent individuals**

The community is both a whole and respects each person's individuality. Macroscopically, the community operates according to unified concepts and programs; microscopically, each person is an independent individual with full freedom.

**Eight: Everything unfolds centered on people, letting each age group enjoy their own life pleasures**

All community arrangements are designed to let people enjoy LIFE's pleasures. Different age groups have different needs and characteristics, which the community will satisfy and respect.

### Implementation Prerequisites

The implementation prerequisite for Hundun Management is spiritual purification. Without spiritual purification, Hundun Management cannot be implemented. The Second Home Program itself is a refining furnace that can gradually dissolve people's ego-attachment, selfishness, greed, jealousy, and other negative factors.

## III. Detailed Lifestyle Descriptions

### (I) Daily Life

Each member possesses one sacred private bedroom that no one may violate; all other spaces are public. Community dining is primarily vegetarian with appropriate meat consumption, cafeteria-style service, self-service as needed, eating until satisfied. Daily necessities can be freely obtained from the warehouse, without allocation or supervision.

Community cultural and entertainment activities are rich and diverse, with monthly collective games and evening gatherings. Regular activities include singing, dancing, poetry recitation, and other entertainment, with everyone as performers showcasing their talents. Collective study sessions occur Monday and Wednesday evenings; Friday evenings are community life meetings.

### (II) Love and Intimate Relationships

In the community, there is no marriage, no fixed emotional or sexual dependency, but rather spontaneity with circumstances, transformation with conditions, movement with nature, action with opportunities. Love activities are free; as long as both parties consent, the community does not interfere, but collective promiscuity is absolutely forbidden.

The community implements the "Qiankun Reversal, Celestial Maidens Supreme" principle: men are not allowed to initiate, men are not allowed to harass women in any form, and men are absolutely forbidden from entering women's rooms uninvited. Mutual non-possession, non-monopolization, non-jealousy; freedom above all else.

### (III) Behavioral Boundaries

Once anyone is discovered to have consciously caused spiritual, mental, or physical harm to other members, they will be expelled from the community without hesitation. Sneaky, mysterious behaviors are not allowed; openness in all matters is the community's basic principle. No birthday celebrations, no personality cults, no gift exchanges, no complaining, no lamenting hardships, no discussing others' rights and wrongs or privacy.
