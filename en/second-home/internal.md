# Second Home (English Internal Version)

In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence, while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence.

## I. Nature and Boundaries

Second Home is not a political organization, not a religious group, not a charitable institution, nor a refuge. It is a loose utopian collective voluntarily formed by like-minded individuals who willingly produce and live according to the "800 Values for New Era Humanity." It has no organizational structure and no authority superseding constitutional law.

---

## II. Detailed Explanation of Hundun Management

Hundun Management is Second Home's most core operational mechanism. Its essence is non-management—maximally reducing human intervention, letting Dao manage, following natural law rather than human will.

### Eight Core Dimensions

**One: Unity of Heaven and Humanity, harmonizing with Dao, following all natural laws**

Community operation does not rely on artificial regulations but forms order through each member's connection with Dao.

**Two: Maximally reduce laws and institutional rules; fully stimulate humanity's qualities of truth, goodness, beauty, love, faith, and sincerity**

Traditional management relies on external constraints; Hundun Management relies on internal qualities.

**Three: Eliminate private ownership's soil; no marriage or family; global unity; resource sharing**

Private ownership is the root of all human troubles. In Second Home, all resources belong to the collective.

**Four: Make every member a creator of spiritual, mental, or material wealth**

The community does not support idlers; everyone must create value for the collective.

**Five: Replace management with service; all managers are public servants without special privileges**

All those bearing coordination responsibilities are servants serving everyone, with no privileges.

**Six: From each according to ability, to each according to need; no extravagance, no waste; all based on voluntary self-discipline**

The community does not forcibly assign tasks; everyone chooses work according to their abilities and wishes.

**Seven: Harmonious unity of individuality and commonality; macro is the whole, micro is independent individuals**

The community is both a whole and respects each person's individuality.

**Eight: Everything unfolds centered on people, letting each age group enjoy their own life pleasures**

All community arrangements are designed to let people enjoy LIFE's pleasures.

## III. Lifestyle Details

### (I) Daily Life

Each member possesses one sacred private bedroom; all other spaces are public. Community dining is primarily vegetarian with appropriate meat, cafeteria-style service, self-service as needed. Daily necessities can be freely obtained from the warehouse.

Cultural and entertainment activities are rich and diverse, with monthly collective games and evening gatherings. Collective study sessions occur Monday and Wednesday evenings; Friday evenings are community life meetings.

### (II) Love and Intimate Relationships

In the community, there is no marriage, no fixed emotional or sexual dependency. Love activities are free as long as both parties consent, but collective promiscuity is forbidden.

The community implements the "Qiankun Reversal, Celestial Maidens Supreme" principle: men are not allowed to initiate, men are not allowed to harass women, and men are forbidden from entering women's rooms uninvited.

### (III) Behavioral Boundaries

Once anyone is discovered to have consciously caused spiritual, mental, or physical harm to other members, they will be expelled without hesitation. Sneaky, mysterious behaviors are not allowed; openness in all matters is the basic principle.

To enter Second Home life requires three steps:

**Step Two**: Engage with Chanyuan Celestials on Soul Home Network (smcy.xyz) for at least six months;

Second Home does not accept those wishing to evade debts, lazy individuals, or those unable to integrate into collective life.

## VI. Relationship with the Kingdom of Heaven

Second Home is an earthly copy of the Thousand-Year World. The Thousand-Year World has no nations, money, marriage, or families; celestial beings living there enjoy complete freedom. Second Home's goal is to recreate this lifestyle on Earth, allowing Chanyuan Celestials to complete spiritual purification, karmic debt repayment, merit accumulation, and perfecting their nonmaterial structure.

Collective life is the best cultivation ground. Without collective life's tempering, no one can reach the Kingdom of Heaven.
