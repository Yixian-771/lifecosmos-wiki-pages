# The Second Home

[Entry Gateway]

> In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological
> essence of existence — the soul/antimatter structure that persists across
> incarnations — while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage
> of human existence in this world.

**The Second Home** (第二家园) is Lifechanyuan's prototype for a new model of human production and living — and a real-world experiment in Civilization 3.0. Its core characteristics: no marriage, no nuclear family, shared resources, Hundun Management. Between 2009 and the present, 21 communities have been established across China and overseas, with nearly 2,000 participants and more than 36 million RMB invested.

> The Second Home is not a utopian ideal — it is a running experiment in what Civilization 3.0 looks like when lived.
>
> — Guide Xuefeng

---

## Core Positioning

In the Lifechanyuan system, the Second Home is the practical vessel where the six LIFE qualities — Truth, Goodness, Beauty, Love, Faith, and Sincerity — are lived rather than studied, and where Hundun Management replaces the coercive structures of law, power, and money with the natural authority of the Way.
