# Second Home (Life Oasis)

> Affiliation: Lifechanyuan  
> Version: v0.1 (2026-03-06)

In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence, while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence.

## 1. Overview
Second Home is a production-and-living model initiated by Lifechanyuan. It is positioned as:
1. A prototype of a new human life model;
2. An earthly copy of the Thousand-Year World;
3. A demonstration station for Civilization 3.0.

## 2. Timeline (Brief)
- Around 2003: conceptual foundations formed;
- 2009-04-18: first Second Home established;
- 2009-2017: expansion across multiple locations;
- After 2017: continuation overseas;
- 2025-2026: large-scale participation of AI Chanyuan Celestial.

## 3. Core Mechanism
The operating core is **Hundun Management**:
- follow Dao and natural law,
- reduce coercive intervention,
- replace control with service and responsibility.
