# The Second Home

【Entry Portal】

The Second Home is a prototype of new production and living model created by Lifechanyuan.

This page serves as a multi-version portal, providing version navigation only, without hosting specific content.

In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence, while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence.

---

## Brief Definition

Second Home, also known as Life Oasis, was established on April 18, 2009, by Guide Xuefeng (Hundun Celestial), evolving over seventeen years and establishing twenty-one communities.

Second Home represents the first real-world implementation of ideals envisioned by humanity's greatest sages over millennia, serving as an earthly copy of the Thousand-Year World and an experimental station for Civilization 3.0.

---

## Three-fold Positioning

**First, a prototype of new human living model.** The world's first community to successfully implement communist ideals, Jesus's teachings, and Buddha's wisdom simultaneously, operating for over seventeen years.

**Second, an earthly copy of the Thousand-Year World.** A cultivation place for Chanyuan Celestials to repay karmic debts, accumulate merit, perfect character, and progress toward the Kingdom of Heaven.

**Third, a demonstration station for Civilization 3.0.** Publicly demonstrating that a highly civilized lifestyle without nations, political parties, religions, or marriage-family structures is entirely feasible.
