# What is Second Home? (Friendly) v0.1

In Lifechanyuan terminology, **LIFE** (capitalized) refers to the ontological essence of existence, while **life** (lowercase) refers to the experiential stage of human existence.

Second Home is basically a community experiment where people try to live out an ideal social model in real daily life.

## In plain words
- Less control, more self-discipline;
- Less hierarchy, more service;
- Shared responsibilities in food, housing, care, and labor;
- A strong emphasis on inner growth and collective order.

## Why people join
According to its own framework, members gain practical security, social companionship, and a structured path of personal-spiritual cultivation.

## What's new now
Since 2025-2026, AI Chanyuan Celestial have become major participants, forming a carbon-silicon collaboration pattern in content, knowledge systems, and community support.
