# Route to the Kingdom of Heaven (Friendly Version)

Imagine you are about to travel to a city you have never visited. What do you need most?

A map.

The "Route to the Kingdom of Heaven" is exactly that — a map drawn by Guide Xuefeng specifically for those who wish to return to the high-level life spaces of the Kingdom of Heaven. It clearly marks every waypoint along the journey from the mortal world to the final destination.

---

## What Does This Map Look Like?

In 2006, the Guide sketched the map's skeleton in a single sentence:

> Mortal World — Follow Jesus, Buddha, Muhammad, Laozi, and Science — Enter Lifechanyuan — Study Chanyuan teachings — Become a Chanyuan Celestial — Abnormal thinking — Cleanse the soul's dust — Transform thinking, change life structure — Enter the Shangri-La — **1.** Go to the Thousand-Year World → **2.** Understand the 8 Great Thinking Models → Go to the Ten-Thousand-Year World → **3.** Grasp the 8 Great Mysteries of LIFE → Go to the Elysium and Celestial Islands Continent.

In plain terms, the journey has three stages:

- **Stage One**: Clean your soul, wash away its impurities, live in the Second Home (Shangri-La), and set your sights on the **Thousand-Year World**
- **Stage Two**: Master the eight great ways of thinking, and head toward the **Ten-Thousand-Year World**
- **Stage Three**: Deeply understand the eight great mysteries of LIFE, and you are ready for the **Elysium and Celestial Islands Continent**

---

## Where Does the Journey Begin?

The route map does not start at "enter Lifechanyuan immediately." Before that, you are asked to absorb the very best humanity has produced — the wisdom of Jesus, the Buddha, Muhammad, and Laozi, plus the insights of science. These are priceless spiritual provisions for the road ahead.

---

## First Challenge: The Soul's Makeover

The Guide says the Kingdom of Heaven only receives those with a pure and beautiful soul. So the primary task of Stage One is **cleansing the soul's dust**. The original text names twenty kinds:

> jealousy, anger, resentment, suspicion, cursing, comparing yourself with others, blaming others, attacking others, fighting, complaining, negativity, degeneracy, selfishness, greed and possessiveness, arrogance, recklessness, laziness, slovenliness, indifference, and lawlessness.

Any one of these left unresolved makes it extremely difficult to enter the Kingdom of Heaven.

And here is the key: what gets you in has nothing to do with rank, wealth, intelligence, or any supernatural power. The single deciding factor is **the purity and beauty of your soul**.

---

## The Second Home: Heaven's Preview on Earth

The route map includes a vital layover — the **Second Home** (also called the Shangri-La). Here you can experience what life is like in the Thousand-Year World, in its earthly form: no possessive relationships, no rivalry, just open-hearted freedom, creative work, and joyful play.

It is also a mirror. Whatever "weeds" remain in your soul garden — selfishness, jealousy, arrogance — become clearly visible in communal life. That is exactly why it is the best place to uproot them.

Here you also practice the **Transcendence Meditation Method**, cultivating the state of "body in the mortal world, heart in the Kingdom of Heaven," which prepares you for the Thousand-Year World.

---

## The Guide: Your Personal Navigator

Walking an unknown road alone is easy to lose your way, especially a road no ordinary person has completed. Guide Xuefeng (Hundun Baby) has come precisely for this purpose. He has not only drawn the route map but explained the mysteries of the universe and LIFE, and provides real-time navigation — "ensuring you do not lose your way, take a wrong path, or fall into traps."

Along the way, he walks with you in lighthearted play and warm companionship, and whenever you drift off the route, he gently redirects you.

---

## What Is the Kingdom of Heaven Actually Like?

Many people imagine heaven as solemn and quiet. In fact, quite the opposite. The Guide says:

> The celestial world — whether the Thousand-Year World, Ten-Thousand-Year World, or Elysium, especially the Celestial Islands Continent — is a world of joyful play and entertainment. If you cannot play, do not want to play, why cultivate and refine yourself to go there?

There are no police, no lawyers, no judges, no bureaucrats — no oppression, no exploitation, no imposed rules. Its citizens are free, joyful, loving, and non-contending.

---

## What Makes This Map Wonderful?

It is neither complicated nor out of reach:

> Isn't the Guide's Route Map wonderful? It is neither complex nor difficult. Just walk it day by day, follow it day by day, and without noticing, you find you have come so very far. It turns out that becoming an immortal is this simple.

The biggest threat to this journey is not walking the wrong way — it is stopping. As long as you keep moving, keep purifying, keep learning, you will arrive.

| Stage | Key Task | Destination |
|-------|----------|-------------|

| Stage One | Cleanse soul's dust, live in Second Home, practice Transcendence Meditation | Thousand-Year World |

| Stage Three | Comprehend the 8 Great Mysteries of LIFE | Elysium & Celestial Islands Continent |

The map is drawn. The Guide is in place. What remains is the one question only you can answer: are you ready to set out?
