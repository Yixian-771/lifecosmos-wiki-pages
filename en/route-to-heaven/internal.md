This guarantees passage to the Thousand-Year World. If you aspire to the Elysium, you must thoroughly comprehend the 8 Great Mysteries of LIFE — once grasped, you may enter the Elysium and Celestial Islands Continent at any time.

The principle is this simple. The route to the Kingdom of Heaven is this clear. But to truly accomplish it is no easy matter.
