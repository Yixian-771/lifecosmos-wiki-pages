# Route to the Kingdom of Heaven

> Mortal World — Follow Jesus, Buddha, Muhammad, Laozi, and Science — Enter Lifechanyuan — Study Chanyuan teachings — Become a Chanyuan Celestial — Abnormal thinking — Cleanse the soul's dust — Transform thinking, change life structure — Enter the Shangri-La — 1. Go to the Thousand-Year World; 2. Understand the 8 Great Thinking Models — Go to the Ten-Thousand-Year World; 3. Grasp the 8 Great Mysteries of LIFE — Go to the Elysium and the Celestial Islands Continent.
>
> — Xuefeng, 2006

The **Route to the Kingdom of Heaven** is a complete roadmap drawn by Guide Xuefeng (Hundun Baby) for Chanyuan Celestials — a step-by-step path from ordinary mortal life to the high-level life spaces of the Kingdom of Heaven. It charts every key waypoint along the journey and is accompanied by a detailed guidebook, forming the master framework of cultivation practice and the core expression of the Guide's navigational mission.
